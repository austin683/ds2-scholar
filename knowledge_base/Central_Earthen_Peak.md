#  Central Earthen Peak

* * *

##  Location:

behind a fog wall at the opposite to the windmill while can burnt down with torch   

##  Bosses:

  * None

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here respawns the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * [Pharros Lockstone](/Pharros+Lockstone "Dark Souls 2 Pharros Lockstone")
  * [Spell Quartz Ring](/Spell+Quartz+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Spell Quartz Ring")
  * [Mannikin](/Masked+Manikin "Dark Souls 2 Masked Manikin")
  * [Grave Warden](/Grave+Warden "Dark Souls 2 Grave Warden")
  * [Desert Sorceress](/Desert+Sorceress "Dark Souls 2 Desert Sorceress")

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here spawns the following new items/enemies:

  * None

##  Special Notes:

  * does not reset windwill
  * the only known location for respawning Desert Sorceress
