**Leydia White Set** is an Attire or Armor in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

  
  
  
  
  
_"Hood/Robe/Gloves of a Leydia Pyromancer._   
_Leydia apostles, in their conceit, occupied the Undead Crypt and misused death._   
_This invoked the ire of Fenito, who branded them as transgressors."_

###  Set Location

  * Dropped by [Leydia Pyromancers](/Leydia+Pyromancer "Dark Souls 2 Leydia Pyromancer") in [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt").

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
