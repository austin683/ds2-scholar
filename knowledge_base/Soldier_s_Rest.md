#  Soldiers' Rest

* * *

##  Location:

[Cardinal Tower](/Cardinal+Tower) -> After you obtained the [Soldiers Key](/Soldiers+Key) you have to go down the ladder in the ground and go right, and then left again into a room with a staircase, turn immediately right. There is a door in front of you that you can open with the Soldiers Key. Go down there and through the hall, leave the hall through the door and go over the little wooden planks. Go left till you find a hole in a wall to your left, go through there and then left. You now can see a little arch in front of you leading to the bonfire.   
  

##  Bosses:

  * The Last Giant
  * The Pursuer

  

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here will respawn the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * Leather Armor set
  * Old Ironclads
  * Hollow Wariors

  

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here spawns the following new items/enemies:

  * Item
  * Item
  * Dark Spirit Siegmeyer
