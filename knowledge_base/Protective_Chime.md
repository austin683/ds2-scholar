**Protective Chime** is a **[Sacred Chime](/Chimes "Dark Souls 2 Chimes")** and one of the available **[Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons")** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). Protective Chime **is a catalyst for miracles and hexes. To acquire it, one must find a chest behind a false wall in the [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep"), accessed through [Belfry Sol](/Belfry+Sol "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Sol"). The chime has slightly weaker hex power compared to the [Archdrake Chime](/Archdrake+Chime "Dark Souls 2 Archdrake Chime"), but its casting speed is very high. Its main strength lies in its swift casting, even though its scaling is average.

> Sacred chime commonly used by clerics of Lindelt. A catalyst for miracles and hexes.   
>   
>  A sacred chime serves to conduct the power of miracles, but in the past, something entirely different served the same purpose. 

### How to get Protective Chime in Dark Soul 2

  * Inside a chest behind a fake wall in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep"). Go through Belfry Sol and take the stairs down at the end, pass the corner and the fake wall will be on your right.

### DS2 Protective Chime Hints and Tips

  * Slightly worse hex power than the [Archdrake Chime](/Archdrake+Chime#fex "Dark Souls 2 Archdrake Chime#fex"), but with a very high casting speed.
  * 351 lightning bonus at 50 faith. Just 4 lower than clerics.
  * Stronger than clerics up until 39faith then cleric beats it at 43faith.
