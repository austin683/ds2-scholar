**Alonne Knight leggings** is a **legging** and a part of [Alonne Knight Set](/Alonne+Knight+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Alonne Knight leggings Description

_"Leggings worn by Alonne Knights._

_The bonds of the Alonne Knights, who served the Old Iron King, were mightier than the land's iron, but in the endthe knights were subsumed by the flames that brought the castle down."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Dropped by [Alonne Knight](/Alonne+Knight) and [Alonne Knight Captain](/Alonne+Knight+Captain).
