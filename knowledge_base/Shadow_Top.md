_"Used to hide in the cover of night. Those who are especially adept assassins are often hired as bodyguards._

_In an attempt to stave off the curse, King Vendrick hired shadowmen to put down the Hollows, but before long they were Hollowed themselves"_

### Location

  * Drop from [Suspicious Shadow](/Nimble+Shadow "Dark Souls 2 Nimble Shadow") (NG+) or with the use of a [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") at the [No Man's Wharf](/No+Man's+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No Man's Wharf") bonfire.
  * 2 shadows appear with the boss. Drop rate is "Rare" even with stacked item discovery and rusted coins.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Shadow Set](/Shadow+Set "Dark Souls 2 Shadow Set").
  * Upgraded from [Titanite](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard").
  * Can drop duplicates.
