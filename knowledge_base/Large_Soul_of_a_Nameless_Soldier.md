**Large Soul of a Nameless Soldier** is a type of [Consumable](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Large Soul of a Nameless Soldier** , a substantial soul from an Undead soldier who has succumbed to the fate of Hollowing. Use it to acquire many souls. The soul, even in undeath, remains the essence of life. When consumed, it imparts 1000 souls. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are consumable items categorized as remnants of fallen warriors, consuming souls collects a certain number of souls that is added to the player's stock. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are required for players to become stronger in the game by raising their soul level as well as buying and upgrading their equipment.

> Large soul of a nameless Undead soldier who has long ago gone Hollow.  
> Use to acquire many souls.  
> The soul is the source of all life, and even in undeath, or hollowing, the mind seeks souls.
