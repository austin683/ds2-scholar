**Masterless Glencour** is an [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**. Masterless Glencour** is a summonable NPC phantom, who can assist the player in various locations throughout the game. He is a warrior who appears to be without a master or allegiance, hence his name. **Masterless Glencour** is clad in a suit of armor and wields a greatsword, making him a formidable ally in battle.

### Masterless Glencour Information in Dark Souls 2

**Masterless Glencour** is an NPC summon for boss fights. Judging by his appearance and name, he could be one of the last surviving [Royal soldiers](/Royal+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Royal Soldier"). He is very helpful during the [Dragonrider](/Dragonrider "Dark Souls 2 Dragonrider") encounter, where he is capable of defeating the boss on his own. Also, able to summon him for the [Old Dragonslayer](/Old+Dragonslayer "Dark Souls 2 Old Dragonslayer") fight in which he has a small chance of parrying the Old Dragonslayer.   
  
In the [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King") DLC, he is also ready to help you if you obtained the [Eye of the Priestess](/Eye+of+the+Priestess "Dark Souls 2 Eye of the Priestess"). His previously invisible summon sign can be found on the bridge leading to the large Gate to the Kingdom, after the first bonfire. He will assist you in the fight against [Aava, the King's Pet](/Aava%2C+the+King%27s+Pet "Dark Souls 2 Aava%2C the King%27s Pet").   
  
He uses a [Zweihander](/Zweihander "Dark Souls 2 Zweihander"), [Varangian Shield](/Varangian+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Varangian Shield"), [Hollow Soldier Helm](/Hollow+Soldier+Helm "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Soldier Helm"), [Heide Knight Chainmail](/Heide+Knight+Chainmail "Dark Souls 2 Heide Knight Chainmail"), [Royal Swordsman Gloves](/Royal+Swordsman+Gloves "Dark Souls 2 Royal Swordsman Gloves") and [Leggings](/Royal+Swordsman+Leggings "Dark Souls 2 Royal Swordsman Leggings"). His appearance seems to be that of a Royal Soldier who has long been on the road and has picked up additional equipment on his journey.

### Masterless Glencour Encounters in Dark Souls 2

  * [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide's+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide's Tower of Flame"). Near the [Dragonrider](/Dragonrider "Dark Souls 2 Dragonrider") boss fog.
  * [Belfry Luna](/Belfry+Luna "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Luna"). Under the second set of stairs leading to the [Belfry Gargoyle](/Belfry+Gargoyle "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Gargoyle") boss fight.
  * [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce"). On the bridge leading into the Kingdom.

### Dark Souls 2 Masterless Glencour Trivia

  * As soon as he's summoned he will perform the "Bow" gesture beside this he will also use the "Warmup" often while waiting for you.
  * Recommended kill the Heide's Knight in chest before summoning Glencour in the Dragonrider boss fight. He will go fight immediately with the Knight after summoning, taking risk fall off corner.
  * Upon defeating Aava he will perform the "Warcry" gesture.
  * During the fights against the [Old Dragonslayer](/Old+Dragonslayer "Dark Souls 2 Old Dragonslayer") and the Dragon Rider, **Masterless Glencour** is extremely helpful, as he has an extremely powerful sword slam attack that will stumble both enemies, leaving them wide open to attack.
  * In Scholar of the first Sin you can also summon Glencour at [Belfry Luna](/Belfry+Luna "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Luna").
  * Masterless Glencour fights in a way very similar to from the original **Dark Souls**.
