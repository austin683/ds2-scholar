**Black Witch Veil** is a [Helm](/Helms "Dark Souls 2 Helms") in Dark Souls 2. It is part of the [Black Witch Set](/Black+Witch+Set "Dark Souls 2 Black Witch Set").

_"Veil worn by Zullie the Witch, seducer of Alva the Wayfarer. Boasts excellent magic defense._

_When Zullie the witch learned of Alva's dedication to Saint Serrate she used all manner of tricks and deceit to ruin him, but in the end she would spend her life with him, supporting his endeavors."_

### Location

  * Rare drop off of the [Dual Avelyn Bell Keeper](/Dual+Avelyn+Bell+Keeper "Dark Souls 2 Dual Avelyn Bell Keeper") enemy in [Belfry Sol](/Belfry+Sol "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Sol").
  * (Sotfs) Guaranteed drop from the [Gutter Denizen](/Gutter+Denizen "Dark Souls 2 Gutter Denizen") invader who appears after lighting every sconce in [the Gutter](/The+Gutter "Dark Souls 2 The Gutter").

### Black Witch Veil Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * In Sotfs, it possesses 1,000 [Curse](/Curse "Dark Souls 2 Curse") resistance, granting effective immunity to Curse while equipped.
