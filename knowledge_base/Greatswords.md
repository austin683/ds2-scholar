**Greatswords** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are a type of [Weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") that are usually identified by having a robust move set, offering wide and horizontal slashes as well as strong thrusthing attacks. Greatswords are some of the most used Weapons in Dark Souls 2, they're a bit slow and heavy as opposed to wielding a [Straight Sword](/Straight+Swords "Dark Souls 2 Straight Swords"), they also consume more stamine each swing, but they make up for it in terms of damage output. To wield Greatswords, you would need to invest mostly on [Strength](/Strength "Dark Souls 2 Strength"), they would also require a moderate amount of stat investment in order to maximize its potential depending on the type of Greatsword you have. This page covers a list of all Greatswords in Dark Souls 2.
