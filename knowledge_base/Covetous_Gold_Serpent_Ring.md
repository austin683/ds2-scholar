**Covetous Gold Serpent Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Covetous Gold Serpent Ring** , a golden band bearing a snake, the avatar of Zinder, the god of desire. Greed is often condemned, but only a coward shies away from seizing every opportunity. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A gold ring depicting the snake, both the servant and manifestation of the god of desire, Zinder.
> 
> Greed is traditionally viewed as a vice, but only a coward sees every chance as something to fear."

### Dark Souls 2 Covetous Gold Serpent Ring Effects

**Durability** :

  * 0 = 90
  * +1 = 70
  * +2 = 75

**Weight** :

  * +0=1.0
  * +1=1.2
  * +2=1.5

Effect

  * Increases [Item Discovery](/Item+Discovery "Dark Souls 2 Item Discovery") by 50
  * +1 = 75
  * +2 = 100

### Covetous Gold Serpent Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep") \- After the Smelter Demon, go into the room with the moving platforms, and go up the ladder in the middle of the room. Take a right to go up the stairs, and go up the ladder. There, you will find a few Greatbow Alonne Knights. Drop down the platforms until you reach a lava-filled cauldron, the corpse inside contains the ring. **Increases drop rate by 0.50%**
  * +1: Received from [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") in the [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep"). Select his talk option after spending at least 10,000 souls. Dropped if you kill [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") **Increases drop rate by 0.75%**
  * +2: [Belfry Gargoyle](/Belfry+Gargoyle "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Gargoyle") (NG+ or after you use a Bonfire Ascetic on the [Belfry Luna](/Belfry+Luna "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Luna"): [Upper Ramparts](/Upper+Ramparts "Dark Souls 2 Upper Ramparts") bonfire) **Increases drop rate by 1%** (NG+ Only)

### DS2 Covetous Gold Serpent Ring Tips and Notes 

  * Effects stack with other sources of Item Discovery.
  * 0.5%, 0.75% and 1% bonus multiplied by number of drop entries to get a drop.
