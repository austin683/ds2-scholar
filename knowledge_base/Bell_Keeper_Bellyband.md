_"An old bellyband that are oddly comfortable. Belonged to a Bell Keeper._

_To this day, the forbidden love of the Prince of Alken and the Princess of Venn manipulates these marionettes._

_Surely they never imagined that their dolls would outlast their own kingdoms."_

### Location

  * Defeat 100 Hosts when invading as a Grey Phantom or 100 Black Phantoms that invade the Belfries while in the Bell Keeper covenant.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Bell Keeper Set](/Bell+Keeper+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab).
