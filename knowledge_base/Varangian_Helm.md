_" Helm worn by Varagians, that terrorized nearby seas. The coastline stretches far Drangleic. Beyond this Northern Sea is an unexplored continent, said to be the home of things inhuman."_

### Location

  * Dropped in [No-Man's Wharf](/No-Man%27s+Wharf) by Varangian Bandits.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Varangian Set](/Varangian+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Upgrades#Ore).
