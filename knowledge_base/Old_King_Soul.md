**Old King Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. This once magnificent soul retains its influence over the land, enduring the eons though now diminished. Consume to amass numerous souls or employ it in the creation of something of great value. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the ineffable. This once magnificent soul continues to exert influence over the land, even after the eons have reduced it to these remnants.  
> Use it to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Old King Soul Usage in DKS2

  * Can be used to gain 75,000 souls
  * Traded with [Straid of Olaphis ](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") with 10,000 souls for the [Blinding Bolt](/Blinding+Bolt "Dark Souls 2 Blinding Bolt") Miracle.
  * Traded with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") with 10,000 souls for the [Dragonslayer Greatbow](/Dragonslayer+Greatbow "Dark Souls 2 Dragonslayer Greatbow").

### Dark Souls Old King Soul Location

  * Dropped by killing the [Old Iron King](/Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Old Iron King") on NG+ (or using [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") to make world NG+)

### Old King Soul Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * It is possible that it is the soul of Gwyn, Lord of Cinder. The fact that it can be traded for Blinding Bolt, the description of which mentions the "God of Sun", points towards Gwyn.
