**Jester's Tights** is a **Legging** and a part of [Jester's Set](/Jester's+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Jester's Tights Description

"A boldly-colored jester's cap/robe/gloves/tights. A nice bit of fun to try on..."  
"Jesters are more than festival fixtures; some have a second face, hidden from public view."

Effect: Reduces falling damage

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Bought from Magerold of Lanafir inside the Iron Keep.
  * Good all around armor with a low weight

### Notes

  * Tights Effect: Reduce falling damage
  * Requires 14 Dex. to wear.
  * Helm increases item discovery
  * Gloves increases souls gained(10%)
  * Chestpiece blocks critical hits (cannot backstab)
  * Legs reduce fall damage.
  * Upgraded with normal Titanite.
  * Worn by Jester Thomas
