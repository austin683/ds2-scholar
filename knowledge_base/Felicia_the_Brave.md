**Felicia the Brave** is an [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**. Felicia the Brave** is a phantom, and can be summoned by players to aid them against enemies or bosses.

### Felicia the Brave Information in Dark Souls 2

  * Felicia the Brave is an NPC you can summon in the Shrine of Amana.
  * In Scholars of the First Sin she is also available as a summon for the Ruined Sentinels boss fight in the Lost Bastille.
  * She wields a [Heide Greatlance](/Heide+Greatlance "Dark Souls 2 Heide Greatlance") and a [Tower Shield](/Tower+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Tower Shield").
  * She has been known to inflict friendly-fire ()
  * It is best to equip miracles that heal allies before summon her. She tends to charge forward on her own, making it hard to keep until the boss unless you heal her.

### Felicia the Brave Encounters in Dark Souls 2

[Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana").  _Dark Souls II:_ Found in the hut just past the Ogre, from the **Crumbled Ruins** bonfire.  
_Dark Souls II: Scholar of the First Sin_ : Found near to **Rhoy's Resting Place** bonfire.  
[Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille"). Found in the room with the barred windows, explosive barrels and a gate giving access to the [Ruin Sentinels](/Ruin+Sentinel "Dark Souls 2 Ruin Sentinel") passage.
