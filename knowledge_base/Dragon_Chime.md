**Dragon Chime** is a **[Sacred Chime](/Chimes "Dark Souls 2 Chimes")** and one of the available **[Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons")** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**. **Dragon Chime** is a sacred chime shaped like a dragon, and it serves as a catalyst for both [Miracles](/Miracles "Dark Souls 2 Miracles") and [Hexes](/Hexes "Dark Souls 2 Hexes"). The chime is best when infused with Lightning using a [Boltstone](/Boltstone "Dark Souls 2 Boltstone"), providing the highest lightning attack in the game. Its high casting speed makes it ideal for casting powerful spells like [Sunlight Spears](/Sunlight+Spear "Dark Souls 2 Sunlight Spear") and [Blinding Bolt](/Blinding+Bolt "Dark Souls 2 Blinding Bolt"). 

> A sacred chime shaped as a dragon. A catalyst for miracles and hexes.   
>   
>  This chime sat long in the Dark Chasm, but still one senses a sublime purity.   
>   
>  Only those with the deepest faith can wield this chime, but they will be rewarded with greatly amplified spells. 

### How to get Dragon Chime in Dark Souls 2

  * Defeat the boss [Darklurker](/Darklurker "Dark Souls 2 Darklurker") and then talk to [Darkdiver Grandahl](/Darkdiver+Grandahl "Dark Souls 2 Darkdiver Grandahl").
  * Drops from killing [Darkdiver Grandahl](/Darkdiver+Grandahl "Dark Souls 2 Darkdiver Grandahl").

### DS2 Dragon Chime Hints and Tips

  * It is wise to infuse this chime with Lightning through the use of a [Boltstone](/Boltstone "Dark Souls 2 Boltstone") at [Steady Hand Mcduff](/Steady+Hand+Mcduff "Dark Souls 2 Steady Hand Mcduff"). This creates, with the required 50 Faith, the highest lightning AR of any chime in the game.
  * Among the miracle-focused chimes, the **Dragon Chime** has the highest casting speed in game. Excellent for throwing [Sunlight Spears](/Sunlight+Spear "Dark Souls 2 Sunlight Spear") and [Blinding Bolts](/Blinding+Bolt "Dark Souls 2 Blinding Bolt").
  * Upgraded with: [Twinkling Titanite](/Ore "Dark Souls 2 Ore")
  * 473 lightning bns with 50 faith.
