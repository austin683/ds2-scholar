### Hollow Infantry Gloves Description

_"Gloves worn by Hollowed infantry."_  
_"A piece of basic, minimal equipment. Worse yet, they're worn and nearly falling apart. Not recommended unless your options are spent. "_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Drop from [Hollow Infantry](/Hollow+Infantry).

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shard, Large Titanite Shard, Titanite Chunk, Titanite Slab](/Ores)
  * The Infantry Set is the exact same weight, but is superior in every other way.
