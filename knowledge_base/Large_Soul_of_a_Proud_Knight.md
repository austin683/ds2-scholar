**Large Soul of a Proud Knight** is a type of [Consumable](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Large Soul of a Proud Knight** , the substantial essence of a once-proud Undead knight, now ensnared by Hollowing. Consume to amass a substantial number of souls. In undeath, as in life, the yearning for souls persists. Yields 3000 souls when consumed. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are consumable items categorized as remnants of fallen warriors, consuming souls collects a certain number of souls that is added to the player's stock. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are required for players to become stronger in the game by raising their soul level as well as buying and upgrading their equipment.

> Large soul of a proud Undead knight who has long ago gone Hollow.  
> Use to acquire many souls. The soul is the source of all life, and even in undeath, or hollowing, the mind seeks souls.

### Large Soul of a Proud Knight Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Grants 3000 souls when consumed.
