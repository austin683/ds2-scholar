**Halberds** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are a type of [Weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") that's similar to [Spears](/Spears "Dark Souls 2 Spears"), it is most effective when the tip or blade of the Halberd hits the enemy. Halberds also have a long reach, most of their movesets consists of slashing attacks, which covers a wider range and good when faced against multiple opponents. Halberds are slower than Spears, but they deal more damage. Some Halberds can also do thrusting attacks, and those that do can also be used while guarding with a [Shield](/Shields "Dark Souls 2 Shields"). This page covers a list of all Halberds in Dark Souls 2.
