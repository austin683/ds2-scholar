**Thunder Quartz Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). The **Thunder Quartz Ring** , a token for Melfian Magic Academy students meeting specific standards, offers enhanced lightning defense through its lightning quartz. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A ring bestow upon students of a certain standard at the Melfian Magic Academy. It's lightning quartz increases lightning defense. Pyromancer Glocken crafted so many of these replicas that they became widespread, such that his fellow magicians harshly scolded him for undermining lessons gained from harsh training.

### Dark Souls 2 Thunder Quartz Ring Effects

**Durability** :

  * +0 = 140
  * +1 = 110
  * +2 = 90
  * +3 = 75

**Weight** :

1.0

**Effects:**

  * Increases lightning defense by 50
  * +1 = 80
  * +2 = 100
  * +3 = 150

### Thunder Quartz Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Regular: Sold by [Rosabeth of Melfia](/Rosabeth+of+Melfia "Dark Souls 2 Rosabeth of Melfia") for 2,000 souls.
  * Ring +1: Found in a chest in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep") that can be reached after using a [Pharros' Lockstone](/Pharros+Lockstone "Dark Souls 2 Pharros Lockstone") or after going through [Belfry Sol](/Belfry+Sol "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Sol").
  * Ring +2: 1 sold by [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan "Dark Souls 2 Royal Sorcerer Navlaan") for 8,400 souls when it becomes possible to buy items from him.
  * Ring +3: DLC [Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King"). Near the second bonfire in [Shulva](/Shulva+-+Sanctum+City "Dark Souls 2 Shulva - Sanctum City"), on a corpse in one of the moving pillars that you use to get to the second bonfire. In the second closest pillar to the bonfire. On the higher ledge of the pillar, the ledge under it has a corpse with two Darknight Stones.
