# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Rotten+PVP

Psn: Octopus_Gang   

####  Description: Each attack you land replenishes your health. Slice your enemy down and make his pain your rejuvenation.

###  Starting class: Knight

Stats: Sl 135- Sl 85   

  * VGR- 40 - 25
  * END- 40 - 20 - Endurance is needed last.
  * VIT- 35 - 20 - Vitality is needed the earliest
  * ATN- Base
  * STR- 26 - 26 - 26 to use the [Large Club](/Large+Club) in PVE
  * DEX-9 - 9
  * ADP- 25 - 20
  * INT- Base
  * FTH- Base

  

###  Equipment:

  * Right Hand: [Butcher's Knife](/Butcher%27s+Knife) Any element, or poison.
  * Left Hand: [Parrying Dagger](/Parrying+Dagger) +0 Optional.
  * Armour: Under 70%
  * Rings: [Ring of Steel Protection](https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Ring+of+Steel+Protection), [Old Leo Ring](/Old+Leo+Ring), [Ring of Blades](/Ring+of+Blades), [Old Sun Ring](/Old+Sun+Ring)

  
Butcher's Knife uses [petrified dragon bones](/Petrified+Dragon+Bone). You should possibly infuse this with poison only because, it does heal you every hit so when pvping it is a good length fight. You will most likely poison your opponent. When fighting this weapon has a good variety of moves one hand and two hand.
