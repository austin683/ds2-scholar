**Vitality** is an [Attribute](/Stats "Dark Souls 2 Stats") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Vitality** determines your character's equipment load, physical defence, and poison resistance. Attributes can be increased by assigning points into them when [Leveling Up](/Level "Dark Souls 2 Level").

### Vitality Information

An attribute that determines your maximum equipment load.

  * **Raises equipment load:** 1.5 equip load per point (0-29), 1 equip load per point (30-49), 0.5 equip load per point (50-70), 0.5 equip load per two points (71-99).
  * **Raises physical defense:** (see [**Strength](/Strength "Dark Souls 2 Strength")**for physical defense details)**
  * Raises poison resistance**
  * Slightly increases HP for each point: +2 HP (0-20), +1 HP (21-50), +0 HP (51-99)
  * The Swordsman begins with the lowest Vitality at 4 and the Bandit starts with the highest Vitality at 11.
  * In order to maintain a fast roll your current equipment load must be 70% or less.
  * Maximum equipment load can be increased further by wearing the [Royal Soldier's Ring](/Royal+Soldier%27s+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Royal Soldier%27s Ring"), [Dragon Rings](/First+Dragon+Ring "Dark Souls 2 First Dragon Ring"), [Penal Mask](/Penal+Mask "Dark Souls 2 Penal Mask") or [Aurous Set](/Aurous+Set "Dark Souls 2 Aurous Set").
  * Base stat at SL 0 is 31.
