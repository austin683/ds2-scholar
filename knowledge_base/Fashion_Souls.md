# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Fashion+Souls

**Fashion Souls** in **Dark Souls** 2 refers to aesthetically desirable and cosplay outfits that show player creativity for [Character Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds"). This page showcases community-created outfit combinations. You can add your own fashion by replicating the formatting below.

## Fashion Souls: Dark Souls 2

  * In order to edit the section, click the pencil button on the top right where it states "edit".
  * You need a [Fextralife Account](https://fextralife.com/forums/ucp.php?mode=register "Fextralife Account") to upload images
  * Images should be reduced to a maximum width of 250px for the thumbnail
  * Please name images in all lowercase, without spaces: name_of_your_fashion.jpg (for example)
