**Hard Leather Boots** is a **legging** and a part of [Hard Leather Set](/Hard+Leather+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Hard Leather Boots Description

"Helmet made of iron."  
"Armor/Gauntlets/Boots made of sturdy leather.  
A commonplace piece of armor. No embellishments. Very solid."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Starting gear for the Warrior Class
  * Sold by Maughlin the Armourer in Majula.

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with Titanite Shard, Large Titanite Shard, Titanite Chunk, Titanite Slab.
