#  Inside's Yolo Build

  

  * SL 150+
  * Vgr 40 / End 20 / Vit 30 / Atn 13 / Str 50 / Dex 18 / Adp 20 / Int base / Faith base
  * Rings: Cloranthy +2, Third Dragon/Royal Soldier +2, Ring of Blades +2, Ring of Giants +2, etc.
  * Gear: King's Crown +5, King's Armor +5, Engraved Gaunts +5, etc leggings (Throne Watcher +5)
  * Weapon: King's Ultra Greatsword +5
  * Alt Weapons: Pyromancy Flame +10, Dragonrider's Bow +5
  * No Shield

  
You're the boss. You have the crown, the cape, and collar trimmed in fur. You do 450+175 damage before buffs. You'll 1-2-3 shot players with your weapon. You buff it with Flame Weapon and catch people off guard with Fire Tempest. You can shoot people for 1/5th their life per arrow while you roll around. YOLO
