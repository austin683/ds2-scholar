**Faintstone** is a type of [Upgrade Material](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Faintstone** , a transformed version of titanite. Employ it to infuse weapons with magic or to grant magic resistance to shields. Sorcerers at the Melfian Magic Academy once endeavored to enchant titanite with diverse elements, though their attempts are rumored to have met with failure. [Upgrade Materials](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades") are items that are used to strengthen the statistical value and to increase the effect of a certain piece of equipment.

> An altered state of titanite. Use to imbue weapons with magic, or to provide magic reduction to shields  
> Sorcerers at the Melfian Magic Academy once attempted to imbue titanite with various elements, but are said to have failed. Someone must have succeeded though; what else would explain this stone?

### Faintstone Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Can only be used at Blacksmith [Steady Hand McDuff](/Steady+Hand+McDuff "Dark Souls 2 Steady Hand McDuff").
  * Imbues weapons with magic or provides magic reduction to shields.

### DKS2 Faintstone Location

  * One of the items that can be received from The [Sparkling Sisters Dyna and Tillo](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo") in exchange for a [Smooth and Silky Stone](/Smooth+%26+Silky+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Smooth %26 Silky Stone").
  * One can be obtained in the [Dragon Shrine](/Dragon+Shrine "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Shrine"), on the corpse near the Priest who casts the lightning orbs at you.
  * [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros"), first Pharros' contraption after going up the ladder will unlock a path below it with a chest containing one and a [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * 3 are available for purchase from [Stone Trader Chloanne](/Stone+Trader+Chloanne "Dark Souls 2 Stone Trader Chloanne") after having the King's Ring.

### Faintstone Drops from in DKS2

  * [Basilisks](/Basilisk "Dark Souls 2 Basilisk") (Early farming in the game possible from the 1st Basilisk that spawns after depetrifying Rosabeth on the route to Shaded Woods. There is a very convenient bonfire located in the same room, allowing for farming)
  * [Leydia Witches](/Leydia+Witch "Dark Souls 2 Leydia Witch")
  * [Leydia Pyromancers](/Leydia+Pyromancer "Dark Souls 2 Leydia Pyromancer") ( Can be farmed infinitely by ringing the bell before the boss fog gate. )
  * Dragon Knights ( From Dragon Shrine )
