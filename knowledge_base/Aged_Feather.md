**Aged Feather** is a special tool item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Aged Feather** an aged bird feather. Returns the player to the last rested bonfire. Can be used repeatedly, as long as you are not over the weight limit. This item has unlimited uses. [Tools](/Tools "Dark Souls 2 Tools") are unique, reusable items which assist the player during various facets of gameplay ranging from basic communication to assisting in boss encounters.

> An aged bird feather. Return to last bonfire rested at.  
> Can be used repeatedly. The child of the dragon, sequestered away from the world, imagined a world of boundless possibilities from the mere sight of a feather.

### Dark Souls 2 Aged Feather Usage

  * Return the player to the last bonfire unless you are over the weight limit.
  * Unlimited uses.
  * Player keeps this item permanently, even when going into NG+
  * The Aged Feather and other warping items cannot be used if player or NPC invaders/phantoms are present.

### Aged Feather Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Given to you by the [Emerald Herald](/Emerald+Herald "Dark Souls 2 Emerald Herald") when you enter [Dragon Aerie](/Dragon+Aerie "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Aerie") and talk to her at the start of the area.
  * Alternatively obtained by killing the [Emerald Herald](/Emerald+Herald "Dark Souls 2 Emerald Herald"). Strongly discouraged, this will require you to revive her for 2,500 souls, at her tombstone each time you wish to use her services.
