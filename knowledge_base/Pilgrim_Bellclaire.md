**Pilgrim Bellclaire** is an [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**. Pilgrim Bellclaire** is a summonable NPC phantom, who can aid the player in battles against tough [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses"). She is a sorceress who wields powerful magic spells, making her a valuable ally in combat. Bellclaire's summon sign can be found in various locations throughout the game, and she can be summoned to assist the player in defeating enemies and overcoming challenges.

### Pilgrim Bellclaire Information in Dark Souls 2

  * NPC summon available at the [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille") and in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle") (SOTFS) / [Throne of Want](/Throne+of+Want "Dark Souls 2 Throne of Want")
  * She wields the [Staff of Amana](/Staff+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Staff of Amana") and the [Priest's Chime](/Priest's+Chime "Dark Souls 2 Priest's Chime")
  * She wears the [Lion Warrior Helm](/Lion+Warrior+Helm "Dark Souls 2 Lion Warrior Helm"), [Black Hollow Mage Robe](/Black+Hollow+Mage+Robe "Dark Souls 2 Black Hollow Mage Robe"), and the [Heide Knight Gauntlets](/Heide+Knight+Gauntlets "Dark Souls 2 Heide Knight Gauntlets") and [Leggings](/Heide+Knight+Leggings "Dark Souls 2 Heide Knight Leggings")
  * She uses Soul Arrows and Soul Spears and NEVER uses the Priest Chime.

### Pilgrim Bellclaire Encounters in Dark Souls 2

[Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille"). In an open cell on the right side of the hallway just before the [Ruin Sentinels](/Ruin+Sentinel "Dark Souls 2 Ruin Sentinel") fog wall.  
[Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle"). Right next to the [Dragonriders](/DragonRider\(Second+Encounter\) "Dark Souls 2 DragonRider\(Second Encounter\)") fog wall.
