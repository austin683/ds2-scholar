**Royal Rat Vanguard Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Royal Rat Vanguard Soul** , the soul of the Rat King's vanguard in the underground realm. This loyal servant assesses the worthiness of those who seek an audience with the Rat King. Obtain this soul by defeating the [Royal Rat Vanguard](/Royal+Rat+Vanguard "Dark Souls 2 Royal Rat Vanguard") in the [Grave of Saints](/Grave+of+Saints "Dark Souls 2 Grave of Saints"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Rat King's vanguard, of the underground realm.  
> The Rat King's loyal servant judges the worthiness of those who seek royal audience.  
> Use the special soul of this soldier to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Royal Rat Vanguard Soul Usage in DKS2

  * Consuming gives 6000 souls.
  * Trade with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for [Toxic Mist](/Toxic+Mist#fex "Dark Souls 2 Toxic Mist#fex").

### Dark Souls 2 Royal Rat Vanguard Soul Location

  * Obtained by defeating the [Royal Rat Vanguard](/Royal+Rat+Vanguard "Dark Souls 2 Royal Rat Vanguard") in [Grave of Saints](/Grave+of+Saints "Dark Souls 2 Grave of Saints").
