**Twilight Herb** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Twilight Herb** , a subdued grey herb concealed amidst the craggy terrain of lofty mountains. This herb replenishes a specific number of spell uses. The relentless pursuit of this precious herb, often at great peril, has prompted the prohibition of its harvest in certain areas. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A grey herb that grows hidden among the rocky surfaces of tall mountains. Restores a certain amount of spell uses. There is no end to the scores of people who risk life and limb to forage for this valuable herb, which led to a banning of its harvest in some regions

### Twilight Herb Usage in DKS2

Restorative item.

  * Restores 50% of the total number of spell uses

### Dark Souls 2 Twilight Herb Location

  * x5 Sold by [Carhillion of the Fold](/Carhillion+of+the+Fold "Dark Souls 2 Carhillion of the Fold") for 2,400 souls.
  * One can be found below the Shaded Ruin bonfire, either by jumping out the window or using Fragrant Branch of Yore. In a chest guarded by a Basilisk.
  * Also occasionally dropped by Mummies in Lost Bastille.
  * One is dropped by a non-respawning [Poison Moth](/Poison+Moth "Dark Souls 2 Poison Moth") near the [Shaded Ruins](/Shaded+Ruins "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Ruins") bonfire. Jump down to where you would normally find the petrified [Lion Warrior](/Lion+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Lion Warrior"), and look up at the wall to his left.
