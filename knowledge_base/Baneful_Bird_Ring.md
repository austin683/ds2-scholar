**Baneful Bird Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in **Dark Souls 2 (DS2)**. **Baneful Bird Ring** , a circlet worn by Raime, the betrayer. Lessens stamina depletion while blocking with a shield. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> Ring of Raime the traitor. Reduces stamina loss when blocking with a shield. Raime was an agile swordsman who served King Vendrick, but after resigning from his post, he reconsidered his way of life and was born again as a stalwart warrior."

### Dark Souls 2 Baneful Bird Ring Effects

**Durability** : 45

**Weight** :

  * 2.0

**Effect:**

  * Increasing shield stability by 5, reducing stamina damage taken while blocking. See tips and notes.

### Baneful Bird Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Next to the [Throne Floor](/Throne+Floor "Dark Souls 2 Throne Floor") bonfire on a corpse hanging on a railing behind some cogs half buried in ash, you must have the [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower") activated with the [Scorching Iron Scepter](/Scorching+Iron+Scepter "Dark Souls 2 Scorching Iron Scepter") to do this. Jump over when the elevator is up, and quickly cross over, go over to the corpse and claim the ring!
  * [Location video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6sAagTOfZGg)

### DS2 Baneful Bird Ring Tips and Notes

  * It get's more effective the higher the shield's stability stat.
  * A Cleric's Parma has 20 stability (25 with the ring), so it takes 80% stamina damage (75% with the ring), making it **6.25% more effective**. A +5 Havel's Greatshield have 80 stability (85 with the ring), meaning it takes 20% stamina damage (15% with the ring), so it's **25% more effective** at reducing stamina damage.
  * Just for the sake of clarity, a +5 Drangleic Shield has 63 stability, with the ring's +5, it now has 68 stability, so it's stamina damage taken got reduced from 37% to 32%, a **13,5% increase** in effectivess.
