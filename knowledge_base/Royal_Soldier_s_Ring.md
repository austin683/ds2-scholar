**Royal Soldier's Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. The **Royal Soldier's Ring** is a ring adorned with a soldier's emblem, enhancing carrying capacity. Bestowed upon warriors who showcased exceptional valor in the service of King Vendrick, these rings were reserved for those steadfast fighters who relied solely on their formidable strength in every battle. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A ring bearing a soldier's seal. Increases the load that can be carried.
> 
> These rings were granted to warriors who distinguished themselves in the service of King Vendrick. The king favored simple warriors who staked their every battle on strength alone.

### Royal Soldier's Ring Effects in Dark Souls 2

**Durability** :

  * +0 = 120
  * +1 = 90
  * +2 = 75

**Weight** :

  * +0=0.2
  * +1=0.5
  * +2=0.8

**Effect:**

  * Increases maximum load by 10%
  * +1 = 15%
  * +2 = 20%

### Dark Souls 2 Royal Soldier's Ring Locations

  * Behind a physically breakable wall in [No-man's Wharf](/No-man%27s+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No-man%27s Wharf"), located in the room with pots and two sitting Varangians. The "breakable wall" is located in the room with pots and two sitting Varangians, close to the shortcut bridge. Be prepared for the crystal lizard behind the wall,
  * +1: In a metal chest behind the far-most, right-hand door in the room with many [Ruin Sentinels](/Ruin+Sentinel "Dark Souls 2 Ruin Sentinel") in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle").
  * +2: Drops from [Velstadt, the Royal Aegis](/Velstadt%2C+the+Royal+Aegis "Dark Souls 2 Velstadt%2C the Royal Aegis") in NG+ or with a [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") in NG. (NG+ only)

### DS2 Royal Soldier's Ring Tips and Notes

  * If the player's equipment load percentage drops below one of the threshold values (30%, 50%, 70%, 100%) as a result of wearing this ring, then they will receive the associated benefits, such as faster stamina regeneration and greater roll distance.
  * Wearing this ring can reduce the effectiveness of [Flynn's Ring](/Flynn's+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Flynn's Ring").
