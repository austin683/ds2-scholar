**Dragon Charm** is a type of healing consumable in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Dragon Charm** , an esteemed charm. It heals poison and provides a substantial restoration of health. This scarce and age-old charm is believed to carry the essence of dragons. Whether it is a product of human craftsmanship or possesses a more mystical origin remains unclear. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A blessed charm.  
> Cures poison and greatly restores HP.  
> A rare, ancient charm that is said to be endowed with the power of dragons. It is not clear whether it is a man-made creation, or of some other nature.

### Dragon Charm Usage in DKS2

  * Cures poison and restores 1500 HP
  * Seems also to cure toxic. (Used to cure toxic status during Sinh, Slumbering Dragon battle)

### Dark Souls 2 Dragon Charm Location

  * Dropped from [Desert Sorceress](/Desert+Sorceress "Dark Souls 2 Desert Sorceress") in Earthen Peak
  * [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros") chest treasure : on entry to the area, take the steps to the left then go left past the stone sconce, an enemy will be hugging it.
  * Dropped by [Amana Priestess](/Amana+Priestess "Dark Souls 2 Amana Priestess") in the shrine of Amana
  * Dropped by [Drakekeeper](/Drakekeeper "Dark Souls 2 Drakekeeper") in [Dragon Shrine](/Dragon+Shrine "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Shrine")
  * Dropped by [Sanctum Priestess](/Sanctum+Priestess "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Priestess") in Dragon's Sanctum
