**Alva leggings** is a **legging** and a part of [Alva Set](/Alva+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Alva leggings Description 

_"Leggings worn by Alva the Wayfarer. Light but offers very high defense._

_Alva crossed many a land in search_ _of a cure for Saint Serreta's sickness,__but failed and relinquished his knighthood._

_Alva was once wracked with guilt and remorse,__but rediscovered his purpose in life with the_ _aid of the witch who once_ _plotted against him."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Sold by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer) after speanding 15,000 souls at his shop.

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Titanite").
