# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Ultra+Greatswords

**Ultra Greatswords** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are a type of [Weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") that's sluggish and heavy, being the largest types of weapon in the game, but can offer high damage output in each swing. Due to their immense size and weight, it is one of the few weapon categories that can take advantage hyper armor, indicating that [Poise](/Poise "Dark Souls 2 Poise") is a must for these weapons. Ultra Greatswords can also deal high poise damage, making every attack stagger your opponent very easily. The weight of Ultra Greatswords can put players at a disadvantage as it requires them to invest large amounts of [Endurance](/Endurance "Dark Souls 2 Endurance"), [Strength](/Strength "Dark Souls 2 Strength"), and [Vitality](/Vitality "Dark Souls 2 Vitality"), the last one being the most important as the player swings an Ultra Greatsword weapon, it can almost likely leave them open for a counter-attack. Do note that when an Ultra Greatsword is wielded with two hands, it cannot be parried. This page covers a list of all Ultra Greatswords in Dark Souls 2.
