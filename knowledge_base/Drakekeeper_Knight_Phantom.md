**Drakekeeper Knight Phantom** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Stationed at the castle entrance, the **Drakekeeper Knight Phantom** is adorned in Drakekeeper's armor and wields a Warpick as its weapon. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Drakekeeper Knight Phantom Information

Guards the entrance to the castle, wearing Drakekeeper's armour and holding a Warpick as a weapon.
