_"Skeleton lord Crown.__High dark defense._  
_The very men who were ordered to hunt down the Undead were themselves hollowed, and founded a kingdom of bone."_

### Location

  * Sold by Titchy Gren accessible in a small area after the [Executioner's Chariot](/Executioner%27s+Chariot) boss fight.
  * Must kill the Skeleton Lords before Titchy will sell them to the player.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Bone King Set](/Bone+King+Set).
  * Upgrade with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
