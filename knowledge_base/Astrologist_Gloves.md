### Astrologist Gloves Description

_"Gauntlets worn by astrologists in Melfia._  
_Astrologists believe that magical powers_  
 _can be obtained at moments of special_  
 _heavenly alignment._

_Even the collective wisdom of the_  
 _Melfian Magic Academy cannot pull magic_  
 _out of the sky, but the ideas of the_  
 _astrologists led to the creation of_  
 _new and very useful magic devices. "_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Can be purchase from [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan). it is required to finish his quest.
  * Dropped by [Astrologist](/Astrologist).
