_"Mask/Robes worn by fume sorcerers._   
_After the Old Iron King sunk into the lava, scores of men were dispatched to this land to tap the replete stores of iron._   
_But they soon lost their nerve when faced with the child of Dark, and all but the most steadfast of them became servants of the black fog."_

###  Set Location

  * Dropped by [Fume Sorcerers](/Fume+Sorcerer "Dark Souls 2 Fume Sorcerer") in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower").

###  Fume Sorcerer Set Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
  * Upgraded stats are only 50% higher and physical defenses are only 25% higher.
  * Mask is the only piece offering Poise bonus.
