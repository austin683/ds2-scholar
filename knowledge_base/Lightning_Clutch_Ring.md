**Lightning Clutch Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in **Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)**. The **Lightning Clutch Ring** , adorned with an image of a hand clasping a stone, augments lightning attack potential at the expense of physical defense. Its mysterious origin hints at a connection with darker deities, and mastering its benefits demands skill from the wearer. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> Ring depicting a hand grasping a stone. Increses lightning attack, but reduces physical defense. The origin of this ring is unknown, but its design suggests one of the darker deities. Effective use of this ring requires skill on the part of the wearer."

### Dark Souls 2 Lightning Clutch Ring Effects

**Durability** : 30

**Weight** :

2.0

**Effect:**

  * Increases Lightning AR by 30 before infusion modification, reduces Physical Defenses by 80

### Lightning Clutch Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * After entering the [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum") there is a closed door on the left side, use the [Eternal Sanctum Key](/Eternal+Sanctum+Key "Dark Souls 2 Eternal Sanctum Key") to open it. Inside there are 2 Sanctum Priestess and 5 chests. The ring is inside the 2nd chest from the left.

### DS2 Lightning Clutch Ring Tips and Notes

  * The ring affects lightning damage on both weapons and miracles with the cost of the defense reduction. This ring's effect does stack with the [Sun Seal](/Sun+Seal "Dark Souls 2 Sun Seal").
