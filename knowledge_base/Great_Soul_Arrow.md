**Great Soul Arrow** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Great Soul Arrow** , an advanced sorcery more potent than the Soul Arrow, effective against enemies resistant to physical attacks, and its power is linked to the caster's intelligence. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "A spell superior to the Soul Arrow.  
> More powerful, and effective at damaging enemies resistant to physical attacks.
> 
> The strength of this spell is influenced by one's intelligence."

### Dark Souls 2 Great Soul Arrow Acquired From

  * [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants"), in the room with the ballistas and 3 hollow soldiers
  * [No-Man's Wharf](/No-Man's+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No-Man's Wharf"), sold by [Carhillion of the Fold](/Carhillion+of+the+Fold "Dark Souls 2 Carhillion of the Fold") for 3,000 souls
  * [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep"), sold by [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") for 3,000 souls

### Great Soul Arrow Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Shoots a projectile that deals magic damage. Casts faster than [Great Heavy Soul Arrow](/Great+Heavy+Soul+Arrow "Dark Souls 2 Great Heavy Soul Arrow") but does less damage.
  * Direct upgrade of [Soul Arrow](/Soul+Arrow "Dark Souls 2 Soul Arrow"), same casting speed, higher [INT](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") requirement, fewer spell casts, higher damage (noticeable on higher [INT](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") as well)
