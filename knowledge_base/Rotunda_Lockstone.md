**Rotunda Lockstone** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Rotunda Lockstone** , a stone that rotates the Majula Rotunda. Insert it into the central pillar to align the rotunda with a different passageway. Devoid of magical properties, yet a testament to fine engineering. Acquired by defeating Licia of Lindeldt. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> A stone which rotates the Majula Rotunda. Insert into the central pillar to align the rotunda with a different passageway. Nothing magical about it, but a fine feat of engineering.

### Dark Souls 2 Rotunda Lockstone Usage

  * Changes the passage in the Majula Rotunda without having to pay 2000 souls.
  * It is treated as a key instead of an item, it disappears from your inventory after you start [New Game Plus](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus").

### Rotunda Lockstone Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Received after defeating [Licia of Lindeldt](/Licia+of+Lindeldt "Dark Souls 2 Licia of Lindeldt").
  * Use the [Crushed Eye Orb](/Crushed+Eye+Orb#fex "Dark Souls 2 Crushed Eye Orb#fex") while in the Majula Rotunda with Licia of Lindeldt to invade her as a red phantom.
