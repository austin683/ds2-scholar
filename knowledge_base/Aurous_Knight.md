**Aurous Knight** is an [enemy](/enemies "Dark Souls 2 enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Aurous Knight** , a spectral wanderer adorned in the Aurous Set, materializes aboard the ship when the Bonfire Intensity surpasses 2. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Aurous Knight Information in Dark Souls 2

_"A Roaming Soul wearing the Aurous Set appears on board of the ship when the Bonfire Intensity is over 2. Does not respawn unless Bonfire Intensity level increases_ _"_

### Dark Souls 2 Aurous Knight Location

  * [No-man's Wharf](/No-man's+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No-man's Wharf")
