**Hollow Soldier Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Hollow Soldier Set](/Hollow+Soldier+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Hollow Soldier Leggings Description

"Leggings worn by Hollowed Royal Soldiers. Appears to be crafted with quality materials, but is highly degraded and close to falling apart. Wearing this will be of little use."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Drops off of the hollowed soldier's located in the Forest of Giants area.

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite shards, large shards and chunks.](/Ores)
