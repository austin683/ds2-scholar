**Dragon Head Stone** is a special [covenant](/covenants "Dark Souls 2 covenants") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Dragon Head Stone** , a stone imbued with the power of dragons. Morphs the head into that of a fire-breathing dragon, a transformation that persists until death. Converts the head slot item into the Black Dragon Helm and provides a fire-breathing ability upon subsequent use. The fire-breathing damage is relatively low, primarily useful for staggering enemies.[Tools](/Tools "Dark Souls 2 Tools") are unique, reusable items which assist the player during various facets of gameplay ranging from basic communication to assisting in boss encounters.

> A stone containing the strength of dragons. Transforms the head into that of a fire-breathing dragon, an effect that will remain until death.  
> The zealots who sought everlasting life are said to have conducted rites to imbue stones with the strength of dragons, but their (there) are no records confirming their success.

### Dark Souls 2 Dragon Head Stone Usage

  * Turns head slot item into Black Dragon Helm
  * Grants a firebreathing ability when the stone is used again. The fire-breathing damage is pretty low and is only good for staggering enemies at most.
  * The flame does not break objects.
  * The effect remains until you die. 

### Dragon Head Stone Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Obtained from [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") in Iron Keep, after attaining rank 1 in the [Dragon Remnants](/Dragon+Remnants "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Remnants") covenant
