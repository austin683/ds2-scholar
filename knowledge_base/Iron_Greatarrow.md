**Iron Greatarrow** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Iron Greatarrows** , oversized projectiles crafted from iron for use with Greatbows. Offering enhanced effectiveness compared to regular arrows, their substantial weight limits their use exclusively to Greatbows. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Standard greatarrows made of iron.  
> More effective than regular arrows, but considerably heavier.

### Iron Greatarrow Usage in DKS2

  * Can only be used with Greatbows.
  * Thrust Damage / 200 Physical ATK

### Dark Souls 2 Iron Greatarrow Location

  * Can be bought from [Steady Hand McDuff](/Steady+Hand+McDuff "Dark Souls 2 Steady Hand McDuff") merchant in [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille"), for 120 souls each. ∞ supply.
  * Can be obtained by killing Alonne Knights (greatbow) and Alonne Greatbow Captains in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep").
