**Imperious Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Imperious Set](/Imperious+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Imperious Leggings Description

_"Helmet/Armor/Gauntlets,Leggings worn by once-proud knights._  
_Relics of a party who long ago attempted to conquer the Undead Crypt._  
_For this sin their deaths have been postponed, so they may serve as crypt sentries.__"_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Armor can be dropped by the enemies with twin shields in the Undead Crypt.
  * Parts drop from the second red phantom in crystal cove right before the bridge over the crystal bed (NG+).

### Notes

  * Upgraded with Titanite Shard, Large Titanite Shard, Titanite Chunk, Titanite Slab
