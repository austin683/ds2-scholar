**Corrosive Egg Crawlers (Corrosive Egg Insect)** is an [enemy](/enemies "Dark Souls 2 enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Corrosive Egg Crawlers** , tiny insects adorned with eggs that emit particles, damaging equipment durability. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Corrosive Egg Crawlers Information

Small insects with eggs covering their bodies. Capable of emitting particles that damage equipment durability.

### Corrosive Egg Crawlers Location in Dark Souls 2

  * [Shulva, Sanctum City](/Shulva+-+Sanctum+City "Dark Souls 2 Shulva - Sanctum City")
  * [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon's+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon's Sanctum")
  * [The Gutter](/The+Gutter "Dark Souls 2 The Gutter") (SOTFS only)
