**Llewellyn Shoes** is a **Legging** and a part of [Llewellyn Set](/Llewellyn+Set "Dark Souls 2 Llewellyn Set") in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Llewellyn Shoes Description

_"__Shoes reinforced with rare_ geisteel _. Excellent defensive gear that is both light and sturdy."_

_"Quality equipment that is both light and strong. Crafted by the castle's resident master smith Llewellyn, and supplied only to a selected few._

_His work easily identified by its lack of ostentation, Llewellyn focused solely on an economy of simplicity and strength."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * The armor set drops from the stone statues outside of the Looking-Glass Knight boss fight in Drangleic Castle

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite") (Only goes to +5)
  * The gloves can be dropped from the various stone soldiers around Drangleic.
  * The stone Twinblade knights immediately before the Looking Glass Knight fogwall drop this set. (Confirmed)
  * The helm that was previously associated with this set during the network test is now part of the [Drangleic Set](/Drangleic+Set "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Set")
  * Chancellor Wellager wears this set.
