**Tseldora Manchettes** is a **gauntlet** and a part of [Tseldora Set](/Tseldora+Set "Dark Souls 2 Tseldora Set") in Dark Souls 2.

### Tseldora Manchettes Description

_"Outfit worn by settlers of Tseldora. Rather fancy but with low defence and unfit for battle._  
_Its design recalls Tseldora's glory days, the peak of the brightstone boom."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Drops from [Hollowed Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") (Prisoners with spiders on their back) within the Brightstone Cove area.
  * The full set is dropped by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer")**** if he is killed.

### Notes

  * Helm increases souls by 2.5%
  * Chest increases souls by 5%
  * Gloves increase souls by 10%
  * Leggings increase souls by 5%
  * The bonuses are multiplicative if multiple armor pieces are worn.
  * Upgraded with Titanite shards.
  * Every piece of this set increases the souls obtained from enemies.
