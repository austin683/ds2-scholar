### Hexer's Gloves Description

_"Armor worn by a hexer. Belonged to Felkin the Outcast.  
Those who have a taste of dark are drawn into its vortex and rarely return.  
Perhaps it appeals to something deep within the human soul.__"_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Acquired from [Felkin the Outcast](/Felkin+the+Outcast) after talking to him while having 20 Intelligence and 20 Faith (Ring of Knowledge/Ring of Prayer do not count)
  * Purchased from Hag Merchant Melentia in Majula after killing Felkin the Outcas

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
