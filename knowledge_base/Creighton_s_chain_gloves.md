### Creighton's Chain Gloves Description

_"Atypical steel gloves._  
_Belonged to Creighton the Wanderer._  
_Its design resembles that of the knight order of the eastern land of Mirrah, but with some odd differences that catch the eye._  
_Perhaps it is a finely-crafted imitation."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Sold by [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia") after killing [Creighton the Wanderer](/Creighton+the+Wanderer "Dark Souls 2 Creighton the Wanderer").
  * Obtained from [Creighton the Wanderer](/Creighton+the+Wanderer "Dark Souls 2 Creighton the Wanderer") by helping him during his fight with Pate.

### Notes

  * Upgrade with [Titanite Shard](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard").
