**Armor Sets** from [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King") in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are additional [DLC](/DLC "Dark Souls 2 DLC") content features that can be encountered or obtained by purchasing and accessing the Ivory King DLC from the Lost Crowns DLC Trilogy. A number of equipment pieces and armor sets can be accessed by playing the DLC. This page features each of the available new equipment pieces found and their individual stats and information. For all new content and available DLCs, view the Dark Souls 2 [DLC](/DLC "Dark Souls 2 DLC") page.
