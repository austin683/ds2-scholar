**Demon of Song Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Demon of Song Soul,** the soul of the eerie Demon of Song dwelling in the Shrine of Amana. Consume for 12,000 Souls. Alternatively, trade it with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") to obtain the [Spotted Whip](/Spotted+Whip "Dark Souls 2 Spotted Whip"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Demon of Song at the Shrine of Amana.  
> When the demon developed a taste for human flesh, it was contained within the Shrine of Amana, but the line of priestesses who looked after the shrine and appeased the creature have died off.  
> Use the special soul of this demon to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Demon of Song Soul Usage in DKS2

  * Consuming gives 12,000 Souls.
  * Can be traded with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") to get the [Spotted Whip](/Spotted+Whip#fex "Dark Souls 2 Spotted Whip#fex")

### Dark Souls 2 Demon of Song Soul Location

  * Obtained by defeating the [Demon of Song](/Demon+of+Song "Dark Souls 2 Demon of Song") in [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana")
