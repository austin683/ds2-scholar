### Leggings of Aurous Description

_"Leggings of kings of the desert land of Jugo. First worn by Aurous, the land's heroic founder._  
_According to legend, the armor of Aurous is composed of a mysterious substance that cannot be seen by cowards. What do your eyes tell you?"_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * The entire visible set drops from a Dark Spirit at No-Man's Wharf on the ship in NG+ and above (_Spawns once per playthrough,[bonfire ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") required)_.
  * Dark Spirit does drop [Awestones](/Awestone "Dark Souls 2 Awestone") (Rare Drop).
