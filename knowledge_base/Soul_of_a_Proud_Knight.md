**Soul of a Proud Knight** is a type of [Consumable](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of a Proud Knight** , the potent essence of a once-proud Undead knight, now lost to Hollowing. Consume to amass a wealth of souls. In undeath, as in life, the yearning for souls persists. Yields 2000 souls when consumed. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are consumable items categorized as remnants of fallen warriors, consuming souls collects a certain number of souls that is added to the player's stock. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are required for players to become stronger in the game by raising their soul level as well as buying and upgrading their equipment.

> Soul of a proud Undead knight who has long ago gone Hollow.  
> Use to acquire many souls.  
> The soul is the source of all life, and even in undeath, or hollowing, the mind seeks souls.

### Soul of a Proud Knight Usage in DKS2

  * Grants 2000 souls when consumed.

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of a Proud Knight Location

  * 1 in the [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep") ([Threshold Bridge](/Threshold+Bridge "Dark Souls 2 Threshold Bridge") Bonfire
  * 1x found on a corpse in the lower part of [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide%27s Tower of Flame"), behind the petrified enemy and the [old Knight](/old+Knight "Dark Souls 2 old Knight").
  * Dropped by the [Ogre](/Ogre "Dark Souls 2 Ogre") in [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants")
