**Black Firebomb** is a type of [Projectile](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2).** **Black Firebomb** , a bisque urn filled with black powder that explodes, causing significant fire damage. [Projectiles](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") are offensive items that are usually thrown at hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") to inflict a small amount of damage.

> Bisque urn filled with black powder. Explodes, inflicting great fire damage. Utilizes a powerful explosive developed in the fallen kingdom of Forossa.  
> Black firebombs can cause harm to their owners when misused, and Forossa law defined stringent standards for their safe storage.

### Dark Souls 2 Black Firebomb Usage

  * Attack Projectile: Throw at enemies to deal fire damage.
  * Can be used to explode barrels and break walls.

### Black Firebomb Location in DKS2

  * Available as map treasure in [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt"), [The Gutter](/The+Gutter "Dark Souls 2 The Gutter"), [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak"), [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants").
  * Sold by [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep").
