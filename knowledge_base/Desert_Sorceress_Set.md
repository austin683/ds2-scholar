**Desert Sorceress Set** is an Attire or Armor in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

  
  
  
  
_"Sorceress hood from the distant land of Jugo. Appears to be a plain, thin fabric, but is permeated with powerful magic._   
_Desert Sorceresses have enchanting looks, and they use them to catch people off guard._   
_Oddly enough, even those who are perceptive enough to realize the ploy fall prey to their seductions with alarming regularity."_

###  Set Location

  * Dropped by [Desert Sorceresses](/Desert+Sorceress "Dark Souls 2 Desert Sorceress") in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak").

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
  * Hood grants +3 to INT.
  * Gloves offer second highest magic and lightning defense (both almost equal to that of [Lion Mage](/Lion+Mage+Set "Dark Souls 2 Lion Mage Set") cuffs).
  * Skirt offers second highest magic defense and second lightning defense (almost equal to that of [Lucatiel's](/Lucatiel's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Lucatiel's Set") boots, [Priestess](/Priestess+Set "Dark Souls 2 Priestess Set") and [White Priest](/White+Priest+Set "Dark Souls 2 White Priest Set") skirts).
