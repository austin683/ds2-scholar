_"Attire commonly worn by peasants._

_Normal workwear, more fit for farming than fighting"_

### Location

  * Dropped by Peasants in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora").
  * The +5 Peasant Attire and Helm are dropped by the [Peasant Red Phantom](/Phantoms "Dark Souls 2 Phantoms") in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Peasant's Set](/Peasant+Set "Dark Souls 2 Peasant Set").
  * Upgraded from [Titanite](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard").
