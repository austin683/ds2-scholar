**Fang Key** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Fang Key** , an odd key shaped like a feral beast's fang. Opens the door to release [Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex"), a skilled blacksmith specializing in the trade of [Boss Soul Weapons](/Boss+Soul+Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Boss Soul Weapons"). [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> A strange key shaped like a wild beast's fang. What manner of dilettante would create such a key, and what might it unlock?

### Fang Key Usage in DKS2

  * Unlocks the door to free [Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex"), a blacksmith that trades [Boss Soul Weapons](/Boss+Soul+Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Boss Soul Weapons")
  * Room also contains a [Fragrant Branch of Yore.](/Fragrant+Branch+of+Yore "Dark Souls 2 Fragrant Branch of Yore")

### Dark Souls 2 Fang Key Location

  * Use a [Fragrant Branch of Yore](/Fragrant+Branch+of+Yore "Dark Souls 2 Fragrant Branch of Yore") on the petrified [Lion Clan Warrior](/Lion+Clan+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Lion Clan Warrior") to the right across a tree-bridge near the [Shaded Ruins Bonfire](/Shaded+Ruins "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Ruins") in Shaded Woods. Killing the Lion Clan Warrior after unpetrifying him will give you the key
  * [Video of Key Location](http://youtu.be/vAyP00xksTI)
