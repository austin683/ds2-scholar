#  Drifter Swordsman Aidel 

  
  
  
He's one of the 2 NPC summons that can be summoned at the [Iron Passage](/Iron+Passage "Dark Souls 2 Iron Passage") and in the [Memory of the Old Iron King](/Memory+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Memory of the Old Iron King"). He wears a prisoner hood, Creighton's chainmail, the Engraved Gauntlets and Faraam Boots. He wields a Stone Twinblade and a Stone Parma.   

###  Notes:

  * As soon as he's summoned he will use a Hello! carving.
  * Does critical hits often thanks to the [Engraved Gauntlets](/Engraved+Gauntlets "Dark Souls 2 Engraved Gauntlets").
