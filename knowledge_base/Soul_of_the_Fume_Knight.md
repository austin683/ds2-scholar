**Soul of the Fume Knight** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of the Fume Knight** , the essence of the formidable knight that stands guard in the dungeons of Brume Tower. Raime, once a distinguished knight, became captivated by the Bride of Ash and took residence in this realm of smoke and fog. Obtain this soul by defeating the [Fume Knight](/Fume+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Fume Knight"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the fume knight that guards the dungeon of Brume Tower.  
> Raime was a distinguished knight who became infatuated with the Bride of Ash, and settled in this land of smoke and fog.  
> The wondrous soul of this knight can be used to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Soul of the Fume Knight Usage in DKS2

  * Trade with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for 
    * [Fume Sword](/Fume+Sword#fex "Dark Souls 2 Fume Sword#fex")
    * [Fume Ultra Greatsword](/Fume+Ultra+Greatsword#fex "Dark Souls 2 Fume Ultra Greatsword#fex")
  * Consuming gives 16 000 souls

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of the Fume Knight Location

  * Drops from [Fume Knight](/Fume+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Fume Knight")
