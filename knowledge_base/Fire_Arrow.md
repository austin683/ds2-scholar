**Fire Arrow** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Fire Arrows** , projectiles infused with flames and coated with Charcoal Pine Resin. Armaments enhanced with fire prove efficacious against adversaries susceptible to magic and fire, such as Undead or beasts. Particularly beneficial for those without magical abilities or those who have depleted their magical resources. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> "Arrows imbued with fire. Tipped with Charcoal Pine Resin. Weapons fortified with fire are effective against  
> against foes vulnerable to magic and fire, such as Undead or beasts. Useful in the short term for those who have learned no magic, or whose magic is exhausted."

### Fire Arrow Usage in DKS2

  * Note: "against" is repeated in the game. It is not a typo.
  * Ammunition for Bows

### Dark Souls 2 Fire Arrow Location

  * [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") carries an unlimited supply for 60 souls each (After beating any Great Soul boss).
  * 10 are found on a corpse near the elevator to the Last Giant bossfight in the Forest of Fallen Giants.
  * 20 in the Iron Keep (Threshold Bridge) on a corpse behind the illusory wall
