### Tattered Cloth Manchettes Description

"Gauntlets favored by pyromancers. They appear tattered but in fact the fabric is reinforced with pyromancy.  
Magic is no show. It is an art that allows mere mortals to glimpse into the very fabric of what is and may be."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Dropped by the Spider Monkeys in [No-man's Wharf](/No-man%27s+Wharf)
  * Full set can be found in The Gutter
