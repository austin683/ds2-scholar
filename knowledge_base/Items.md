# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Items

**Items** in Dark Souls 2 are found in game either on bodies, dropped from slain enemies, rewards, sold by merchants, through trade with [Dyna & Tillo](/Dyna+and+Tillo), or from chests.

  
**Be warned:** While a chest carries the risk of being a [Mimic](/Mimic), it is possible to destroy wooden chests with a few hits. If you do this, the items they contain will be destroyed, and in their place will be [Rubbish](/Rubbish). Also you can hit the chest once (melee or from safe distance), if a healthbar appears above the chest, finish him. 2. Or simply equip anything from the moon butterfly set and stand near the chest. A mimic will start to get a poison mist from your gear. Alternatively, look closely; mimics have locks.
