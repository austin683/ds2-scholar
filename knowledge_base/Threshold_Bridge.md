  * Boss: [Smelter Demon](/Smelter+Demon)
  * [Pursuer ](/Pursuer)with [Ring of Blades +1](/Ring+of+Blades)
  * [Human Effigy](/Human+Effigy)
  * [Pharros' Lockstone](/Pharros%27+Lockstone)
  * [Fire Arrow](/Fire+Arrow) x20
  * [Soul of a Proud Knight](/Soul+of+a+Proud+Knight)
  * [Repair Powder](/Repair+Powder)
  * [Large Soul of a Nameless Soldier](/Large+Soul+of+a+Nameless+Soldier)
  * [Red Leech Troches](/Red+Leech+Troches)

  * [Alonne Knight Captain](/Alonne+Knight+Captain) (red) x4
  * [Alonne Knight](/Alonne+Knight) (red)

  

##  Special Notes:

The [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic) on the right side of the bridge near the bonfire does NOT respawn when buring an Ascetic at the Bonfire.   

##  Notable Summoning/PvP Spots:

The Bridge is an extremely popular spot for PvP because a lot of Dragon Remnants and invaders put their sign here, this spot is also a popular area for fight clubs.
