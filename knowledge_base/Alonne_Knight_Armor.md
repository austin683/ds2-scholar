**Alonne Knight Armor** is a [Chest Piece](/Chest+Pieces "Dark Souls 2 Chest Pieces") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). Armor worn by the Alonne Knights. Part of the [Alonne Knight Set](/Alonne+Knight+Set "Dark Souls 2 Alonne Knight Set"), it provides high poise for a relatively low weight. **Alonne Knight Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor worn by Alonne Knights. The bonds of the Alonne Knights, who served the Old Iron King, were mightier than the land's iron, but in the end the knights were subsumed by the flames that brought the castle down.

### Alonne Knight Armor Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Dropped by Alonne Captains in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep"). Identified by their lightning sword or red bow.

### Alonne Knight Armor Information in Dark Souls 2

  * of the [Alonne Knight Set](/Alonne+Knight+Set "Dark Souls 2 Alonne Knight Set"). Pieces include [Alonne Knight Helm](/Alonne+Knight+Helm "Dark Souls 2 Alonne Knight Helm"), [Alonne Knight Gauntlets](/Alonne+Knight+Gauntlets "Dark Souls 2 Alonne Knight Gauntlets") and [Alonne Knight Leggings](/Alonne+Knight+Leggings "Dark Souls 2 Alonne Knight Leggings")
  * The set offers very high poise for relatively low weight.
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab").
