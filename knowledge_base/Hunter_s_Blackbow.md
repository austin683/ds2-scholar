Requirements: STR 9 / E, DEX 25 / S (*A)

Weapon Type: Bow

Attack Type: Projectile/Strike

Enchantable: Yes

Special: No

*When infused: 108 physical dmg/108 elemental dmg and B scaling with chosen element. Dex scaling changes to A. 

**Hunter's Blackbow** is a [weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") in **Dark Souls 2**. The **Hunter's Blackbow** excels in precision and speed with its high [Dexterity](/Dexterity "Dark Souls 2 Dexterity") scaling. While it may lack significant physical damage, its exceptional range and quick firing make it a favored choice for marksmen who prioritize accuracy over sheer power.

> A black bow designed for long distances. Difficult to handle at first, and requiring some amount of practice to master.   
>   
>  The hunting Goddess Evlana was no goddess at all, but rather a brave and highly skilled bow huntress. Long after her demise, the passing of lore transformed her into a deity.

### How to get Hunter's Blackbow in Dark Souls 2

  * Found in the chest down a ladder at the second bonfire before the [Twin Dragonriders](/Twin+Dragonriders "Dark Souls 2 Twin Dragonriders") boss at [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle")

### DS2 Hunter's Blackbow Hints and Tips

  * at 40Dex/6Strength the fully upgraded bow has an attack rating of 251(155+96)
  * Fastest rate of fire. Can be fired four times in quick succession at 125 Stamina.
