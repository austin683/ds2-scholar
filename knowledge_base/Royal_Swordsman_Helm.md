_"Helmet of a royal swordsman. Its shape provides defense while allowing great mobility. Stripped of ornamentation, this particular helm/armor is designed strictly for battle._

_King Vendrick supplied his bravest men with the best armor available to face the great giants, but very few returned alive."_

### Location

  * Drop from [Hollow Royal Swordman](/Royal+Swordsman "Dark Souls 2 Royal Swordsman")
  * Drop from Dark Phantom Guthry (Doors of Pharros).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Royal Swordsman Set](/Royal+Swordsman+Set "Dark Souls 2 Royal Swordsman Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab").
