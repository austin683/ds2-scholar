**Hawk Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). The **Hawk Ring** , is a ring adorned with a hawk engraving, significantly extends the range of arrows. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A ring graced with the engraving of the hawk. Extends the range of arrows. Blue eyed Durgo, the nomadic bowman, had many valiant victories in battle, half owing to the boon of this ring.

### Dark Souls 2 Hawk Ring Effects

**Durability** : 130

**Weight** :

  * 0.2

**Effect:**

  * Extends the range of arrows by 7.55%

### Hawk Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Looted off of a body in the Fire Lizard pit below Cardinal Tower bonfire.[Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants")
