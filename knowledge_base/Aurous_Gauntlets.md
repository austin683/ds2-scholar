### Aurous Gauntlets Description

_"Gauntlets of kings of the desert land of Jugo._  
_First worn by Aurous, the land's heroic founder._

_According to legend, the armor of Aurous_  
 _is composed of a mysterious substance that_  
 _cannot be seen by cowards._  
_What do your eyes tell you?"_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Rare dropped by [Aurous Knight phantom](/Aurous+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Aurous Knight") in [No-man's Wharf](/No-man's+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No-man's Wharf") (Bonfire Intensity 2+).
