**Tower Key** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Tower Key** , a key that opens the door to [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower"). In times long past, this tower served as a relentless source of iron production. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> Key that opens the door to Brume Tower. Long ago, the tower used to produce a seemingly endless supply of iron. The King used it to create iron soldiers  
> and an iron castle. Legend has it he even tried his hand at forging a dragon out of iron.

### Dark Souls 2 Tower Key Usage

  * Opens the door that leads to the entrance of [Iron Passage](/Iron+Passage "Dark Souls 2 Iron Passage").
  * Opens the metal door near the **Foyer bonfire** in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower").
  * Opens the door that leads to the top of the tower where the [Memory of the Old Iron King](/Memory+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Memory of the Old Iron King") is triggered.

### Tower Key Location in DKS2

  * In an ash field guarded by 3 [Iron Warriors](/Iron+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Iron Warrior") in lower [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower").
  * The ash field can be found after taking the [Scorching Iron Scepter](/Scorching+Iron+Scepter "Dark Souls 2 Scorching Iron Scepter") to the device near the foyer bonfire and activating the elevators. Once you activate the elevators, take the elevator down one level. To the left will be an entrance to a hallway that leads to a room with barrel warriors and beyond that the ashen field. There will be numerous bodies and items lying around on the ashen field. The 3 Iron Warriors will emerge from the ash as you walk around. You can run back to the hallway so that you can fight the iron warriors individually. Once you defeat them, you can find the tower key on one of the bodies hanging near the edge of the cliff.
