#  Fire Keepers' Dwelling

* * *

##  Location:

After you wake up on the altar walk straight over the ramp through the gap in the rockface then over the wooden bridge and into the house.   
After the video sequence walk out of the house on the opposite side you entered. The bonfire is just outside the house.
