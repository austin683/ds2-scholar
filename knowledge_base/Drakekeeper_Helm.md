"Giant Onyx Helm.  
Something dark indeed eats away at the drakekeepers, eternal guardians of the shrine."

### Location

  * Dropped by [Drakekeepers](/Drakekeeper "Dark Souls 2 Drakekeeper") in [Dragon Shrine](/Dragon+Shrine "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Shrine").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Drakekeeper Set](/Drakekeeper+Set "Dark Souls 2 Drakekeeper Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab").
