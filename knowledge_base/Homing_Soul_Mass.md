**Homing Soul Mass** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Homing Soul Mass** , a sorcery that conjures multiple soul masses above the caster's head, and upon release, these ethereal entities home in on and assail their target. The master sorcerer who crafted this spell has faded into anonymity over time. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "Multiple soul masses appear above the head. When fired, they home in on their target. This spell was said to have been devised by a master sorcerer, but his name is long forgotten."

### Dark Souls 2 Homing Soul Mass Acquired From

  * Sold by [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for 6000 souls.
  * Found in [Shaded Ruins](/Shaded+Ruins "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Ruins"). Right of the path leading to the boss fog, there is a hole covered by boards. Standing on it will cause the boards to break and you will drop into a room containing [Darkdiver Grandahl](/Darkdiver+Grandahl "Dark Souls 2 Darkdiver Grandahl") and a chest.

### Homing Soul Mass Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * At 24 INT 3 orbs are cast, at 30 INT 4 orbs, and at 40 INT 5 orbs.
  * Orbs last 30 seconds or until they collide with a character or object and are unaffected by the [Lingering Dragoncrest Ring](/Lingering+Dragoncrest+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Lingering Dragoncrest Ring").
  * Possible reference to Big Hat Logan, since he also sold a Homing Soul Mass.
  * Its damage scales with Magic BNS only (the strength of the catalyst doesn't matter).
  * Not affected by [Unleash Magic](/Unleash+Magic "Dark Souls 2 Unleash Magic").
