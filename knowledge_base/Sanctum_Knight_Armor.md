**Sanctum Knight Set** is a [Chest Piece](/Chest+Pieces "Dark Souls 2 Chest Pieces") in Dark Souls 2. It is part of the [Sanctum Knight Set](/Sanctum+Knight+Set "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Knight Set").

_Armor of the sanctum knights._

_The sanctum knights renounced their own flesh to eternally guard the sanctum from Sir Yorgh and his Drakeblood Knights._

### Location

  * Dropped from [Sanctum Knights](/Sanctum+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Knight") in [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum")
  * Helm only in room with 5 knights' corpses in [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum")
  * Helm also can be dropped as other parts of set.

### Sanctum Knight Armor Notes

  * Part of the [Sanctum Knight Set](/Sanctum+Knight+Set "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Knight Set").
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
