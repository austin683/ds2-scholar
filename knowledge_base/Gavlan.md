**Lonesome Gavlan** is an NPC in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). Lonesome Gavlan can be encountered in various locations throughout the game. He is a merchant specializing in the sale of various items, particularly those related to poison. **Gavlan** is known for his distinctive accent and repetitive speech patterns, often repeating his own name and phrases such as "Gavlan wheel, Gavlan deal" and "Many deal, many thanks". 

### Lonesome Gavlan Information in Dark Souls 2

  * Allows the player to sell their own wares in return for souls, similar to Kingseeker Frampt in Dark Souls 1.
  * If he is killed in [No-Man's Wharf](/No+Man%27s+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No Man%27s Wharf") a gravestone will be in his place which costs 3500 souls to talk to him.
  * He will gift you [Gyrm Greataxe](/Gyrm+Greataxe "Dark Souls 2 Gyrm Greataxe") after purchasing 15,000 souls worth of goods from him and selecting "Talk" (selling goods works as well)

### Lonesome Gavlan Encounters in Dark Souls 2

  * [No-Man's Wharf](/No-man's+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No-man's Wharf").  _Dark Souls II_ : Found upstairs in a building far up in the wharf with many [Dark Stalkers](/Dark+Stalker "Dark Souls 2 Dark Stalker"). He appears here first and will leave after being visited here to go to [Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley").
  * _Dark Souls II: Scholar of the First Sin_ : In the room next to the movable portcullis, where you can summon [Lucatiel of Mirrah](/Lucatiel+of+Mirrah "Dark Souls 2 Lucatiel of Mirrah").
  * [Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley"). Found in a small room on the left side of the dug out burrow, beside the first encountered [Mounted Overseer](/Mounted+Overseer "Dark Souls 2 Mounted Overseer") near the Poison Pool bonfire. He will leave after being visited here to go to [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros").
  * [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros"). He will stay here permanently. He is found in the room, up the stairs outside of the Gyrm's Respite bonfire. His stock of items is unlimited here.
