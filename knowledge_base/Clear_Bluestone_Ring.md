**Clear Bluestone Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Clear Bluestone Ring** is a ring that once belonged to Fiorenza, the singular prosperous merchant of Volgen. This ring accelerates spell casting and was a testament to Fiorenza's wealth amassed through the acquisition of luxury items and rare collectibles. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A ring that belonged to Fiorenza, the only truly moneyed merchant in Volgen. Shortens spell casting time. Fiorenza used his riches to buy up trinkets of luxury and rare collectibles, but was ruined after years of excessive debauchery.

### Dark Souls 2 Clear Bluestone Ring Effects

**Durability** :

  * 0 = 120
  * +1 = 90
  * +2 = 75

**Weight** :

  * +0=0.2
  * +1=0.5
  * +2=0.8

**Effect:**

  * Shortens spell casting time by 20%
  * +1 = 35%
  * +2 = 40%

### Clear Bluestone Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Sold by [Carhillion of the Fold](/Carhillion+of+the+Fold "Dark Souls 2 Carhillion of the Fold").
  * +1: In an iron chest in the misty area of [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods").
  * +2: 
    * [The Skeleton Lords](/The+Skeleton+Lords "Dark Souls 2 The Skeleton Lords") (NG+). Using a [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") at the Undead Lockaway bonfire to respawn the Skeleton Lords will work for this if you are currently on a NG playthough.
    * [Armourer Dennis](/Armourer+Dennis "Dark Souls 2 Armourer Dennis") has a small chance to drop it as well at the Iron Keep bridge, where [Fencer Sharron](/Fencer+Sharron "Dark Souls 2 Fencer Sharron") may invade first.
