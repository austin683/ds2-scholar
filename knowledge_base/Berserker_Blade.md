**Berserker Blade** is a **[Katana](/Katanas "Dark Souls 2 Katanas")** and one of the available **[Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons")** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). Berserker Blade** is a rare drop from the [Mad Warrior](/Mad+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Mad Warrior") in [Belfry Sol](/Belfry+Sol "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Sol") and also obtained from [Prowlers](/Prowlers "Dark Souls 2 Prowlers") in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower") in the [Crown Of The Old Iron King DLC](/Crown+Of+The+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown Of The Old Iron King"). Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite"), it is the shortest among the [Katanas](/Katanas "Dark Souls 2 Katanas"). Its strong attack has a lengthy wind-up and is generally considered inferior to other katanas' delayed thrusting and slashing attacks.

> The katana of a berserker, by its name. The thick, shiny black blade cuts exquisitely. Its origins and owner are unknown, but at some point the blade became known by this name. 

### How to get Berserker Blade in Dark Souls 2

  * Rare drop from the [Mad Warrior](/Mad+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Mad Warrior") in [Belfry Sol](/Belfry+Sol "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Sol").
  * Drops from [Prowlers](/Prowlers "Dark Souls 2 Prowlers") in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower") in the Crown Of The Old Iron King DLC.

### DS2 Berserker Blade Hints and Tips

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite")
  * The shortest of the [Katanas](/Katanas "Dark Souls 2 Katanas").
  * While trying to get this item, resting at the bonfire while the phantom is in your world will cause him to despawn, so checking if the Phantom is there is always required.
  * MUST be in Bell Covenant for the Mad Warrior to have a chance at spawning. Mad Warrior keeps spawning infinitely, as long as you are in Bell Keeper's Covenant.
  * If you want the best possible chance of getting the Berserker Blade, you'll want to increase your item drop rate as much as possible.
  * Does not require NG+/Bonfire Ascetic (does significantly increase drop rate).
  * It's strong attack has an incredibly long wind up and is for the most part inferior to other kantanas' delayed thrusting attack and delayed slashing attack.

Parameter Bonus: Strength, Dexterity, Magic, Fire, Lightning and Dark bonuses - The scaling multiplier applied to the [Attack: stat]. Scaling quality is from highest to lowest as follows: S/A/B/C/D/E.  
The higher the player's [Str, Dex, Mag, Fire, Lightning, Dark] stat, the higher the [Attack Bonus: Stat] is (found on the player status screen). The higher the scaling letter, the higher the percent multiplier applied to the [Attack: Stat].  
This resulting bonus damage is added to the base physical damage of the weapon and is shown in the equipment screen in blue numbers as a "+ X".  
Attack Type: Defines what kind of moveset the weapon has.  
Physical Damage falls under three categories: [Thrust](/Thrust+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Thrust Damage") (T), [Slash](/Slash+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Slash Damage") (Sl), [Strike](/Strike+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Strike Damage") (St).  
Elemental Damage Types: [Magic](/Magic+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Magic Damage"), [Fire](/Fire+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Fire Damage"), [Lightning](/Lightning+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Lightning Damage"), [Dark](/Dark+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Dark Damage").  
Auxiliary Effects: [Bleed](/Bleed "Dark Souls 2 Bleed"), [Poison](/Poison "Dark Souls 2 Poison"), [Petrification](/Petrification "Dark Souls 2 Petrification"), and [Curse](/Curse "Dark Souls 2 Curse").  
Stability: How well the player keeps stance after being hit.  
Durability: The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!".  
Weight: How much the item weighs when equipped.
