**Varangian Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Varangian Set](/Varangian+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Varangian Leggings Description

_"Leggings worn by Varagians, that terrorized nearby seas. The coastline stretches far Drangleic. Beyond this Northern Sea is an unexplored continent, said to be the home of things inhuman."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Dropped in [No-Man's Wharf](/No-Man%27s+Wharf) by Varangian Bandits.

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shard, Large Titanite Shard, Titanite Chunk, Titanite Slab](/Ores)
