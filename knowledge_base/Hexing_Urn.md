**Hexing Urn** Is a type of [Projectile](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2).** **Hexing Urn** , a hexed black lacquer urn that explodes, causing dark damage. [Projectiles](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") are offensive items that are usually thrown at hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") to inflict a small amount of damage.

> Hexed black lacquer urn. Explodes, inflicting dark damage. With the dark art of hexing strictly forbidden, who would dare fill an urn with such power?  
> The very origin of the art are unclear, yet another reason hexing is feared.

### Dark Souls 2 Hexing Urn Usage

  * Explodes to deal dark damage.

### Hexing Urn Location in DKS2

  * Sold by [Titchy Gren](/Gren "Dark Souls 2 Gren") for 350 souls.
  * 10 can be found just before the second bonfire in [Harvest Valley](/harvest+valley "Dark Souls 2 harvest valley"), in the area with the two Mounted Beasts. Climb the first ladder as you are coming from the first bonfire.
