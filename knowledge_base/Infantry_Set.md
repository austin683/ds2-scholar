**Infantry Set** is an Attire or [Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor") in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

> Helmet/Armor/Gloves/Boots worn by Drangleic infantry.  
>    
>  A piece of basic, minimal equipment. At least it's light and easy-to-use.

###  Set Location

  * Sold by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") (2'600 sould, minus helm).
  * Helm is found in [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants"), in a chest above a yard filled with powder barrels, right next to bomb-throwing [Hollow Soldiers](/Hollow+Royal+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Royal Soldier").

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
