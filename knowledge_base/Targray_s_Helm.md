_"The Blue Sentinels have vowed to uplift_ _themselves from tireless training and_ _selfless adherence to order and justice."_

### Location

  * Purchased from [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia") after murdering [Targrey](/Blue+Sentinel+Targray "Dark Souls 2 Blue Sentinel Targray"), found after killing the [Old Dragonslayer](/Old+Dragonslayer "Dark Souls 2 Old Dragonslayer"), or by getting rank 3 in the Blue Sentinels covenant and talking to targray.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Targray's Helm](/Targray's+Helm "Dark Souls 2 Targray's Helm").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab").
