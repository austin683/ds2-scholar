**Darknight Stone** is a type of [Upgrade Material](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Darknight Stone** , a transformed variant of titanite. Utilize it to infuse weapons with darkness or to bestow darkness resistance to shields. Legends recount the efforts of sorcerers at the Melfian Magic Academy who sought to instill titanite with a range of elements, only to meet with apparent failure. [Upgrade Materials](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades") are items that are used to strengthen the statistical value and to increase the effect of a certain piece of equipment.

> An altered state of titanite. Use to imbue weapons with darkness, or to provide darkness reduction to shields.  
> Sorcerers at the Melfian Magic Academy once attempted to imbue titanite with various elements, but are said to have failed. Someone must have succeeded though; what else would explain this stone?

### Dark Souls 2 Darknight Stone Usage

  * Can only be used at Blacksmith [Steady Hand McDuff](/Steady+Hand+McDuff "Dark Souls 2 Steady Hand McDuff")
  * Used to imbue equipment with darkness. This reduces the Strength and Dexterity scaling but adds Intelligence and Faith scaling. (Different weapons add different tiers of scaling)

### Darknight Stone Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Given in exchange for [Smooth & Silky Stone](/Smooth+%26+Silky+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Smooth %26 Silky Stone") ([Small Smooth & Silky Stone](/Small+Smooth+%26+Silky+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Small Smooth %26 Silky Stone") also works) with [Dyna and Tillo.](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo")
  * The [Crystal Lizard](/Crystal+Lizard "Dark Souls 2 Crystal Lizard") on the bridge at the bottom of the [Grave of Saints](/Grave+of+Saints "Dark Souls 2 Grave of Saints"), right before the entrance to [The Gutter](/The+Gutter "Dark Souls 2 The Gutter").
  * Inside a Chest in the [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt"), passing straight through the door which leads to the boss corridor.
  * Found on a corpse near one of the drakes in [Dragon Aerie](/Dragon+Aerie "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Aerie").
  * Found on Apostles in [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt") (Keeping a few of their tombstones up means quick and infinite respawning. Just hit the bell)
  * 3 are sold by [Stone trader Chloanne](/Stone+trader+Chloanne "Dark Souls 2 Stone trader Chloanne") after obtaining the [King's Ring](/King%27s+Ring "Dark Souls 2 King%27s Ring").
  * 2 are found in a body in the moving platforms in [Shulva - Sanctum City](/Shulva+-+Sanctum+City "Dark Souls 2 Shulva - Sanctum City"), in the DLC [Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King")

### DKS2 Darknight Stone Drops from:
