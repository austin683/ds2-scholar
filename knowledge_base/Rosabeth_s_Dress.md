#  Rosabeth's Dress

This item seems to exist only in the game code.   
  
Even when trying to equip the item, or pick it up, the game will say your inventory is full, or allow you to try and equip it but then not actually equip.   
  
Info:   
_A terribly worn dress._   
_Belonged to Rosabeth of Melfia._   
  
_Rosabeth adored this dress, despite the_   
_fact it has no bearing on pyromancy._   
_If it suits your fancy, by all means, try it on._
