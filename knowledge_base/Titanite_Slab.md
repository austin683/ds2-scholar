**Titanite Slab** is a type of [Upgrade Material](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Titanite Slab** , a legendary piece of titanite discovered in an ancient layer of earth, considered a divine gift. This slab is one of the original pieces that bestowed the world with various forms of titanite. According to legends, titanite slabs were crafted to forge the weapons of the gods. They are crucial for reinforcing equipment, allowing enhancements up to +10. [Upgrade Materials](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades") are items that are used to strengthen the statistical value and to increase the effect of a certain piece of equipment.

> Titanite Slab used to reinforce equipment. Reinforces equipment up to +10.
> 
> Titanite was discovered in an ancient layer of earth, and is said to be a gift of the gods. One of the original slabs that provided the world with all forms of titanite.
> 
> Titanite slabs were created to smith the weapons of the gods, or so the legends say.

### Titanite Slab Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * [Reinforce](/upgrades "Dark Souls 2 upgrades") standard equipment up to +10 (Only one is required)

### DKS2 Titanite Slab Location

  * After killing 10 masters as a gray phantom, go back to the leader of the [Bell Keepers](/Bell+Keepers "Dark Souls 2 Bell Keepers") covenant and talk to him to receive the [Titanite Slab](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab")
  * Sometimes given as a reward instead of a Titanite Chunk for killing host as a gray phantom for [Bell Keepers](/Bell+Keepers "Dark Souls 2 Bell Keepers") covenant. Not known exactly but roughly 1 slab for every 10-15 kills.
  * Sold by [Stone Trader Chloanne](/Stone+trader+Chloanne "Dark Souls 2 Stone trader Chloanne") (only 1)
  * Defeat the first area boss in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora")
  * Trade [Small Smooth & Silky Stones](/Small+Smooth+%26+Silky+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Small Smooth %26 Silky Stone"), for a higher chance, use a [Petrified something](/Petrified+something "Dark Souls 2 Petrified something") with [Dyna and Tillo (rare)](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo")
  * Lower Brightstone Tseldora Cave Area in NG+. It is in the room that connects the first section of the town to the second.
  * Received from [Steady Hand McDuff](/Steady+Hand+McDuff "Dark Souls 2 Steady Hand McDuff") the Blacksmith after giving him the dull ember and spending 50k souls in his shop, of which 12k needs to be for weapon infusion. (Unlocks the "Smith for Life" Achievement/trophy)
  * 1 in an iron chest behind Pharros Contraption in [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants"), under room with 4 ballistas
  * 1 in the poison mist in [Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley") near 2 [banediggers](/banedigger "Dark Souls 2 banedigger"). ( near the entrance to the room with [Heirs of the Sun ](/Heirs+of+the+Sun "Dark Souls 2 Heirs of the Sun") covenant )  

  * 1 on a body in the poison mist before the entrance to the bottom of [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak")
  * 1 in an iron chest [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower").
  * 1 in an iron chest in [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum") in the room with the 2 Sanctum Knight coffins.
  * 1 in an iron chest in [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce") in the room with the once-frozen mimic.

  * [The Imperfect](/The+Imperfect "Dark Souls 2 The Imperfect") near the third bonfire of [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum").
  * [Dragonrider enemy](/Dragonrider+enemy "Dark Souls 2 Dragonrider enemy") in [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana") and [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt").
  * Stone Knights (horse headed stone knights in Kings passage) (Uncommon)
