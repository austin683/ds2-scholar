**Soul Spear** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul Spear** , an advanced sorcery crafting souls into a spear that penetrates enemies, inflicting substantial damage. Although attributed to a forgotten master sorcerer, the spell's origin remains shrouded in mystery. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "Sorcery that fires souls shaped into a spear.  
> Pierces enemies, causing heavy damage.  
>   
> This spells was said to have been devised by a master sorcerer, but his name is long forgotten."

### Soul Spear Acquired From

  * Corpse in the hut, in [Huntsman's Copse.](/Huntsman%27s+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman%27s Copse") [(Location Video)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bkNd3ZChjcw)
  * Bought from [Grave Warden Agdayne](/Grave+Warden+Agdayne "Dark Souls 2 Grave Warden Agdayne") for 12,000 souls

### Soul Spear Notes

  * Shoots a projectile that deals magic damage. Pierces through multiple enemies. Roughly the same casting speed as [Great Heavy Soul Arrow](/Great+Heavy+Soul+Arrow "Dark Souls 2 Great Heavy Soul Arrow") yet over twice as powerful per shot, and has a slightly wider bolt and longer range. A less effective [Crystal Soul Spear](/Crystal+Soul+Spear "Dark Souls 2 Crystal Soul Spear").
  * Can be farmed via burning [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") at the second bonfire in Huntsman's Copse.
