**Eternal Sanctum Key** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**.**Eternal Sanctum Key** , a key to the inner chamber of the Eternal Sanctum. The Sunken King constructed this sanctum to shelter Sinh the Dragon. Unlocks the door at the entrance of [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon's+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon's Sanctum"), leading to the [Priestess' Chamber](/Priestess'+Chamber "Dark Souls 2 Priestess' Chamber") bonfire and the [Cave of the Dead](/Cave+of+the+Dead "Dark Souls 2 Cave of the Dead"). Accessible when coming through the bridge from Shulva - Sanctum City. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> Key to the inner chamber of the Eternal Sanctum. The Sunken King erected the Eternal Sanctum to shelter Sinh the Dragon.  
> The sanctum appears to be a solemn temple, but it is filled with devilish creatures that ensure no trespasser shall cause offense.

### Eternal Sanctum Key Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Opens the door at the entrance of [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon's+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon's Sanctum") (coming through the bridge from [Shulva - Sanctum City](/Shulva+-+Sanctum+City "Dark Souls 2 Shulva - Sanctum City")). That path leads to the [Priestess' Chamber](/Priestess'+Chamber "Dark Souls 2 Priestess' Chamber") bonfire and the [Cave of the Dead](/Cave+of+the+Dead "Dark Souls 2 Cave of the Dead").

### Dark Souls 2 Eternal Sanctum Key Location

  * In [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum"), on a corpse in the room with three [Sanctum Knights](/Sanctum+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Knight") and several of their armor. From the "spike room", go up the stone ladder, and cross the two bridges. Follow the stairway down, and you'll be in the room.
