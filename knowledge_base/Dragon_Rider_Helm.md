_"Helm worn by the Dragonriders, King Vendrick's royal guard.According to legend, the Dragonriders straddled not horses, but wyrms."_

### Location

  * Can be bought by defeating the [Twin Dragonriders](/Twin+Dragonriders "Dark Souls 2 Twin Dragonriders") in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle"), from the [Magelord of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") in NG+. 
    * Alternatively can be acquired in [Iron Keep](/iron+keep#.U0QjQvl_st0 "Dark Souls 2 iron keep#.U0QjQvl_st0") when the 1st bonfire's intensity at 2 (NG+ or [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic")).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Dragonrider Set](/Dragonrider+Set "Dark Souls 2 Dragonrider Set").
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * Offers second highest physical defenses, except for strike defense; thrust defense is the highest among helms.
