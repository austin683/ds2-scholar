**Royal Rat Authority Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Royal Rat Authority Soul** , the soul of the Royal Rat Authority in the underground realm. Those who pledge allegiance to the Rat King must possess the courage to confront His trials. Acquired by defeating the [Royal Rat Authority](/Royal+Rat+Authority "Dark Souls 2 Royal Rat Authority") in the [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Royal Rat Authority, of the underground realm.  
> Those who choose to serve the Rat King must have the courage to face His challenges.  
> Use the special soul of this rat to acquire numerous souls. or to create something of great worth.

### Dark Souls 2 Royal Rat Authority Soul Usage

  * Consuming gives 6000 Souls.
  * Trade with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for [Acid Surge](/Acid+Surge#fex "Dark Souls 2 Acid Surge#fex").

### Royal Rat Authority Soul Location in DKS2

  * Obtained by defeating the [Royal Rat Authority](/Royal+Rat+Authority "Dark Souls 2 Royal Rat Authority") in [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros").
