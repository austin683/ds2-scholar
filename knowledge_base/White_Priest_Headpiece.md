_"Traditional Headpiece worn by Drangleic clerics._  
  
_The clerics of Drangleic were not viewed with particular reverence, and their positions were only preserved as a nod to tradition._  
  
_It is customary for clerics to wear different garb depending on their sex, but the reason for this practice is unknown. This is a men's headpiece."_

### Location

  * Purchased from [Cromwell The Pardoner](/Cromwell+The+Pardoner).

### Notes

  * Part of the [White Hollow Mage Set](/White+Hollow+Mage+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab).
  * Headpiece increases 1+ [Faith](/Faith).
