**Old Knight Leggings** is a **Legging** in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Old Knight Leggings Description

"Old undated Leggings.  
How old could this nearly-crumbling armor be?  
Has extremely low durability.  
Sometimes, just as a thing falls to pieces, it unleashes its last flash of great power..."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Dropped by old knights in [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide%27s Tower of Flame")

### Notes

  * Part of the [Old Knight Set](/Old+Knight+Set "Dark Souls 2 Old Knight Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shards, Titanite Chunks and Titanite Slab](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores")
  * The least durable heavy armor, extremely vulnerable to [King's Ultra Greatsword](/King%27s+Ultra+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 King%27s Ultra Greatsword")
