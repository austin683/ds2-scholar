**Slash Damage** is one of the [Damage Types](/Damage+Types "Dark Souls 2 Damage Types") in **Dark Souls 2**. It is one of the seven main Damage Types and belongs to the Physical Damage category. Damage Types are a major part of the calculation that determines how much damage is dealt in [Combat](/Combat "Dark Souls 2 Combat") and are affected by many factors including your equipment, spells and temporary buffs.

### Dark Souls 2 Slash Damage Guide

**Slash Damage** is a type of Physical Damage dealt by both players and enemies alike. Slash Damage is physical damage that differs from [Strike Damage](/Strike+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Strike Damage") or [Thrust Damage](/Thrust+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Thrust Damage") types. Slash Damage is often effective against most unarmored enemies and beetles.

All Physical Damage can be increased with any variant of the [Ring of Blades](/Ring+of+Blades "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Blades"), and all incoming Physical Damage can be reduced by any variant of the [Ring of Steel Protection](/Ring+of+Steel+Protection "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Steel Protection"), [armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor"), and blocking.
