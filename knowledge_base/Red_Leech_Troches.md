**Red Leech Troches** are a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Red Leech Troches** , troches emitting an unusual charred aroma. Temporarily bolster bleeding resistance. Verbal histories frequently recount Saint Elizabeth's uniquely crafted medicines and potions, which continue to see widespread use today. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Troches with an oddly charred odor. Temporarily boosts bleeding resist.  
> Verbal histories often mention Saint Elizabeth; her uniquely concocted medicines and potions are still widely used today.

### Red Leech Troches Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Boosts bleed Defense by 200 for 90 seconds

### Dark Souls 2 Red Leech Troches Location

  * [The Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep")
  * [Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley")
  * Dropped by [Amana Aberrations](/Amana+Aberration "Dark Souls 2 Amana Aberration") (Lizardmen) in the [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana")
  * Sold by the [Rat King](/Rat+King "Dark Souls 2 Rat King") for 1600 souls.
