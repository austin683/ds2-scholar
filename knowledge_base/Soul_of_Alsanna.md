**Soul of Alsanna** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of Alsanna** , the oracle of Eleum Loyce, who maintains a silent vigil in prayer. This Child of Dark, reverent toward the impending apocalypse, dedicates herself to a ritual aimed at pacifying the raging flame. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game. Redirect to [Soul of Alsanna, Silent Oracle](/Soul+of+Alsanna,+Silent+Oracle "Dark Souls 2 Soul of Alsanna, Silent Oracle") page.

### Soul of Alsanna Usage in DKS2

  * Trade 12,000 souls and the Soul of Alsanna to [Ornifex](/weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 weaponsmith Ornifex") to acquire [Eleum Loyce](/Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Eleum Loyce") (Curved Sword)
  * Consume it for 30,000 souls

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of Alsanna Location

  * Trade 50x [Loyce Soul](/Loyce+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Soul") to [Alsanna, Silent Oracle](/Alsanna%2C+Silent+Oracle "Dark Souls 2 Alsanna%2C Silent Oracle").
  * Or kill [Alsanna, Silent Oracle](/Alsanna%2C+Silent+Oracle "Dark Souls 2 Alsanna%2C Silent Oracle") with ranged attack.
