**Faith** is an [Attribute](/Stats "Dark Souls 2 Stats") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Faith** determines your character's lightning bonus, lightning defence, and casting speed. Attributes can be increased by assigning points into them when [Leveling Up](/Level "Dark Souls 2 Level").

### Faith Information

Attribute required for miracles and other spells. Boosts proficiency in lightning and fire ATK and DEF. Boost spell casting speed.

  * **Raises[Casting Speed](/Casting+Speed#fex "Dark Souls 2 Casting Speed#fex")**
  * **Raises fire, lightning, dark, bleed bonus**
  * **Raises fire, lightning, dark, bleed resistance**
  * Slightly increases HP for each point: +2 HP (0-20), +1 HP (21-50), +0 HP (51-99)
  * The Sorcerer begins with the lowest Faith at 4 and the Cleric starts with the highest Faith at 12.
  * [Miracles](/Miracles "Dark Souls 2 Miracles") and [Hexes](/Hexes "Dark Souls 2 Hexes") require a minimum faith level to cast. Some special weapons also have a faith requirement.
  * The soft cap for faith bonuses appears to be at 40 faith.
  * See here for [equipment with faith requirements](/Equipment+with+stat+requirements "Dark Souls 2 Equipment with stat requirements")
