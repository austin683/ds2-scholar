#  Armourer Dennis

  
Armorer Dennis is a Red Phantom Invader. Only available in the Scholar of the First Sin edition for Xbox One, PS-4 and DX11.

### 

###  Encounters
    
    
    | Locations |  |
    | --- | --- |
    | Forest of Fallen Giants | Spawns in the room containing the Resting Giant (Giant Tree) next to the Soldier's Rest Bonfire room |
    | Iron Keep | Spawns to left of the Iron Keep 'main' door. Will invade after resting at any bonfire once Fencer Sharron has invaded. |
    

###  Equipment

  * [Blue Flame](/Blue+Flame)
  * [Grand Spirit Tree Shield](/Grand+Spirit+Tree+Shield)
  * [Northwarder Hood](/Northwarder+Set)
  * [Black Robes](/Black+Robes)
  * [Black Boots](/Black+Boots)

###  Drops

  * [Human Effigy](/Human+Effigy)
  * [Clear Bluestone Ring (+2) (rare)](/Clear+Bluestone+Ring)

###  Notes

  * Greets the player with the "Point" gesture.
  * When he kills a summoned phantom, or even a summoned dark spirit, he will do the "Warmup" gesture.
  * Uses the following [sorceries](/sorceries): [Great Heavy Soul Arrow](/Great+Heavy+Soul+Arrow), [Soul Spear](/Soul+Spear), [Soul Greatsword](/Soul+Greatsword), [Soul Flash](/Soul+Flash "Soul Flash") and [Soul Vortex](/Soul+Vortex).
