**Minotaur Helm** is a [Helm](/Helms "Dark Souls 2 Helms") in Dark Souls 2.

_An ornamental helm commissioned by the Old Iron King._   
_An awkwardly repurposed iron ornament, and as such, remains a stuffy, heavy thing._   
_Wearing it gives one the sensation of being smothered in iron._

### Location

  * Dropped by [Iron Warriors](/Iron+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Iron Warrior") in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower") and [Iron Passage](/Iron+passage "Dark Souls 2 Iron passage").
  * [Scholar of the First Sin] Also dropped by NPC invader [Oliver the Collector](/Oliver+The+Collector "Dark Souls 2 Oliver The Collector") in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep") (rare).

### Minotaur Helm Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
  * Upgraded stats are only 50% higher.
  * The highest Poise among helmets (even higher than that of [Gyrm Warrior Greathelm](/Gyrm+Warrior+Set "Dark Souls 2 Gyrm Warrior Set")).
