### Leydia White Gauntlets Description

"_Gloves of a Leydia Pyromancer._  
_Leydia apostles, in their conceit, occupied the Undead Crypt and misused death._  
_This invoked the ire of Fenito, who branded them as transgressors."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Drops from [Leydia Pyromancer](/Leydia+Pyromancer) (the wraiths that spawn from the special tombstones) in the [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt).
