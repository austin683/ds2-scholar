**Mastodon Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Mastodon Set](/Mastodon+Set) in **Dark Souls** **2**.

### Mastodon Leggings Description

"Leggings worn by the Primal Knights of Drangleic castle.  
Their weight would normally crush a man, but the Primal Knights wear them as if they were silk, so fearsome is their brutal strength.  
The King restored a forbidden, long-lost art to create these inhuman abominations."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Dropped by the Primal Knights at Drangleic Castle.

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [titanite](/titanite)
  * this set's gloves and boots has the highest phyiscal defense among all armors under normal titanite upgrading path.
  * While [Gyrm Warrior Set](/Gyrm+Warrior+Set)has the highest physical defense for headpiece (greathelm) and armor, [Mastodon Set](/Mastodon+Set) has the highest physical defense for gloves and boot, among all armors under normal titanite upgrading path.
