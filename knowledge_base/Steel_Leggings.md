**Steel Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Steel Set](/Steel+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Steel Leggings Description

_"A relic of an order of knights said to be the mightiest warriors of their time."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * This armor is acquired from Memory of Orro.
  * Found in a chest behind an Illusory Wall next to the second Pharros' contraption.

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shard](https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Titanite+Shard), [Large Titanite Shard](https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Large+Titanite+Shard), [Titanite Chunk](https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Titanite+Chunk), and **[Titanite Slab](https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Titanite+Slab).**
