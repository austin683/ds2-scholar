**Heavy Homing Soul Arrow** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Heavy Homing Soul Arrow** , a sorcery created by Straid, the renowned mage of ancient Olaphis. Launches robust and potent soul arrows that diligently pursue their targets to the farthest reaches. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "One of the sorceries devised by Straid, the great mage of ancient Olaphis.   
> Fires thick, powerful soul arrows that seek their targets to the ends of the earth"

### DKS2 Heavy Homing Soul Arrow Acquired From

  * [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") traded for the [Ruin Sentinel Soul](/Ruin+Sentinel+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Ruin Sentinel Soul")
