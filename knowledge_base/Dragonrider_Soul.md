**Dragonrider Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Dragonrider Soul** , the soul of a Dragonrider who faithfully served King Vendrick. In the days of old, dragonriders mounted wyrms, striking fear into the hearts of their foes with unparalleled strength. Consume a Dragonrider soul to gain numerous souls or use it to create a valuable item. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of a Dragonrider, who faithfully served King Vendrick.  
> Long ago, the dragonriders mounted wyrms, and were feared on the battlefield for their unparallelled strength.  
> Use a Dragonrider soul to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Dragonrider Soul Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Can be consumed 6,000 Souls.
  * Can be Traded with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") and 1,500 souls each for either: 
    * [Dragonrider's Halberd](/Dragonrider%27s+Halberd#fex "Dark Souls 2 Dragonrider%27s Halberd#fex")
    * [Dragonrider Twinblade](/Dragonrider+Twinblade#fex "Dark Souls 2 Dragonrider Twinblade#fex")
    * [Dragonrider Bow](/Dragonrider+Bow#fex "Dark Souls 2 Dragonrider Bow#fex")
    * [Dragonrider Greatshield](/Dragonrider+Greatshield#fex "Dark Souls 2 Dragonrider Greatshield#fex")

### Dark Souls 2 Dragonrider Soul Location

  * Obtained by defeating [Dragonrider](/Dragonrider "Dark Souls 2 Dragonrider") in [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide%27s Tower of Flame").
  * Obtained by defeating the [Twin Dragonriders](/DragonRider%28Second+Encounter%29 "Dark Souls 2 DragonRider%28Second Encounter%29") in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle").
