**Brigand Set** is an Attire or Armor in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds).

  
  
  
  
  
_"Hood/Armor/Gauntlets/Trousers donned by looting brigands."_   
_Hood: "Mountains surround Drangleic on three sides. Most travelers who try to cross them end up hapless prey to the countless brigands lurking there."_   
_Rest of set: "This armor may appear shoddy, but is in fact a very functional piece of work, its materials meticulously crafted together."_

###  Set Location

  * Found in [No-Man's Wharf](/No-Man%27s+Wharf), in a chest in living quarters (past the bridge, up the stone stairs and through the carts on the right).

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Titanite").
