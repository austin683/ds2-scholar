**Traveling Merchant Set** is an Attire or Armor in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds).

  
  
  
_"Hat/Coat/Gloves/Boots worn by merchants from Lanafir,"_   
_helm adds: "where blue represents knowledge. Wearing this increases item discovery."_   
_Armor adds: "The eagle is normally a symbol of strength, but in Lanafir it symbolizes wealth."_   
_All: "Lanafir is the farthest land to the south and follows a strict edict of isolationism. The rare visitor from Lanafir is always a little odd.""_   
  
  
  
  

###  Set Location

  * Starting armor of Explorer class.
  * Sold by [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia) (4'630).

  
  

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Upgrades#Ore).
  * Hat raises [drop rate](/Mechanics+Research).
