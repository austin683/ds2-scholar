**Very Good Carving** is a special multiplayer item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Very Good Carving** , faces carved from aged tree wood that speak when thrown to the ground. Specifically crafted to express apologies, this carving says 'Very Good' and can be used limitlessly to communicate with other players in [Co-op or PvP](/Online "Dark Souls 2 Online") modes. [Tools](/Tools "Dark Souls 2 Tools") are unique, reusable items which assist the player during various facets of gameplay ranging from basic communication to assisting in boss encounters.

> Faces carved from the wood of aged trees. They speak when thrown upon the ground. The fine craftsmanship of these faces is  
> evident even after much aging, but no one knows even the name of the artist. This face says "Very Good!" Doesn't it look cheerful?

### Dark Souls 2 Very Good Carving Usage

  * Used to communicate with other players in [Co-op or PvP](/Online "Dark Souls 2 Online") modes.
  * When used, this carving says "Very Good."
  * This item has unlimited uses.

### Dark Souls 2 Very Good Carving Location

  * Bought from [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") in the [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep") for 2,000 souls.
