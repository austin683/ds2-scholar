_"Metal helm worn by Knights._

_Despite the thin metal used, the grooved texture gives it added protection._  
_Many knights favor this/these helmet (armor/gauntlets/leggings) for its/their fine craftsmanship, and its/their like has been passed down the generations."_

### Location

  * At the bottom of the tower in [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame), past the sentinel in the flooded hall, on the way to [No-Man's Wharf](/No-Man%27s+Wharf).
  * +5 variants drop from black [phantom](/Phantoms) in [New Game Plus](/New+Game+Plus) near [Mild-mannered Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate) located in [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Knight Set](/Knight+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab).
