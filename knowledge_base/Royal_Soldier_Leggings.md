**Royal Soldier Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Royal Soldier Set](/Royal+Soldier+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Royal Soldier Leggings Description

"Royal Drangleic soldier leggings.  
Equipment crafted by the royal blacksmith. Finely polished and ready for battle."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Sold by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer) in [Majula](/Majula)

### Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite)
  * Must have spent 1000+ souls for the Royal Soldier set to appear in his inventory
