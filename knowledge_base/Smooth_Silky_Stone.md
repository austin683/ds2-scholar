**Smooth & Silky Stone** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A perfectly smooth and silky stone.  
> Use to restore HP.  
> The shine of this stone is no ordinary polish, and can only be achieved over a long period. Some in this land are in search of such mystical stones.

### Prism Stone Usage

  * Restores 20% of the user's HP.
  * Can be traded for medium-tier items with [Dyna & Tillo](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo") in [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt").
  * [Dyna & Tillo Item Trading](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PkP5qodpl3o)

### Prism Stone Location

  * x10 reward for achieving the [Rat King Covenant](/Rat+King+Covenant "Dark Souls 2 Rat King Covenant") rank 2.
  * At the beginning near the hut of the 3 [Firekeepers](/Firekeepers "Dark Souls 2 Firekeepers"), near the waterfall. You can see it from the first bridge to the left. It's a small smooth and silky stone but if you use bonfire ascetic or go there NG+ you will get 3 smooth and silky stones.
  * Reward for fulfilling your duty as a shade using the [Small White Sign Soapstone](/Small+White+Sign+Soapstone "Dark Souls 2 Small White Sign Soapstone").
  * Up the stairs to the right before the Lost Sinner boss in [Sinners' Rise](/Sinners'+Rise "Dark Souls 2 Sinners' Rise") (need the [Bastille Key](/Bastille+Key "Dark Souls 2 Bastille Key")).
  * x5 in a chest in Memory of Vammar near Drummond.
  * Drop from the [Banedigger](/Banedigger "Dark Souls 2 Banedigger") (Dark Magic Giants) in [Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley").
  * Drop from [Amana Aberrations](/Amana+Aberration "Dark Souls 2 Amana Aberration") (Lizardmen) in [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana").
  * Drop from the [Undead Devourer](/Undead+Devourer "Dark Souls 2 Undead Devourer") (pig) in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora") near the first bonfire ([Royal Army Campsite](/Royal+Army+Campsite "Dark Souls 2 Royal Army Campsite")). (Roughly 1/4 drop rate)
  * Drop from [Invisible Hollows](/Invisible+Hollows "Dark Souls 2 Invisible Hollows") (SotFS)

### Prism Stone Notes and Tips

  * **Dyna & Tillo will not accept stacked stones.** You may stand in the nest, select "Leave" and drop them individually to receive a reward. You may drop many individual stones one at a time and receive all the rewards at once.
  * You will always receive a reward of the same or a greater tier value.
