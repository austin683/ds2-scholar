**Ammunition** in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)** are items that can be used with [Bows](/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows"), [Greatbows](/Greatbows "Dark Souls 2 Greatbows") and [Crossbows](/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows"), a player can equip two types of bolts and arrows at the same time. Ammunition serves as a consumable essential for utilizing ranged weapons like Bows and Crossbows. Arrows and Bolts, among other types, are employed to deal damage to hostile characters, including [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").
