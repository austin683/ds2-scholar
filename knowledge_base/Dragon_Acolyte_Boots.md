**Dragon Acolyte Boots** is a **legging** and a part of [Dragon Acolyte Set](/Dragon+Acolyte+Set "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Acolyte Set") in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Dragon Acolyte Boots Description

_"Boots worn by acolytes of Aldia.  
Several of the greatest minds converged in Aldia to weave strange new rituals, but rumors suggest that during the course of their work their thoughts were not their own."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Dropped from the [Dragon Acolyte](/Dragon+Acolyte "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Acolyte") just before the Guardian dragon.
  * Mask is guaranteed drop from petrified troll (does not respawn) under the skeletal dragon

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with Titanite Shards
  * The Giant caged Basilisk has a rare chance to drop a Dragon Sage Hood
