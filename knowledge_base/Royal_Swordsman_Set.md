**Royal Swordsman Set** is an Attire or [Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor") in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

> Helmet/Armor/Gloves/Leggings of a royal swordsman. Its shape provides defense while allowing great mobility.  
>   
> Stripped of ornamentation, this particular helm/armor is designed strictly for battle.  
>   
> King Vendrick supplied his bravest men with the best armor available to face the great giants, but very few returned alive.

###  Set Location

  * Dropped by [Royal Swordsmen](/Royal+Swordsman "Dark Souls 2 Royal Swordsman") in [The Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille") and [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle").
  * Dropped by NPC invader [Bowman Guthry](/Bowman+Guthry "Dark Souls 2 Bowman Guthry") encountered in [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros") and [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora").
  * [Scholar of the First Sin] Royal Swordsmen are also found in [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants"), on the path to [Pursuer](/Pursuer "Dark Souls 2 Pursuer").
  * The Chestpiece also drops from Maldron the Assasin in Eleum Loyce dlc

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
