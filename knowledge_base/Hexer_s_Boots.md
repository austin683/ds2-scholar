**Hexer's Boots** is a **legging** and a part of [Hexer's Set](/Hexer's+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Hexer's Boots Description

"Armor worn by a hexer. Belonged to Felkin the Outcast.  
Those who have a taste of dark are drawn into its vortex and rarely return.  
Perhaps it appeals to something deep within the human soul.."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Starting gear for the Warrior Class
  * Sold by Maughlin the Armourer in Majula.

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with Titanite Shard, Large Titanite Shard, Titanite Chunk, Titanite Slab.
