**Ivory King Helm** is a [Helm](/Helms "Dark Souls 2 Helms") in Dark Souls 2. It is part of the [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King") DLC.

     _"Helm of the Ivory King of Eleum Loyce._
    
    
     _The Ivory King kept his countenance from his people. The Knights of Loyce dutifully followed suit, never unhinging their helmets in public view."_

### Location

  * Sold by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") once 50 [Loyce Souls](/Loyce+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Soul") are collected and [Alsanna](/Alsanna%2C+Silent+Oracle "Dark Souls 2 Alsanna%2C Silent Oracle") has been spoken to (spend 15k souls at Maughlin's shop to unlock boss armor sets).

### Ivory King Helm Notes

  * Part of the [Ivory King Set](/Ivory+King+Set "Dark Souls 2 Ivory King Set")
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * Absorbs 5 HP from each defeated foe, but only if the wearer is female.
