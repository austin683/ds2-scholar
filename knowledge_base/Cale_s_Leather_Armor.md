_"Armor_  _of a light mirrah foot soldier. belonged to Cale the Cartographer._

_Apparently Cale procured this inexpensively second-hand, but it's surprisingly well-made.__"_

### Location

  * Talk to Cale in abandoned house after lighting all regular bonfires.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Cale's Set](/Cale's+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Ores).
