**Oliver The Collector** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Oliver The Collector Information in Dark Souls 2

  * Only appears in Dark Souls II: Scholar of the First Sin.
  * He can wield lots of different weapons, including the [Puzzling Stone Sword](/Puzzling+Stone+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Puzzling Stone Sword"), the [Majestic Greatsword](/Majestic+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 Majestic Greatsword"), the [Smelter Hammer](/Smelter+Hammer "Dark Souls 2 Smelter Hammer"), the [Bone Fist ](/Bone+Fist "Dark Souls 2 Bone Fist") and the broken [Santier's Spear](/Santier's+Spear "Dark Souls 2 Santier's Spear").
  * He wears an heavy armor making difficult to stagger him.

### Dark Souls 2 Oliver The Collector Location

  * [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep"): after the Belfry Sol, in the upper part of Iron Keep, he will invade you near the wooden chest containing the [Destructive Great Arrows](/Destructive+Greatarrow "Dark Souls 2 Destructive Greatarrow").

### Oliver The Collector Drops in DKS2

### DKS2 Oliver The Collector Strategy Tips

  * **He will invade you wielding the Santier's spear and the majestic greatsword. Beware that he will constantly change weapons during the fight.**
