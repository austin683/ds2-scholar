**Bandit Leggings** is a **legging** and a part of [Bandit Set](/Bandit+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Bandit Leggings Description

_"Boots of bandits of the Forossan outskirts.  
The territory of Forossa became lawless after the kingdom fell to war. Citizens became bandits, and scattered to other lands." _

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Starting Bandit Class
