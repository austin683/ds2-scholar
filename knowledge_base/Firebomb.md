**Firebomb** is a type of [Projectile](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2).** **Firebomb** , a ceramic urn filled with black powder that detonates, causing fire damage. [Projectiles](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") are offensive items that are usually thrown at hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") to inflict a small amount of damage.

> Bisque urn filled with black powder. Explodes, inflicting fire damage. Though this urn is a crude and commonplace weapon, it owes its continued usage to its simplicity and effectiveness in a variety of situations.

### Firebomb Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Explodes to deal fire damage.
  * Can be used on barrels to trigger explosions and break walls.

### Dark Souls 2 Firebomb Location

  * Sold by [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia") for 200 souls.
  * Drops from Hollow Royal Soldiers. (the ones that attack you with firebombs)

### Firebomb Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Can be deflected when enemies throw them.
