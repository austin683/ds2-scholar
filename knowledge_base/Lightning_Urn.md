**Lightning Urn** is a type of [Projectile](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2).** **Lightning Urn** , a petite clay urn surrounded by lightning that explodes, delivering lightning damage. [Projectiles](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") are offensive items that are usually thrown at hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") to inflict a small amount of damage.

> A small clay urn enwreathed in lightning. Explodes, inflicting lightning damage. Created in Melfia, a land that flourishes with magic and pyromancy. Though the lightning contained in the urn lacks potency, it is a boon to any inexperienced traveler.

### Dark Souls 2 Lightning Urn Usage

  * Explodes to deal lightning damage.

### Lightning Urn Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Sold by [Head of Vengarl](/Head+of+Vengarl "Dark Souls 2 Head of Vengarl") for 400 souls. (Unlimited stock)
