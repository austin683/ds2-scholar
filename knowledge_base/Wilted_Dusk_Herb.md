**Wilted Dusk Herb** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2). Wilted Dusk Herb** , a withered and pale stem of dusk herb. This herb significantly replenishes spell uses. The vibrant efficacy of a blooming dusk herb is fleeting, as it quickly withers. Locating such a herb is an exceptionally challenging task. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A wilted and faded stalk of dusk herb. Restores a great deal of spell uses. A dusk herb in bloom works wonders, but will wilt in the blink of an eye. Needless to say, finding such an herb is of the utmost difficulty.

### Wilted Dusk Herb Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Restores 70% of all spell uses when consumed.

### Dark Souls 2 Wilted Dusk Herb Location

  * Dropped by [Desert Sorceress](/Desert+Sorceress "Dark Souls 2 Desert Sorceress") in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak").
  * Dropped by Petrified Statue in front of caged [Great Basilisk](/Great+Basilisk "Dark Souls 2 Great Basilisk") in [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s Keep")
  * 3 near Cale in Forest of Fallen Giant NG+.
  * Sold by [Chancellor Wellager](/Chancellor+Wellager "Dark Souls 2 Chancellor Wellager") in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle") for 4,000 souls in limited quantity.
  * On the roof after the 2 archers in the Memory of Orro
  * Recommended to farm this herb from [Desert Sorceress](/Desert+Sorceress "Dark Souls 2 Desert Sorceress")
