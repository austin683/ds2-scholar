**Straight Swords** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are a type of [Weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") that provides balance and offers a variety of attacks such as thrusts and slashes. Some Straight Swords cannot thrust, but they make up for it by having high damage output. Straight Swords are one of the most versatile weapon types in Dark Souls 2, they are perfect for infusions, maximizing the weapon's offensive capabilities. Straight Swords also save up your stamina, but the hits can be less effective. With it's slashing move, you can hit multiple enemies at the same time as long as they're close enough. When you're trying to keep your distance, you can always rely on its thrust move, but do note that not all Straght Swords have a thrusting move. Straight Swords work well when paired with a [Shield](/Shields "Dark Souls 2 Shields"), which allows you to quickly follow up with an attack after blocking. Straight Swords are also compatible with nearly all types of builds, making them extremely versatile. This page covers a list of all Straight Swords in Dark Souls 2.
