**Aldia Key** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Aldia Key** , a key utilized in Aldia's mansion. King Vendrick condemned his elder brother to this mansion as they sought the truth through different means, leading to the withering of their familial ties. Found in Scholar of the First Sin Edition, dropped by the bone dragon at the entrance to [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia's+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia's Keep"). [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> Key used in the mansion of Aldia. King Vendrick condemned his own elder brother to the mansion. They both sought the truth, but through different means, and their fervor meant the eventual withering of their familial ties.

### Dark Souls 2 Aldia Key Usage

  * Opens the door on the right in [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s Keep") Main Corridor. This allows you to circle behind the second Cyclops and kill from behind.

### Aldia Key Location in Dark Souls 2

  * **Location for Scholar of the First Sin Edition**
    * The key is dropped by the bone dragon at the entrance to [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s Keep"). You need to ignite the four braziers in the area, which will spawn four invaders. After that, returning to the entrance will trigger the dragon's animation.
  * Location of The Braziers: 
    * First floor of the entrance hall, right behind the head of the dragon skeleton.
    * Third floor of the entrance hall (where the dragon skeleton lies), take a right turn in front of the basilisk in the cage. The brazier can be found right before the metal chest on the left hand side.
    * In the hallway with the paintings that leads to the boss, in an alcove on the left hand side.
    * Outside the corrosive pool, which is located near the second bonfire.
  * Location for the normal edition 
    * Located on a corpse in the Acid-Pit left from the second bonfire in [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s Keep"), guarded by two Mutant Dogs. 
      * Be careful of equipment poison (It will break your armor and rings), try luring the 2 monsters out of the cage first to obtain the items.
