**Soul of a Brave Warrior** is a type of [Consumable](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of a Brave Warrior** , the soul of a valiant Undead warrior who succumbed to Hollowing long ago. Consume to acquire a substantial number of souls. In undeath, as in life, the pursuit of souls remains. Yields 5000 souls when consumed. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are consumable items categorized as remnants of fallen warriors, consuming souls collects a certain number of souls that is added to the player's stock. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are required for players to become stronger in the game by raising their soul level as well as buying and upgrading their equipment.

> Soul of a brave Undead warrior who has long ago gone Hollow.  
> Use to acquire a large amount of souls.  
> The soul is the source of all life, and even in undeath, or hollowing, the mind seeks souls.

### Soul of a Brave Warrior Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Grants 5000 souls when consumed

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of a Brave Warrior Location

  * Undead Purgatory, after the boss fight
  * Dragon Aerie, under the first dragons wing
