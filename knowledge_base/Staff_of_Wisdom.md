**Staff of Wisdom** is a **[Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves")** and one of the available **[Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons")** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Staff of Wisdom. **Staff of Wisdom** is a sorcery-dedicated staff from the lost land of [Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis"). It cannot be used to cast [Hexes](/Hexes "Dark Souls 2 Hexes"), but its crystal tip greatly enhances spell strength. 

> Staff of the lost land of Olaphis. Sorcery-dedicated catalyst.   
>   
> Cannot be used to cast hexes. The crystal at the tip of the staff greatly augments spell strength.   
>   
> Already a finely-crafted staff, made even more effective by its crystal, the brilliance of which has been polished over time with powerful magic. 

### How to get Staff of Wisdom in Dark Souls 2

  * [Dragon Shrine, ](/Dragon+Shrine "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Shrine")shortly after first bonfire by walking up the nearby steps (to the left if exiting the bonfire room), killing the Drakekeeper, and then breaking the crates behind him to reveal an area to use a Pharros Lockstone. If activated it will reveal a fog wall behind the player with a chest containing The Armor Of Judgment (Velka's Armor from Dark Souls 1) along with The Staff Of Wisdom.
  * **SOFS** : [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora"). Army Camp Bonfire. Jump down the well and proceed to the house. Inside the house is a [Mimic](/Mimic "Dark Souls 2 Mimic") with Dark Leggings and Staff of Wisdom.

### DS2 Staff of Wisdom Hints and Tips

  * Best sorcery staff in the game, especially when infused with Magic. It greatly boosts sorcery damage.
  * Cannot be used to cast hexes.
  * Casts from higher up than most staves, allowing many enemies to get under spells when close, but can be advantageous when casting over barriers
  * Due to its sheer length, a close-up locked-on spell can sometimes score as a headshot, especially against taller enemies. Not something to rely on, but an unexpected boon nonetheless.
  * Upgrades with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * +5 50int magic infused gives 493 mag atk.

Parameter Bonus: Strength, Dexterity, Magic, Fire, Lightning and Dark bonuses - The scaling multiplier applied to the [Attack: stat]. Scaling quality is from highest to lowest as follows: S/A/B/C/D/E.  
The higher the player's [Str, Dex, Mag, Fire, Lightning, Dark] stat, the higher the [Attack Bonus: Stat] is (found on the player status screen). The higher the scaling letter, the higher the percent multiplier applied to the [Attack: Stat].  
This resulting bonus damage is added to the base physical damage of the weapon and is shown in the equipment screen in blue numbers as a "+ X".  
Attack Type: Defines what kind of moveset the weapon has.  
Physical Damage falls under three categories: [Thrust](/Thrust+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Thrust Damage") (T), [Slash](/Slash+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Slash Damage") (Sl), [Strike](/Strike+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Strike Damage") (St).  
Elemental Damage Types: [Magic](/Magic+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Magic Damage"), [Fire](/Fire+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Fire Damage"), [Lightning](/Lightning+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Lightning Damage"), [Dark](/Dark+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Dark Damage").  
Auxiliary Effects: [Bleed](/Bleed "Dark Souls 2 Bleed"), [Poison](/Poison "Dark Souls 2 Poison"), [Petrification](/Petrification "Dark Souls 2 Petrification"), and [Curse](/Curse "Dark Souls 2 Curse").  
Stability: How well the player keeps stance after being hit.  
Durability: The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!".  
Weight: How much the item weighs when equipped.
