_"Helm worn by acolytes of Aldia._

_Several of the greatest minds converged in Aldia to weave strange new rituals, but rumors suggest that during the course of their work their thoughts were not their own.__"_

### Location

  * Mask is guaranteed drop from Petrified [Ogre](/Ogre "Dark Souls 2 Ogre") (does not respawn) under the [Skeletal Dragon](/Petrified+Dragon+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Petrified Dragon Bone").
  * Can be dropped from the [Dragon Acolytes](/Dragon+Acolyte "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Acolyte") just before the [Guardian Dragon](/Guardian+Dragon "Dark Souls 2 Guardian Dragon").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Dragon Acolyte Set](/Dragon+Acolyte+Set "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Acolyte Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard").
  * The Giant caged Basilisk has a rare chance to drop a [Dragon Sage Hood](/Dragon+Sage+Hood "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Sage Hood").
