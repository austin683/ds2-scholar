_"An old Helmet that is oddly comfortable. Belonged to a Bell Keeper. To this day, the forbidden love of Prince of Alken and the Princess of Venn manipulates these marionettes. Surely they never imagined that their dolls would outlast their own kingdoms."_

### Location

  * Reach Rank 3 in the [Bell Keeper covenant](/Bell+Keepers "Dark Souls 2 Bell Keepers") by invading as a grey spirit or by killing grey invaders in either [Belfry Luna](/Belfry+Luna "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Luna") or [Belfry Sol](/Belfry+Sol "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Sol").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Bell Keeper Set](/Bell+Keeper+Set "Dark Souls 2 Bell Keeper Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab").
