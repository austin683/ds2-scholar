Psn: Octopus_Gang 

####  Description: Build does not use a shield, attack attack roll. Cast attack. Turtle is dead. After Sl 135 if you want to go higher, increase ATN, or ADP.

###  Starting class: Sorcerer

note- First thing you want to do is get the stats to use the [Fire Longsword](/Longsword "Dark Souls 2 Longsword") 1 handed.   
Stats: Sl 136 - Sl 79 

  * VGR- 30 - 17
  * END- 15 - 12
  * VIT- 15 - 12
  * ATN- 30 - 30
  * STR- 10 - 10
  * DEX- 16 - 12
  * ADP- 25 - 15
  * INT- 48 - 20
  * FTH- Base

###  Equipment:

  * Right Hand: Magic [Uchigatana](/Uchigatana "Dark Souls 2 Uchigatana")
  * Left Hand: [Witchtree Branch](/Witchtree+Branch "Dark Souls 2 Witchtree Branch") or [Staff of Wisdom](/Staff+of+Wisdom "Dark Souls 2 Staff of Wisdom") \- Staff of Wisdom is stronger Witchtree Branch is faster Witchtree branch you get mid game, Wisdom is late game. Choose for pvp.
  * Armour: [Chaos Hood](/Chaos+Hood "Dark Souls 2 Chaos Hood"), , , The pants you can find. I have [Vengarl's Boots](/Vengarl%27s+Boots "Dark Souls 2 Vengarl%27s Boots") right now, [Black Tunic Hood](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor"), until you can get Chaos Hood
  * Rings: [Ring of Steel Protection](/Ring+of+Steel+Protection "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Steel Protection"), [Clear Bluestone Ring](/Clear+Bluestone+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Clear Bluestone Ring"), [Old Sun Ring](/Old+Sun+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Old Sun Ring"), [Ring of Blades](/Ring+of+Blades "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Blades")
