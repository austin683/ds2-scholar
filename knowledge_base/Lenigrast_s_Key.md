**Lenigrast's Key** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Lenigrast's Key** , the key belonging to Blacksmith Lenigrast of Majula. In Majula, the haven for the banished, lies a collection of forsaken old houses. One of these, skillfully converted by Lenigrast, serves as his workshop. This key unlocks the door to Blacksmith Lenigrast's shop in Majula. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> Key of Blacksmith Lenigrast of Majula. Majula, the land of exiles, is comprised of abandoned old houses, one of which Lenigrast has fashioned into a workshop.

### Dark Souls 2 Lenigrast's Key Usage

  * Opens the door of [Blacksmith Lenigrast's](/Blacksmith+Lenigrast "Dark Souls 2 Blacksmith Lenigrast") shop in Majula.

### Lenigrast's Key Location in DKS2

  * Buy from [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag melentia") for 1000 souls. You can first meet her in the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants") at the Cardinal Tower bonfire
