#  Central Gutter

* * *

##  Location:

  
This Bonfire is up above the path to Black Gulch   
  
The only reason you'd want to use Bonfire Ascetics here is for multiple copies of the Dark Fog hex, found on the ground level below, or for multiple Great Clubs, found on a rock ledge on the way down.   
  

##  Bosses:

  * None
