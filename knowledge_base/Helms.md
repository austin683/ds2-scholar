**Helms** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are pieces of [Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor") that's worn on the head for protection. Each piece of armor has different defense and resistance values. It provides players with certain bonuses that can aid in different combat situations. There are also no set bonuses when it comes to equipping set armor pieces, only the specific piece of armor's bonus will be applied when equipped. This was done so that players can fully customize their builds to their liking, each with different buffs and advantages, without having to think about set bonuses. Helms for the entire game and DLCs are available in this page as a sortable table. To sort them by their respective properties, click on the Header Rows (icons). 

### What are Helms in Dark Souls 2?

In Dark Souls II, **Helms** are a type of headgear or headpiece that players can equip to enhance their character's defense and appearance. Helms are part of the broader category of armor and play a crucial role in protecting the head, one of the vulnerable areas of a character. Each helm has its own set of defensive stats, resistances, and weight, influencing the overall effectiveness and playstyle of a character.

Players can acquire helms by finding them in the game world, purchasing them from merchants, or receiving them as drops from defeated enemies. Like other armor pieces, helms contribute to a character's overall defenses and fashion, allowing players to tailor their appearance and playstyle based on their preferences and the challenges they face.

_Dark Souls 2 showcases a broad variety of helmets_  

It's important for players to consider the weight and protective attributes of helms, as these factors can affect mobility and the ability to withstand different types of attacks. Additionally, Dark Souls 2 features a wide variety of helms with distinctive designs, contributing to the diverse visual aesthetics within the game.

#### DS2 Helms Hidden Stats

In Dark Souls 2, there are different helmets or headgear items that possess hidden statistics or properties not explicitly mentioned in their in-game descriptions. These hidden stats can include various attributes such as increased intelligence, faith, casting speed, or additional effects like extra spell casts. Players may discover these hidden benefits by equipping and comparing the headgear, observing changes in their character's stats, particularly in areas like intelligence, faith, or other relevant attributes.
