**Undead Crypt Knight** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)****. Undead Crypt Knight** , towering guardians burdened with the curse of protecting the Undead Crypt from intruders. Armed not with conventional weapons but dual colossal greatshields, they employ these slab-like defenses to batter any who dare to trespass.****[Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt Knight Information

Huge knights cursed with guarding the [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt") from trespassers. Equipped with no weapons but two massive, slab-like greatshields with which they will pummel the player.

### Undead Crypt Knight Location in Dark Souls 2

  * [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt")

### DKS2 Undead Crypt Knight Drops

  * [Imperious Set](/Imperious+Set "Dark Souls 2 Imperious Set")
  * [Reeve's Greatshield](/Reeve%27s+Greatshield "Dark Souls 2 Reeve%27s Greatshield")
  * [Orma's Greatshield](/Orma%27s+Greatshield "Dark Souls 2 Orma%27s Greatshield")
  * NG: 1,900 Souls,NG+ 3,800 Souls, NG+7: 7,600 Souls.

### Undead Crypt Knight Strategy Tips in DKS2

  * **Heavenly Thunder** is extremely effective against them, as they are weak to lightning damage and many of the bolts will strike from behind their shields.
  * For the first one, you can also stand on the left of him, backed away from him as much as possible and use magic at them, he will not move and the attacks will be angled in a way, that they are not blocked by the shields.

### Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt Knight Move Set

  * Combo: Standard attack, a swipe with each of his shields, 
    * Physical Damage
    * Can be Blocked
  * Shield Slam Combo: Standard Attack, slams his shields into the ground, may do it 3 times in a row, 
    * Physical Damage
    * Can be Blocked
