**Rotten Pine Resin** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Rotten Pine Resin** , this variant has turned poisonous. Applies a toxic effect to right-hand weapons. Afflicted foes experience a gradual loss of HP over time. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> This Rotten Pine Resin has gone poisonous. Applies poison to right-hand weapons. Poisoned foes slowly lose HP over time.

### Rotten Pine Resin Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Boosts existing poison damage by 30%
  * Adds 20 poison attack value of the right-hand weapon for 90 seconds

### Dark Souls 2 Rotten Pine Resin Locations

  * Sold by [Lonesome Gavlan](/Lonesome+Gavlan "Dark Souls 2 Lonesome Gavlan") for 2000 souls each.
  * Drops from [Rupturing Hollow](/Rupturing+Hollow "Dark Souls 2 Rupturing Hollow") in [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille") and [The Gutter](/The+Gutter "Dark Souls 2 The Gutter")
  * Drops from [Artificial Undead](/Artificial+Undead "Dark Souls 2 Artificial Undead") In [Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley")
