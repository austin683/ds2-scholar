**Soul of The Rotten** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of The Rotten** , the soul of the monstrous entity dwelling deep within the Gutter. The Rotten embraces all, serving as a sanctuary for all things unwanted or discarded. Consuming this soul grants 25,000 Souls. Alternatively, trade it with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") to obtain the [Butcher's Knife](/Butcher's+Knife "Dark Souls 2 Butcher's Knife"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Rotten, who writhes deep within the Gutter.  
> The Rotten embraces all, in the sanctuary for all things unwanted or tossed away.  
> Use the special soul of the Rotten to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Soul of The Rotten Usage in DKS2

  * Consuming gives 25 000 Souls.
  * Trade with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for [Butcher's Knife](/Butcher%27s+Knife#fex "Dark Souls 2 Butcher%27s Knife#fex").

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of The Rotten Location

  * Obtained by defeating [The Rotten](/The+Rotten "Dark Souls 2 The Rotten") in [Black Gulch](/Black+Gulch "Dark Souls 2 Black Gulch").
