**Emit Force** is a Miracle in Dark Souls 2. To cast a Miracle, you must use a chime or Special Weapons that can cast Miracle.

> "A miracle derived from Force.  
> Emits a forward-moving shockwave.  
>   
> Throughout history, there have been many examples of imitative miracles.  
> When discerning original from imitator is nigh impossible, which is which hardly matters."

### Acquired From

  * Sold by [Blue Sentinel Targray](/Blue+Sentinel+Targray "Dark Souls 2 Blue Sentinel Targray") after joining the [Blue Sentinels](/Blue+Sentinels "Dark Souls 2 Blue Sentinels") covenant.
  * Found in [No Man's Wharf](/No+Man's+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No Man's Wharf"). 
  * Sold by [Cromwell the Pardoner](/Cromwell+the+Pardoner "Dark Souls 2 Cromwell the Pardoner").
