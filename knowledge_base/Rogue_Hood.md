_"Hood worn by Hollowed thieves._  
  
_The hood is primarily intended to hide the face of the wearer, and is of no practical value as defensive gear._  
  
_Despite their crude appearance, this/these armor/gauntlets/leggings is surprisingly sturdy."_

### Location

  * Drops from Hollowed thieves enemies in [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman%27s+Copse).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Rogue Set](/Rogue+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab).
