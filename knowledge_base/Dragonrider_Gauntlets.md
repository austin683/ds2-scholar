### Dragonrider Gauntlets Description

_"Gauntlets worn by King Vendrick's loyal Dragonriders._  
_According to legend, the Dragonriders went into battle astride not horses, but wyrms._

_Aspiring Dragonriders who had not the mettle to handle their training were torn apart by their wyrms, and those who survived emerged with deific strength."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Sold by [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") after defeating the [Dragonriders](/DragonRider "Dark Souls 2 DragonRider") ([Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle"))
