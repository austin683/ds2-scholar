**Lances** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are a type of [Weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") that's also similar to [Spears](/Spears "Dark Souls 2 Spears") in terms of range. They are heavier and slower than Spears but have higher damage in both physical and poise damage, which means Lances can be better if range and stagger are what the player seeks as an advantage. Lances can also be considered as a "heavy spear". Lances work best when used for a counterattack, it is also effective for holding choke points when facing a group of enemies. Like Spears, it works best when utilized at narrow spaces. This page covers a list of all Lances in Dark Souls 2.
