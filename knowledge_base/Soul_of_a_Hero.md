**Soul of a Hero** is a type of [Consumable](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of a Hero** , consume to amass a substantial number of souls. Grants 10,000 souls when consumed. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are consumable items categorized as remnants of fallen warriors, consuming souls collects a certain number of souls that is added to the player's stock. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are required for players to become stronger in the game by raising their soul level as well as buying and upgrading their equipment.

> Soul of a hero of legend who has long ago gone Hollow. Use to acquire a large amount of souls

###  Soul of a Hero Usage

  * Can only be consumed for 10.000 Souls.

###  Soul of a Hero Location

  * [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora"). At the bottom, before the area with the giant nets, in the room with many spiders, a locked door and two switch-operated doors, behind a bench on the right.
  * [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle"). After the first bonfire, after the big room to the right on a corpse under the stairs.
  * [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana"). Right of the second fog, near the water's edge.
  * [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum"), at the spike trap staircase, farmable by burning [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") at Tower of Prayer bonfire.
