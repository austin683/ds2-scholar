**Possessed Armor** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Animated by Nadalia's presence, **Possessed Armors** in Brume Tower wield both swords and great bows, adapting their attacks based on the player's proximity. If the player seeks refuge behind obstacles, the Armor can levitate its bow for a clear shot. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Possessed Armor Information in Dark Souls 2

Possessed Armors are empty armor vessels given life by Nadalia found in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower") of the Old Iron King DLC. They can use Both a sword and a Great bow depending on the Player's distance. When the player cowers behind the environment, the Armor can levitate its bow into the air until it has a clear shot of the player. When in the presence of an [Ashen Idol](/Ashen+Idol "Dark Souls 2 Ashen Idol"), defeated Armor's will revive in a few seconds of being defeated. Some Armor's are inactive on the floor until the player gets close._

### Dark Souls 2 Possessed Armor Strategy Tips

  * Take care when he falls on the ground, as often it starts a spin attack
  * Well timed Great Hammer's 2H R2 attacks (PANCAKE) make so Possessed Armor can't get up and attack you. 

### Possessed Armor Move Set in DS2

  * Two hit combo
  * Two-handed attack
  * Great Bow (normal and levitating)
