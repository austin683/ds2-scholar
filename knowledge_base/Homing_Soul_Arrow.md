**Homing Soul Arrow** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Homing Soul Arrow** , a sorcery crafted by Straid of Olaphis, propelling soul arrows that relentlessly pursue their designated targets. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "One of the sorceries devised by Straid, the great mage of ancient Olaphis.  
>   
> Fires souls arrows that follow their targets to the ends of the earth."

### Dark Souls 2 Homing Soul Arrow Acquired From

  * Purchased from [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for 6,500 souls.
  * Purchased from [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for 6,500 souls.
