**Blue Sentinel Targray** is an [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") in **Dark Souls 2.****Blue Sentinel Targray** can be found in the [Cathedral of Blue](/Cathedral+of+Blue "Dark Souls 2 Cathedral of Blue"). He is the leader of the [Blue Sentinels](/Blue+Sentinels "Dark Souls 2 Blue Sentinels") covenant, a group of noble knights dedicated to protecting the innocent and punishing the guilty. **Targray** offers the player the opportunity to join the Blue Sentinels covenant, allowing them to be summoned to defend members of the Way of Blue covenant from invaders. **Targray** is a noble and honorable character, dedicated to upholding justice and righteousness in the world of Drangleic.

### Blue Sentinel Targray Information in Dark Souls 2

  * Leader of the Blue Sentinels covenant.
  * Must have a [Token of Fidelity](/Token+of+Fidelity "Dark Souls 2 Token of Fidelity") in inventory to access all dialog, Targray as a merchant, and the [Blue Sentinels](/Blue+Sentinels "Dark Souls 2 Blue Sentinels") covenant.
  * Teaches Duel Bow gesture
  * If you kill him, and you want to purchase/enter covenant again, go to his spot and you will find a grave. Pray to it with 6,000 souls and he will appear as a ghost. Talk to him to enter covenant again.
  * Upon reaching Rank 3 in the Blue Sentinels covenant, use the talk option in his dialog menu. He will give you his full armor set. (can also be bought from [Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia") after killing him.)

### Blue Sentinel Targray Encounters in Dark Souls 2

  * [Cathedral of Blue](/Cathedral+of+Blue "Dark Souls 2 Cathedral of Blue"). After defeating [Old Dragonslayer](/Old+Dragonslayer "Dark Souls 2 Old Dragonslayer").

### Blue Sentinel Targray Drops in Dark Souls 2
