# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Curse

**Curse** is a [Status Effect](/Status+Effects "Dark Souls 2 Status Effects") in **Dark Souls 2**. It cannot be utilized by the player in any way, and is instead a constant danger as once procced it will lower the player's health by a determinate amount. The player has an innate Curse Resistance stat affected by [Attunement](/Attunement "Dark Souls 2 Attunement") and [Adaptability](/Adaptability "Dark Souls 2 Adaptability"), and other sources for Curse Resistance will have to come from spells and equipment, at 900 Curse Resistance the player will become immune to the Status Effect.

### Dark Souls 2 Curse Guide

In order to be cursed, the buildup meter must be filled, and once it has the player will have their maximum HP lowered by roughly 5%, which is the equivalent of dying and contributes to [Hollowing](/Hollowing "Dark Souls 2 Hollowing") if the player wasn't hollowed before. Curse can be procced several times, capping at 10 times leading to a 50% health reduction. There will be no effect afterwards.

### Raising Resistance to Curse

  * [Black Witch Veil](/Black+Witch+Veil "Dark Souls 2 Black Witch Veil") (1000 points, which effectively makes the player immune to Curse)
  * [Cursebite Ring](/Cursebite+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Cursebite Ring") (400 points)
  * [Hollow Skin](/Hollow+Skin "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Skin") (1000 points, which effectively makes the player immune to Curse)
  * [Human Effigy](/Human+Effigy "Dark Souls 2 Human Effigy") reverses the effect of Hollowing after Curse has been procced
  * [Iron Flesh](/Iron+Flesh "Dark Souls 2 Iron Flesh") (200 points for 25 seconds)
  * [King's Crown](/King's+Crown "Dark Souls 2 King's Crown"), [Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King"), [Crown of the Old Iron King](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King"), [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King")   
after [Vendrick's Blessing](/Vendrick's+Blessing "Dark Souls 2 Vendrick's Blessing") has been obtained grant Curse immunity
  * Leveling [Attunement](/Attunement "Dark Souls 2 Attunement") and [Adaptability](/Adaptability "Dark Souls 2 Adaptability") stats, which raises Curse Resistance
  * [Perseverance](/Perseverance "Dark Souls 2 Perseverance") (300 points for 120 seconds)
  * [Ring of Binding](/Ring+of+Binding "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Binding") lessens the effect of Hollowing after Curse has been procced
  * [Ring of Resistance](/Ring+of+Resistance "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Resistance") (120/200 points)
  * [Yorgh's Spear](/Yorgh's+Spear "Dark Souls 2 Yorgh's Spear") (70 points when equipped)

### Dark Souls 2 Curse Notes and Tips

  * Phantoms can't be afflicted with Curse.
  * Curse will bypass the [Ring of Life Protection's](/Ring+of+Life+Protection "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Life Protection") effect.
  * The Curse icon is used to represent [Caitha, Goddess of Tears](/Caitha,+Goddess+of+Tears "Dark Souls 2 Caitha, Goddess of Tears") on the [Name-engraved Ring](/Name-engraved+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Name-engraved Ring").
