**Alonne Captain Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Alonne Captain Set](/Alonne+Captain+Set "Dark Souls 2 Alonne Captain Set") in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Alonne Captain Leggings Description

_"Leggings worn by Sir Alonne, who served the Old Iron King._

_Sir Alonne came to this land from the east, chose to serve a little-known and unestablished lord, and helped him become the Old Iron King._

_Then, at the end of the very peak of his sire's rule, Sir Alonne set out again, in search of lands yet unknown."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Sold by [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") after defeating [Sir Alonne](/Sir+Alonne "Dark Souls 2 Sir Alonne").
