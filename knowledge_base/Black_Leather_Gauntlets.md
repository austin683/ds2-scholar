### Black Leather Gauntlets Description

_"Leather gloves dyed Black. Allows the wearer to blend in with the dark. Often used by those with ill-intent.Meticulously crafted, and provides fair defense. "_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Sold by [Laddersmith Gilligan](/Laddersmith+Gilligan) for 800 souls in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak) and 1,000 souls in [Majula](/Majula).
