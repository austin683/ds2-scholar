**Great Heavy Soul Arrow** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Great Heavy Soul Arrow** , an even mightier sorcery, shooting a formidable, dense soul arrow that demands skillful handling from the caster. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> A spell superior to Heavy Soul Arrow. Fires an even stronger soul arrow.   
> Difficult to handle, but very powerful. Only the well-trained can handle these arrows".

### Dark Souls 2 Great Heavy Soul Arrow Acquired From

Available to purchase from [Carhillion of the Fold](/Carhillion+of+the+Fold "Dark Souls 2 Carhillion of the Fold") or [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir"). A copy can also be obtained in a chest in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak") past the locked door near [Mild Mannered Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate"). To get past the door go up the ramp, turn right, and carefully drop down.

### Great Heavy Soul Arrow Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Shoots projectile that deals magic damage. The highest damage-dealing spell in the [Soul Arrow](/Soul+Arrow "Dark Souls 2 Soul Arrow") family. Excellent for PvE questing and mob disposing.
  * This spell has the longest cast time of all the soul arrows, so use it carefully.
