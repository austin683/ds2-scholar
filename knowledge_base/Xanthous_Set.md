**Xanthous Set** is an Attire or Armor in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds).

  
  
  
  
  
_"This blindingly-bright yellow costume is said to have been worn by Xanthous King._   
_Perhaps the heinously towering head cloth crown served some purpose, or perhaps it was some strange display of power"_   
_Despite the costume's being in near tatters, its yellow hues are mysteriously unfaded."_

###  Set Location

  * Given by [Darkdiver Grandahl](/Darkdiver+Grandahl) upon reaching rank 3 in [Pilgrims of Dark](/Pilgrims+of+Dark) covenant (that includes killing [Darklurker](/Darklurker)). 
    * Note: this armor set is only given once per character; killing Darklurker again on NG+ won't help.

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
  * Crown offers the highest strike defense (even higher than that of [Gyrm Warrior](/Gyrm+Warrior+Set) Greathelm; other physical defenses are rather low) and the highest curse resistance (not counting the two helms that negate curse completely).
  * Although a minor change, the crown is considerably shorter than in previous titles.
