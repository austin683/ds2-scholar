**Soul of the Pursuer** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of the Pursuer** , the unique soul of the relentless Pursuer who haunts Drangleic. Consume this soul to acquire 8,000 souls or trade it with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") along with 1,500 souls for either the [Pursuer's Ultra Greatsword](/Pursuer's+Ultra+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 Pursuer's Ultra Greatsword") or the [Pursuer's Greatshield](/Pursuer's+Greatshield "Dark Souls 2 Pursuer's Greatshield"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Pursuer, who lurks in Drangleic. The Pursuer, who seeks the bearer of the sign, will not rest until his target is slain.  
> Use the special soul of the Pursuer to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Soul of the Pursuer Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Consuming it gives you 8,000 souls
  * Can be Traded with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") and 1,500 souls each for either: 
    * [Pursuer's Ultra Greatsword](/Pursuer%27s+Ultra+Greatsword#fex "Dark Souls 2 Pursuer%27s Ultra Greatsword#fex")
    * [Pursuer's Greatshield](/Pursuer%27s+Greatshield#fe "Dark Souls 2 Pursuer%27s Greatshield#fe")

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of the Pursuer Location

  * Obtained by defeating the [Pursuer](/Pursuer "Dark Souls 2 Pursuer") at the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants")
  * You have two options of where to fight him in the Forest of the Fallen Giants.  
You can either, face him on top of the battlement terrace, right after you climb up the tree-root to the castle wall.   
This fight is a one time only appearance, and if you lose or run away he will move on to his boss area further inside the keep.
  * You will need the [Soldiers Key](/Soldiers+Key#fex "Dark Souls 2 Soldiers Key#fex") to access his boss area, which means you must first defeat [The Last Giant](/The+Last+Giant "Dark Souls 2 The Last Giant").  
The Pursuer will stay in the boss arena until defeated.  
Note that he will still invade you at his locations trought the game even if you decide to not fight him.  
For more information check his Boss Page
