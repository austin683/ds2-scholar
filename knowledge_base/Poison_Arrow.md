**Poison Arrow** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Poison Arrows** , projectile weaponry infused with a toxic venom and coated in Rotten Pine Resin. Striking adversaries with these arrows induces a gradual deterioration of their health over time. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Arrows imbued with poison. Tipped with Rotten Pine Resin. Poisoned foes slowly lose HP over time.

### Poison Arrow Usage in DKS2

  * Ammunition for bows
  * 50 Physical Damage
  * 300 flat Poison Buildup
  * Takes roughly four to five arrows for the poison to take effect.

### Dark Souls 2 Poison Arrow Location

  * Dropped by archers in [Huntsman's Copse](/Hunstman%27s+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Hunstman%27s Copse").
  * Sold by [Gavlan](/Gavlan "Dark Souls 2 Gavlan") for 60 souls each. 
    * He sells an unlimited supply of these once he settles down for good in Door of Pharros

### Poison Arrow Notes and tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Most enemies get poisoned in 4 successive shots with these arrows, adding a significant amount of damage for little risk.
