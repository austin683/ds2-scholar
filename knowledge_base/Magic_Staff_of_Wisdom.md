# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Magic+Staff+of+Wisdom

###   
**Acquired From**

  * [Dragon Shrine](/Dragon+Shrine "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Shrine") right behind the first [Drakekeeper](/Drakekeeper "Dark Souls 2 Drakekeeper") where you will see a pharos lockstone, then break the hidden wall where you see an iron chest with the Judgement Set (Mask of Judgment, Robe of Judgment, Manchettes of Judgment, Tights of Judgment) and [Staff of Wisdom](/Staff+of+Wisdom "Dark Souls 2 Staff of Wisdom").
  * [Royal Army Campsite](/Royal+Army+Campsite "Dark Souls 2 Royal Army Campsite") by jumping down the well and throwing a loyds talismen at the chest.(scholar of the first sin).

**Hints and Tips:**

  * Note: Use Magic Enchantment to increase the damage of Magic attacks.

###   
**Required in Build:**

  * Use [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite") to refine.
  * Required: 15 [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite") and 11320 Souls to +5
