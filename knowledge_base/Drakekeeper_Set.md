**Drakekeeper Set** is an Attire or Armor in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds).

  
  
  
  
_"Giant onyx armor (helmet/gauntlets/leggings)._   
_Something dark indeed eats away at the drakekeepers, eternal guardians of the shrine."_

###  Set Location

  * Dropped by [Drakekeepers](/Drakekeeper "Drakekeepers") in [Dragon Shrine](/Dragon+Shrine "Dragon Shrine").

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Titanite").
  * Gauntlets offer the highest physical defenses among armor pieces upgraded with regular Titanite. Other best pieces are: [Gyrm Warrior Greathelm](/Gyrm+Warrior+Set) and chestpiece, [Steel](/Steel+Set) / [Mastodon](/Mastodon+Set) gauntlets and Mastodon / [Alonne Knight](/Alonne+Knight+Set) leggings.
  * Resembles _Tower Knight_ boss from _Demon's Souls_.
