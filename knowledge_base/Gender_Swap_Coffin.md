**The Gender Swap Coffin** is an interactable in Dark Souls 2. Located in [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt") it allows players to alter the sex of their character, without the need to start all over again.

### Gender Swap Coffin Notes

  * It is protected by two [Ogres](/Ogre "Dark Souls 2 Ogre"), one in plain sight, the other in hiding.
  * A [Pursuer](/Pursuer "Dark Souls 2 Pursuer") will also appear in Sotfs.
  * Changes the body from male to female or female to male.
  * Changes male voice to female voice or female voice to male voice.
  * Lightly alters facial characteristics.
  * Cannot change hair, tattoos, name or basic facial characteristics.
  * All changes may be reversed, by reentering the coffin.
