#  Soldier Key

  
_Key to the Soldier's Door in the Forest of Fallen Giants._   
_A fort was erected in the forest to face the Giants, but now the soldiers are lost and Hollowed._   
_They are enfeebled, but not without honor, and continue to steadfastly defend their country._   
  
**Usage**

  * Opens Various doors around The [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants")
  * Opens path to Soldier's Rest bonfire
  * Opens path to the [Pursuer](/Pursuer "Dark Souls 2 Pursuer") boss fight and a route to the [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille")

  
**Location**

  * Drops from [The Last Giant](/The+Last+Giant "Dark Souls 2 The Last Giant")
