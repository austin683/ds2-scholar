###   
Gauntlets of Aurous (Invisible) Description

_"Gauntletsof kings of the desert land of Jugo. First worn by Aurous, the land's heroic founder._  
_According to legend, the armor of Aurous is composed of a mysterious substance that cannot be seen by cowards. What do your eyes tell you?"_

###   
Where to Find \ Location

  * Spend 16000 souls at [Maughlin's](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") shop in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula") then come back when you have zero souls. Talk to him and he'll give you the set.
  * Armor can be dropped by Dark Spirit [Rhoy the Explorer](/Rhoy+the+Explorer "Dark Souls 2 Rhoy the Explorer") in [Grave of Saints](/Grave+of+Saints "Dark Souls 2 Grave of Saints").

### 

### Notes

  * Part of the [Invisible Aurous Set](/Invisible+Aurous+Set "Dark Souls 2 Invisible Aurous Set").
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * Raises equipment load (Helm & Gloves =+1% Equipment Load, Chest and legs= +1.5% Equipment load. Total 5% for full set).
