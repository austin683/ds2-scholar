# Memories of the Ancients

  
There are the 4 memories that can be accessed by using [Ashen Mist Heart](/Ashen+Mist+Heart): you should interact with the dead Giants on the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants) and the dead Dragon in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora). There is a new memory, added with the [dlc](/dlc) from July 22nd [Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King).

They can be done in any order, co-op and pvp are not possible within the areas and you have limited access time.

  * [Memory of Vammar](/Memory+of+Vammar)
  * [Memory of Orro](/Memory+of+Orro)

  * [Memory of Jeigh](/Memory+of+Jeigh)
  * [Dragon Memories](/Dragon+Memories)

  * [Memory of the King](/Memory+of+the+King) \- Added via DLC, dialogue from [Vendrick](/Vendrick)
  * [Memory of the Old Iron King](/Memory+of+the+Old+Iron+King) \- Added via DLC
