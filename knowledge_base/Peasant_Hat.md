_"Attire commonly worn by peasants._

_Normal workwear, more fit for farming than fighting"_

### Location

  * Dropped by Peasants in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora")
  * The [Peasant Attire](/Peasant+Attire "Dark Souls 2 Peasant Attire") (chest) +5 and Helm +5 are drops of the [Peasant Red Phantom](/Phantoms "Dark Souls 2 Phantoms") in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora")

### Notes

  * Part of the [Peasant Set](/Peasant+Set "Dark Souls 2 Peasant Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores").
