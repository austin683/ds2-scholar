**Ring of Steel Protection** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). The **Ring of Steel Protection** bestows upon the wearer the safeguard of steel, enhancing physical defense. Legend holds it as the ring of the long-forgotten Knight King, whose heroic exploits have faded from memory, even among the most venerable scholars. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> Wearer gains the protection of steel. Increases physical defense. Said to be the ring of the once legendary Knight King, though his tales are long forgotten, and even the greatly wizened have no recollection of his exploits

### Dark Souls 2 Ring of Steel Protection Effects

**Durability** :

  * +0 = 140
  * +1 = 110
  * +2 = 90

**Weight** :

  * +0=1.0
  * +1=1.5
  * +2=2.0

**Effect:**

  * Increases Strike/Thrust/Slash defense by 50 and reduces damage taken by PVE sources by 10%
  * +1 Increases Strike/Thrust/Slash defense by 75 and reduces damage taken by PVE sources by 10%
  * +2 Increases Strike/Thrust/Slash defense by 100 and reduces damage taken by PVE sources by 12.5%

### Ring of Steel Protection Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * [Crestfallen Saulden](/Crestfallen+Saulden "Dark Souls 2 Crestfallen Saulden"), given to you after dying 100 times, also drops if you kill him.
  * +1: [Lucatiel of Mirrah](/Lucatiel+of+Mirrah "Dark Souls 2 Lucatiel of Mirrah"), given to you when you talk to her in the third location you find her.
  * +2: [Looking Glass Knight](/Looking+Glass+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Knight"), only with Bonfire intensity 2+ at the [Central Castle Drangleic](/Central+Castle+Drangleic "Dark Souls 2 Central Castle Drangleic") bonfire
