**Rhoy the Explorer** is an enemy in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Rhoy the Explorer** , encountered at the Grave of Saints, appears as a Red Spirit with transparent aurous armor and wields invisible shotels. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Rhoy the Explorer Information in Dark Souls 2

A Red Spirit with transparent aurous armor and invisible shotels

### Dark Souls 2 Rhoy the Explorer Location

  * [Grave of Saints](/Grave+of+Saints "Dark Souls 2 Grave of Saints")

### Rhoy the Explorer Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * This phantom will only invade if you are offline.
  * He wears the [right](/Illusory+Ring+of+the+Exalted "Dark Souls 2 Illusory Ring of the Exalted") and [left](/Illusory+Ring+of+a+Conqueror "Dark Souls 2 Illusory Ring of a Conqueror") illusory rings.
  * Follows normal respawn rules.
  * Will not respawn until intensity 2, after which he will respawn 12 times per intensity.
  * Regulated by Harval's Resting Place bonfire in Grave of Saints.
  * Highly resistant to Dark, but vulnerable to Magic and Lightning.
  * Doesn't invade if you are in the [Rat King Covenant](/Rat+King+Covenant "Dark Souls 2 Rat King Covenant").
  * He's a great source of [Awestones](/awestone "Dark Souls 2 awestone"). If you want to get the [Vanquisher's Seal](/Vanquisher%27s+Seal "Dark Souls 2 Vanquisher%27s Seal") quickly, just farm him with [Bonfire Ascetics](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic").
  * Very rarely drops an additional awestone, along with the regular one.
  * Other Notes and Tips go here.
  * Has a Bleed infused Shotel even though he drops a non infused one.
