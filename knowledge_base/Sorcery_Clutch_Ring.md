**Sorcery Clutch Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in **Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)**. The **Sorcery Clutch Ring** , featuring an engraving of a hand holding a stone, amplifies magic attack capabilities while diminishing physical defense. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> Ring depicting a hand grasping a stone. Increases magic attack, but reduces physical defense. The origin of this ring is unknown, but its design suggests one of the darker deities. Effective use of this ring requires skill on the part of the wearer."

### Dark Souls 2 Sorcery Clutch Ring Effects

**Durability** : 30

**Weight** :

  * 2.0

**Effect:**

  * Increases Magic Attack Rating by 30
  * Decreases Physical Defense by 80

### Sorcery Clutch Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Backtrack from the Lowermost Floor Bonfire in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower") and take the elevator up. After the first enemy, follow the short path to the left to find an iron chest containing this ring.

### DS2 Sorcery Clutch Ring Tips and Notes

  * Magic AR increase varies.
  * Regular weapons infused with magic only receive 15AR while special weapons with innate magic damage benefit more from this ring.
  * Higher base magic damage generally results in a higher bonus.
