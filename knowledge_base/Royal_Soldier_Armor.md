_"Royal Drangleic soldier armor._

_Equipment crafted by the royal blacksmith. Finely polished and ready for battle."_

### Location

  * Sold by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer) in [Majula](/Majula).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Royal Soldier Set](/Royal+Soldier+Set).
  * Upgraded from [Titanite](/Titanite+Shard).
  * Must have spent 1000+ souls for the Royal Soldier set to appear in his inventory.
