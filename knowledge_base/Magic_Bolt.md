**Magic Bolt** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Magic Bolt** , crossbow bolts infused with magic. Tipped with Aromatic Ooze. Weapons enhanced with magic are especially potent against heavily armored foes or creatures with thick scales. Crossbows, paired with specialized bolts, offer an option for warriors with low dexterity to deal elemental damage. Available for purchase from Chancellor in Drangleic Castle at a cost of 80 souls per bolt, with an unlimited supply. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Bolt imbued with magic. Tipped with Aromatic Ooze. Weapons fortified with magic are particularly effective against heavily armored foes or creatures with thick scales.  
> Crossbows and specialized bolts allow warriors with low dexterity to inflict elemental damage.

### Magic Bolt Usage in DKS2

  * Crossbow ammunition
  * 70 Physical Damage
  * 160 Magic Damage

### Dark Souls 2 Magic Bolt Location

  * Sold by Chancellor in [Drangleic Castle ](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle")for (80) souls per bolt in an Unlimited supply
  * 30 found in Shaded Woods after the Shaded Ruins bonfire, in a wooden chest underneath the bridge, go down a slope after the gap separating the two bridge parts.
