**Toxic Mist** is a [Pyromancy](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Toxic Mist** , a pyromancy of obscure origin. Conjures a potent cloud of poisonous mist that steadily corrodes the target's body, causing damage over time. To cast a [Pyromancy](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies"), you must use a Flame or Special Weapons that can cast Pyromancy.

> "A fringe pyromancy of unknown origin.  
> Creates a powerful cloud of poison mist.  
>   
> The poison gradually erodes the target's body, inflicting damage all the while.  
> Whoever created this spell did not believe his enemies deserved a swift death."

### Dark Souls 2 Toxic Mist Acquired From

  * [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis"), trade him 1,500 souls and the [Royal Rat Vanguard Soul](/Royal+Rat+Vanguard+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Royal Rat Vanguard Soul").

### Toxic Mist Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Applies the "Toxic" status effect, which is similar to poison. It is possible to apply toxic and poison on an enemy at the same time.
  * Deals ~1400 damage over 20 seconds. The damage isn't fixed. It's in a range of 1380 - 1485.
  * Builds up at the same rate as Poison Mist.
  * Poison buildup is unaffected by Crest of the Rat or Sanctum Soldier Gauntlets
  * The duration is unaffected by the Lingering Dragoncrest Ring.
  * This is a superior version of Poison Mist. It deals ~25% more damage and ticks much faster, which applies greater pressure on an enemy in PvP.
