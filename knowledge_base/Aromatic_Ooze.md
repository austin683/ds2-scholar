**Aromatic Ooze** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Aromatic Ooze** , a mysterious sticky substance radiating a pale white light. Applies magic to the right-hand weapon. The treated weapon deals magic damage for a brief period. Especially effective against heavily armored foes or creatures with thick scales. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A mysterious sticky substance that radiates a pale white light. Applies magic to right-hand weapon.  
> The affected weapon inflicts magic damage for a short time.  
> Particularly effective against heavily armored foes or creatures with thick scales.

### Aromatic Ooze Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Boosts existing magic damage by 50%
  * Adds 50 magic attack value of the right-hand weapon for 90 seconds
  * Outperforms [Crystal Magic Weapon](/Crystal+Magic+Weapon "Dark Souls 2 Crystal Magic Weapon") by a significant margin. Aromatic Ooze is the magic equivalent of Resonant Weapon, boosting damage by +50 and the overall magic damage by another 50%.
  * When used on standard or Raw weapons that can have resins applied to them, the damage bonus can easily exceed that gained from Crystal Magic Weapon. Suggested examples include the Crescent Axe and the Blue Flame.

### Dark Souls 2 Aromatic Ooze Location

  * [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants")
  * [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman%27s+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman%27s Copse") \- Across the bridge of the second bonfire. In front of the big guys with two scythes.
  * Sold by [Laddersmith Gilligan](/Laddersmith+Gilligan "Dark Souls 2 Laddersmith Gilligan") (2000 souls, unlimited.)
  * Explorer starting class item
  * Dropped by Undead Hounds in The Gutter.

### Aromatic Ooze Trivia in DKS2

  * Functions similarly to the [Sticky White Stuff](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Sticky+White+Slime "Dark Souls 2 Sticky White Slime") in Demon's Souls
