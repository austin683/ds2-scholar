### Drangleic Gauntlets Description

"Traditional Drangleic Helm/Armour/Gauntlets/Leggings). Belonged to Captain Drummond.  
Drummond's ancestors have served Drangleic for generations, principally as defenders of the great fort, but Captain Drummond is the last in this proud line."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * The [Drangleic Set](/Drangleic+Set) (without Helm) can be found after you defeat the [Pursuer](/Pursuer). Walk past the area you fought him in and you will see a hole in the ground. Dropping into the hole will reveal a corpse, which will include the Drangleic Set as well as a greatsword and shield.
