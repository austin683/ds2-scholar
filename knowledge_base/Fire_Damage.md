**Fire Damage** is one of the [Damage Types](/Damage+Types "Dark Souls 2 Damage Types") in **Dark Souls 2**. It is one of the seven main Damage Types and belongs to the Elemental Damage category. Damage Types are a major part of the calculation that determines how much damage is dealt in [Combat](/Combat "Dark Souls 2 Combat") and are affected by many factors including your equipment, spells and temporary buffs.

### Dark Souls 2 Fire Damage Guide

**Fire Damage** is a type of Elemental Damage dealt by both players and enemies alike. Fire Damage is elemental damage that falls outside of [Magic Damage](/Magic+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Magic Damage"), [Lightning Damage](/Lightning+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Lightning Damage"), and [Dark Damage](/Dark+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Dark Damage") types. Fire Damage is often effective against most unarmored enemies and basilisks.

Most [Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") can be upgraded via the Fire Upgrade Path in order to imbue them with Fire Damage properties. There are also Weapons that can be found which innately deal Fire Damage. See the [Upgrades](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades") and individual weapon pages for details.

Most offensive [Pyromancies](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies") deal Fire Damage.
