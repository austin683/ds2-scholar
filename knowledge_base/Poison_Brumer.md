**Poison Brumer (Poison Horn Beetle)** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Poison Brumer** , a lethargic enemy, lacks pursuit capabilities and moves at a very slow pace. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Poison Brumer (Poison Horn Beetle) Information in Dark Souls 2

_"Are these beetles the source of the poison that fills the earth below the mines, or are they merely the only insects that can survive in these conditions, either way, they suit the Queen`s tastes perfectly.__"_

### Dark Souls 2 Poison Brumer (Poison Horn Beetle) Strategy Tips

  * Very slow enemy that can't chase you at all.
  * Just smack it! Hit it before its mist attack, use range or wait it out if you are too late.
  * Pyromancies are very effective and convenient (fire, not poison)

### Poison Brumer (Poison Horn Beetle) Move Set in DS2

  * Melee
  * Poison Mist
  * Acid Mist
