**Mytha, the Baneful Queen Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Mytha, the Baneful Queen Soul, the essence of Mytha, the Baneful Queen, who once resided in the Earthen Peak. Driven by a desperate desire for the King's love, she poisoned herself in pursuit of beauty, leading to monstrous consequences. The Queen's tragic tale is one of the compelling madness known as love. Consuming this soul rewards you with 10,000 Souls. Trade it with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for [Mytha's Bent Blade](/Mytha's+Bent+Blade "Dark Souls 2 Mytha's Bent Blade"). Obtain this soul by defeating [Mytha, the Baneful Queen](/Mytha,+the+Baneful+Queen "Dark Souls 2 Mytha, the Baneful Queen") in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of Mytha, the Baneful Queen, who lives in the Earthen Peak.  
> The Queen sought the King's affection, even poisoning herself to attain beauty, despite the monstrous consequences. All for the compelling madness known as love.  
> Use the special soul of the Queen to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth

### Mytha, the Baneful Queen Soul Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Consuming gives 10,000 Souls
  * Trade with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for [Mytha's Bent Blade](/Mytha%27s+Bent+Blade "Dark Souls 2 Mytha%27s Bent Blade")

### DKS2 Mytha, the Baneful Queen Soul Location

  * Obtained by defeating [Mytha, the Baneful Queen](/Mytha%2C+the+Baneful+Queen "Dark Souls 2 Mytha%2C the Baneful Queen") in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak").
