**Tseldora Robe** is a [Chest Piece](/Chest+Pieces "Dark Souls 2 Chest Pieces") in Dark Souls 2.

_"O_ _utfit worn by settlers of Tseldora. Rather fancy but with low defence and unfit for battle._

_Its design recalls_  _Tseldora's glory days, the peak of the brightstone boom.__"_

### Location

  * Drops from [Hollowed Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") (Prisoners with spiders on their back) within the [Brightstone Cove](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora") area.
  * The full set is dropped by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer")**[](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer")** if he is killed.

### Tseldora Robe Notes

  * Part of the [Tseldora Set](/Tseldora+Set "Dark Souls 2 Tseldora Set"). 
    * Every piece of this set increases the souls obtained from enemies.
    * The bonuses are multiplicative if multiple armor pieces are worn.
  * Upgraded from [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab").
  * Chest increases souls by 5%
