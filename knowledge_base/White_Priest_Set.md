###  Set Location

  * Sold by [Cromwell The Pardoner](/Cromwell+The+Pardoner "Dark Souls 2 Cromwell The Pardoner") (18,9k).

  
  

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
  * Headpiece grants +1 to Faith.
  * Skirt offers second highest lightning defense (almost equal to that of [Desert Sorceress](/Desert+Sorceress+Set "Dark Souls 2 Desert Sorceress Set") skirt, [Lucatiel's](/Lucatiel's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Lucatiel's Set") boots and [Priestess](/Priestess+Set "Dark Souls 2 Priestess Set") skirt).
