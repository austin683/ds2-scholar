**Retainer Robe** is an Attire or [Armor](/Chest+Pieces "Dark Souls 2 Chest Pieces") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki").

  
  
_Robe of the retainers in the cathedral city._

_Long ago, the retainers attended to the priestesses,__but with them gone, the retainers were left to wander frigid Eleum Loyce without purpose._

### Location

  * [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King") DLC, dropped from [Retainers](/Retainer+Rogue "Dark Souls 2 Retainer Rogue") sitting around [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce").
  * They will only turn hostile after [Alsanna](/Alsanna,+Silent+Oracle "Dark Souls 2 Alsanna, Silent Oracle") stops the blizzard.

### Retainer Robe Notes

  * Upgraded with Regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite")
  * Does not come with the hood worn by the Retainers.
