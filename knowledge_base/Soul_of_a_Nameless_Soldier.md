**Soul of a Nameless Soldier** is a type of [Consumable](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of a Nameless Soldier** , a soul from an Undead soldier whose identity has long been forgotten, lost to the ravages of Hollowing. Use it to acquire many souls. Even in undeath, the soul remains the wellspring of life. When consumed, it imparts 800 souls. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are consumable items categorized as remnants of fallen warriors, consuming souls collects a certain number of souls that is added to the player's stock. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are required for players to become stronger in the game by raising their soul level as well as buying and upgrading their equipment.

> Soul of a nameless Undead soldier who has long ago gone Hollow.  
> Use to acquire many souls.  
> The soul is the source of all life, and even in undeath, or hollowing, the mind seeks souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of a Nameless Soldier Usage

  * Grants 800 souls when consumed.

### Soul of a Nameless Soldier Location in DKS2

  * 1x on the ledge under [Dyna &amp; Tilo](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo") in [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt")
  * 1x after the 2nd [Old Knight](/Old+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Old Knight"), hanging over the railing in [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide%27s Tower of Flame")
