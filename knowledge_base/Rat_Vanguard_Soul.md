_Soul of the Rat King's vanguard, of the underground realm._   
  
_The Rat King's loyal servant judges the worthiness of those who seek royal audience._

6000 souls   
  
Trade in [Straid](/Straid+of+Olaphis) and gain [Toxic Mist](/Toxic+Mist) ( [Acid Surge](/Acid+Surge) is from the Royal Rat Authority)
