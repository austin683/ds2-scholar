**Ancient Dragon Seal** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in **Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)**. **Ancient Dragon Seal** , a ring affiliated with the Dragon Remnants covenant, slightly enhances Status Flask HP recovery. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> Ring of the Dragon Remnants covenant. Slightly increases Estus Flask HP recovery. Possessing a dragon scale is proof of its owner's prowess. Strength should always be displayed boldly, and meted out justly. Members of the covenant who bear dragon scales can fight with one another. The survivor of the duel claims the dragon scale of the opponent.

### Dark Souls 2 Ancient Dragon Seal Effects

**Durability** : 120

**Weight** :

  * 0.2

**Effect:**

  * Covenant ring for would-be dragons. Estus Flask use restores 50 HP more.

### Ancient Dragon Seal Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Join the [Dragon Remnants](/Dragon+Remnants "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Remnants") in Iron Keep via Magerold of Lanafir after giving him the Petrified Egg

### DS2 Ancient Dragon Seal Tips and Notes

  * Not very much of an increase, so not the best way to fill a ring slot.
