**Infantry** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Enlisted infantrymen of the Drangleic Army, these brave but lightly-equipped men, locked in combat with the Giants in flashback areas, display weariness from battle and diminishing morale. They may also misidentify the player as a threat. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Infantry Information

Enlisted infantrymen of the Drangleic Army, these brave but lightly-equipped men are weary from battle and suffering waning morale.  
They are found locked in combat with the Giants in several locations throughout the flashback areas and may also misidentify the player as a threat.

The non-hollowed version of the [Hollow Infantry](/Hollow+Infantry "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Infantry").
