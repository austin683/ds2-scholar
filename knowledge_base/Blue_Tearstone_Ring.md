**Blue Tearstone Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Blue Tearstone Ring** is a precious ring, adorned with a rare tearstone, awakens in moments of peril, enhancing the wearer's physical defense. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A ring set with a rare tearstone. Reacts when the wearer is in danger, temporarily increasing its wearer's physical defense. Caitha, goddess of tears, mourns those who have lost loved ones by shedding pure tears of blue. It is said that the stone set in this ring is one such tear.

### Dark Souls 2 Blue Tearstone Ring Effects

**Durability** : 110

**Weight:**

  * 1.0

**Effect:**

  * Increases defense by 35% when HP is below 30%

### Blue Tearstone Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * [Belfry Luna](/Belfry+Luna "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Luna"): in a chest on the first floor. To reach the chest you must fall through the floor in the second story.
  * [Way of the Blue](/Way+of+the+Blue "Dark Souls 2 Way of the Blue"), Rank 3 reward.

### DS2 Blue Tearstone Ring Tips and Notes

  * This ring's defense bonus applies to all damage types not just physical, poison damage is not reduced by the ring however the ring will defend against buildup, this is also true for all other status effects.
