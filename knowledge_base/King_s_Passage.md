## King's Passage

King's Passage is a [Location](/Locations "Dark Souls 2 Locations") in **Dark Souls 2**. This area separates [Drangleic C](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle")[astle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle")**and the**[Shrine Of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana"). this area can only be accessed after obtaining the [Key to King's Passage](/Key+to+King's+Passage "Dark Souls 2 Key to King's Passage").

### King's Passage Walkthrough

### Access

To access King's Passage you need to obtain the [Key to King's Passage](/Key+to+King's+Passage "Dark Souls 2 Key to King's Passage"). The key is located in one of the three chest in room with trapped [Milfanito](/Milfanito "Dark Souls 2 Milfanito"), in [Drangleic C](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle")[astle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle").

### Short & Sweet

When you open the door with key you find yourself in a long, straight corridor with [Stone Knights](/Stone+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Stone Knight") on either side, four of which will stand up as you enter. There will also be 3x [Alluring Skull](/Alluring+Skull "Dark Souls 2 Alluring Skull") on your right. Continue forward and find a [Lifegem](/Lifegem "Dark Souls 2 Lifegem") on a skeleton on the left, and a [Petrified Something](/Petrified+Something "Dark Souls 2 Petrified Something"), further ahead. At the end, before the fog gate you'll have to face a [Syan Soldier](/Syan+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Syan Soldier").[Benhart of Jugo](/Benhart+of+Jugo "Dark Souls 2 Benhart of Jugo")'s summon sign can also be found right before the boss fog. When you are ready, proceed.

### Scholar of the First Sin 

When you open the door with the key, you will find yourself in a long, straight corridor with [Stone Knights](/Stone+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Stone Knight") on either side, four of which will stand up as you enter. There will also be 3x [Alluring Skull](/Alluring+Skull "Dark Souls 2 Alluring Skull") on your right. The skeleton on the left, after the first Stone Knight, contains [Lifegem](/Lifegem "Dark Souls 2 Lifegem") on the right, before the last Stone Knight, contains a [Soul of a Proud Knight](/Soul+of+a+Proud+Knight#fex "Dark Souls 2 Soul of a Proud Knight#fex") and a [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite#fex "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite#fex") and the last on the left contains a [Petrified Something](/Petrified+Something "Dark Souls 2 Petrified Something"). At the end, before the fog gate you'll find a summon sign for [Ashen Knight Boyd](/Ashen+Knight+Boyd "Dark Souls 2 Ashen Knight Boyd") on the right, and a summon sign for [Benhart of Jugo](/Benhart+of+Jugo "Dark Souls 2 Benhart of Jugo") to the left. When you're ready, proceed.

### Boss Fight: [Looking Glass Knight](/Looking+Glass+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Knight")

After taking care of the Knight, head straight out off the boss areana, but dont use the elevator yet. Instead, circle around the elevator to the little opening behind it, and turn to the left, there is a iron chest with a [Soul Bolt](/Soul+Bolt "Dark Souls 2 Soul Bolt"), a [Spell Quartz Ring +2](/Spell+Quartz+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Spell Quartz Ring") and 3x [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") in it. After looting the chest, head back to the elevator and use it to ride down to the [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana").
