**Dark Spirits** in **Dark Souls 2** are a more powerful "red" version of regular enemies, normally found in NG+ cycles. Dark Spirits are are red invading phantoms. Killing them is necessary for obtaining the [Lingering Dragoncrest Ring + 2](/Lingering+Dragoncrest+Ring#fex "Dark Souls 2 Lingering Dragoncrest Ring#fex"). Summoned red phantoms via organized pvp do not count as Dark Spirits for the ring purpose.
