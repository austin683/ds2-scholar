# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Greatshields

**Greatshields** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are types of [Shields](/Shields "Dark Souls 2 Shields") that are heavier than than other shields, they're the largest and heaviest among other shield types and provide the most protection. They are often used by players who have high [Strength](/Strength "Dark Souls 2 Strength"), as they require a good amount to use. Greatshields does not have the ability to parry but instead, it is replaced by a Shield Bash which is good for stunning opponents. It's a good move for both offense and defense as players can still block incoming attacks throughout the animation of this move, highly effective when facing multiple enemies and boss encounters. This page covers a list of all Greatshields in Dark Souls 2.
