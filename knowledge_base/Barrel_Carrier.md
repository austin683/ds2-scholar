**Barrel Carrier** is an [enemy](/enemies "Dark Souls 2 enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. This type of enemy can be found at the [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower"). [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Barrel Carrier Information in Dark Souls 2

A hollow carrying a barrel full of an explosive substance.

### Dark Souls 2 Barrel Carrier Location

  * [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower")

### Barrel Carrier Drops in DS2

  * [Black Firebomb](/Black+Firebomb "Dark Souls 2 Black Firebomb")
  * [Human Effigy](/Human+Effigy "Dark Souls 2 Human Effigy")

### DS2 Barrel Carrier Strategy Tips

  * Dispatch them with Fire Bow / Fire Crossbow / or any projectile which deals fire damage, can be used for breaking walls.
  * Detonate them with pyromancy
