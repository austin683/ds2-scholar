**Petrified Something** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Petrified Something** , an enigmatic petrified artifact. Surprisingly pleasing to the touch, despite its outward appearance. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> An unidentified petrified object. Pleasant to the touch, despite its looks.  
> A rare and peculiar thing, to be certain, but one without a known purpose.

### Petrified Something Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Trade with [Dyna & Tillo](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo") for a random high-tier item.

### Dark Souls 2 Petrified Something Location

  * In [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt"), after lighting all scones, drops from the red knight which appears (Scholar of the First Sin on Xbox1/PS4/PC version only, confirmed that lighting all the sconces does nothing for players on Xbox360/PS3).
  * As starting [Gifts](/Gifts "Dark Souls 2 Gifts") when you make your character.
  * In The Forest of Fallen Giants, right before dropping down to kill the firebomb enemies who throw bombs down into to area across from the ballistas, walk towards the lit sconce and kill the enemy sitting on the ground next to it. He is invisible but he casts a visible shadow that can be seen on the floor. Can also drop regular [Smooth and Silky Stone](/Smooth+&+Silky+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Smooth & Silky Stone") instead if you are unlucky. (only on Scholar of the First Sin edition)
  * Within a hidden room near [Upper Earthen Peak's](/Upper+Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Upper Earthen Peak") bonfire.
  * After [The Rotten](/The+Rotten "Dark Souls 2 The Rotten") boss fight, in a chest to the right.
  * After the [Old Iron King](/Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Old Iron King") boss fight, in a chest to the left.
  * Possible drop from any of the dragons in [Dragon Aerie](/Dragon+Aerie "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Aerie").
  * Rare drop from [Mimics](/Mimic "Dark Souls 2 Mimic").
  * In a chest located in a door to the left of [Captain Drummond](/Captain+Drummond "Dark Souls 2 Captain Drummond") in the [Memory of Vammar](/Memory+of+Vammar "Dark Souls 2 Memory of Vammar"). Only appears in NG+ and beyond. ([Bonfire Ascetics](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") takes its place in NG.)
  * Three of them were temporarily available in the basement chest in [Cale](/Cale+the+Cartographer "Dark Souls 2 Cale the Cartographer")'s mansion in Majula as part of a DLC promotional event.
  * One can be found in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower"). From the Foyer bonfire, jump the gap, then find and defeat a couple of enemies. Open the doors in this room to find the item.

### Petrified Something Notes and Tips in DKS2

  * Dyna & Tillo **will not accept stacked stones**. You may stand in the nest, select "Leave" and drop them individually to receive a reward. You may drop multiple individual stones, one at a time, and receive all the rewards at once.
  * You will always receive a reward of the same or lower tier value.
