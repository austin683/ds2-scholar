_"Hood worn by Leydia witches._  
  
_Leydia apostles worship Galib, god of disease. They once resided in the Undead Crypt beside Fenito._  
  
_The Leydia witches grew conceited, and began to manipulate both the onset and curing of disease, making themselves the effective gatekeepers of the crypt."_

### Location

  * Dropped by [Leydia Witch](/Leydia+Witch) (spell casting enemies wearing this set) in the [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Leydia Black Set](/Leydia+Black+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
  * Grants additional 1 [Intelligence](/Intelligence).
