**Syan's Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Syan's Set](/Syan's+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Syan's Leggings Description

_"Replica of the armor of the loyal knight Syan. This solidly-crafted gear offers_ high _defense._  
_Sir Syan was widely known as the Kingdom's most loyal knight, and when the Giants invaded, he volunteered to lead the advance party, but he was slaughtered most dishonorably._  
_The King commissioned replicas of Syan's_ accoutrements _and bestowed them on promising knights, but not long after they donned the armor did most go thoroughly mad."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Found on Knights in Drangleic Castle
  * Also found on Knights in the Undead Crypt, before the boss room(s)

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shard, Large Titanite Shard, Titanite Chunk, Titanite Slab](/Ores)
