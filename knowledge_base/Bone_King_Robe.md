_"Skeleton lord Armor.__High dark defense._

_The very men who were ordered to hunt down the Undead were themselves hollowed, and founded a kingdom of bone."_

### Location

  * Sold by Titchy Gren accessible in a small area after the [Executioner's Chariot](/Executioner%27s+Chariot "Dark Souls 2 Executioner%27s Chariot") boss fight.
  * Must kill the Skeleton Lords before [Titchy](/Titchy+Gren "Dark Souls 2 Titchy Gren") will sell it to the player.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Bone King Set](/Bone+King+Set "Dark Souls 2 Bone King Set")
  * Upgraded with: [Twinkling Titanite](/Upgrades#Ore "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades#Ore").
