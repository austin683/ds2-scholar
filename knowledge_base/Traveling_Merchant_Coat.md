_"Coat_ _worn by merchants from Lanafir, where blue represents knowledge. Wearing this increases item discovery._

_Lanafir is the farthest land to the south and follows a strict edict of isolationism. The rare visitor from Lanafir is always a little odd."__"_

### Location

  * Sold by [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia) by the Cardinal Tower bonfire in the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants), or in [Majula](/Majula) when dialogue is exhausted.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Traveling Merchant Set](/Traveling+Merchant+Set).
  * Upgraded from [Titanite](/Titanite+Shard).
