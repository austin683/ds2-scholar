**Looking Glass Leanings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Looking Glass Set](/Looking+Glass+Set "Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Set") in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Looking Glass Leanings Description

"Leggings worn by the Looking Glass Knight. Made of metal, but have high lightning defense.  
Those who wish to serve the king loyal warriors must take the King's Passage and face the Looking Glass Knight.  
Those who fail the test are sacrificed by the merciless specular monstrosity."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Bought from Maughlin the Armorer after killing the Looking Glass Knight.

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite")
