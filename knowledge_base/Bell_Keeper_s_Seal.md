**Bell Keeper's Seal** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). Bell Keeper's Seal, is the Ring of the Bell Keeper covenant. Embrace this covenant and don this ring to be instantly summoned to the domain of an invader of the bell keepers. The paired bells stand as a symbol of the love between two beings eternally kept apart. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> Ring of the Bell Keeper covenant. Join this covenant and wear this ring to be automatically summoned to the world of an invader of the bell keepers domain. The twin bells symbolize the love between two lovers who could never be united. The bell keepers are their eternal guardians. For that is their love, that is their curse."

### Dark Souls 2 Bell Keeper's Seal Effects

**Durability** : 120

**Weight** :

  * 0.2

**Effect:**

  * Ring for the Bell Keeper covenant. Allows invasions as a grey spirit to defend the Bell Tower

### Bell Keeper's Seal Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Join the [Bell Keepers](/Bell+Keepers "Dark Souls 2 Bell Keepers") covenant at [Belfry Luna](/Belfry+Luna "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Luna")
  * Join the [Bell Keepers](/Bell+Keepers "Dark Souls 2 Bell Keepers") covenant at [Belfry Sol](/Belfry+Sol "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Sol")
