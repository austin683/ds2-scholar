**Lloyd's Talisman** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Lloyd's Talisman** , an amulet wielded by cleric knights. Prevents Estus recovery within a confined area. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Talisman used by cleric knights. Blocks Estus recovery within a limited area.
> 
> It is said that the cleric knights used these talismans to hunt down accursed Undead.
> 
> Cleric knights fight with pride, and by blocking the recovery of the Undead, they can also fight with impunity.

### Lloyd's Talisman Usage in DKS2

  * Applies a [debuff](/Buffs+&+Debuffs "Dark Souls 2 Buffs & Debuffs") that blocks [Estus Flask](/Estus+Flask "Dark Souls 2 Estus Flask") usage within the area its thrown for 60 seconds.
  * If used on a [Mimic](/Mimic "Dark Souls 2 Mimic") chest, the [Mimic](/Mimic "Dark Souls 2 Mimic") opens its mouth and allows you to take the item without taking aggro.

### Dark Souls 2 Lloyd's Talisman Locations

  * 10 are sold by [Sweet Shalquoir](/Sweet+Shalquoir "Dark Souls 2 Sweet Shalquoir"), the Cat merchant in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula"), for 1600 souls a piece, unlimited after acquiring the [King's Ring](/King%27s+Ring "Dark Souls 2 King%27s Ring").
  * Treasure in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula") at the [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods") entrance, first door on the left in the room with the petrified woman.
  * 2 are found on a corpse in shaded woods when sticking to the right, Replaced with [Gower's Ring of Protection](/Gower%27s+Ring+of+Protection "Dark Souls 2 Gower%27s Ring of Protection") on NG+.
  * Next to a door just before the first switch which brings up a platform in the [Dragonrider](/Dragonrider "Dark Souls 2 Dragonrider")'s boss room in [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide%27s Tower of Flame").
