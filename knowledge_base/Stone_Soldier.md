**Stone Soldier** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. In a kneeling position, **Stone Soldiers** remain motionless and only become active when players draw close. The first encounter with them is in the large room beside the [Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle") entrance bonfire. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Stone Soldier Information

The Stone Soldier is an enemy in Dark Souls II. It can first be encountered in the large room beside the castle entrance bonfire

  * Soldiers remain motionless in a kneeling position and do not attack players until they draw close.
  * After being approached, soldiers will go through a lengthy ~3 second animation where they come to life and become hostile.

### Stone Soldier Location in Dark Souls 2

  * [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle") \- Near King's Gate bonfire

### DKS2 Stone Soldier Drops

  * [Llewellyn Shield](/Llewellyn+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Llewellyn Shield")
  * [Stone Soldier Spear](/Stone+Soldier+Spear "Dark Souls 2 Stone Soldier Spear")
  * [Titanite Slab](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab")
  * Souls: NG: 350 NG+: 700 NG+7: 1,400

### Stone Soldier Strategy Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Weapons with 30+ poise impact will stun them
  * They can be damaged while they are coming to life
