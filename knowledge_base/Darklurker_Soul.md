**Darklurker Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Soul of the Darklurker. Discovered within the Dark Chasm of Old, this soul is the residual essence of an ancient, dissipated being. Use the unique soul to amass a considerable number of souls, or craft something of significant value. Obtainable by defeating the boss [Darklurker](/Darklurker "Dark Souls 2 Darklurker"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of what lurks in the Dark Chasm.  
> The Dark Chasm of Old is the remnant of some ancient, dissipated being.  
> Use the special soul of what lurks within to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Dark Souls 2 Darklurker Soul Usage

  * Consume for 22,000 souls
  * Trade for [Lifedrain Patch](/Lifedrain+Patch#fex "Dark Souls 2 Lifedrain Patch#fex") with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis").

### Darklurker Soul Location in DKS2

  * Defeat Boss [Darklurker](/Darklurker "Dark Souls 2 Darklurker")
