**Cale's Set** is an Attire or Armor in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

  
  
  
  
_"Helmet/Armor/Leggings of a light mirrah foot soldier. belonged to Cale the Cartographer._   
_Apparently Cale procured this inexpensively second-hand, but it's surprisingly well-made."_

###  Set Location

  * Talk to Cale in abandoned house after lighting all regular bonfires.

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with normal Titanite
  * The helmet is part of Royal Sorcerer Navlaan's storyline.
  * Hood grants +1 to END and +2 to DEX

###  Piece Information & Upgrades

First row = Regular. Second row = +10
