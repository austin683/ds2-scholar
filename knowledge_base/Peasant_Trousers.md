**Peasant Trousers** is a **Legging** and a part of [Peasant Set](/Peasant+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Peasant Trousers Description

"Attire is commonly worn by peasants.  
Normal workwear, more fit for farming than fighting"

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Dropped by Peasants in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora)
  * The Peasant Attire (chest) +5 and Peasant's Helm +5 is a drop of the Peasant Red Phantom in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora)
  * 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with Titanite Shard, Large, Chunk and Slab
