_"Eerily shaped helmet/armor/gauntlets/leggings spoken of in legend, crafted with the scales of the Black Dragon.  
  
  
The Black Dragon was long considered a mere legend, but this proves its existence."_

### Location

  * Achieve Rank 1 in the [Dragon Remnants](/Dragon+Remnants) covenant to obtain the [Dragon Head Stone](/Dragon+Head+Stone) for the helmet.
  * Achieve Rank 2 in the [Dragon Remnants](/Dragon+Remnants) covenant to obtain the [Dragon Torso Stone](/Dragon+Torso+Stone) for the rest of the set.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Black Dragon Armor](/Black+Dragon+Armor). 
    * This set provides fair physical defense and excellent elemental defense at an incredibly low weight
    * Cannot be removed unless the wearer dies. [Ring of Life Protection](/Ring+of+Life+Protection) is recommended in this case if you want to purposefully unequip the set.
    * This set gives 0 poise when worn. The only poise you get is from your natural [Adaptability](/Adaptability) and [Endurance](/Endurance).
  * Cannot be upgraded.
