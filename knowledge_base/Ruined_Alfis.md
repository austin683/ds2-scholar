**Ruined Alfis** is a [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") in **Dark Souls 2**

###  Ruined Alfis Information

  * Summoned as a Shade in the dark hall full of skeletons between the [Cardinal Tower](/Cardinal+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Cardinal Tower") and [Soldier's Rest](/Soldier%27s+Rest "Dark Souls 2 Soldier%27s Rest") in [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants") (Scholar of the First Sin only)
  * Can be summoned as a Phantom in [Cave of the Dead](/Cave+of+the+Dead "Dark Souls 2 Cave of the Dead") in the [Crown of the Sunken King DLC ](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King")
  * in the Forest of the Fallen Giants, he can be a powerful ally against the invading NPC [Armourer Dennis](/Armourer+Dennis "Dark Souls 2 Armourer Dennis"), if you are at low level
  * Casts [Cast Light](/Cast+Light "Dark Souls 2 Cast Light") (Can be useful), [Crystal Soul Spear](/Crystal+Soul+Spear "Dark Souls 2 Crystal Soul Spear"), and [Homing Soul Arrow](/Homing+Soul+Arrow "Dark Souls 2 Homing Soul Arrow") Sorceries, and [Dark Hail](/Dark+Hail "Dark Souls 2 Dark Hail") Hex
  * Wears [Dragon Sage Hood](/Dragon+Sage+Hood "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Sage Hood") and [Dragon Acolyte Set](/Dragon+Acolyte+Set "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Acolyte Set")
  * Uses [Bat Staff](/Bat+Staff "Dark Souls 2 Bat Staff")
