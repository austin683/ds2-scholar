#  Scorpioness Najka Soul

image   
  
_Soul of Scorpioness Najka._   
_Nakja was born of the misdeeds of an ancient being, a frail soul from the beginning, that soon succumbed to madness._   
_Use the special soul of Scorpioness Najka to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth._   
  

##  Usage

  * Consuming gives 8000 Souls.
  * Trade with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis) for [Soul Shower](/Soul+Shower).

  
  

##  Location

  * Obtained by defeating [Scorpioness Najka](/Scorpioness+Najka) in [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Forest).
