_"Strange mask worn by Aldia warlocks. Grants dark defense, and increased soul acquisition._

_Warlocks in Aldia gave rise to wicked things, and even forbidden rituals upon themselves. No one knows if they were born mad, or if their own misdeeds drove them over the edge."_

### Location

  * Dropped by NPC invader [Merciless Roenna](/Merciless+Roenna "Dark Souls 2 Merciless Roenna") in [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman's+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman's Copse") (rare).
  * Dropped by [Aldia Warlock](/Aldia+Warlock "Dark Souls 2 Aldia Warlock") in [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana") (rare), found behind the doors above Rise of the Dead bonfire (only hollows can pass). [No Warlock there in SotFS]
  * Dropped by Aldia Warlock in [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s Keep") (in a storage locked with [Aldia Key](/Aldia+Key "Dark Souls 2 Aldia Key")).
  * [Scholar of the First Sin] Dropped by petrified [Lion Warrior](/Lion+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Lion Warrior") in [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods"), the one farthest from Shaded Ruins bonfire. 
    * [Scholar of the First Sin] Dropped by regular [Lion Warriors](/Lion+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Lion Warrior") (rare) in the same area (Confirmed in NG+) (Confirmed in NG on Xbox One).

### Notes

  * There is no Warlock Set available. 
  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
  * Offers the highest magic & dark defense and petrification resistance, as well as second highest lightning defense and curse resistance (not counting the two helms that negate curse completely).
  * Requires 14 [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") then adds 2+ more.
  * Increases soul income by 2.5%.
  * This mask is worn by [Prowling Magus](/Prowling+Magus+and+Congregation "Dark Souls 2 Prowling Magus and Congregation").
