**Headless Vengarl** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. The **Headless Vengarl** , once Vengarl of Forossa, now a decapitated body. Seemingly reanimated by its bloodlust from a brutal war, it roams in search of battles from its past. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Headless Vengarl Information in Dark Souls 2

The decapitated body of Vengarl of Forossa. Separated from its head, during a brutal war long ago and now somehow ambulatory once again, it wanders in search of the battles it knew in life as though reanimated by its bloodlust...   
Packs a punch but is easier to stagger than he looks, and can be quickly taken out if you get the first strike.
