#  Ironhearth Hall

* * *

##  Location:

Second bonfire in the [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep). After you defeat the [Smelter Demon](/Smelter+Demon), through a gate on the far side of the arena, then up some stairs.   
  

##  Bosses:

  * No bosses connected to this bonfire

  

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here respawns the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * Simpleton's spice
  * Covetous Gold Serpent Ring
  * Item
  * Item
  * Alonne knight
  * Alonne captain
  * Ironclad soldiers
  * Mimic
  * Crystal Lizard

  

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here spawns the following new items/enemies:

  * Item
  * Item
  * Enemy
  * 2x Alonne knight Dark Spirit (one guarding the chest on the lava rocks, and one standing on one of the platforms in the cylindrical room)
  * Alonne captain Dark Spirit (standing at the top of the stairs, facing the pharros contraption that spews water)
