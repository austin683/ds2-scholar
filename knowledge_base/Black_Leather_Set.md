**Black Leather Set** is an Attire or [Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor") in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

> Armor/Leather gloves/Leather boots dyed black.  
>   
> Allows the wearer to blend in with the dark. Often used by those with ill-intent. Meticulously crafted, and provides fair defense.

###  Set Location

  * Sold by [Laddersmith Gilligan](/Laddersmith+Gilligan "Dark Souls 2 Laddersmith Gilligan") (4,3k) who is found in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak") and later in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula").

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
  * The 'Thief Mask' looks dramatically different on male and female characters: the male version covers all but the eyes, whereas the female version is a small mask that only covers the mouth.
