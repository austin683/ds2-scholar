**Dexterity Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in **Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)**. **Dexterity Ring** , an oddly contorted iron ring. Enhances the dexterity of the wearer. During the era dominated by the Old Iron King, the ruler wielded a profound mastery over iron, manipulating it as if he commanded the very forces of life and creation. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> An oddly deformed iron ring. Increases the dexterity of its wearer. The Old Iron King ruled over a great age of iron. The King's power allowed him to mold iron freely, as if he held sway over the forces of life and creation."

### Dark Souls 2 Dexterity Ring Effects

**Durability** : 120

**Weight** :

  * 1.5

**Effect:**

  * Increases [Dexterity](/Dexterity "Dark Souls 2 Dexterity") by 5

### Dexterity Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * DLC [Crown of the Old Iron King Rings](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King"): From the Throne Floor Bonfire walk up the stairs to where the first Idol of Nadalia is found and head left towards some stairs leading down. Descend the stairs behind the throne and the ring can be found on a corpse against some metal bars. ,[Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower")
