**Lingering Flame** is a [Pyromancy](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Lingering Flame** , a pyromancy crafted by Straid, the esteemed mage of ancient Olaphis. Unleashes a stationary fireball that erupts upon the approach of foes. [Pyromancies](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies") are offensive fire spells that do not demand [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith") or [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") prerequisites and can be executed with a [Pyromancy Flame](/Pyromancy+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancy Flame") or [Dark Pyromancy Flame](/Dark+Pyromancy+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Dark Pyromancy Flame"). The fire damage scales with [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") and [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith"), yet there are no specific stat requirements for casting pyromancies. However, opponents resistant to fire damage remain unaffected by [Pyromancy Spells](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies").

> "One of the pyromancies devised by Straid, the great mage of ancient Olaphis. Release a static fireball that explodes when foes draw near.  
>   
> Straid was an oddly gifted mage, well-versed in both sorceries and pyromancies, but his curious temperament never allowed him to settle down in one place for very long."

### Dark Souls 2 Lingering Flame Acquired From

  * Bought from [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis").
  * Occasional drop from the Undead Jailers in The Lost Bastille.
  * **SotFS only:** can also drop from the Undead Jailer in No-Man's Wharf on top of the pirate ship. 

### Lingering Flame Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Leaves a floating fireball in midair in front of the player that explodes when an enemy gets within a certain range.  
This can be useful in PVP because it allows to player to lock down certain positions. Either the opponent walks into the mine and takes damage or has to find a certain way to avoid them while buying you some time in the process. 
  * Also used by [Desert Sorceress](/Desert+Sorceress "Dark Souls 2 Desert Sorceress").
