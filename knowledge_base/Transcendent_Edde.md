#  Transcendent Edde 

Updated Aug 24, 2015 10:29 am    

  * Phantom that can be summoned for [Sinh, the Slumbering Dragon](/Sinh%2C+the+Slumbering+Dragon)
  * Wields [Black Dragon Greatsword](/Black+Dragon+Greatsword) and [Black Dragon Shield](/Black+Dragon+Shield)
  * Wears full [Black Dragon Set](/Black+Dragon+Set) but will not utilize [Dragon Head Stone](/Dragon+Head+Stone) or [Dragon Torso Stone](/Dragon+Torso+Stone)
  * Does "Duel Bow" gesture upon defeating Sinh
