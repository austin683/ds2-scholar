# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Mounted+Overseer

**Banedigger (Mounted Overseer)** is an enemy in Dark Souls 2.

  

###  Strategy tips

  * Try attacking from the above as they can't aim up. This only applies to the non-scholar addition of Dark Souls 2, as in the SotFS edition the Mounted Overseers **can** aim up.

    
    
    | HP | Weak | Resists | Respawns? | Respawns? |
    | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
    | NG: 1,200 / NG+: 2,250 / NG+7: 2,700 | Magic | ? | Yes | Yes |
    

**Location** : [Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley")   
**NG** : Yes   
**Move set**

  * Dark Orb: Immune to Profound Still, due to projectile not being spell. 
    * Dark Damage
    * Can be blocked.
  * Melee: Used at close range. 
    * Physical Damage
    * Can be blocked/Parried.

**Drops**

  * [Smooth & Silky Stone](/Smooth+%26+Silky+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Smooth %26 Silky Stone")
  * [Sunlight Medal](/Sunlight+Medal "Dark Souls 2 Sunlight Medal")
  * NG: 750 Souls,NG+ 1,500 Souls, NG+7: 1,875 Souls.
