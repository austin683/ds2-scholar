_"Hood worn by Hollowed Drangleic mages.  
  
Drangleic mages wore different garb depending on their sex. What function this served, however, is unknown, as with many old practices. This is a women's hood/robe. "  
_

### Location

  * Drop from [Necromancer](/Necromancer) in [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman%27s+Copse) and [Undead Purgatory](/Undead+Purgatory) (they do respawn in Undead Purgatory, in case of farming for the hood and [Lizard Staff](/Lizard+Staff)).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Black Hollow Mage Set](/Black+Hollow+Mage+Set)
  * Increases [Intelligence](/Intelligence) 1 point.
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Upgrades#Ore).
  * Male variant is [White Hollow Set](/White+Hollow+Set). 
    * Note that the hood is inferior and heavier than the white hood despite being rare, while the black robe is better than the white robe despite heavier.
  * It is indicated that the necromancers in Huntsman's Copse are female, it can be identified by the mask.
  * The mask is skinned from the Necromancer's face, without lower jaw.
