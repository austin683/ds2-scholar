**Flame Quartz Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). The **Flame Quartz Ring** is a reward for Melfian Magic Academy students meeting set standards, featuring fire-based quartz for heightened fire defense. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A ring bestowed upon students of a certain standard of the Melfian Magic Academy. It's fire-based quartz increases fire defense. There are rumors that Pyromancer Glocken has made a hefty profit selling these replicas.

### Dark Souls 2 Flame Quartz Ring Effects

**Durability** :

  * +0 = 140
  * +1 = 110
  * +2 = 90
  * +3 = 75

**Weight** :

  * +0=0.2
  * +1=0.5
  * +2=0.8
  * +3=1.0

**Effect:**

  * Increases fire defense by 50
  * +1 = 80
  * +2 = 100
  * +3 = 150

### Flame Quartz Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Sold by [Rosabeth of Melfia](/Rosabeth+of+Melfia "Dark Souls 2 Rosabeth of Melfia") for 2,000 souls.
  * +1: [Forest of the Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants"), down the Cardinal Tower bonfire. Found in the chest guarded by salamanders in the flame pit.
  * +2: [Guardian Dragon](/Guardian+Dragon "Dark Souls 2 Guardian Dragon"), random possible drop from the non-respawning dragons in Dragon Aerie.
  * +3: [Crown of the Old Iron King Rings DLC](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King") Found on the floor in front of an Iron Warrior guarding a big metal gate after the second bonfire. - [Location Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GyotwXrM8W8&feature=youtu.be)

### DS2 Flame Quartz Ring Tips and Notes

  * One of the things you should use to make it possible to walk on the lava rocks in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep").
  * Very useful against the [Smelter Demon](/Smelter+Demon "Dark Souls 2 Smelter Demon"). Or you can use Flash Sweat to have the same fire resistance.
  * Can be useful against players who use Pyromancy and/or fire imbued weapons in PvP.
