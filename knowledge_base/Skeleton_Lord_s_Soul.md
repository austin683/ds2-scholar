**Skeleton's Lord Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. The Soul of a Skeleton Lord is a manifestation of the ruler who once held dominion deep within the Huntsman's Copse. Entrusted with the duty to apprehend all Undead, the agents appointed for this mission succumbed to the curse, leading to their demise. You can obtain the Skeleton's Lord Soul by defeating the Skeleton Lords in Huntsman's Copse. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of a Skeleton Lord, who reigned from deep within the Huntsman's Copse.  
> The Old Iron King commanded the capture of all Undead, but those charged with the task were overcome by the curse.  
> Use the special soul of the Skeleton Lord to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Skeleton's Lord Soul Usage in DKS2

  * Can be consumed for 6,000 Souls.
  * Trade with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for [Roaring Halberd](/Roaring+Halberd#fex "Dark Souls 2 Roaring Halberd#fex").

### Dark Souls 2 Skeleton's Lord Soul Location

  * Obtained by defeating [The Skeleton Lords](/The+Skeleton+Lords "Dark Souls 2 The Skeleton Lords") in [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman%27s+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman%27s Copse").
