_"Armor worn by the Primal Knights of Drangleic castle._

_Their weight would normally crush a man, but the Primal Knights wear them as if they were silk, so fearsome is their brutal strength._

_The King restored a forbidden, long-lost art to create these inhuman abominations."_

### Location

  * Dropped by the [Primal Knights](/Primal+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Primal Knight") at [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Mastodon Armor](/Mastodon+Armor "Dark Souls 2 Mastodon Armor").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab").
