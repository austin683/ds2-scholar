**Bleeding Serum** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Bleeding Serum** , a jar of carnivorous plant secretions that applies a bleeding effect to right-hand weapons, causing afflicted foes to experience a gradual loss of max HP. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A jar of carnivorous plant secretions. Applies bleeding effect to right-hand weapon. Bleeding foes' max HP is lowered.

### Bleeding Serum Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Boosts existing bleed damage by 30%
  * Adds 20 bleed attack value of the right-hand weapon for 90 seconds

### Dark Souls 2 Bleeding Serum Location

  * Can be bought from [Laddersmith Gilligan](/Laddersmith+Gilligan "Dark Souls 2 Laddersmith Gilligan") for 2000 Souls each
  * (x3) can be found in a chest blocked by a petrified Lion Clan Warrior in Shaded Woods
  * Drops from [Amana Aberrations](/Amana+Aberration "Dark Souls 2 Amana Aberration") in Shrine of Amana (uncommon)
