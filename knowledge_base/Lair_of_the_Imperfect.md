#  Lair of the Imperfect

>> [Bonfires](/Bonfires "Dark Souls 2 Bonfires")   
Updated Aug 15, 2014 12:23 pm 

* * *

##  Location:

In lower [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum"), follow the stairs going down from the "spike room", you will eventually reach a fogwall. Going through the fogwall, you will access the Lair of the Imperfect. The bonfire is directly ahead, across the lair. Be aware that [Jester Thomas](/Jester+Thomas "Dark Souls 2 Jester Thomas") will invade you in the room with the fogwall.  

##  Bosses:

  * No boss in this area

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here respawns the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * 3x [Petrified Dragon Bone](/Petrified+Dragon+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Petrified Dragon Bone")
  * 1 Soul of a Great Hero
  * 2x Dried Root
  * 1 Drakeblood Greatsword
  * 1 Human Effigy (Up the elevator)
  * 5x [The Imperfect](/The+Imperfect "Dark Souls 2 The Imperfect")
  * Jester Thomas

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here spawns the following new items/enemies:

  * Item
  * Item
  * Enemy
  * Enemy

##  Special Notes:

  * Decent place to farm Petrified Dragon Bones since the Imperfect drop them quite frequently.
  * Best place to farm dragon scales, got 30 scales after 4 ascetics.
