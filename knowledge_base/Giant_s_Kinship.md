**Giant's Kinship** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Giant's Kinship** , employed to access the second boss fight at the Throne of Want against Nashandra, obtaining the Lord Soul. Essential for progressing through the game cycle. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> Scholar of the First Sin added the following:  
> The flames roar, but will soon begin to fade, and only a worthy heir might burnish their light. What is it, truly, a claimant of the throne could desire?

### Giant's Kinship Usage in DKS2

  * Used to gain access to the 2nd boss fight at the [Throne of Want](/Throne+of+Want "Dark Souls 2 Throne of Want") for the Lord Soul, [Nashandra](/Nashandra "Dark Souls 2 Nashandra").
  * This item is necessary for completing the game cycle.

### Dark Souls 2 Giant's Kinship Location

  * Gained upon killing [Giant Lord](/Giant+Lord "Dark Souls 2 Giant Lord")
