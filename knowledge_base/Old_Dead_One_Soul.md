**Old Dead One Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Old Dead One Soul** , a soul that once wielded great influence over the land, persisting in its echoes even as the eons whittle it down to these remnants. Consume the Old Dead One Soul to amass numerous souls or craft something of substantial value. Obtained by defeating [The Rotten](/The+Rotten "Dark Souls 2 The Rotten") on NG+ and beyond. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the ineffable.  
> This once magnificent soul continues to exert influence over the land, even after the eons have reduced it to these remnants.

### Old Dead One Soul Usage in DKS2

  * Trade with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") with 5000 souls for [Numbness](/Numbness#fex "Dark Souls 2 Numbness#fex") [hex](/Hexes "Dark Souls 2 Hexes").
  * Trade with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") to get the [Crypt Blacksword](/Crypt+Blacksword#fex "Dark Souls 2 Crypt Blacksword#fex").
  * Can be consumed to acquire 60,000 souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Old Dead One Soul Location

  * Dropped by killing [the Rotten](/The+Rotten "Dark Souls 2 The Rotten") on NG+ and beyond. (Can be obtained using [Bonfire Ascetic ](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic"))

### Old Dead One Soul Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Speculation: A probable remnant of the soul of [Gravelord Nito](https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Nito "Dark Souls 2 Nito").
