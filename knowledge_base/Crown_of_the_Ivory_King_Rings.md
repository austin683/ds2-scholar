**Crown of the Ivory King Rings** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** refers to special rings found in the [Dark Souls 2: Crown of the Ivory King DLC](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King"). These rings provide unique effects to aid your character in the challenging DLC environments. Alongside other items in the expansion, these rings are essential for overcoming tough foes and the icy landscape. To obtain these rings, explore the new areas thoroughly and complete specific objectives within the DLC.
