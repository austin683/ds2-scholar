### Monastery Long Gloves Description

"A traditional sacred headcloth/longshirt/gloves/skirt crafted at Lindelt Monastery. Made of lightweight, flowing white fabric.

The ceremonial design suggests that they revered whatever it was they watched over."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Dropped from a Black phantom found behind a painting in [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s Keep") (NG+).
