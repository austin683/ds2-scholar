_Armor of the[Black Drakeblood Knights](/Black+Drakeblood+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Black Drakeblood Knight"),_   
_who came from a land long forgotten._   
  
_The Drakeblood Knights, who worshipped the blood of dragons, were led by Sir Yorgh in a siege of the Eternal Sanctum, but sunk into the Sanctum when the sleeping dragon awoke._   
  
_The red cloth wrapped around the black armor represents the sacred blood of dragons._   
  
  
  

###  Set Location

  * Found in upper [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon's+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon's Sanctum"), close to final drop to [Dragon's Rest](/Dragon%27s+Rest "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Rest"), on a corpse next to 5 empty chests just below Sanctum Interior bonfire (the one hidden behind a completely nondistinctive wall).

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
  * Upgraded stats are only 50% higher and physical defenses are only 25% higher.
