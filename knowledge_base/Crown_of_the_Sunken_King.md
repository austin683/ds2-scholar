**Crown of the Sunken King** is a **[DLC](/DLC "Dark Souls 2 DLC")** for **Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)** , released on July 22nd 2014. The DLC was later bundled as part of the [Scholar of the First Sin Edition](/Dark+Souls+II+Scholar+of+the+First+Sin "Dark Souls 2 Dark Souls II Scholar of the First Sin") of the game. This DLC is the first pack of the Lost Crowns trilogy. The trilogy contains the following Downloadable Content released in the following order: [Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King"), [Crown of the Old Iron King](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King"), [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King"). These DLC releases added new content to the Dark Souls 2 experience, including some new [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies"), [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses"), [Locations](/Locations "Dark Souls 2 Locations") and [Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons"). Minor modifications have also been made to some details and descriptions during the [Scholar of the First Sin](/Dark+Souls+II+Scholar+of+the+First+Sin "Dark Souls 2 Dark Souls II Scholar of the First Sin") Remaster. All episodes were sold for either $9.99 a piece, or added as part of the Dark Souls II Season Pass for $24.99. [Official Trailer Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g_24S4j-jWU)

> "The Crown of the Sunken King sends players on a quest to reclaim the Crown that King Vendrick once owned. With a entirely new areas to explore within the Dark Souls 2 universe, players will find pyramids, underground caverns, and unknown foes. It is said that the Ancient Crown is buried deep below the surface, but surely it cannot sit unguarded. Explore in search of a crown that holds the strength of lords from times long past.

### All Lost Crowns Trilogy DLC

This [DLC](/DLC "Dark Souls 2 DLC") was released s a trilogy that was released one after the other with about a month apart. Players were able to purchase these DLC packs individually or as a bundle. Those who purchased the Season Pass had access to all three DLC packs as they were released. Purchasing the remastered release, [Scholar of the First Sin](/Dark+Souls+II+Scholar+of+the+First+Sin "Dark Souls 2 Dark Souls II Scholar of the First Sin") also gave players access to all three DLC packs.

  * [Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King") \- July 22, 2014
  * [Crown of the Old Iron King](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King") \- August 26, 2014
  * [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King") \- September 30, 2014

###  Accessing the Crown of the Sunken King DLC

After buying the DLC an item is added to the inventory: the [Dragon Talon](/Dragon+Talon "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Talon"). The player must go to the primal bonfire chamber in the [Black Gulch](/Black+Gulch "Dark Souls 2 Black Gulch") and examine an obelisk to be teleported to a chamber with a locked door that opens with the [Dragon Talon](/Dragon+Talon "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Talon"). Players that don't have the DLC can be summoned into it by placing their signs in this chamber. The signs will show up inside the DLC.

In [Scholar of the First Sin](/Scholar+of+the+First+Sin "Dark Souls 2 Scholar of the First Sin"), the [Dragon Talon](/Dragon+Talon "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Talon") is no longer placed into your inventory. You now need to go to [Black Gulch](/Black+Gulch "Dark Souls 2 Black Gulch") and kill the giants before the final bonfire and the boss fight with [The Rotten](/The+Rotten "Dark Souls 2 The Rotten") to receive the [Forgotten Key](/Forgotten+Key "Dark Souls 2 Forgotten Key"), which opens doors in [Black Gulch](/Black+Gulch "Dark Souls 2 Black Gulch") and [the Gutter](/the+Gutter "Dark Souls 2 the Gutter"), as well as a door in [the pit](/the+pit "Dark Souls 2 the pit") in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula"). Inside the door in the pit will be a room with three chests and a corpse; the [Dragon Talon](/Dragon+Talon "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Talon") can be looted off the corpse.
