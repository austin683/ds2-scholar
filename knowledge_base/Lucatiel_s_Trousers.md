**Lucatiel's Trousers** is a **Legging** and a part of [Imported Set](/Imported+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Lucatiel's Trousers Description

"_Worn by knights while on travel. Belonged to Lucatiel of Mirrah. Only those who have distinguished themselves on the battlefield were admitted into the elite ranks of_ Mirrah's _official order of knights. It is common to hear of a peasant's dream of striving for knighthood as an escape from hardship, but who would ever think it possible?"_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Helm/mask obtained as a drop after killing [Lucatiel of Mirrah](/Lucatiel+of+Mirrah).
  * Torso, legs, and gloves obtained from [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia) after killing [Lucatiel of Mirrah](/Lucatiel+of+Mirrah).
  * Will also be given to you by talking to her after summoning her to three bosses where she survives the encounter.

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite)
