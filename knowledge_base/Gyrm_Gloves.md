### Gyrm Gloves Description

_"Gloves of the nomadic Gyrm. Excellent poise but heavy"_  
_"The stocky Gyrm are kind-natured, but humans deemed them impure, and drove them underground."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Found on regular [Gyrm](/Gyrm) (The ones with the anvil on a chain) in [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros)

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shard, Large Titanite Shard, Titanite Chunk, Titanite Slab](/Ores)
