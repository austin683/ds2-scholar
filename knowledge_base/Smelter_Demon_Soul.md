**Smelter Demon Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Smelter Demon Soul** , the essence of the Smelter Demon that haunts the castle submerged in a pool of iron. Consuming this soul grants 8,000 Souls. You can trade it with Straid to obtain the Smelter Sword or trade with Ornifex for the Aged Smelter Sword [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Smelter Demon, that haunts the castle that sunk into a pool of iron.  
> The Old Iron King's life was taken by a mass of iron that had been given a soul. Was this metal goliath there from the beginning, or was it a product of the king's conceit?  
> Use the special soul of the Smelter Demon to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Dark Souls 2 Smelter Demon Soul Usage

  * Consuming gives 8000 Souls
  * Trade with [Straid](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") to get the [Smelter Sword](/Smelter+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Smelter Sword")

  * Trade with [Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") to get the [Aged Smelter Sword](/Aged+Smelter+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Aged Smelter Sword")

### Smelter Demon Soul Location in DKS2

  * Obtained by defeating the [Smelter Demon](/Smelter+Demon "Dark Souls 2 Smelter Demon") in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep").
  * Obtained by defeating the [Smelter Demon](/%28Blue%29+Smelter+Demon "Dark Souls 2 %28Blue%29 Smelter Demon") in [Iron Passage](/Iron+Passage "Dark Souls 2 Iron Passage").
