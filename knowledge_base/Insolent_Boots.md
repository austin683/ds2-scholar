**Insolent Boots** is a **Legging** and a part of [Insolent Set](/Insolent+Set "Dark Souls 2 Insolent Set") in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Insolent Boots Description

"Leggings worn by once-proud clerics.  
Relics of a party who long ago attempted to conquer the Undead Crypt.  
For this sin their deaths have been postponed, so they may serve as crypt sentries."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Bought from Grave Warden Agdayne (Undead Crypt)
  * Costs 5400/9000/6300/7200 souls

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite")
  * Helmet increases faith by 2
