**Lightning Arrow** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Lightning Arrows** , projectiles charged with the power of lightning and adorned with Gold Pine Resin. Armaments enhanced with lightning prove efficacious against adversaries resistant to magic and fire. A convenient option for individuals lacking magical proficiency or those with depleted magical reserves. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Arrow imbued with lightning. Tipped with Gold Pine Resin. Weapons fortified with lightning are effective against foes that are resistant to magic and fire.  
> Useful in the short term for those who have learned no magic, or whose magic is exhausted.

### Lightning Arrow Usage in DKS2

  * Bow ammunition
  * 50 Physical Damage, 140 Lightning Damage

### Dark Souls 2 Lightning Arrow Location

  * Sold by [Chancellor Wellager](/Chancellor+Wellager "Dark Souls 2 Chancellor Wellager") in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle"). Unlimited supply, cost 60 souls.

### Lightning Arrow Notes & Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Lightning Arrows do not arc over distance, bypassing the bow's inherent range modifiers.
