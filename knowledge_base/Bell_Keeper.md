**Bell Keeper** is an [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Aggressive dwarves, dedicated to protecting the bell. They charge with axes or use bows and firebombs. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Bell Keeper Information in Dark Souls 2

Aggressive little dwarves, dedicated to protecting the bell. They appear as grey phantoms and will charge you with their axes held high, be warned as they can hit rather hard. Some of them can use bows, while others wield firebombs.  
The bells are the symbols of forbidden love, and the marionettes who defend them do so with zealous passion.They've been watching over, and ringin', the bells forever. If you seek to stop them, or to ring a bell for yourself, you'll have to face these tiny terrors

  * Allows the player to join the [Bell Keepers Covenant](/Bell+Keepers "Dark Souls 2 Bell Keepers")

### Dark Souls 2 Bell Keeper Encounters

[Belfry Luna](/Belfry+Luna "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Luna"). Near the entrance to the belfry, sitting on a table.  
[Belfry Sol](/Belfry+Sol "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Sol"). Right in front of the entrance to the belfry, sitting on a table.

### DS2 Bell Keeper Drops

  1. [Rusted Coin](/Rusted+Coin "Dark Souls 2 Rusted Coin")
  2. The black phantom versions have a very rare chance to drop the [Flame Swathe](/Flame+Swathe "Dark Souls 2 Flame Swathe") pyromancy.
  3. The black phantom versions have a very rare chance to drop the [Fire Snake](/Fire+Snake "Dark Souls 2 Fire Snake") pyromancy. (Tested in SOTF)
