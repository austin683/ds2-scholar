**BoltStone** is a type of [Upgrade Material](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Boltstone** , a modified version of titanite. Employ it to infuse weapons with lightning or to grant lightning resistance to shields. Fabled tales speak of sorcerers at the Melfian Magic Academy attempting to instill titanite with diverse elements, but their endeavors are thought to have met with failure. [Upgrade Materials](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades") are items that are used to strengthen the statistical value and to increase the effect of a certain piece of equipment.

> An altered state of titanite. Use to imbue weapons with lightning, or to provide lightning reduction to shields.  
> Sorcerers at the Melfian Magic Academy once attempted to imbue titanite with various elements, but are said to have failed. Someone must have succeeded though; what else would explain this stone?

### Boltstone Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Can only be used at Blacksmith [Steady Hand McDuff](/Steady+Hand+McDuff "Dark Souls 2 Steady Hand McDuff")
  * Used to imbue equipment with lightning. This reduces the Strength and Dexterity scaling but adds a Faith scaling. (Different weapons add different tiers of scaling).

### Dark Souls 2 Boltstone Location

  * Unlimited amount sold by [Blue Sentinel Targray](/Blue+Sentinel+Targray "Dark Souls 2 Blue Sentinel Targray") for 7,000 souls.
  * Randomly given in exchange for [Smooth & Silky Stone](/Smooth+%26+Silky+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Smooth %26 Silky Stone") with [Dyna and Tillo](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo").
  * Sold by [Stone Trader Chloanne](/Stone+Trader+Chloanne "Dark Souls 2 Stone Trader Chloanne") after obtaining the [King's Ring](/King%27s+Ring "Dark Souls 2 King%27s Ring").
  * Dropped by a [Crystal Lizard](/Crystal+Lizard "Dark Souls 2 Crystal Lizard") in [Shaded Ruins](/Shaded+Ruins "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Ruins") near [Manscorpion Tark](/Manscorpion+Tark "Dark Souls 2 Manscorpion Tark").
  * Found on a corpse near the first dragon in [Dragon Aerie](/Dragon+Aerie "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Aerie"). Can be farmed using [Bonfire Ascetics](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic"), alongside a lot of other useful upgrade materials.
  * 3 Boltstones are found in a chest in the [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon's+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon's Sanctum") ([Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King") DLC) along with 3 [Bleedstones](/Bleed+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Bleed Stone").
  * 3 in an iron chest in Frozen [Eleum Loyce](/Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Eleum Loyce") in the area with the visible Witch Trees.
