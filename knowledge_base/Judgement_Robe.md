_"A robe honoring an ancient goddess. Sin was said to be her domain, but the name of the goddess is long forgotten."_

_"Little is known about what purpose the robe served, only that it was worn by a person of great authority."_

### Location

  * Found in [Dragon Shrine](/Dragon+Shrine "Dragon Shrine"), in a chest past an illusory wall revealed with Pharros' contraption, under the stairs next to the first [Drakekeeper](/Drakekeeper "Drakekeeper").
  * Dropped (in pieces) by a [Darkdweller](/Darkdweller "Darkdweller") found next to Under Castle Drangleic bonfire in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Drangleic Castle").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Judgment Set](/Judgment+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Twinkling Titanite").
  * The appearance and item description of this set indicates that these are the clothes worn by [Cromwell the Pardoner](/Cromwell+the+Pardoner "Cromwell the Pardoner"), as well as  _Oswald of Carim_ in  _Dark Souls_.
