### Royal Swordsman Gloves Description

"Gauntlets of a royal swordsman. Its shape provides defense while allowing great mobility. Stripped of ornamentation, this particular helm/armor is designed strictly for battle.  
King Vendrick supplied his bravest men with the best armor available to face the great giants, but very few returned alive."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Drop from [Hollow Royal Swordman](/Royal+Swordsman "Dark Souls 2 Royal Swordsman")
  * Drop from Dark Phantom Guthry (Doors of Pharros)

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shard, Large Titanite Shard, Titanite Chunk, Titanite Slab](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores")
