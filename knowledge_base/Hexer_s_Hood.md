_"Helm worn by a hexer. Belonged to Felkin the Outcast._

_Those who have a taste of dark are drawn into its vortex and rarely return._

_Perhaps it appeals to something deep within the human soul."_

### Location

  * Acquired from [Felkin the Outcast](/Felkin+the+Outcast "Dark Souls 2 Felkin the Outcast") after talking to him while having 20 [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") and [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith") (Ring of Knowledge/Ring of Prayer do not count).
  * Purchased from [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia") in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula") after killing Felkin the Outcast.
  * ### **Notes**

  * Part of [Hexer's Set](/Hexer's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Hexer's Set")
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * Hood increases number of casts for each spell by 10% at a minimum of one additional spell cast and adds +1 [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith") and [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence").
