**Nameless Usurper** is an [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### DKS2 Nameless Usurper Information

  * Spawns only once, cannot be revived via [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic").
  * Killing [Licia of Lindelt](/Licia+of+Lindeldt "Dark Souls 2 Licia of Lindeldt") will prevent [Nameless Usurper](/Nameless+Usurper "Dark Souls 2 Nameless Usurper") invasions.
  * **NOTE:** Killing Licia (even through invasion) will end all progress towards the Gathering of Exiles achievement on the current playthrough.
  * It is most likely that **Licia** is **Nameless Usurper**.

### Nameless Usurper Encounters in DKS2

  * [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle"). From the **King's Gate** bonfire, continue through the castle until you reach the room with the cursed painting of [Nashandra](/Nashandra "Dark Souls 2 Nashandra"). She will spawn in the hallway just before the open air walkways.
  * [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt"). From the **Undead Ditch** bonfire, continue through the crypt until you reach the room filled with multiple Wall Warriors. She will spawn in the hallway to the left.
