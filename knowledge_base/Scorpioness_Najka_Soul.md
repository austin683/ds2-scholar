**Scorpioness Najka Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Scorpioness Najka Soul** , the soul of Scorpioness Najka. Born from the misdeeds of an ancient being, Najka started as a frail soul, which eventually succumbed to madness. Acquired upon defeating Scorpioness Najka. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Scorpioness Najka.  
> Najka was born of the misdeeds of an ancient being, a frail soul from the beginning, that soon succumbed to madness.  
> Use the special soul of Scorpioness Najka to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Dark Souls 2 Scorpioness Najka Soul Usage

  * Consuming gives 8,000 souls
  * Trade it and 1,500 souls to [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for [Soul Shower](/Soul+Shower#fex "Dark Souls 2 Soul Shower#fex")

### Scorpioness Najka Soul Location in DKS2

  * Received upon defeating [Scorpioness Najka](/Scorpioness+Najka "Dark Souls 2 Scorpioness Najka")
