**Lightning Damage** is one of the [Damage Types](/Damage+Types "Dark Souls 2 Damage Types") in **Dark Souls 2**. It is one of the seven main Damage Types and belongs to the Elemental Damage category. Damage Types are a major part of the calculation that determines how much damage is dealt in [Combat](/Combat "Dark Souls 2 Combat") and are affected by many factors including your equipment, spells and temporary buffs.

### Dark Souls 2 Lightning Damage Guide

**Lightning Damage** is a type of Elemental Damage dealt by both players and enemies alike. Lightning Damage is elemental damage that falls outside of [Magic Damage](/Magic+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Magic Damage"), [Fire Damage](/Fire+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Fire Damage"), and [Dark Damage](/Dark+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Dark Damage") types. Lightning Damage is often effective against most armored enemies and mimics.

Most [Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") can be upgraded via the Lightning Upgrade Path in order to imbue them with Lightning Damage properties. There are also Weapons that can be found which innately deal Lightning Damage. See the [Upgrades](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades") and individual weapon pages for details.

Most offensive [Miracles](/Miracles "Dark Souls 2 Miracles") deal Lightning Damage.
