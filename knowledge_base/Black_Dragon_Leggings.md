**Black Dragon Leggings** is a **legging** and a part of [Black Dragon Set](/Black+Dragon+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Black Dragon Leggings Description

  
"Eerily shaped helmet/armor/gauntlets/leggings spoken of in legend, crafted with the scales of the Black Dragon.

  
The Black Dragon was long considered a mere legend, but this proves its existence."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Achieve Rank 1 in the [Dragon Remnants](/Dragon+Remnants) covenant to obtain the [Dragon Head Stone](/Dragon+Head+Stone) for the helmet.
  * Achieve Rank 2 in the Dragon Remnants covenant to obtain the [Dragon Torso Stone](/Dragon+Torso+Stone) for the rest of the set.

### 

### Notes

  * Cannot be upgraded.
  * This set provides fair physical defense and excellent elemental defense at an incredibly low weight
  * Cannot be removed unless the wearer dies. [Ring of Life Protection](/Ring+of+Life+Protection) is recommended in this case if you want to purposefully unequip the set.
  * This set gives 0 poise when worn. The only poise you get is from your natural ADP and END
