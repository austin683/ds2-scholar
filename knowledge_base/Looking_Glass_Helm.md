_"Mask worn by the Looking Glass Knight. Made of metal, but have high lightning defense._

_Those who wish to serve the king loyal warriors must take the King's Passage and face the Looking Glass Knight._

_Those who fail the test are sacrificed by the merciless specular monstrosity."_

### Location

  * Bought from [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") after killing the [Looking Glass Knight](/Looking+Glass+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Knight").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Looking Glass Set](/Looking+Glass+Set "Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Set").
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
