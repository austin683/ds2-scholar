_"Black Helmet worn by the executioner of the Undead Purgatory. The spikes are designed to evoke fear._

_The executioner mercilessly put down countless Undead, but how could he know that it was never his own will, but that of the chariot horse?"_

### Location

  * The full [Executioner Set](/Executioner+Set "Dark Souls 2 Executioner Set") can be purchased from [Titchy Gren](/Gren "Dark Souls 2 Gren"), only AFTER entering [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Executioner Set](/Executioner+Set "Dark Souls 2 Executioner Set").
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite.](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite")
  * Worn by the character riding the Chariot in the boss fight.
