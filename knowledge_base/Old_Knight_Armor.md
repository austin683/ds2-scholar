_"Old undated Armor.__How old could this nearly-crumbling armor be?_

_Has extremely low durability._

_Sometimes, just as a thing falls to pieces, it unleashes its last flash of great power..."_

### Location

  * Dropped by old knights in [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide%27s Tower of Flame").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Old Knight Set](/Old+Knight+Set "Dark Souls 2 Old Knight Set").
  * Upgraded from [Titanite](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard").
  * The least durable heavy armor, extremely vulnerable to [King's Ultra Greatsword](/King%27s+Ultra+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 King%27s Ultra Greatsword").
