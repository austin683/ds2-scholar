_"Helm worn by Hollowed Royal Soldiers. Appears to be crafted with quality materials, but is highly degraded and close to falling apart. Wearing this will be of little use."_

### Location

  * Drops off of the [Hollow Soldiers](/Hollow+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Soldier") located in the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants").  

### Notes

  * Part of the [Hollow Soldier Set](/Hollow+Soldier+Set "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Soldier Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab").
