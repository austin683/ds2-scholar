**Undead Laborer (Hollow Prisoner)** is an enemy in Dark Souls 2.   

  

  

_Torch Hollow. As if a lifetime of toil wasn't enough, these workers continue their duties even in death. And this time, their job is to kill you. Probably as in life, these under-appreciated, down-trodden souls simply aren't equipped for the task -- they face you unarmed_
