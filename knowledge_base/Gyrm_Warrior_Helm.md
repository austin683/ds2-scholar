_"Warrior helm of the nomadic Gyrm. Excellent poise and defense, but very heavy._

_Most Gyrm descendants refuse contact with outsiders, and live with a sense of deep contempt for those who exiled them"_

### Location

  * Dropped by Gyrm warriors in [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Gyrm Warrior Set](/Gyrm+Warrior+Set)
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Ores).
  * While [Gyrm Warrior Set](/Gyrm+Warrior+Set) has the highest physical defense for headpiece (greathelm) and armor, [Drakekeeper Set](/Drakekeeper+Set) has has the highest physical defense for gauntlets, [Mastodon Set](/Mastodon+Set) has the highest physical defense for boot, among all armors under normal Titanite upgrading path.
