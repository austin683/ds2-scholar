**Endurance** is an [Attribute](/Stats "Dark Souls 2 Stats") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Endurance** determines your character's stamina, physical defence, and poise. Attributes can be increased by assigning points into them when [Leveling Up](/Level "Dark Souls 2 Level").  

### Endurance Information

  * **An attribute that determines your overall Stamina.**
  * **Raises stamina** : +2 stamina per point (0-20), +1 stamina per point (21-99).
  * **Raises physical defense** : (see Strength for physical defense details)
  * **Raises[Poise](/Poise#fex "Dark Souls 2 Poise#fex")**: (in conjunction with Adaptability, scales with the lower stat compared to Endurance)  

    * Note: Base stamina at 0 Endurance is set to 80
  * Slightly increases HP for each point: +2 HP (0-20), +1 HP (21-50), +0 HP (51-99)
  * The Cleric begins with the lowest Endurance at 3 and the Swordsman starts with the highest Endurance at 8.
  * The speed of your stamina regeneration is tied to your equip load, decreasing the higher your percentage of equipement worn is.
