### Varangian cuffs Description

_"Gauntlets worn by Varagians, that terrorized nearby seas. The coastline stretches far Drangleic. Beyond this Northern Sea is an unexplored continent, said to be the home of things inhuman."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Dropped in [No-Man's Wharf](/No-Man%27s+Wharf) by Varangian Bandits.
  * Dropped by [Varangian Sailor](/Varangian+Sailor).
