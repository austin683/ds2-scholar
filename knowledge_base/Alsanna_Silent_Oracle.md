**Alsanna, Silent Oracle** is an [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**.** Alsanna, Silent Oracle is a character in [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King"). She is the queen of [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce"), a [child of Dark](/Children+of+Dark "Dark Souls 2 Children of Dark"), and the bride of the Ivory King. **Alsanna** is known for her silence, which is said to be a result of a terrible burden she bears. Despite her silence, **Alsanna** is a powerful and enigmatic figure, with a deep connection to the land of Eleum Loyce.

### Alsanna, Silent Oracle Information in Dark Souls 2

  * Alsanna is an NPC guarding the entrance to [Old Chaos](/Old+Chaos "Dark Souls 2 Old Chaos") in the [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King") DLC. She is at the [Grand Cathedral](/Grand+Cathedral "Dark Souls 2 Grand Cathedral")
  * To reach her, you must have up to 50 [Loyce Souls](/Loyce+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Soul") in your possession and have killed the [Burnt Ivory King](/burnt+ivory+king "Dark Souls 2 burnt ivory king"). You will receive rewards for the souls you have, and the stairs leading to her will slowly clear of ice.
  * Obtain the [Soul of Alsanna, Silent Oracle](/Soul+of+Alsanna%2C+Silent+Oracle "Dark Souls 2 Soul of Alsanna%2C Silent Oracle") by having 50 [Loyce Souls](/Loyce+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Soul") or by killing her with ranged weaponry.
  * Talk to her to learn that she is a shard of soul of Manus, just like [Nashandra](/Nashandra "Dark Souls 2 Nashandra"), and has also found herself a powerful king to cling to.

### Alsanna, Silent Oracle Encounters in Dark Souls 2 

[Grand Cathedral](/Grand+Cathedral "Dark Souls 2 Grand Cathedral"). Found at the top of the staircases in the **Grand Cathedral**.

  3. **15** x Loyce Soul = [Loyce Armor](/Loyce+Armor "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Armor")
  4. **35** x Loyce Soul = [Loyce Helm](/Loyce+Helm "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Helm")
  5. **50** x Loyce Soul = [Soul of Alsanna, Silent Oracle](/Soul+of+Alsanna "Dark Souls 2 Soul of Alsanna"), unlocks [Ivory King Set](/Ivory+King+Set "Dark Souls 2 Ivory King Set") purchase at [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula").
