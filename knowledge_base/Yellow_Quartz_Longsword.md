**Yellow Quartz Longsword** is a **[Straight Sword](/Straight+Swords "Dark Souls 2 Straight Swords")** and one of the available **[Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons")** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**. **Yellow Quartz Longsword** can be purchased from [Chancellor Wellager](/Chancellor+Wellager "Dark Souls 2 Chancellor Wellager") for 8000 souls, located at the beginning of [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle"). While it features slightly lower attack power compared to the standard Longsword, the Yellow Quartz Longsword offers an extended attack range. It comes with lower durability but compensates with the highest counter damage among straight swords, on par with the [Fume Sword](/Fume+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Fume Sword").

### How to get Yellow Quartz Longsword in Dark Souls 2

  * Pre-order Bonus Weapon and part of Black Armor Edition.
  * Sold by [Chancellor Wellager](/Chancellor+Wellager "Dark Souls 2 Chancellor Wellager") for 8000 souls. He is at the very start of [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle"). He will be a transparent ghost so don't miss him. He is located on the landing up the stairs leading to the throne room.

### DS2 Yellow Quartz Longsword Hints and Tips

  * Has slightly worse attack power than the standard [Longsword](/Longsword "Dark Souls 2 Longsword") but with longer attack range, lower durability but also the highest counter damage of any straight sword in the game, tied with the [Fume Sword](/Fume+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Fume Sword").
  * Has a full suite of slashing attacks, similar to the [Broadsword](/Broadsword "Dark Souls 2 Broadsword"), [Varangian Sword](/Varangian+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Varangian Sword"), [Black Dragon Sword](/Black+Dragon+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Black Dragon Sword"), [Broken Straight Sword](/Broken+Straight+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Broken Straight Sword") and [Drakekeeper's Sword](/Drakekeeper%27s+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Drakekeeper%27s Sword").

Parameter Bonus: Strength, Dexterity, Magic, Fire, Lightning and Dark bonuses - The scaling multiplier applied to the [Attack: stat]. Scaling quality is from highest to lowest as follows: S/A/B/C/D/E.  
The higher the player's [Str, Dex, Mag, Fire, Lightning, Dark] stat, the higher the [Attack Bonus: Stat] is (found on the player status screen). The higher the scaling letter, the higher the percent multiplier applied to the [Attack: Stat].  
This resulting bonus damage is added to the base physical damage of the weapon and is shown in the equipment screen in blue numbers as a "+ X".  
Attack Type: Defines what kind of moveset the weapon has.  
Physical Damage falls under three categories: [Thrust](/Thrust+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Thrust Damage") (T), [Slash](/Slash+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Slash Damage") (Sl), [Strike](/Strike+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Strike Damage") (St).  
Elemental Damage Types: [Magic](/Magic+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Magic Damage"), [Fire](/Fire+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Fire Damage"), [Lightning](/Lightning+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Lightning Damage"), [Dark](/Dark+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Dark Damage").  
Auxiliary Effects: [Bleed](/Bleed "Dark Souls 2 Bleed"), [Poison](/Poison "Dark Souls 2 Poison"), [Petrification](/Petrification "Dark Souls 2 Petrification"), and [Curse](/Curse "Dark Souls 2 Curse").  
Stability: How well the player keeps stance after being hit.  
Durability: The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!".  
Weight: How much the item weighs when equipped.
