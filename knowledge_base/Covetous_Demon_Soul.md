**Covetous Demon Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Covetous Demon Soul** , the essence of the Covetous Demon, a glutton that devours all in its path. Consuming this soul bestows 6,000 Souls. Trade it with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") to obtain the [Bone Scythe](/Bone+Scythe "Dark Souls 2 Bone Scythe"). You can acquire this soul by defeating the Covetous Demon in Earthen Peak. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Covetous Demon, that devours all things.  
> Eating is an expression of desire. There once was a man whose deep affections were unrequited. He transformed into the Covetous Demon, which only made him lonelier than before.  
> Use the special soul of the Covetous Demon to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Covetous Demon Soul Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Consuming gives 6000 Souls.
  * Trade with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for [Bone Scythe](/Bone+Scythe#fex "Dark Souls 2 Bone Scythe#fex").

### DKS2 Covetous Demon Soul Location

  * Obtained by defeating the [Covetous Demon](/Covetous+Demon "Dark Souls 2 Covetous Demon") in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak")
