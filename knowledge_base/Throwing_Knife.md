**Throwing Knife** is a type of [Projectile](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2).****Throwing Knife** , employed as a projectile. This straightforward weapon is easily wielded. While its range is confined, and it inflicts minimal damage, it remains a valuable tool in the absence of other ranged weapons. [Projectiles](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") are offensive items that are usually thrown at hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") to inflict a small amount of damage.

> Used as a projectile. This simple weapon is easily utilized. Its range is limited, and it deals very little damage, but even still, it is a valuable tool in the absence of other ranged weapons.

### Throwing Knife Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Equip in item slots and use to throw at enemies.
  * Can be used locked on or locked off. Can be manually aimed with [Binoculars](/Binoculars "Dark Souls 2 Binoculars").
  * Dexterity Scaling is equivalent to A; i.e. the weapon has 50% scaling for a DEX attack bonus. 
  * Great for a surprise kill when the opponent is very low on health in PVP.

### Dark Souls 2 Throwing Knife Location

  * Sold by [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia") 100 Souls (Unlimited Stock) in Cardinal Tower. As you progress through the game she will relocate herself in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula").
