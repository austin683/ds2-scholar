**Soul of Aava, the King's Pet** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of Aava, the King's Pet** , embodying the essence of Aava, the guardian appointed by the Ivory King. Among the seven beasts under the Ivory King's command, Aava was entrusted with the protection of the monarch's cherished offspring of Dark. Obtainable by vanquishing [Aava, The King's Pet](/Aava,+The+King's+Pet "Dark Souls 2 Aava, The King's Pet"), in [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of Aava, who served the Ivory King.   
> Each of the Ivory King's seven beasts was conferred a specific duty. Aava's was the guardianship of the King's beloved child of Dark.   
> The wondrous soul of this beast can be used to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Soul of Aava, the King's Pet Usage in DKS2

  * Trade it along with 8,000 souls for the [Ivory Straight Sword](/Ivory+Straight+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Ivory Straight Sword") from [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex")
  * Use it for 16,000 souls

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of Aava, the King's Pet Location

  * Defeat [Aava, The King's Pet](/Aava%2C+the+King%27s+Pet "Dark Souls 2 Aava%2C the King%27s Pet") at [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce")
