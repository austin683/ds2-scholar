**Ring of Resistance** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Ring of Resistance** , featuring four blessed gems, enhances resistance against poison, bleeding, petrification, and curses. Crafted in the lost land of Olaphis, this creation instilled such fear that its sorcerer creator became widely dreade. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A protective ring adorned with four blessed gems. Increases resistance to poison, bleeding, petrification, and curses. A creation of the lost land of Olaphis. The sorcerer who created this was so powerful that he became widely feared

### Dark Souls 2 Ring of Resistance Effects

**Durability** : 110

**Weight** :

  * +0 = 0.8
  * +1 = 1.5

**Effect:**

  * Increases Poison/Toxic/Bleed/Petrify/Curse RES by 120
  * +1 Increases Poison/Toxic/Bleed/Petrify/Curse RES by 200

### Dark Souls 2 Ring of Resistance Locations

  * Regular: Dropped by [Cromwell the Pardoner](/Cromwell+the+Pardoner "Dark Souls 2 Cromwell the Pardoner")
  * Regular: Given to you by [Cromwell the Pardoner](/Cromwell+the+Pardoner "Dark Souls 2 Cromwell the Pardoner"). (After having 35 Faith.)
  * Rng +1: Treasure in[Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce"). By the invisible phantoms who use ballistas in the frozen stairs. Look for a corpse with the ring.

### DS2 Ring of Resistance Tips and Notes

  * Getting 30 Faith and equipping a [Ring of Prayer](/Ring+of+Prayer "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Prayer") (Increases Faith by 5) will **NOT** get you the Ring of Resistance.
  * Ring of Resistance+1 is a **DLC Item** : [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King").
