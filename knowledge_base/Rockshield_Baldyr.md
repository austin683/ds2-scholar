**Rockshield Baldyr** is an [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") in Dark Souls**2**

### Encounters

[Shulva, Sanctum City](/Shulva+-+Sanctum+City "Dark Souls 2 Shulva - Sanctum City"). On the bridge leading to the **Priestess Chamber** bonfire. 

### Notes

  * Wears the Steel Set
  * Wields the Winged Spear and Stone Parma
  * Throws a Lloyd's Talisman at you at the start of the invasion
  * Has extremely high poise

### Drops

Winged Spear + 5 (about 10% chance)
