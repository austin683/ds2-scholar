**Red Tearstone Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). The **Red Tearstone Ring** , adorned with a precious tearstone. Activates when the wearer faces peril, momentarily enhancing their physical attack strength. Caitha, the goddess of tears, grieves for the unjustly departed, shedding tears as crimson as blood. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A ring set with a rare tearstone. Reacts when the wearer is in danger, temporarily increasing its wearer's physical attack power. Caitha, goddess of tears, mourns the undeserving dead, shedding tears as red as blood. It is said that the stone set in this ring is one such tear

### Dark Souls 2 Red Tearstone Ring Effects

**Durability** : 110

**Weight** :

  * 1.5

**Effect:**

  * Boosts physical attack by 20% when HP is below 30%
  * (according to tests, this value is exactly damage x 1,255)

### Red Tearstone Ring Location in Dark Souls 2

  * At the tree on a muddy island between [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods") and [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s Keep") passage.

### DS2 Red Tearstone Ring Tips and Notes

  * As of Calibration 1.10 in SOTFS, this ring no longer boosts magic damage of any type.
  * Poison yourself with the sanctum mace and use a moss clump as soon as the effect activates, one of the fastest and safest methods to activate it without relying on enemies.
