**Crimson Water** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Crimson Water** , its origin shrouded in mystery, provides a substantial restoration of both HP and spell uses. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Crimson water of unknown origin.  
> Greatly restores HP and spell uses.  
> Those who have experienced the powerful effect of this dimly lucent red water care not that its very origins are completely unknown.

### Dark Souls 2 Crimson Water Usage

  * Restores 1500 HP and 70% of spell uses

### Crimson Water Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Treasure in a chest across a gap at the top balcony at [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak").
  * x5 Sold by [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan "Dark Souls 2 Royal Sorcerer Navlaan") for 4,200 souls each.
  * Treasure found on a corpse in Memory of Vammar.

###  Crimson Water Drops from:

  * Drops from [Amana Priestess](/Amana+Priestess "Dark Souls 2 Amana Priestess") in Shrine of Amana (Common).
  * Drops from [White hollow Mage](/White+Hollow+Mage "Dark Souls 2 White Hollow Mage") in Brightstone Cove.
  * Drops from [Sanctum Priestess](/Sanctum+Priestess "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Priestess") in Sunken King DLC.
  * Possible drop from trading Smooth & Silky Stone with the Crows.
