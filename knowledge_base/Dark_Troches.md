**Dark Troches** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Troches with an inexplicably pungent odor. Temporarily boosts dark defense.  
> Verbal histories often mention Saint Elizabeth; her uniquely concocted medicines and potions are still widely used today.

### Dark Troche Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Boosts dark DEF by 150 for 90 seconds

### Dark Souls 2 Dark Troche Location

  * Sold by [Cromwell the Pardoner](/Cromwell+the+Pardoner "Dark Souls 2 Cromwell the Pardoner") for 1200 souls.
  * 1x found on a corpse in the lower section of [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide%27s Tower of Flame"), behind the petrified enemy and after the [Old Knight](/Old+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Old Knight").
