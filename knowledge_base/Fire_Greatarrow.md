**Fire Greatarrow** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Fire Greatarrows** , powerful projectiles designed for Greatbows and infused with the essence of fire. Enhanced with Charcoal Pine Resin, these arrows are highly effective against adversaries susceptible to fire, including Undead and beasts. Exclusively compatible with Greatbows, they deliver Thrust-type damage with a bonus—a minor detonation upon impact akin to a Firebomb, dealing additional splash Fire damage to nearby opponents. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Greatarrows imbued with fire. Tipped with Charcoal Pine Resin. Weapons fortified with fire are effective against against foes vulnerable to fire, such  
> as Undead or beasts.* If your require magic but have learned none yourself, these will serve you well.

### Fire Greatarrow Usage in DKS2

  * Can only be used with Greatbows.
  * Deals Thrust-type damage to a single target. It will also unleash a minor detonation similiar to a Firebomb upon imapct, dealing minor splash Fire damage to nearby opponents.

### Dark Souls 2 Fire Greatarrow Location

  * Sold by [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan "Dark Souls 2 Royal Sorcerer Navlaan") 180 each ∞ supply (Only if you free him then talk to him while human, or talking to him while dead after having completed his assassination contracts)
  * 10 in a trapped chest in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle")
