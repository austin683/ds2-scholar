**Agdayne's Black Kilt** is a **Legging** and a part of [Agdayne's Set](/Agdayne's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Agdayne's Set") in **Dark Souls 2**

### Agdayne's Black Kilt Description

"Kilt worn by Agdayne of the Undead Crypt.

Life itself is suffering; or karma,  
as some have called it.

The embrace of death awaits all things,  
But does death mean an end to suffering?_"_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Gifted by [Grave Warden Agdayne](/Grave+Warden+Agdayne "Dark Souls 2 Grave Warden Agdayne") after obtaining the [King's Ring](/King's+Ring "Dark Souls 2 King's Ring") from the [Vendrick's](/Vendrick "Dark Souls 2 Vendrick") chamber.
  * Sold by [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia") after you killed [Grave Warden Agdayne](/Grave+Warden+Agdayne "Dark Souls 2 Grave Warden Agdayne").
