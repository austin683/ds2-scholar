**Ring of War** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in **Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)**. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc. [Unused Items](/Unused+Items "Dark Souls 2 Unused Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/About+Dark+Souls+2 "About Dark Souls 2") (DKS2) are Items listed in the game code, but not available in-game.

> An iron ring granted to vanguard soldiers. Increases physical defense.

Though the ring's properties are a great boon to warriors, few were happy to accept the ring, as it also meant that the wearer would be sent out to die on the front lines."

### Ring of War Effects

  * Increases physical defense.

### Ring of War Location

  * Unavailable 

### Notes

  * Removed from the final version of the game.
  * Has the same icon as the [Ivory Warrior Ring](/Ivory+Warrior+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Ivory Warrior Ring")
