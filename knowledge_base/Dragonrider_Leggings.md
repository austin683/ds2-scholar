**Dragonrider Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Dragonrider Set](/Dragonrider+Set "Dark Souls 2 Dragonrider Set") in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Dragonrider Leggings Description

_"Kilt worn by Agdayne of the Undead Crypt._

_Life itself is suffering; or karma,__as some have called it._

_The embrace of death awaits all things,__But does death mean an end to suffering?"_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Sold by [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") after defeating the [Dragonriders](/DragonRider "Dark Souls 2 DragonRider") in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle"). (NG+)
