**Knight Phantom** is an [enemy](/enemies "Dark Souls 2 enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Knight Phantom Information in Dark Souls 2

_"A common knight of old, still fighting in defense of the realm. While you'd normally expect such a heavy warrior to sport a shield, he dual wields a Heide Knight Sword and a Dagger instead.__"_

### Dark Souls 2 Knight Phantom Location

  * [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants")

### DKS2 Drops Knight Phantom

  * [Knight Set](/Knight+Set "Dark Souls 2 Knight Set") +5
  * [Heide Knight Sword](/Heide+Knight+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Heide Knight Sword")
  * Bleed [Dagger](/Dagger "Dark Souls 2 Dagger") +5
  * NG+ 3,000 Souls, NG+7: 6,000 Souls.
