**Monastery Skirt** is a **Legging** and a part of [Imported Set](/Imported+Set) in Dark Souls 2.

### Monastery Skirt Description

"A traditionally sacred skirt crafted at Lindelt Monastery. Made of lightweight, flowing white fabric.

The ceremonial design suggests that they revered whatever it was they watched over."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Pieces of the armor can be dropped from a Black phantom found behind a painting in [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep) (NG+).

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
  * Is the armor set of the Painting Guardians from Dark Souls 1.
  * Farming advice: bring a lot of lightning and poison arrows for the two trolls before the boss entrance. They don't aggro if you have a little bit of distance.
