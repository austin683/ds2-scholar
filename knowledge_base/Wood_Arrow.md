**Wood Arrow** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Wood Arrows** , affordable wooden projectiles. Easily obtained but lack significant threat. Bows offer a safe means to engage enemies from a distance, contingent upon equipping a compatible arrow type. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Inexpensive wooden arrows. Easily acquired, but not very threatening. Bows are excellent tools for attacking foes from a safe distance, but require  
> the equipping of some type of arrow.

### Wood Arrow Usage in DKS2

  * Bow ammunition
  * 50 Physical ATK

### Dark Souls 2 Wood Arrow Location

  * (Majula) [Blacksmith Lenigrast](/Blacksmith+Lenigrast "Dark Souls 2 Blacksmith Lenigrast") sells an unlimited quantity at (10) souls each.
  * (Lost Bastille) [Steady Hand McDuff](/Steady+Hand+McDuff "Dark Souls 2 Steady Hand McDuff") sells an unlimited quantity at (10) souls each.
