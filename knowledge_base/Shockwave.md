**Shockwave** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Shockwave** , a spell devoid of direct harm, it releases a substantial shockwave to topple foes, leaving them vulnerable to attacks. Deemed by some as mere child's play, yet for a creative caster, it stands as a lethal tool. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "Does not inflict damage, but unleashes a large shockwave, toppling opponents.  
>   
> Intended to stun opponents and make them vulnerable to attack. Some call this child's play, but for an innovative caster, it is a deadly tool."

### Dark Souls 2 Shockwave Acquired From

  * [Carhillion of the Fold](/Carhillion+of+the+Fold "Dark Souls 2 Carhillion of the Fold") for 1800 souls.

### Shockwave Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2
