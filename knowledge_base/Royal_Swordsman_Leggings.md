**Royal Swordsman Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Royal Swordsman Set](/Royal+Swordsman+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Royal Swordsman Leggings Description

"Armor of a royal swordsman. Its shape provides defense while allowing great mobility. Stripped of ornamentation, this particular helm/armor is designed strictly for battle.  
King Vendrick supplied his bravest men with the best armor available to face the great giants, but very few returned alive."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Sold by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer) in [Majula](/Majula)

### Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite)
  * Must have spent 1000+ souls for the Royal Soldier set to appear in his inventory
