## **Brightstone Cove Tseldora**

**Brightstone Cove Tseldora** is a [Location](/Locations "Dark Souls 2 Locations") in **Dark Souls 2**. It is a large area full of dangerous enemies consisting of upper, Royal Army Campsite area and lower, Brightstone Cove Tseldora itself. It has 3 bonfires, one of which is locked away, and two bosses.

### Notes

  * In the village area, lure the boar all the way through the level to the room across the thorn bridge, into the spider falling room. Have it eat the mushroom in that room, and the [Pickaxe](/Pickaxe "Dark Souls 2 Pickaxe") will appear.
  * On NG+ giant Boars appear in the royal camp, and [The Duke's Dear Freja](/The+Duke%27s+Dear+Freja "Dark Souls 2 The Duke%27s Dear Freja") may appear on the cliff just before the first cavern.
  * In NG+ and upwards, when you are in the narrow corridor after the first cliff with the boulders, and preceded by an earthquake, [The Duke's Dear Freja ](/The+Duke%27s+Dear+Freja "Dark Souls 2 The Duke%27s Dear Freja")will appear. Any damage you deal to her at this stage will carry on to the "real" boss fight later on.
  * The 20x [Heavy Bolt](/Heavy+Bolt "Dark Souls 2 Heavy Bolt") trap chest is replaced by 20x [Magic Bol](/Magic+Bolt "Dark Souls 2 Magic Bolt") on NG+. 
    * In NG+, in the illusionary wall in the same house, you will still be able to obtain the same loot as in NG, however you will have to fight a Dark Spirit to do so.

### Brightstone Cove Tseldora Walkthrough

**NOTE:** _Ensure you aren't in the Company of Champions when you decide to head to the boss, as you need to summon an NPC here to complete their questline._

### Obtaining the Wisest Staff

Entering from [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros"), you will find yourself in a room with a broken fountain and several jars. One of the jars to the left contains a corpse with a [Radiant Lifegem](/Radiant+Lifegem "Dark Souls 2 Radiant Lifegem"). Exiting the room will bring you to an open area with the Royal Army Campsite Bonfire right in front of you. In this area are several [Hollow Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant"), [Hollow Falconers](/Falconer "Dark Souls 2 Falconer"), and larger versions of the [Undead Devourers](/Undead+Devourer "Dark Souls 2 Undead Devourer") (Pigs) that you might remember from [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula"). While they may not appear to be too much of a threat, the [Hollow Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant") have[ Dark](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades")-enchanted weapons, which make them not only swing pretty hard, but also cause curse build-up!

From the**Bonfire** , continue to the left and dispatch the first of many [Hollow Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant"), standing by 4 corpses with a total loot of: 3x [Soul Of a Nameless Soldier](/Soul+Of+a+Nameless+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Soul Of a Nameless Soldier"), 1x [Large Soul Of a Proud Knight](/Large+Soul+Of+a+Proud+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Large Soul Of a Proud Knight"). While still using the tents and structures as cover from the archer enemies -feel free to take them out with ranged attacks- on the upper levels, proceed right, deeper into the camp to clear the ground floor of [Falconers](/Falconer "Dark Souls 2 Falconer") and [Pigs](/Undead+Devourer "Dark Souls 2 Undead Devourer"). Once you have, attack the scaffolding with a corpse on it to drop the loot and grab the 15x [Lacerating Arrows](/Lacerating+Arrow "Dark Souls 2 Lacerating Arrow"). Look for the ruined building and break the urns beside it to reveal a corpse holding a [Large Soul Of a Nameless Soldier](/Large+Soul+Of+a+Nameless+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Large Soul Of a Nameless Soldier"). The doorway of this building is blocked off so we're heading to the well on the right, Instead. If you can't find it, it's beneath the ramp holding one of the archers. Loot the corpse hanging over the well for a [Titanite Shard](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard"), then head up the ramp to run off it, and down the well. At the bottom of the well is a corpse holding 3x [Holy Water Urn](/Holy+Water+Urn "Dark Souls 2 Holy Water Urn") and another [Pig](/Undead+Devourer "Dark Souls 2 Undead Devourer"). Continue on and up the ladder to enter the building with the entrance that was blocked by rubble. Inside is a [Mimic](/Mimic "Dark Souls 2 Mimic") that drops the [Dark Leggings](/Dark+Armor "Dark Souls 2 Dark Armor"), and the best sorcery staff in the game, the [Staff of Wisdom](/Staff+of+Wisdom "Dark Souls 2 Staff of Wisdom"). Climb up the rubble to exit the house.

### Handicapped Mini-Boss

From here, head back to the bonfire and to the right of it this time, to arrive at the cliff edge. Follow the path down until you see a ladder. BEWARE as there is a trap that sets a huge rolling boulder down the platform above, and if it connects, will knock you down to your death. Use ranged attacks to kill the [Peasant](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant") at the bottom of the ladder then make a beeline for it, picking up the [Soul Of a Nameless Soldier](/Soul+Of+a+Nameless+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Soul Of a Nameless Soldier") before you rush up. There's a total of three [Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant") and two boulder traps -one has almost definitely been set off, so set the other off by breaking its wooden support, to collect a [Soul Of a Brave Warrior](/Soul+Of+a+Brave+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Soul Of a Brave Warrior"). Head back down and continue along the path ahead.

Hanging off the ledge on the right, is a corpse holding [Large Titanite Shard](/Large+Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Large Titanite Shard"). Continue on to find more[ Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant") guarding a large door. Once inside, head down past the first large room and into the second, which has multiple [Ducal Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") lying in wait, to ambush you. Kill them using a mix of range and melee attacks, then loot the Trap Chest for 20x [Heavy Bolt](/Heavy+Bolt "Dark Souls 2 Heavy Bolt"). Continue through the iron door ahead to reach a room with a Boss Fog Gate. Breaking the urns here will grant you a [Human Effigy](/Human+Effigy "Dark Souls 2 Human Effigy"). Before jumping in to progress, head straight from the door you just entered, to find an Illusory Wall -press the "use" button on your respective console to uncover it. Head up the stairs and down the hall (be carefull of the [Ducal Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") hanging from the walls) to find a Water Urn and a corpse holding [Priestess Set](/Priestess+Set "Dark Souls 2 Priestess Set"). You can head back to the boss fog now, but there's one important link in a questline worth finishing , and extremely easy to miss or forget.

#### Summoning Benhart of Jugo

This is only **one of four** possible summons to successfully complete Benhart's questline, and you and everyone in Drangleic wants that [shiny, shiny sword](/BlueMoon+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 BlueMoon Greatsword") of his. Return back up to the Royal Army Campsite. Head **inside the tent near the building that had the mimic in it** to find the summon sign for [Benhart of Jugo](/Benhart+of+Jugo "Dark Souls 2 Benhart of Jugo").

BOSS FIGHT: [Prowling Magus and Congregation](/Prowling+Magus+and+Congregation "Dark Souls 2 Prowling Magus and Congregation")

### Pate or Creighton 

Killing this boss nets you a nifty 7,000 souls and a [Titanite Slab](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab"). Go through the door ahead and face immediately left to find a ladder in the alcove of the wall. Head up to reach NPC [Cromwell The Pardoner](/Cromwell+the+Pardoner "Dark Souls 2 Cromwell the Pardoner"), 3 more (non-aggresive) [Ducal Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") and multiple corpses to plunder a total of 1x [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic"), 1x [Cleric's Small Shield](/Cleric%27s+Small+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Cleric%27s Small Shield"), 3x [Fading Soul](/Fading+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Fading Soul"), and 1x [Skeptic's Spice](/Skeptic%27s+Spice "Dark Souls 2 Skeptic%27s Spice"). Speaking to him, if your Faith is above 35, will grant you a [Ring of Resistance](/Ring+of+Resistance "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Resistance"). When you are ready, head back down the ladder. If you can do high range damage, you can move towards the cliff edge and try to snipe [Crystal Lizard](/crystal+lizard "Dark Souls 2 crystal lizard") on the ledge below, but do not jump down to grab the loot, as you will be there very soon anyway.

Head back to the boss door but this time enter the cave via the entrance on the right. Turn left before the ladder to access the **Chapel Threshold Bonfire**. Head down and look under the stairs to find [Ducal Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider"), and a corpse holding a [Soul Of A Proud Knight](/Soul+Of+A+Proud+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Soul Of A Proud Knight"). There will also be another passive [Ducal Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") on the wall; this could possibly be an indicator of things to come. The room through the doorway, holds some urns containing 2x[ Wilted Dusk Herb](/Wilted+Dusk+Herb "Dark Souls 2 Wilted Dusk Herb"), but most importantly, an ambush of [Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") as soon as you enter. Move forward to a [Hollow Mage](/Hollow+Mage "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Mage") facing away from you, on the ledge, a very easy backstab target.

Take a zip-line which will bring you to the [Crystal Lizard](/Crystal+Lizard "Dark Souls 2 Crystal Lizard") mentioned before. Head left towards the jars. Inside the building will be [Mild Mannered Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate") and [Creighton Of Mirrah](/Creighton+of+Mirrah "Dark Souls 2 Creighton of Mirrah") battling it out (if you have fully followed their storyline thus far). Choose an NPC to side with and attack the other:

  * Loot the dead NPC and talk to the living NPC after the battle, exhausting their dialogue to receive the [Tseldora Den Key](/Tseldora+Den+Key "Dark Souls 2 Tseldora Den Key").
  * If you sided with [Creighton](/Creighton+of+Mirrah "Dark Souls 2 Creighton of Mirrah"), you'll also receive his armor set. 
    * Unless you've already collected his set from [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak"), killing [Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate") will also grant you his armor set.
  * While it may feel scummy, it is generally a good idea to also kill whoever you sided with, because (as you probably already know) they are both scumbags and wish you death.

Break the jars in this room to reveal a corpse holding 3x [Charcoal Pine Resin](/Charcoal+Pine+Resin "Dark Souls 2 Charcoal Pine Resin"). There is a small room behind half-broken wooden door which does not contain any loot, however houses a [Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") that emerges from a hole in the wall when the bookshelf hiding it, is destroyed. Leave this small room and head into the far left corner of the bigger room, into the bigger hole in the wall. Go through the first door and to find a corpse holding a [Torch](/Torch "Dark Souls 2 Torch"), on the right. Past the second door is another [Crystal Lizard](/Crystal+Lizard "Dark Souls 2 Crystal Lizard"), killing it yields 2x [Titanite Chunk](/Titanite+Chunk "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Chunk") and a [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite"). On the left side of the ledge, kill the [Spider Drone](/Spider+Drone "Dark Souls 2 Spider Drone") sitting there and the [Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") hanging on wall. From here you can notice another ledge down below (there is a jar on it), drop down carefully. This ledge is circular so it does not matter which way to go, just pay attention to the [Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") on the wall. You will end up in the room with two more [Spider Drones](/Spider+Drone "Dark Souls 2 Spider Drone") and a [Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider"). Smash a bookshelf to reveal the lever which will lower a platform above. To reach it, head back up the small stairs and make a carefully aimed jump towards it. In the chest is a [Southern Ritual Band](/Southern+Ritual+Band "Dark Souls 2 Southern Ritual Band") [ +1](/Southern+Ritual+Band "Dark Souls 2 Southern Ritual Band"). From here the best things to do next is to simply [Homeward Bone](/Homeward+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone") and head back across the zip-line.

### An Orn Friend

Next three parts are **optional** , which can be done now, and will require [Homeward Bone](/Homeward+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone") usage, or skip and return after second bonfire is opened. You will always start from the ledge outside the house where [Mild Mannered Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate") and [Creighton Of Mirrah](/Creighton+of+Mirrah "Dark Souls 2 Creighton of Mirrah") fought (zip-line brings you there), this place will be referred to as the **starting point**.

  1. Right beside the entrance to [Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate")'s house, across the gap, you can see two stone platforms. Sprint to the first platform (you do not have to jump) and then drop down to the second. Loot a [Titanite Chunk](/Titanite+Chunk "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Chunk") and a [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite") from the corpse. [Homeward Bone](/Homeward+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone") to the starting point.
  2. From the center of the ledge jump across the gap to the corpse in a small building on a wall (at first glance it will look like distance is too big for a jump but in reality its not). Grab the [Large Titanite Shard](/Large+Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Large Titanite Shard"). [Homeward Bone](/Homeward+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone") to the starting point.
  3. Head left past the zip-line onto the wooden platform and drop down to reach a corpse holding a [Large Titanite Shard](/Large+Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Large Titanite Shard"). You need to drop down again, now onto the platform where a [Hollow Mage](/Hollow+Mage "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Mage") resides, but this time it requires more precision, take it slow. Grab the [Estus Flask Shard](/Estus+Flask+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Estus Flask Shard") from the corpse. [Homeward Bone](/Homeward+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone") **but now do not take the zip-line**.

Standing where the zip-line starts, turn right and drop down to the ledge below. Enter the doorway to reach a room with a big hole in the floor and two [Hollow Mages](/Hollow+Mage "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Mage") standing near it. Notice all the [Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") in the hole and on the ceiling. It is recommended to kill them all now or they will ambush you later. Go through the small doorway ahead to reach a ladder. Climb down to reach a room with the third **Bonfire** and a door. Head out the door to reach the big open area with a sand pit in the center. There is a chance [Bowman Guthry](/Bowman+Guthry "Dark Souls 2 Bowman Guthry") will invade you at this point.

Before going any further its a good idea to wipe out all enemies in this area: a [Hollow Mage](/Hollow+Mage "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Mage") in front, several [Ducal Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") to your right, a hidden [Basilisk](/Basilisk "Dark Souls 2 Basilisk"), a red [Red Crystal Lizard](/Red+Crystal+Lizard "Dark Souls 2 Red Crystal Lizard") and a [Hollow Mage](/Hollow+Mage "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Mage") to your left. To your right, you'll find a corpse holding a [Petrified Dragon Bone](/Petrified+Dragon+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Petrified Dragon Bone"), grab it and go back, then proceed into the first door. Inside you'll find an old friend if you freed her from the [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods"), [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex"). In her room, you'll find jars and bookshelves to the left. Break them to find corpses holding a [Flame Butterfly](/Flame+Butterfly "Dark Souls 2 Flame Butterfly") and a [Pharros' Lockstone](/Pharros%27+Lockstone "Dark Souls 2 Pharros%27 Lockstone"). Head back outside and carefully go around the sand pit. To your left, you will notice another door, open it to enter a room with several [Ducal Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") and a [Spider Drone](/Spider+Drone "Dark Souls 2 Spider Drone"). Near the bracier, there is [Bashful Ray](/Bashful+Ray "Dark Souls 2 Bashful Ray")'s summon sign , behind the bookshelves ahead is a room with another [Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") and a chest containing the [Crescent Axe](/Crescent+Axe "Dark Souls 2 Crescent Axe") and [Soul Vortex](/Soul+Vortex "Dark Souls 2 Soul Vortex"). Back in the main room, up the small hill, is a ledge that allows you to drop down to a small room with a corpse holding a [Titanite Chunk](/Titanite+Chunk "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Chunk") on the left, and a door to the right (note that once you drop down this ledge, the only way to return to the earlier part of the level is to do an angled jump back to the ledge).

### Trip to Freja

Through the door is a large courtyard, ahead is a [Basilisk](/Basilisk "Dark Souls 2 Basilisk") and another [Spider Drone](/Spider+Drone "Dark Souls 2 Spider Drone"). Kill them and then head through the half broken wooden door ahead. Kill the [Ducal Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") inside then head up the stairs. In on of the Jars is a corpse holding 2x [Torches](/Torch "Dark Souls 2 Torch") and open the door to find a corpse with a [Large Titanite Shard](/Large+Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Large Titanite Shard"). Drop down back to courtyard. Ignore the obvious bridge for now and head towards the last closed door in this area. Trying to open it gives you the message "Too heavy to open". Smash that piece of kindling down with ranged attacks or else an un-dodgable sand burst will hit you.

Go through the door and open the other door ahead with your **Tseldora Den Key** you received from either of the scumbags from earlier. Two chests are found in this room. The wooden one to the left is a **Trap Chest** containing 10x [Rusted Coin](/Rusted+Coin "Dark Souls 2 Rusted Coin"), and the metal Chest contains the [Engraved Gauntlets](/Engraved+Gauntlets "Dark Souls 2 Engraved Gauntlets") (upon opening the trap chest, you'll hear the dialogue between [Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate") and [Creighton](/Creighton+of+Mirrah "Dark Souls 2 Creighton of Mirrah"). However it does not occur if you killed both of them.) Collect them and head outside. Go through the archway to the bridge. You will notice two corpses to the right, lying in the spikes. Try to kill all the Hollows nearby before dropping from the bridge. Spikes will constantly do damage while you are moving so prepare to use healing items. Loot a [Titanite Shard](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard"), 3x [Titanite Chunk](/Titanite+Chunk "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Chunk"), and a [Titanite Slab](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab") from the first corpse, and 3x [Torch](/Torch "Dark Souls 2 Torch") from the second one.

Now head towards the building, open the door and look up to find multiple [Ducal Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") are on the ceiling, so use ranged attacks or move ahead and let them drop down. To the right is a locked iron door, you need the **Brightstone Key** obtained after the boss battle to open it. To the left are Urns containing 15x [Poison Arrows](/Poison+Arrow "Dark Souls 2 Poison Arrow"), on the right, behind one of the benches, is a corpse containing a [Soul of a Hero](/Soul+of+a+Hero "Dark Souls 2 Soul of a Hero") and a [Gold Pine Resin](/Gold+Pine+Resin "Dark Souls 2 Gold Pine Resin"). Here you can also summon [Manhunter O'Harrah](/Manhunter+O'Harrah "Dark Souls 2 Manhunter O'Harrah") for the upcoming boss fight. On the far wall are two doors, each with it's own lever. The door on the right holds an unnamed **Red Phantom** ,**** and a corpse with 3x [Homeward Bone](/Homeward+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone"). The door on the left holds a [Spider Drone](/Spider+Drone "Dark Souls 2 Spider Drone") and a ladder heading up. Climb it to reach **Freja's Lair**.

In this room, you'll find many spider webs which serve as bridges, which as you step on, will trigger [Ducal Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") to engage you. This area is also very dark, so be sure to bring a lit Torch with you. Drop down from the ledge and move left, hugging the wall. Move down the first web and reach the far side. Behind the first pillar is a corpse holding a [Torch](/Torch "Dark Souls 2 Torch"). Careful, aa s [Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") will drop down near here. Move forward towards the Jars on the far side. In them is a corpse holding [Pharros' Lockstone ](/Pharros%27+Lockstone "Dark Souls 2 Pharros%27 Lockstone"). Move across the web to the left. At the end, drop to the large web to your left and follow it down. Halfway down is a corpse holding [Radiant Lifegem](/Radiant+Lifegem "Dark Souls 2 Radiant Lifegem"). Go down to ground level and kill any spiders that attack. To your left is a small but wide web leading up to a platform above. On the far side, you'll notice a corpse holding a [Twilight Herb](/Twilight+Herb "Dark Souls 2 Twilight Herb"). Return to the ground level and underneath the large web is a corpse holding a [Simpleton's Spice](/Simpleton%27s+Spice "Dark Souls 2 Simpleton%27s Spice"). From here, head to the far right side of the room and behind the last pillar is a corpse holding 5x [Corrosive Urn](/Corrosive+Urn "Dark Souls 2 Corrosive Urn"). As you may have noticed by now, there is a large Fog Door ahead; what you may not have noticed however, is the summon sign for [Ashen Knight Boyd](/Ashen+Knight+Boyd "Dark Souls 2 Ashen Knight Boyd") behind one of the pillars.

BOSS FIGHT: [The Duke's Dear Freja](/The+Duke%27s+Dear+Freja "Dark Souls 2 The Duke%27s Dear Freja")

### Death of Freja

Defeating her, means you can move towards the dragon and pick up the great soul. Head through the cave to the right to reach the [Lord's Private Chamber](/Lord's+Private+Chamber "Dark Souls 2 Lord's Private Chamber") where you will find **Duke of Tseldora** , back turned to the player while focusing on a table in front of him. Defeat the hollowed Duke to receive the [Brightstone Key](/Brightstone+Key "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Key"), a [Fragrant Branch Of Yore](/Fragrant+Branch+Of+Yore "Dark Souls 2 Fragrant Branch Of Yore") and the [Dark Quartz Ring +1](/Dark+Quartz+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Dark Quartz Ring"). Down the stairs to the right is a **Primal Bonfire** \- light it. If this is your fourth primal bonfire you've accessed, which means [Aldia](/Aldia,+scholar+of+the+First+Sin "Dark Souls 2 Aldia, scholar of the First Sin") will appear. Keep in mind that his emerging knocks you back and interrupts bonfire lighting, so after you exhaust his dialog, don't forget to actually light the bonfire. Note: Don't forget to head back to [Manscorpion Tark](/Manscorpion+Tark "Dark Souls 2 Manscorpion Tark") to get the [Black Scorpion Stinger](/Black+Scorpion+Stinger "Dark Souls 2 Black Scorpion Stinger").

Now its time to come back to the **locked iron door** in the first chamber of the building and unlock it with the [Brightstone Key](/Brightstone+Key "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Key") you have just acquired. Once inside the room open another door and prepare for another [Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") ambush. Kill them and loot the highly-valuable chest in the room for a [Black Knight Ultra Greatsword](/Black+Knight+Ultra+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 Black Knight Ultra Greatsword"), a [Great ](/Great+Fireball "Dark Souls 2 Great Fireball") [Fireball](/Great+Fireball "Dark Souls 2 Great Fireball"), and a [Fire Seed](/Fire+Seed "Dark Souls 2 Fire Seed"). There is no way to backtrack out of this area, so either [Homeward Bone](/Homeward+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone") out or use the **Primal Bonfire** to get to [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula"). Assuming this was your fourth Old One slain, head on to the [Shrine Of Winter.](/Shrine+Of+Winter "Dark Souls 2 Shrine Of Winter")

Return when you have the [Ashen Mist Heart](/Ashen+Mist+Heart "Dark Souls 2 Ashen Mist Heart") and activate the crystal within Freja's room to access the [Memories of a Dragon](/Dragon+Memories "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Memories").

### Brightstone Cove Tseldora Walkthrough

### Well Dweller

After having entered the area from the [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros"), continue forward past the break in the short wall, and enter the first tent on the right. Here is the first **Bonfire** of the area. In this area are several [Hollow Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant"), and larger versions of the [Undead Devourers](/Undead+Devourer "Dark Souls 2 Undead Devourer") (Pigs) that you might remember from [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula"). While they may not appear to be too much of a threat, the [Hollow Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant") have[ Dark](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades")-enchanted weapons, which make them swing pretty hard!

Beware of the other tents, as some hide [Hollow Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant"). From the**Bonfire** , head into the tent on the left of the burning corpse pile to find a summon sign for [Benhart of Jugo](/Benhart+of+Jugo "Dark Souls 2 Benhart of Jugo"). Continue to the left and dispatch the first of many [Hollow Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant"), standing by 4 corpses with a total loot of: 3x [Soul Of a Nameless Soldier](/Soul+Of+a+Nameless+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Soul Of a Nameless Soldier"), 1x [Large Soul Of a Proud Knight](/Large+Soul+Of+a+Proud+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Large Soul Of a Proud Knight"). Once you have, attack the scaffolding with a corpse on it to drop the loot and grab the 15x [Lacerating Arrows](/Lacerating+Arrow "Dark Souls 2 Lacerating Arrow"). Look for the ruined building and break the urns beside it to reveal a corpse holding a [Large Soul Of a Nameless Soldier](/Large+Soul+Of+a+Nameless+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Large Soul Of a Nameless Soldier"). The doorway of this building is blocked off so we're heading to the well on the right, instead. Loot the corpse hanging over the well for a [Titanite Shard](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard"), then head up the ramp to run off it, and down the well. At the bottom of the well is a corpse holding 3x [Holy Water Urn](/Holy+Water+Urn "Dark Souls 2 Holy Water Urn"). Continue on and up the ladder to enter the building with the entrance that was blocked by rubble. Inside is a chest containing 5x [Human Effigy](/Human+Effigy "Dark Souls 2 Human Effigy"). Climb up the rubble to exit the house.

### Handicapped Mini-Boss

From here, head back to the bonfire and to the right of it this time, to arrive at the cliff edge. Follow the path down until you see a ladder. BEWARE as there is a trap that sets a huge rolling boulder down the platform above, and if it connects, will knock you down to your death. Make a beeline for the ladder, picking up the [Soul Of a Nameless Soldier](/Soul+Of+a+Nameless+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Soul Of a Nameless Soldier") before you rush up. There's a total of two [Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant") and two boulder traps -one has almost definitely been set off, so set the other off by breaking its wooden support, to collect a [Soul Of a Brave Warrior](/Soul+Of+a+Brave+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Soul Of a Brave Warrior"). Head back down and continue along the path ahead, where you will face another four [Hollow Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant").

Hanging off the ledge on the right, is a corpse holding [Large Titanite Shard](/Large+Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Large Titanite Shard"). Continue on to find more[ Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant") guarding a large door. Once inside, head down past the first large room and into the second, which has multiple [Ducal Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") lying in wait, to ambush you. Kill them using a mix of range and melee attacks, then loot the Trap Chest for 20x [Heavy Bolt](/Heavy+Bolt "Dark Souls 2 Heavy Bolt"). Continue through the iron door ahead to reach a room with a Boss Fog Gate. Breaking the urns here will grant you a [Human Effigy](/Human+Effigy "Dark Souls 2 Human Effigy"). Before jumping in to progress, head straight from the door you just entered, to find an Illusory Wall -press the "use" button on your respective console to uncover it. Head up the stairs and down the hall to find a [Water Urn](/Holy+Water+Urn "Dark Souls 2 Holy Water Urn") and a corpse holding [Priestess Set](/Priestess+Set "Dark Souls 2 Priestess Set"). Head back to the Boss Fog.

BOSS FIGHT: [Prowling Magus and Congregation](/Prowling+Magus+and+Congregation "Dark Souls 2 Prowling Magus and Congregation")

### Pate or Creighton 

Killing this boss nets you a nifty 7,000 souls and a [Titanite Slab](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab"). Go through the door ahead and face immediately left to find a ladder in the alcove of the wall. Head up to reach NPC [Cromwell The Pardoner](/Cromwell+the+Pardoner "Dark Souls 2 Cromwell the Pardoner") and multiple corpses to plunder a total of 1x [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic"), 1x [Cleric's Small Shield](/Cleric%27s+Small+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Cleric%27s Small Shield"), 3x [Fading Soul](/Fading+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Fading Soul"), and 1x [Skeptic's Spice](/Skeptic%27s+Spice "Dark Souls 2 Skeptic%27s Spice"). Speaking to him, if your Faith is above 35, will grant you a [Ring of Resistance](/Ring+of+Resistance "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Resistance"). When you are ready, head back down the ladder. If you can do high range damage, you can move towards the cliff edge and try to snipe [Crystal Lizard](/crystal+lizard "Dark Souls 2 crystal lizard") on the ledge below, but do not jump down to grab the loot, as you will be there very soon anyway.

Head back to the boss door but this time enter the cave via the entrance on the right. Turn left before the ladder to access the **Bonfire**. Head down and look under the stairs to find [Ducal Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider"), and a corpse holding a [Soul Of A Proud Knight](/Soul+Of+A+Proud+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Soul Of A Proud Knight"). There will also be another passive [Ducal Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") on the wall; this could possibly be an indicator of things to come. The room through the doorway, holds some urns containing 2x[ Wilted Dusk Herb](/Wilted+Dusk+Herb "Dark Souls 2 Wilted Dusk Herb"), but most importantly, an ambush of [Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") as soon as you enter. Move forward to a [Hollow Mage](/Hollow+Mage "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Mage") facing away from you, on the ledge, a very easy backstab target.

Take a zip-line which will bring you to the [Crystal Lizard](/Crystal+Lizard "Dark Souls 2 Crystal Lizard") mentioned before. Head left towards the jars. Inside the building will be [Mild Mannered Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate") and [Creighton Of Mirrah](/Creighton+of+Mirrah "Dark Souls 2 Creighton of Mirrah") battling it out (if you have fully followed their storyline thus far). Choose an NPC to side with and attack the other:

  * Loot the dead NPC and talk to the living NPC after the battle, exhausting their dialogue to receive the [Tseldora Den Key](/Tseldora+Den+Key "Dark Souls 2 Tseldora Den Key").
  * If you sided with [Creighton](/Creighton+of+Mirrah "Dark Souls 2 Creighton of Mirrah"), you'll also receive his armor set. 
    * Unless you've already collected his set from [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak"), killing [Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate") will also grant you his armor set.
  * While it may feel scummy, it is generally a good idea to also kill whoever you sided with, because (as you probably already know) they are both scumbags and wish you death.

Break the jars in this room to reveal a corpse holding 3x [Charcoal Pine Resin](/Charcoal+Pine+Resin "Dark Souls 2 Charcoal Pine Resin"). Head into the far left corner of the room, into the hole in the wall. Go through the first door and to find a corpse holding a [Torch](/Torch "Dark Souls 2 Torch"), on the right. Past the second door is another [Crystal Lizard](/Crystal+Lizard "Dark Souls 2 Crystal Lizard"), killing it yields 2x [Titanite Chunk](/Titanite+Chunk "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Chunk") and a [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite"), and to its right, a corpse holding a [Titanite Chunk](/Titanite+Chunk "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Chunk"). On the left side of the ledge, kill the [Spider Drone](/Spider+Drone "Dark Souls 2 Spider Drone") sitting there and cross the wooden bridge. In the room to your left are three more [Spider Drones](/Spider+Drone "Dark Souls 2 Spider Drone") and a door which leads to a small path to the right that has a [White Hollow Mage Robe](/White+Hollow+Set "Dark Souls 2 White Hollow Set") (note that this is only available if the Hollow Mage you killed dropped it), and leads back up above to the wooden bridge. Smash the random objects located to the left to reveal a lever which will lower a platform above. To reach it, head back up the small stairs and make a carefully aimed jump towards it. In the chest here, is a [Southern Ritual Band](/Southern+Ritual+Band "Dark Souls 2 Southern Ritual Band") [ +1](/Southern+Ritual+Band "Dark Souls 2 Southern Ritual Band"). From here the best things to do next is to simply [Homeward Bone](/Homeward+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone") and head back across the zip-line.

### An Orn Friend

Next there are two paths, which can be done now, and one of which will require [Homeward Bone](/Homeward+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone") usage, or skip and return after second bonfire is opened. You will always start from the ledge outside the house where [Mild Mannered Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate") and [Creighton Of Mirrah](/Creighton+of+Mirrah "Dark Souls 2 Creighton of Mirrah") fought (zip-line brings you there), this place will be referred to as the **starting point**.

  1. Head left past the zip-line onto the wooden platform and drop down to reach a corpse holding a [Large Titanite Shard](/Large+Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Large Titanite Shard"). You need to drop down again, now onto the platform with the [Estus Flask Shard](/Estus+Flask+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Estus Flask Shard") on the corpse. [Homeward Bone](/Homeward+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone") **but now do not take the zip-line**.
  2. Take the second zip-line head through the doorway to reach a room with 2 [Hollow Mages](/Hollow+Mage "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Mage") and a [Ducal Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") on the ceiling. Take them out then loot the corpse to the left for 4x [Lightning Urn](/Lightning+Urn "Dark Souls 2 Lightning Urn"). Go through the small doorway ahead to reach a ladder. Climb down to reach a room with a **bonfire** and a door. To your left, there is another [Ducal Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider"), so watch out. Head out the door to reach the sand pit. 
     1. When resting or returning to this bonfire, the [Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") that's located on the ceiling in the previous room will jump down to this room.

To your right, you'll find a corpse holding a [Petrified Dragon Bone](/Petrified+Dragon+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Petrified Dragon Bone"), grab it and proceed into the next door. Inside you'll find an old friend if you freed her from the [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods"), [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex"). In her room, you'll find jars and bookshelves to the left. Break them to find corpses holding a [Flame Butterfly](/Flame+Butterfly "Dark Souls 2 Flame Butterfly") and a [Pharros' Lockstone](/Pharros%27+Lockstone "Dark Souls 2 Pharros%27 Lockstone"). Head back outside and enter the opening in a wall to your left. Behind the bookshelves ahead is a room with another [Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") and a chest containing the [Crescent Axe](/Crescent+Axe "Dark Souls 2 Crescent Axe") and [Soul Vortex](/Soul+Vortex "Dark Souls 2 Soul Vortex"). Back in the main room, up the small hill, is a ledge that allows you to drop down to a small room with a corpse holding a [Titanite Chunk](/Titanite+Chunk "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Chunk") on the left, and a door to the right (note that once you drop down this ledge, the only way to return to the earlier part of the level is to do an angled jump back to the ledge).

### Trip to Freja

Through the door is a large courtyard. Ahead is a [Basilisk](/Basilisk "Dark Souls 2 Basilisk") and a [Spider Drone](/Spider+Drone "Dark Souls 2 Spider Drone"). Kill them and then head through the half broken wooden door ahead. Kill the [Ducal Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") inside then head up the stairs and open the door to find a corpse with a [Large Titanite Shard](/Large+Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Large Titanite Shard"). A scripted Red Phantom will spawn below you, past the archway. Drop down back to courtyard. Ignore the obvious bridge for now and head towards the last closed door in this area. Trying to open it gives you the message "Too heavy to open". Smash that piece of kindling down with ranged attacks or else an un-dodgable sand burst will hit you.

Go through the door and open the other door ahead with your **Tseldora Den Key** you received from either of the scumbags from earlier. Two chests are found in this room. The wooden one to the left is a **Trap Chest** containing 10x [Rusted Coin](/Rusted+Coin "Dark Souls 2 Rusted Coin"), and the metal Chest contains the [Engraved Gauntlets](/Engraved+Gauntlets "Dark Souls 2 Engraved Gauntlets") (upon opening the chest, you'll hear the dialogue between [Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate") and [Creighton](/Creighton+of+Mirrah "Dark Souls 2 Creighton of Mirrah"), revealing both are miscreants; however it does not occur if you killed both of them). Collect them and head outside. Go through the archway to the bridge.

Now head towards the building, and you'll notice two [Spider Drones](/Spider+Drone "Dark Souls 2 Spider Drone") on the second floor of said building. Kill them before you open the door and look up to find multiple [Ducal Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") are on the ceiling, so use ranged attacks or move ahead and let them drop down. To the right is a locked iron door, you need the **Brightstone Key** obtained after the boss battle to open it. To the right of the door is a bench that can be broken to reveal a corpse with a [Soul Of a Hero](/Soul+Of+a+Hero "Dark Souls 2 Soul Of a Hero") and 4x [Gold Pine Resin](/Gold+Pine+Resin "Dark Souls 2 Gold Pine Resin"). On the far wall are two doors, each with their own lever next to them. The door on the right holds a corpse with [Homeward Bone](/Homeward+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone") x3. The door on the left holds a [Spider Drone](/Spider+Drone "Dark Souls 2 Spider Drone") and a ladder heading up. Climb it to reach **Freja's Lair**. In this room, you'll find many spider webs which serve as bridges, which as you step on, will trigger [Ducal Spiders](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") to engage you.

Drop down from the ledge and move left, hugging the wall. Move down the first web and reach the far side. Behind the first pillar is a corpse holding a [Torch](/Torch "Dark Souls 2 Torch"). Careful, aa s [Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") will drop down near here. Move forward towards the Jars on the far side. In them is a corpse holding [Pharros' Lockstone ](/Pharros%27+Lockstone "Dark Souls 2 Pharros%27 Lockstone"). Move across the web to the left. At the end, drop to the large web to your left and follow it down. Halfway down is a corpse holding [Radiant Lifegem](/Radiant+Lifegem "Dark Souls 2 Radiant Lifegem"). Go down to ground level and kill any spiders that attack. To your left is a small but wide web leading up to a platform above. On the far side, you'll notice a corpse holding a [Twilight Herb](/Twilight+Herb "Dark Souls 2 Twilight Herb"). Return to the ground level and underneath the large web is a corpse holding a [Simpleton's Spice](/Simpleton%27s+Spice "Dark Souls 2 Simpleton%27s Spice"). From here, head to the far right side of the room and behind the last pillar is a corpse holding 5x [Corrosive Urn](/Corrosive+Urn "Dark Souls 2 Corrosive Urn"). As you may have noticed by now, there is a large Fog Door ahead; what you may not have noticed however, is the summon sign for [Ashen Knight Boyd](/Ashen+Knight+Boyd "Dark Souls 2 Ashen Knight Boyd") behind one of the pillars.

BOSS FIGHT: [The Duke's Dear Freja](/The+Duke%27s+Dear+Freja "Dark Souls 2 The Duke%27s Dear Freja")

### Death of Freja

Defeating her, means you can move towards the dragon and pick up the great soul. Head through the cave to the right to reach the [Lord's Private Chamber](/Lord's+Private+Chamber "Dark Souls 2 Lord's Private Chamber") where you will find **Duke of Tseldora** , back turned to the player while focusing on a table in front of him. Defeat the hollowed Duke to receive the [Brightstone Key](/Brightstone+Key "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Key"), a [Fragrant Branch Of Yore](/Fragrant+Branch+Of+Yore "Dark Souls 2 Fragrant Branch Of Yore") and the [Dark Quartz Ring +1](/Dark+Quartz+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Dark Quartz Ring"). Down the stairs to the right is a **Primal Bonfire** but be careful as it is guarded by [Vengarl's](/Head+of+Vengarl "Dark Souls 2 Head of Vengarl") Body; to get his armor, kill him.

Now its time to come back to the **locked iron door** in the first chamber of the building and unlock it with the [Brightstone Key](/Brightstone+Key "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Key") you have just acquired. Once inside the room open another door and prepare for another [Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") ambush. Kill them and loot the highly-valuable chest in the room for a [Black Knight Ultra Greatsword](/Black+Knight+Ultra+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 Black Knight Ultra Greatsword"), a [Great ](/Great+Fireball "Dark Souls 2 Great Fireball") [Fireball](/Great+Fireball "Dark Souls 2 Great Fireball"), and a [Fire Seed](/Fire+Seed "Dark Souls 2 Fire Seed"). There is no way to backtrack out of this area, so either [Homeward Bone](/Homeward+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone") out or use the **Primal Bonfire** to get to [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula"). Assuming this was your fourth Old One slain, head on to the [Shrine Of Winter.](/Shrine+Of+Winter "Dark Souls 2 Shrine Of Winter")

Return when you have the [Ashen Mist Heart](/Ashen+Mist+Heart "Dark Souls 2 Ashen Mist Heart") and activate the crystal within Freja's room to access the [Memories of a Dragon](/Dragon+Memories "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Memories").
