**Key to King's Passage** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Key to King's Passage** , a key that grants access to King's Passage. King Vendrick, in his desperate attempts to rid his kingdom of the looming curse, eventually sought refuge through the King's Passage. Use this key to unlock the door that leads to the Looking Glass Knight in Drangleic Castle. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> Key to King's Passage.   
> King Vendrick tried all manner of things to purge the curse that threatened his kingdom.  
> But when every last attempt failed, the King fled through the King's Passage.

### Key to King's Passage Usage in DKS2

  * Opens the door leading to the [Looking Glass Knight](/Looking+Glass+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Knight") in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle")

### Dark Souls 2 Key to King's Passage Location

After defeating the twin Dragon Riders, past the bonfire, take a left and you'll find a little room with an elevator (which you cannot use just yet), a locked door (opens with this key), and a ladder, leading to an upper room with a Golem. Also in the room are seven stone soldiers. Kill one of them near the giant and its soul will drift towards it and activate it, causing it to turn the urn it is holding, allowing you to use the lift. Return to the small room and take the elevator up the tower. It takes you to a room where you can find the Key to King's Passage in a chest, along with a [Soul Vessel](/Soul+Vessel "Dark Souls 2 Soul Vessel"), a [Fire Seed](/Fire+Seed "Dark Souls 2 Fire Seed"), [Strong Magic Shield](/Strong+Magic+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Strong Magic Shield") sorcery, and a caged [Milfanito](/Milfanito "Dark Souls 2 Milfanito"). You can free her later in the game.
