**Sanctum Soldier Gauntlets** are a pair of [Gauntlets](/Gauntlets "Dark Souls 2 Gauntlets") in Dark Souls 2. It is part of the [Crown of the Sunken King DLC](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King+DLC "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King DLC").

_"Gauntlets of the sanctum soldiers. Improves effect of poison weapons._  
_These gauntlets are corroded by the poison of the slumbering dragon, making it difficult to discern their original shape."_

### Location

  * Dropped by [Sanctum Soldiers](/Sanctum+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Soldier") in [Shulva, Sanctum City](/Shulva+-+Sanctum+City "Dark Souls 2 Shulva - Sanctum City").

### Sanctum Soldier Gauntlets Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
  * Upgraded stats are only 50% higher and physical defenses are only 25% higher.
  * Boosts poison buildup on weapons. 
    * Adds 25 to poison attack if a weapon is infused with poison using a poison stone.
    * Adds 50 to poison attack if weapon has poison damage innately.
    * Adds 60 to poison attack if weapon has poison damage innately and is infused with poison using a poison stone.
  * Stacks with [Crest of the Rat](/Crest+of+the+Rat "Dark Souls 2 Crest of the Rat").
