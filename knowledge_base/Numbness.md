**Numbness** is a Hex in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Numbness** , a hex that alters the caster's flesh. Temporarily lessens damage taken but clouds one's vision. The belief that what can't be seen doesn't exist is common, yet this world is filled with too many exceptions to such simplistic notions. To cast a [Hex](/Hexes "Dark Souls 2 Hexes"), you must use a staff/chime or Special Weapons that can cast Hex.

> "A hex that transforms its caster's flesh. Temporarily reduces received damage, but darkens one's sight.
> 
> If it cannot be observed, it does not exist. Certainly a common conception, but one with far too many exceptions in this world.  
> Catalyst: staff"

### Dark Souls 2 Numbness Acquired From

  * Trade the [Old Dead One's soul](/Old+Dead+One+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Old Dead One Soul") and 5000 souls to [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis")

### Numbness Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Reduces all incoming damage by 32% for 20 seconds, however, it will give the player tunnel vision by darkening the sides of the screen. With the changes to spell duration and stats made to this spell, Numbness can last up to 90 seconds.
  * This Hex also darkens the blinding mist within the "Crown of the Ivory King" DLC making certain areas easier than they were designed to be.
