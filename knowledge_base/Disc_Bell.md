**Disc Bell** is a type of [Item](/Items "Dark Souls 2 Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Disc Bell** , a bell featuring an unusual design that can also function as a shield. Uncommon among the clergy of this realm. Offers a marginal enhancement to the potency of miracles. [Unused Items](/Unused+Items "Dark Souls 2 Unused Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)** are Items listed in the game code, but not available in-game.

> Bell with a curious shape. Can be used as a shield.   
> This type of bell is not used by the clergy of these lands. Perhaps it was brought from a foreign clime?   
> Provides a slight increase to the effectiveness of miracles."

### Disc Bell Information in DKS2

    * **Type: Sacred Bell**
    * **Attack Type: Spell**
    * **Weight: 0.5**
    * **Durability: 150**

### Dark Souls 2 Disc Bell Hints and Tips:

  * Allows the use of minor shielding with strong attack allowing miracles to be cast.

### Disc Bell Required in Build in Dark Souls 2:

  * This item was renamed to [Disc Chime](/Disc+Chime "Dark Souls 2 Disc Chime") in the final version of the game.
  * This item has no icon, the icon shown is from the [Disc Chime](/Disc+Chime "Dark Souls 2 Disc Chime")
