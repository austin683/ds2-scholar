**Soul of Lud, the King's Pet** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of Lud, the King's Pet** , encapsulating the essence of Lud, the beast in service to the Ivory King. Assigned a distinct responsibility among the seven beasts under the Ivory King's command, Lud was tasked with mercifully ending the existence of exiles. Acquired by overcoming [Lud, the King's Pet](/Lud+and+Zallen,+the+King's+Pets "Dark Souls 2 Lud and Zallen, the King's Pets"), in the [Frigid Outskirts](/Frigid+Outskirts "Dark Souls 2 Frigid Outskirts"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of Lud, who served the Ivory King. Each of the Ivory King's seven beasts was conferred a specific duty.   
> Lud's was the mercy killing of exiles.   
> The wondrous soul of this beast can be used to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth."

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of Lud, the King's Pet Usage

  * Trade it along with 3,000 souls for the [Loyce Greatsword](/Loyce+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Greatsword") from [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex")
  * Consume it for 16,000 souls.

### Soul of Lud, the King's Pet Location in DKS2

  * Defeat [Lud, the King's Pet](/Lud+and+Zallen%2C+the+King%27s+Pets "Dark Souls 2 Lud and Zallen%2C the King%27s Pets") at [Frigid Outskirts](/Frigid+Outskirts "Dark Souls 2 Frigid Outskirts")
