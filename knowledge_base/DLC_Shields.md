# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/DLC+Shields

**DLC Shields** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are types of [Shields](/Shields "Dark Souls 2 Shields") that you can get only when you've acquired the DLCs for the game, namely [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King") and [Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King"). Note that you would not automatically get the Shields when you purchase the DLCs, you would still need play through the new content and acquire it from there. This page covers a list of all DLC Shields in Dark Souls 2.
