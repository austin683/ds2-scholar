**Throne Defender Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Throne Defender Set](/Throne+Defender+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Throne Defender Leggings Description

_"The Defender has stood by the throne for ages.  
Will his wait be worth the while?"_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Bought from [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer) merchant in [Majula](/Majula) after you have defeated the Throne Defender & Throne Watcher.

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite)
