**Blackweed Balm** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Blackweed Balm** , an extract from a rare plant, temporarily boosts intelligence. This unique fragrant oil influences the mind, providing a fleeting sense of empowerment. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A balm extracted from a rare plant. Temporarily increases Intelligence  
> Info: This special fragrant oil affects the mind, granting a fleeting sense of empowerment.

### Dark Souls 2 Blackweed Balm Usage

  * Increases [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") by 5 for 45 seconds.

### Blackweed Balm Location in Dark Souls 2

  * [Frigid Outskirts](/Frigid+Outskirts "Dark Souls 2 Frigid Outskirts")
