### Manikin gloves Description

"Gloves of the Manikins of Harvest Valley. A fickle queen gave them life, and tore off their faces. How else could she forgive those who dared gaze upon her?"

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Drop from [Manikin](/Manikin) in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak)

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shard, Large Titanite Shard, Titanite Chunk, Titanite Slab](/Ores)
