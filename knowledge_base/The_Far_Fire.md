#  The Far Fire

##  Location:

Coming from [Fire Keepers' Dwelling](/Fire+Keepers%27+Dwelling "Dark Souls 2 Fire Keepers%27 Dwelling") you just walk through the gap in the wall in front of you and follow the path past all the fog walls and proceed through the rock cleft. You will enter the area of [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula"). walk down the path and go through the broken arch. The bonfire is to your far right. 

##  Bosses:

  * No bosses

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

  
Burning a bonfire ascetic here respawns the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * [Binoculars ](/Binoculars "Dark Souls 2 Binoculars")(hidden path on cliff drop)
  * [Cleric's Sacred Chime](/Cleric%27s+Sacred+Chime "Dark Souls 2 Cleric%27s Sacred Chime") (corpse on hidden path on cliff drop)
  * [Morning Star](/Morning+Star "Dark Souls 2 Morning Star") (corpse on hidden path on cliff drop)
  * [Divine Blessing](/Divine+Blessing "Dark Souls 2 Divine Blessing") (on a corpse leaned up against a tree near entrance to Shaded Woods)
  * [Pharros' Lockstone](/Pharros%27+Lockstone "Dark Souls 2 Pharros%27 Lockstone") (inside the Mansion's library)
  * [Pharros' Lockstone](/Pharros%27+Lockstone "Dark Souls 2 Pharros%27 Lockstone") (on a plank in the hole in the middle of town)
  * [Titanite Shard](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard") x3,[Torch ](/Torch "Dark Souls 2 Torch")x3 upstairs in the mansion
  * [Titanite Shard](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard") (wooden chest in Maughlin the Armourer's house), [Moon Butterfly Shield](/Moon+Butterfly+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Moon Butterfly Shield") instead after two Bonfire Ascetics
  * [Lifegem ](/Lifegem "Dark Souls 2 Lifegem")(on a corpse under a tent in the town square)
  * [Short Bow](/Short+Bow "Dark Souls 2 Short Bow") in the Wooden chest in Blacksmith Lenigrast's house)
  * [Radiant Lifegem](/Radiant+Lifegem "Dark Souls 2 Radiant Lifegem") (on a plank in the hole in the middle of town)
  * [Poison Moss](/Poison+Moss "Dark Souls 2 Poison Moss") x3 (on a plank in the hole in the middle of town)
  * [Ring of the Evil Eye +1](/Ring+of+the+Evil+Eye "Dark Souls 2 Ring of the Evil Eye") (on a plank at the bottom of the hole in the middle of town)
  * All [Pharros' Contraptions](/Pharros%27+contraptions "Dark Souls 2 Pharros%27 contraptions") in Grave of Saint
  * [Disc Chime](/Disc+Chime "Dark Souls 2 Disc Chime")
  * Skeletons in the mansion's basement (each one drops a [Human Effigy](/Human+Effigy "Dark Souls 2 Human Effigy"))

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here spawns the following new items/enemies:

  * A new Skeleton below the Mansion will spawn for each ascetic that is burned, each of which will drop a Human Effigy.
  * One new Black phantom will spawn within the tutorial area in a pit you need to drop down into to progress through the tutorial.

##  Special Notes:

  * Can purchase [Moon Butterfly Armor](/Moon+Butterfly+Set "Dark Souls 2 Moon Butterfly Set") set from [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") after using two [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic")
  * The metal chest in the mansion's basement does not respawn upon use of an [ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic").
