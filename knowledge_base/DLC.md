**DLC** for **[Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki") **covers additional downloadable content for the game. Additional DLCs can either be released as a free update or purchased. **Dark Souls II** released their DLC delivered in 3 individual episodes as a trilogy. All episodes were either $9.99 a piece, or were also available for purchase as a set by purchasing the  _Dark Souls II_ Season Pass for $24.99 for the Bundle. Players interested in this content were happy to find out that [Players who own the DLC will be able to summon those who don't into the DLC!](https://fextralife.com/dark-souls-2-dlc-a-sitdown-with-from-software/).

## Dark Souls 2 All DLC Content

#### [Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King")

##### [](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King")

##### $9.99  
July 22, 2014

Embark on a journey to reclaim a crown that Drangleic’s King Vendrick once owned. This perilous quest will lead you through an entirely different world within the DARK SOULS™ II universe, where stepped pyramids span a vast underground cavern. It is said that one of the ancient crowns lay buried deep within these dark caverns; but surely such a valued item cannot sit unguarded. Explore in search of a crown that holds the strength of lords from times long past.

#### [Crown of the Old Iron King](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King")

##### [](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King")

##### $9.99  
August 26th, 2014

"DARK SOULS™ II Crown of the Old Iron King" is the second of 3 large-scale DLC additions to DARK SOULS™ II, and includes all-new stages, maps, boss characters, weapons & armor! In this second DLC, players will find themselves in a world shrouded in ash. Can you find and recover the lost crown?

#### [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King")

##### [](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King")

##### $9.99  
September 30, 2014 [PC/Xbox] / October 1, 2014 [PS3]

"DARK SOULS™ II Crown of the Ivory King" is the third of 3 large-scale DLC additions to DARK SOULS™ II, and includes all-new stages, maps, boss characters, weapons & armor! In this third DLC, players will find themselves in a world shining bright with the glow of treacherous ice. Can you find and recover the lost crown?

#### Dark Souls II Season Pass

##### $24.9**9**

Delve into the Lost Crowns Trilogy with the DARK SOULS™ II Season Pass.  
  
By purchasing this Season Pass, you will get at discounted price the three additional chapters from the Lost Crowns Trilogy and start a journey through underground caverns spanned with stepped pyramids, a massive tower engulfed by black mist or an ancient temple wrung with freezing storms, towering walls and a hollow emptiness.  
  
This quest will lead you through:

  * [Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King")
  * [Crown of the Old Iron King](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King")
  * [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King")

Those 3 chapters will lead you to confront new fearsome bosses and hosts of new enemies through entirely original areas and will respectively be released on July 22nd, August 26th, and September 30th 2014.

Each DLC will be added to your Steam account as they become available.

  * Note: The season pass requires "DARK SOULS™ II" to function.

The definitive edition of DARK SOULS™ II. DARK SOULS™ II: Scholar of the First Sin includes all the DARK SOULS™ II content released to-date in one package and much more!

This bundle includes:

Hardened players will have to forget everything they thought they knew about DARK SOULS™ II.
