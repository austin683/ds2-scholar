**Dragon Acolyte** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Dragon Acolyte** , beings with a scientific appearance and warped orb hoods. While relatively slow, a direct hit from their enchanted weapon or skull shield can be deadly, especially if the player lacks the appropriate armor or stats. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Dragon Acolyte Information in Dark Souls 2

Scientist looking beings with warped orb hoods. They are fairly slow but taking a direct hit from their enchanted weapon or skull shield can prove to be quite deadly if lacking the proper armor and/or player stats.

### Dragon Acolyte Strategy Tips in DKS2

  * If you stun them (try spamming dark orb with a fast staff) while they are pulling out a witching urn or Elizabeth mushroom they will hobble and a slouch instead of walking how they usually do.
