"Amour worn by Varagians, that terrorized nearby seas. The coastline stretches far Drangleic. Beyond this Northern Sea is an unexplored continent, said to be the home of things inhuman."

### Location

  * Dropped in [No-Man's Wharf](/No-Man%27s+Wharf) by Varangian Bandits.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Varangian Armor](/Varangian+Armor).
  * Upgraded from [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab).
