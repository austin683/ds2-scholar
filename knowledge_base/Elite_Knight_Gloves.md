### Elite Knight Gloves Description

"Gloves made of bradden steel. Provides sturdy defense, making it an old-time favorite of elite knights.  
"Bradden steel is used widely in Drangleic. This fine alloy is made from ores mined in the southern kingdom."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Bought from [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") merchant in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula"). One must spend more than 1,000 souls at his shop, leave, rest at a bonfire, and return.
  * Dropped by [Wall Watchmen](/Wall+Watchman "Dark Souls 2 Wall Watchman") in [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce").
  * The Elite Knight Set +5 can drop from the [red phantoms](/Phantoms "Dark Souls 2 Phantoms") that spawn in ng+ (or ascetic) in front of the last king's gate before throne watcher/defender and Nashandra. Unsure if guarunteed drop or not
