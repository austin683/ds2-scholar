**Firedrake Stone** is a type of [Upgrade Material](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Firedrake Stone** , a modified form of titanite. Apply it to infuse weapons with fire or to bestow fire resistance to shields. Legends tell of sorcerers from the Melfian Magic Academy attempting to imbue titanite with various elements, though their efforts are believed to have ended in failure. [Upgrade Materials](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades") are items that are used to strengthen the statistical value and to increase the effect of a certain piece of equipment.

> An altered state of titanite. Use to imbue weapons with fire, or to provide fire reduction to shields.  
> Sorcerers at the Melfian Magic Academy once attempted to imbue titanite with various elements, but are said to have failed. Someone must have succeeded though; what else would explain this stone?

### Firedrake Stone Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Can only be used at Blacksmith [Steady Hand McDuff](/Steady+Hand+McDuff "Dark Souls 2 Steady Hand McDuff")
  * Used to imbue equipment with fire. This reduces the Strength and Dexterity scaling. (Different weapons add different tiers of scaling).

### Dark Souls 2 Firedrake Stone Location

  * Possible to get when trading [Smooth & Silky stone](/Smooth+%26+Silky+stone "Dark Souls 2 Smooth %26 Silky stone") to [Dyna and Tillo](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo").
  * 3 can be purchased from [Stone Trader Chloanne](/Stone+Trader+Chloanne "Dark Souls 2 Stone Trader Chloanne") upon the completion of [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt") for 7,500 souls each.
  * From the salamander area in the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants"), you can use the [Silvercat Ring](/Silvercat+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Silvercat Ring"), the [Fall Control](/Fall+Control "Dark Souls 2 Fall Control") spell, or plunge attack on salamander (lock-on recommended), or use the [Iron Key](/Iron+Key "Dark Souls 2 Iron Key"), which opens the door located near [The](/The+Last+Giant "Dark Souls 2 The Last Giant") [Last Giant](/The+Last+Giant "Dark Souls 2 The Last Giant") boss fight.
  * One is located in the middle of the area of the third [dragon](/Dragon "Dark Souls 2 Dragon") that one fights in [Dragon Aerie](/Dragon+Aerie "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Aerie")
