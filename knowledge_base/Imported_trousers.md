**Imported Trousers** is a **Legging** and a part of [Imported Set](/Imported+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Imported Trousers Description

"Hemp hood that fully covers the face. Provides protection from the elements, and little more."  
"Traveler's tunic/manchettes/trousers. Won't provide much in the way of protection.  
The cursed souls who wander the lands have a strange way of ending up here, as if drawn from afar by some force."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Starting equipment for all players.

### Notes

  * Upgraded with Titanite Shards, Chunks
  * [NG+]The Things Betwixt Black phantom that is wielding a Heavy Crossbow at the third Tutorial tree drops +5 pieces of the Set
