**Iron Arrow** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. I**ron Arrows** , conventional projectiles crafted from iron. Robust and effective in piercing targets. While potent, these arrows come at a price, so use them judiciously. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Standard arrows made of iron. Sturdy arrows that pierce effectively. Iron arrows are powerful, but not cheap. Be certain not to overuse them.

### Iron Arrow Usage in DKS2

  * Bow ammunition
  * 85 Physical ATK

### Dark Souls 2 Iron Arrow Location

  * (Majula) [Blacksmith Lenigrast](/Blacksmith+Lenigrast "Dark Souls 2 Blacksmith Lenigrast") carries 50 for 30 souls each
  * (Lost Bastille) Blacksmith [Steady Hand McDuff](/Steady+Hand+McDuff "Dark Souls 2 Steady Hand McDuff") carries an unlimited supply for 30 souls each
