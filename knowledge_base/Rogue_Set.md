**Rogue Set** is an Attire or [Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor") in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

> Hood favored by Hollowed thieves.  
> The hood is primarily intended to hide the face of the wearer, and is of no practical value as defensive gear.  
>   
> Crude iron armor/leggings worn by the Hollowed thieves who wander the lands of Drangleic.  
> Despite appearances, this armor is/these leggings are surprisingly sturdy.  
>   
> Gauntlets worn by Hollowed thieves.  
> Despite their crude appearance, these gauntlets are surprisingly sturdy.

###  Set Location

  * Dropped by [Hollow Rogues](/Hollow+Rogue "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Rogue") in [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman%27s+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman%27s Copse").

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
