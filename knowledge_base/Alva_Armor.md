**Alva Armor** is a [Chest Piece](/Chest+Pieces "Dark Souls 2 Chest Pieces") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). Armor worn by Alva the Wayfarer. Despite its light weight, it provides very high defense. **Alva Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> "Armor worn by Alva the Wayfarer. Light but offers very high defense. Alva crossed many a land in search of a cure for Saint Serreta's sickness, but failed and relinquished his knighthood. Scholar of the First Sin changed to: Light but offer very high defense.   
> Alva crossed many a land in search of a cure for Saint Serreta's sickness, but failed and relinquished his knighthood.   
> Alva was once wracked with guilt and remorse, but rediscovered his purpose in life with the aid of the witch who once plotted against him." 

### Dark Souls 2 Alva Armor Location

  * After spending 15,000 souls with [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula"), you may purchase this set.

### Alva Armor Information in Dark Souls 2

  * Part of the [Alva Set](/Alva+Set "Dark Souls 2 Alva Set"). Pieces include [Alva Helm](/Alva+Helm "Dark Souls 2 Alva Helm"), [Alva Gauntlets](/Alva+Gauntlets "Dark Souls 2 Alva Gauntlets"), and [Alva Leggings](/Alva+Leggings "Dark Souls 2 Alva Leggings").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab").
  * "Alva" is a Swedish name, although in reality it is only used by females.
