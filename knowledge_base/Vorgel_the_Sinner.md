**Vorgel the Sinner** is an [enemy](/enemies "Dark Souls 2 enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Vorgel the Sinner** is a phantom invader encountered past the Belfry Gargoyles boss fight. Displaying the traits of a warrior, Vorgel wields a spear and shield and wears light armor. While swift and aggressively attacking, he is susceptible to stun-locking, making him more manageable. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Vorgel the Sinner Information in Dark Souls 2

_"Vorgel the Sinner is a phantom invader that appears after the Belfry Gargoyles boss fight. After going down the ladder into an area full of dogs he will invade. He is a warrior type character wielding a spear and shield with light armor. Vorgel is quite fast and can be difficult to block due to his heavily aggressive attacks but he is also very easy to stunlock. Vorgel is a Royal soldier, much like Masterless Glencour and Ashen knight Boyd, However Vorgel is hostile, and is believed to be a traitor. Vorgel Wears the Royal soldier helm, leggings, and gloves, but wears the hollow variant of the Royal soldier armor. Much like the (non-hollowed) soldiers encountered in the memories of giants, Vorgel wields a spear and uses the non hollow variant of the Royal soldier shield.__"_

### Vorgel the Sinner Strategy Tips in DKS2

  * Unlike most red phantoms, Vorgel is relatively easy for more experienced players, but has surprising speed and can catch you off guard easily. The first priority when facing Vorgel is to eliminate the surrounding dogs. Vorgel is easy to sidestep and back stab once the dogs have been taken care of.
  * Alternatively, just use a [Seed of a Tree of Giants](/Seed+of+a+Tree+of+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Seed of a Tree of Giants"). The dogs will turn on him and are able to defeat him without the player even getting involved. 

### Dark Souls 2 Vorgel the Sinner Move Set

  * He uses simple thrusting attacks with his spear that are difficult to parry.
  * Vorgel has the same moveset as the Royal soldiers encountered in the Giant memories, but is more aggressive.
