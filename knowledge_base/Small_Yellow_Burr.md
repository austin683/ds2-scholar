**Small Yellow Burr** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Small Yellow Burr** temporarily enhances lightning defense. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A small yellow burr. Temporarily boosts lightning defense. These burrs bloom magnificently on a shrub every few years, their color and effect determined by soil quality and recent weather. Known to have been used in rituals long ago.

### Small Yellow Burr Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Temporarily boosts lightning defense by 150 for 90 seconds when consumed.

### Dark Souls 2 Small Yellow Burr Location

  * Sold by [Rosabeth of Melfia](/Rosabeth+of+Melfia "Dark Souls 2 Rosabeth of Melfia") for 1,200 souls.
