**Alonne Knight Set** is a [Helm](/Helms) in Dark Souls 2.

_"Helm worn by Alonne Knights._   
_The bonds of the Alonne Knights, who served the Old Iron King, were mightier than the land's iron, but in the end the knights were subsumed by the flames that brought the castle down."_   
  
  

### Set Location

  * Dropped by [Alonne Knights](/Alonne+Knight) within the [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep). They are the standard enemies of the area.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Alonne Knight Set](/Alonne+Knight+Set). 
    * Very high poise for relatively low weight.
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Ores).
