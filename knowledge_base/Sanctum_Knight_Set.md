**Sanctum Knight Set** is an Armor Set in Dark Souls 2.

_(Armor type) of the sanctum knights._

_The sanctum knights renounced their own flesh to eternally guard the sanctum from Sir Yorgh and his Drakeblood Knights._

### Set Location

  * Dropped by [Sanctum Knights](/Sanctum+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Knight") in [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum").
  * Helm is also found in the room with 5 knights' corpses in [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum").

### Sanctum Knight Set Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * Upgraded physical defenses are only 25% higher.
  * Leggings offer second highest [petrification](/Petrification "Dark Souls 2 Petrification") resistance.
  * Leggings reduce [Fall Damage](/Fall+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Fall Damage") and deafens the sound of steps (enemies seem to react less on you).
