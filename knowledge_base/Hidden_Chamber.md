##  Location:

Located in [Black Gulch](/Black+Gulch "Dark Souls 2 Black Gulch").

##  Bosses:

  * [The Rotten](/The+Rotten "Dark Souls 2 The Rotten")

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here respawns the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * Boss: [The Rotten](/The+Rotten "Dark Souls 2 The Rotten")
  * Item: Pharros Lockstone in the pots next to the bonfire
  * No enemies

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here spawns the following new items/enemies:

  * Item
  * Item
  * Enemy
  * Enemy

##  Special Notes:

If you look into the bottomless pit, you'll notice several ledges you can jump down onto. There is a lot more to this area than meets the eye!

From this bonfire it is quite easy to farm titanite and awestones from the cragslippers and darkstriders in the area, though dieing will reset the statues making it somewhat difficult to set up.
