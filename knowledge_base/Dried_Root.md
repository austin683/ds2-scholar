**Dried Root** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Dried Root** , the perennial herb's root from the south, restores HP over an extended period. Its potent medicinal effects are valued by clerics on pilgrimage, even though its pungent odor is detested by wild animals. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Root of a perennial herb native to the south. Restores HP for an extended duration.  
> Info: Wild animals abhor the pungent odor of this root, but clerics on pilgrimage are keenly aware of the value of its powerful medicinal effects.

### Dark Souls 2 Dried Root Usage

  * Restores HP over time. (Restores 1000 HP over 500 seconds.)
  * does not stack with itself

### Dried Root Location in DKS2

  * 3 are dropped from the [strange tree in Shulva City](/Pagan+Tree "Dark Souls 2 Pagan Tree"), in the small area containing [Promised Walk of Peace](/Promised+Walk+of+Peace "Dark Souls 2 Promised Walk of Peace").
  * Land of the Imperfect area, on a corpse.
  * 6 in 3 metal chests behind a locked door in Dragon Sanctum.
