**Sanctum Priestess Tiara** is a [Helm](/Helms "Dark Souls 2 Helms") in Dark Souls 2.

_Tiara of the Eternal Sanctum Priestesses. Prevents effects that block spells.  
_

_This tiara has been warped by poison, adding to its peculiar design. It is said that the priestesses sang to preserve the dragon's deep slumber, but one wonders if a single phrase of their song ever reached the dragon's ears._

### Location

  * Dropped by [Sanctum Priestesses](/Sanctum+Priestess "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Priestess") in [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon's+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon's Sanctum").
  * Also dropped by NPC invader [Fencer Sharron](/Fencer+Sharron "Dark Souls 2 Fencer Sharron") (rare) in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep") and [Dragon Aerie](/Dragon+Aerie "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Aerie").

### Sanctum Priestess Tiara Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
  * Upgraded stats are only 50% higher and physical defenses are only 25% higher.
  * Requires 11 FTH & INT.
  * Blocks the effect of [Profound Still](/Profound+Still "Dark Souls 2 Profound Still") hex (no magical "silence" applied).
