**Large Soul of a Brave Warrior** is a type of [Consumable](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Large Soul of a Brave Warrior** , the substantial soul of a courageous Undead warrior who fell to Hollowing in the distant past. Consume to amass a significant number of souls. In life and undeath, the soul remains a coveted essence. Grants 8000 souls when consumed. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are consumable items categorized as remnants of fallen warriors, consuming souls collects a certain number of souls that is added to the player's stock. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are required for players to become stronger in the game by raising their soul level as well as buying and upgrading their equipment.

> Large soul of a brave Undead warrior who has long ago gone Hollow.  
> Use to acquire a large amount of souls. The soul is the source of all life, and even in undeath, or hollowing, the mind seeks souls.

### Large Soul of a Brave Warrior Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Grants 8000 souls when consumed

### DKS2 Large Soul of a Brave Warrior Location

  * Brightstone Cove Tseldora. After the first boulder on the path, up the ladder and behind the second boulder. (That is a regular soul of a brave warrior in Scholar of the First Sin Edition. Don't know about the original game.)
