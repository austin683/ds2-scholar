#  Hidden Sanctum Chamber

>> [Bonfires](/Bonfires "Dark Souls 2 Bonfires")   
Updated Jul 28, 2014 3:58 am

* * *

##  Location:

Located in the upper inner sanctum. In the area with floor spikes, find the stairs going down. At the bottom of the first flight of stairs, near the [Sanctum Knight](/Sanctum+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Knight"), turn around and look at the wall above the hole on the side of the staircase. There is a switch there, shoot it and it will open a door on the upper part of the room. Head back up the stairs and up the stone ladder in the room directly on the left (guarded by another [Sanctum Knight](/Sanctum+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Knight")). You can now jump over to the bonfire.

##  Bosses:

  * None.

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here respawns the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * [Corrosive Egg Crawlers](/Corrosive+Egg+Crawlers "Dark Souls 2 Corrosive Egg Crawlers")
  * [Black Drakeblood Knight](/Black+Drakeblood+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Black Drakeblood Knight")
  * [Sanctum Knight](/Sanctum+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Knight")
  * [Sanctum Priestess](/Sanctum+Priestess "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Priestess")
  * [Sanctum Soldier](/Sanctum+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Soldier")
  * [The Imperfect](/The+Imperfect "Dark Souls 2 The Imperfect")

Drops:

  * [Drakeblood Set](/Drakeblood+Set "Dark Souls 2 Drakeblood Set")
  * [Sanctum Knight Set](/Sanctum+Knight+Set "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Knight Set")
  * [Sanctum Priestess Tiara](/Sanctum+Priestess+Tiara "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Priestess Tiara")
  * [Sanctum Soldier Gauntlets](/Sanctum+Soldier+Gauntlets "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Soldier Gauntlets")
