#  Burning Items

It is possible to burn items in the Bonfires to benefit from their effects. Three items can be burned in a Bonfire:   

  1. [Bonfire Ascetics](/Bonfire+Ascetic): Increases Bonfire Intensity by 1.
  2. [Human Effigies](/Human+Effigy): Softens the links to other worlds, makes it impossible for others to invade your world.
  3. [Sublime Bone Dusts](/Sublime+Bone+Dust): Increases the power of your [Estus Flask](/Estus+Flask) charge by 1, to a maximum number of +5. It can only be burned in the Far Fire bonfire at [Majula](/Majula).
