**Falconer Boots** is a **legging** and a part of [Falconer Set](/Falconer+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Falconer Boots Description

_"Leggings worn by the Volgen Falconers."  
"Domestic Volgen soldiers are infamously timid, so it is no wonder that this fierce band of mercenary falconers was hired to compensate.  
In practice they serve as bodyguards for the affluent elite, and they serve well, such that nobody dares scrutinize their backgrounds."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Starting gear (no helmet) for Knight Class
  * Sold [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer)
  * Obtained from Volgen Falconer enemies in NG+ in the start area of [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt).
