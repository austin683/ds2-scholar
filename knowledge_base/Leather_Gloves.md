### Leather Gloves Description

"_The hunting goddess Evlana was no goddess at all, but rather a brave and highly skilled bow huntress. Long after her demise, the passing of lore transformed her into a deity._ "

### Where to Find \ Location

  * At the Soldiers Rest bonfire in the Forest of The Fallen Giants it is out and to the left on top of a wooden structure in a chest. (You will need the key from The Last Giant to access this area)

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with titanite shards.
