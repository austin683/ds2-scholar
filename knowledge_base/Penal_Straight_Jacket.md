_"Straightjacket of the Lost Sinner. A tightly cinched belt presses against the waist."_

_"By now, no one knows who this was used to punish, or for what reason."_

### Location

  * Buy from [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer) after defeating the [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille) boss, [Lost Sinner](/Lost+Sinner). You must first spend 1000 souls at his shop and reload the area.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Penal Set](/Penal+Set).
  * Upgraded from [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
