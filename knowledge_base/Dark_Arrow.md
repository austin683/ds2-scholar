**Dark Arrow** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Dark Arrows** , projectiles imbued with the sinister essence of dark flame and coated with Dark Pine Resin. Armaments augmented with dark properties prove advantageous against adversaries susceptible to the shadows, including clerics and enchanted beings. A practical choice for individuals lacking magical expertise or facing depleted magical reservoirs. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Arrows imbued with dark flame. Tipped with Dark Pine Resin. Weapons fortified with dark are effective against those who fear the dark, such as clerics and magical creatures.  
> Useful in the short term for those who have learned no magic, or whose magic is exhausted."

### Dark Souls 2 Dark Arrow Usage

  * Ammunition for bows
  * 50 Physical Damage, 160 Dark Damage

### DKS2 Dark Arrow Location

  * Sold by [Grave Warden Agdayne](/Grave+Warden+Agdayne "Dark Souls 2 Grave Warden Agdayne") for 60 souls.

### Dark Arrow Notes and tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Dark Arrows have the shortest effective range of all arrow types, thus will start to arc noticeably sooner than others.
