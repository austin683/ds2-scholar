**Ruin Sentinel Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **The Ruin Sentinel Soul** , originating from the creation of the Jailer, embodies the essence of the Ruin Sentinel—a guardian devoid of a physical form, only an ethereal soul lingering within the armor. You can obtain this soul as a reward for overcoming the [Ruin Sentinels](/Ruin+Sentinel "Dark Souls 2 Ruin Sentinel") in [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Ruin Sentinel, a creation of the Jailer.  
> The Ruin Sentinel has no corporeal form. It is only an empty soul that haunts the armor.  
> Use the special soul of the Ruin Sentinel to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Ruin Sentinel Soul Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Consuming it gives you 6000 souls.
  * Can be traded with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") and 1,500 souls for:
  * [Heavy Homing Soul Arrow](/Heavy+Homing+Soul+Arrow#fex "Dark Souls 2 Heavy Homing Soul Arrow#fex")

### DKS2 Ruin Sentinel Soul Location

  * Obtained by defeating the [Ruin Sentinels](/Ruin+Sentinel "Dark Souls 2 Ruin Sentinel") in [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille").
