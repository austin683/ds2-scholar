_"A boldly-colored jester's cap/robe/gloves/tights. A nice bit of fun to try on..."_

_"Jesters are more than festival fixtures; some have a second face, hidden from public view."_

### Location

  * Bought from [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir) inside the [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Jester's Set](/Jester's+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab).
  * Good all round armor with a low weight. 
  * Chestpiece blocks critical hits (cannot backstab).
  * Requires 14 [Dexterity](/Dexterity) to wear.
