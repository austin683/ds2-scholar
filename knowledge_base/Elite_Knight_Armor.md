_"Helmet made of bradden steel. Provides sturdy defense, making it an old-time favorite of elite knights._

_"Bradden steel is used widely in Drangleic. This fine alloy is made from ores mined in the southern kingdom."_

### Location

  * Bought from [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer) merchant in [Majula](/Majula). One must spend more than 1,000 souls at his shop, leave, rest at a bonfire, and return.
  * The [Elite Knight Set](/Elite+Knight+Set) +5 can drop from the red phantoms that spawn in NG+ (or ascetic) in front of the last king's gate before throne watcher/defender and Nashandra. Unsure if guaranteed drop or not.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Elite Knight Set](/Elite+Knight+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab).
