**Poison Moss** is a type of healing consumable in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Poison Moss** , a subtle poison-absorbing moss clump. Consuming it counteracts poison and purifies the body. When poison accumulates to a point where it triggers, your health gradually decreases. To ensure survival, it's wise to keep a stock of Poison Moss within reach. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A faintly poisonous clump of moss.When ingested, it counteracts poison and cleanses one's insides.  
> If enough poison builds up in the body to break out and cause poisoning, your HP will start to decrease.  
> If you wish to stay alive, you would do well to keep a supply close to hand.

### Poison Moss Usage in DKS2

  * Reduces poison build-up and cures poison status.

### [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki") Poison Moss Location

  * Purchase from [Lonesome Gavlan](/Gavlan "Dark Souls 2 Gavlan")
  * Drop from [Rotten Vermin](/Rotten+Vermin "Dark Souls 2 Rotten Vermin")
  * Drop from [Plagued Rats](/Hunting+Rat "Dark Souls 2 Hunting Rat") in [Grave of Saints](/Grave+of+Saints "Dark Souls 2 Grave of Saints")
  * Drop from [Poison Butterflies](/Poison+Moth "Dark Souls 2 Poison Moth") in [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman%27s+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman%27s Copse")
