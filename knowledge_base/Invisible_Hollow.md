**Invisible Hollow** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. This enemy remains seated, neither moving nor attacking. It only takes one hit to become visible and perish, but locating them is challenging. The most effective method is to carry a light source and illuminate their shadows. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Invisible Hollow Information

This enemy sits down, neither moving nor attacking.  
They only take one hit to become visible and to die, but finding them is difficult.  
The best way to find them is to carry a light source and illuminate their shadows.

### Dark Souls 2 Invisible Hollow Location

  * There is one in [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants"), near the 2 fire bombers and the rapier guy. Up a ladder, to the right, look for the lit torch sconce. His shadow can be seen to the right of it.
