**Frozen Flower** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Frozen Flower** , a stone ornament resembling a flower. Its chill is sufficient to quell the most resolute heart. Unlocks the gateway to an unexplored route from the Shrine of Winter, discovered on the route to the Grand Castle. Provides entry to the DLC zone [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce"). [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> A stone ornament shaped like a flower.  
> Cold enough to dampen the greatest heart.  
> Opens the door to a new path from the Shrine of Winter, found on the road to the Great Castle

### Dark Souls 2 Frozen Flower Usage

  * Grants access to the DLC Area [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce").

### Frozen Flower Location in DKS2

  * Automatically added to player's inventory for [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King") DLC.
  * In **_Scholar of the First Sin_** , it is in Drangleic Castle, after the first bonfire, located past the first rightmost door in the ruin sentinels' room. The key can be found in a corpse on the room before going upstairs.
