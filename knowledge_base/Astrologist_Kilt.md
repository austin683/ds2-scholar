**Astrologist Kilt** is a **legging** and a part of [Astrologist's Set](/Astrologist's+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Astrologist Kilt Description

_"Robe bottoms worn by astrologists in Melfia._  
_Astrologists believe that magical powers_ _can be obtained at moments of special_ _heavenly alignment._

_Even the collective wisdom of the_ _Melfian Magic Academy cannot pull magic_ _out of the sky, but the ideas of the_ _astrologists led to the creation of_ _new and very useful magic devices."_

__

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Sold by [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan) in [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep) (18,6k) once his questline is fulfilled (be hollow) or once he's released via nearby lever (be human).
  * Dropped by [Astrologists](/Astrologist) in [Iron Passage](/Iron+Passage) (except for [hood](/Black+Hollow+Mage+Set)).
