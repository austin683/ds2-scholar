**Triclops Snake Troches** are a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Triclops Snake Troches** , troches exuding the scent of rust. Temporarily enhance petrify resistance. Verbal histories frequently cite Saint Elizabeth, whose uniquely crafted medicines and potions remain in widespread use today. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Troches that exude the scent of rust. Temporarily boosts petrify resist.  
> Verbal histories often mention Saint Elizabeth; her uniquely concocted medicines and potions are still widely used today.

### Triclops Snake Troches Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Boosts petrify resistance by 200 for 90 seconds

### Dark Souls 2 Triclops Snake Troches Location

  * Sold by The [Rat King](/Rat+King "Dark Souls 2 Rat King") for 2,000 souls each (Unlimited)
  * Found on a corpse at the bottom of a set of stairs in [Belfry Sol](/Belfry+Sol "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Sol"). Paired with a [Human Effigy](/Human+Effigy "Dark Souls 2 Human Effigy").
  * Occasional drop from the [Lion Clan Warriors](/Lion+Clan+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Lion Clan Warrior") in the [Shaded Ruins](/Shaded+Ruins "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Ruins").
