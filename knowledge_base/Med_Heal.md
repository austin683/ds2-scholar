**Med Heal** is a Miracle in Dark Souls 2. To cast a Miracle, you must use a chime or Special Weapons that can cast Miracle.

> "Superior spell to Heal.  
> Restores HP.  
>   
> The strength of miracles are influenced by one's faith.  
>   
> Miracles are tales of the Gods passed down long ago, but only a small number of the original tomes remain in their entirety, and most that exist are restorations."

### Acquired From

  * Purchase from [Licia of Lindeldt](/Licia+of+Lindeldt "Licia of Lindeldt") for 3000 souls.

### Notes

  * Spell also affects allies that step into range very shortly after you finish casting.
