_"Helmet worn by the ferocious Vengarl of Forossa. As Vengarl's helmet suggests, he was not unlike a mad beast on the battlefield."_

### Location

  * Once you've found the [Head of Vengarl](/Head+of+Vengarl) in a clearing in the [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods), exhaust all of his dialogue options. Once you get access to his menu, be sure to learn his [Gesture](/Gestures), but also keep selecting the talk option. Do it repeatedly until he thanks you for your time and gives you Vengarl's Helm.
  * After defeating [The Duke's Dear Freja ](/The+Duke%27s+Dear+Freja)go to her primal bonfire. It's guarded by Vengarl's headless body. Defeat him and talk to his head in Shaded Woods. He give you remaining armor pieces, and also sells his swords.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Vengarl's Set](/Vengarl's+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
  * Helm gives 2+ [Vitality](/Vitality).
