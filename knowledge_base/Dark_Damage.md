**Dark Damage** is one of the [Damage Types](/Damage+Types "Dark Souls 2 Damage Types") in **Dark Souls 2**. It is one of the seven main Damage Types and belongs to the Elemental Damage category. Damage Types are a major part of the calculation that determines how much damage is dealt in [Combat](/Combat "Dark Souls 2 Combat") and are affected by many factors including your equipment, spells and temporary buffs.

### Dark Souls 2 Dark Damage Guide

**Dark Damage** is a type of Elemental Damage dealt by both players and enemies alike. Dark Damage is elemental damage that falls outside of [Magic Damage](/Magic+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Magic Damage"), [Fire Damage](/Fire+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Fire Damage"), and [Lightning Damage](/Lightning+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Lightning Damage") types. Dark Damage is often effective against most unarmored enemies and giants. It is a new damage type that was not present in _Dark Souls_.

Most [Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") can be upgraded via the Dark Upgrade Path in order to imbue them with Dark Damage properties. There are also Weapons that can be found which innately deal Dark Damage. See the [Upgrades](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades") and individual weapon pages for details.

Most offensive [Hexes](/Hexes "Dark Souls 2 Hexes") deal Dark Damage.
