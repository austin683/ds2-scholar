**Petrifying Statue Cluster** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Encountered in the [Cave of the Dead](/Cave+of+the+Dead "Dark Souls 2 Cave of the Dead"), the **Petrifying Statue Cluster** is a lizard adorned with statues on its back, which unleashes petrifying darts at nearby players. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Petrifying Statue Cluster Information in Dark Souls 2

A lizard with a set of statues on its back that shoot petrifying darts at nearby players.

### Petrifying Statue Cluster Location in Dark Souls 2

  * [Cave of the Dead](/Cave+of+the+Dead "Dark Souls 2 Cave of the Dead")

### DS2 Petrifying Statue Cluster Strategy Tips

  * The statues themselves are indestructible, but the underlying creature can be damaged with a 'striking' type weapons, arrows, and spells or items with splash damage (such as Firebombs).
  * With the right positioning, whips are easily able to hit the creatures.
  * It seems to be easier to land a hit when striking their legs or behind from an angle.
