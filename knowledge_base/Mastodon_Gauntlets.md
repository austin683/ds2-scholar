### Mastodon Gauntlets Description

"Gauntlets worn by the Primal Knights of Drangleic castle.  
Their weight would normally crush a man, but the Primal Knights wear them as if they were silk, so fearsome is their brutal strength.  
The King restored a forbidden, long-lost art to create these inhuman abominations."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Dropped by the [Primal Knights](/Primal+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Primal Knight") at [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle").

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with titanite
  * Gloves and boots has the highest phyiscal defense among all armors under normal titanite upgrading path.
  * While [Gyrm Warrior Set](/Gyrm+Warrior+Set "Dark Souls 2 Gyrm Warrior Set")has the highest physical defense for headpiece (greathelm) and armor, [Mastodon Set](/Mastodon+Set "Dark Souls 2 Mastodon Set") has the highest physical defense for gloves and boot, among all armors under normal titanite upgrading path.
