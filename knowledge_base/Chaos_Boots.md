**Chaos Boots** is a **Legging** and a part of [Chaos Set](/Chaos+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Chaos Boots Description

_"Leggings worn by a chaos sorcerer._  
_He calls himself Navlaan, now."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Given to after completing royal sorcerer Navlaans quest when you talk to him as hollow
  * Can be purchased from Merchant Hag Melentia after you kill Navlaan

### Notes

  * Upgraded with Twinkling Titanite
  * The hood improves casting speed and increases intelligence by +3.
  * Requires 16 Int and 16 Faith to use.
  * The chest piece offers the highest fire defense in the game when fully upgraded.
