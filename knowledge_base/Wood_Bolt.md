**Wood Bolt** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Wood Bolt** , budget-friendly wooden bolts. While lacking in intimidation, they offer high portability and ease of use. Crossbow operation necessitates the equipping of bolts, which are shorter than arrows. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Inexpensive wooden bolts. Not very intimidating, but highly portable  
> and easy to use. Use of a crossbow requires the equipping of bolts, which are shorter than arrows.

### Wood Bolt Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Crossbow ammunition
  * 70 Physical Damage
