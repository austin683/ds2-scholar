_"Armor-Distinctively shaped apparel worn by the Knights of Catarina._

_Outside Catarina, they are often ridiculed for their onion-like shape, infuriating the proud knights, but masterfully forged curved design makes it very effective for parrying."_

### Location

  * Rare drop from NPC Dark Spirit Catarina Knight in forest of the giants (NG+).  

    * He also drops Heavy Cross Bow +5 and Broadsword +5
  * SOTFS: Found in [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce") (Crown of the Ivory King DLC), in the frozen mimic chest in the room directly across the bridge from the Expulsion Chamber bonfire. 

### Notes

  * Part of the [Catarina Set](/Catarina+Set "Dark Souls 2 Catarina Set").
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
