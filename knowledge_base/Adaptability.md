**Adaptability** is a primary [attribute](/Stats "Dark Souls 2 Stats") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Adaptability** determines your character's status effect resistances, agility, poise, and poison bonus. Attributes can be increased by assigning points into them when [Leveling Up](/Level "Dark Souls 2 Level").

### Adaptability Information

Attribute governing defense and [agility](/agility "Dark Souls 2 agility"). Boosts poison resist and bleed resist.

  * **Raises poison bonus**
  * **Raises poison, bleed, petrify, curse resistance**
  * **Raises[Agility](/Agility#fex "Dark Souls 2 Agility#fex")**
  * **Raises[Poise](/Poise#fex "Dark Souls 2 Poise#fex")**
  * Slightly increases HP for each point: +2 HP (0-20), +1 HP (21-50)
  * Adaptability has no set soft cap, because all secondary attributes that it affects (such as Agility, resistances, poison bonus, etc) it shares with other primary attributes.
  * The Bandit begins with the lowest Adaptability at 3 and the Explorer starts with the highest Adaptability at 12.
