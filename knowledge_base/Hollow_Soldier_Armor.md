_"Armor worn by Hollowed infantry."_

_"A piece of basic, minimal equipment. Worse yet, they're worn and nearly falling apart. Not recommended unless your options are spent."_

### Location

  * Drops off of the hollowed soldier's located in the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants") area.  

### Notes

  * Part of the [Hollow Soldier Set](/Hollow+Soldier+Set "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Soldier Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+SLab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite SLab").
