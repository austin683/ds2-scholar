#  Exile Holding Cells

# 

* * *

##  Location:

## 
    
    
    |  |
    | --- |
    | Exile Holding Cells |
    

This area is consisted of several cells which are used to imprison those who were exiled to the [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille), according to it's name. There's a particular cell with broken iron bars and a bonfire in the middle of the room.   
This area is accessible after defeating [Flexile Sentry](/Flexile+Sentry) in the ship in the [No-man's Wharf](/No-man%27s+Wharf), after riding the elevator.   
  

##  Bosses:

None   
  

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here respawns the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * Soul Vessel - In the room with the Pharros Lockstone door. The wooden chest will respawn with the Soul Vessel in it every time you ascetic this bonfire.
  * All enemies.

  

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here spawns the following new items/enemies:

  * [Red Phantom Jailer](/Gaoler) at the bridge.
