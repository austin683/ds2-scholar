**Brighstone Key** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Brightstone Key** , a key utilized in Brightstone Cove Tseldora. Lord Tseldora, an eccentric individual fascinated by spiders, established a town and amassed a personal fortune through the mining of brightstone. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> Key used in the Brightstone Cove Tseldora. The eccentric Lord Tseldora, known for his fascination with spiders, built a town and a personal fortune by mining brightstone. One day, the town was overrun, but Lord Tseldora only stood by and watched, eerly contented.

### Brighstone Key Usage in DKS2

  * Leads to a chest containing a [Fire Seed](/Fire+Seed "Dark Souls 2 Fire Seed"), [Great Fireball](/Great+Fireball "Dark Souls 2 Great Fireball"), and the [Black Knight Ultra Greatsword](/Black+Knight+Ultra+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 Black Knight Ultra Greatsword") in the room where spiders fall from the ceiling (on the way to [The Duke's Dear Freja](/The+Duke%27s+Dear+Freja "Dark Souls 2 The Duke%27s Dear Freja") boss fight).
  * When opening the door to the room that contains the chest, it will trigger a spider to spawn. Again when entering the room containing the chest, approx. six spiders will spawn in the previous room.

### Dark Souls 2 Brighstone Key Location

  * Obtained as a drop from [Duke Tseldora](/Duke+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Duke Tseldora") after killing him in the [Lord's Private Chamber](/Lord%27s+Private+Chamber "Dark Souls 2 Lord%27s Private Chamber"). _(Note: If you kill him and do not pick up the items, you can use a bonfire ascetic on the "Lower Brightstone Cove" bonfire to fight the Duke's dear freja again, and there he will be)._
