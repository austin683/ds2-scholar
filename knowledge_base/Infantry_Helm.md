_"Helmet worn by Drangleic infantry._  
_Apiece of basic, minimal equipment. At least it's light and easy-to-use."_

### Location

  * Find this helm in the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants"), in a metal chest above the courtyard filled with explosive barrels.  
  

### Notes

  * Part of the [Infantry Set](/Infantry+Set "Dark Souls 2 Infantry Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores").
