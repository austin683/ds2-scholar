**Ironclad Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Imported Set](/Imported+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Ironclad Leggings Description

"Leggings worn by Ironclad Soldiers. Provides high defense, but is so heavy that it requires great strength to move"  
"The Ironclad Soldiers were minions created by the Old Iron King, their life granted by an enchantment of souls. By now this is again an iron husk, and fit to wear."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Drops off of Ironclad Soldiers in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep)

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shards](/Upgrades#Ore), [Large Titanite Shards](/Upgrades#Ore), [Upgrades](/Upgrades#Ore634635623624Titanite+Chunks), [Titanite Slabs](/Upgrades#Ore)
  * Wearing the Ironclad Armor (chest) makes you immune to the backstabbing animation.
  * Since the developers of Dark Souls love the Berserk series so much they often sneak in equipment that looks like equipment used in the Manga. This set of armour looks like bazuso's from Berserk. (Example)- 
  * Set requires 14 Strength.
