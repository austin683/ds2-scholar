**Poison Throwing Knife** is a type of [Projectile](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2).** **Poison Throwing Knife** , a projectile designed to poison the target rather than cause direct harm. This uncomplicated weapon is easily deployed but possesses limited range and deals minimal damage. [Projectiles](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") are offensive items that are usually thrown at hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") to inflict a small amount of damage.

> A throwing knife. Intended to poison the target more than inflict direct damage.  
> This simple weapon is easily utilized, but has limited range and deals little damage. However, if the knife can cause poison, the target will take damage for some time.

### DKS2 Poison Throwing Knife Usage

  * Use from the items belt to deal physical and poison damage.
  * Can be used locked or unlocked, and can be manually aimed with binoculars.
  * Physical and poison damage both scale with Dexterity.
  * The value is pretty similar to a poison arrow, so it will be around 300 

### Poison Throwing Knife Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Rare drop from claw-wielding [Manikins](/Masked+Manikin "Dark Souls 2 Masked Manikin") in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak").
  * Drop from [Abandoned Hollows](/Abandoned+Hollow "Dark Souls 2 Abandoned Hollow") in [the Gutter](/The+Gutter "Dark Souls 2 The Gutter").
  * Sold by [Lonesome Gavlan](/Gavlan "Dark Souls 2 Gavlan") for 350 souls each. (20 only each encounter, then an unlimited supply in [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros").)
  * Fifteen in a chest to the right of the gate to [Velstadt, the Royal Aegis](/Velstadt,+the+Royal+Aegis "Dark Souls 2 Velstadt, the Royal Aegis")' arena in [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt").
  * 20 on a corpse in [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon's+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon's Sanctum") in the area leading to [Flynn's Ring](/Flynn%27s+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Flynn%27s Ring").
