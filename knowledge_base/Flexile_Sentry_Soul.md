**Flexile Sentry Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Flexile Sentry Soul** , the soul of the Flexile Sentry. This ruthless creature is designed to punish the Undead. Consume its unique soul for a substantial number of souls or employ it in the creation of a valuable item. You can obtain this soul by defeating the [Flexile Sentry](/Flexile+Sentry "Dark Souls 2 Flexile Sentry") at [No-Man's Wharf](/No-Man's+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No-Man's Wharf"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Flexile Sentry. The Flexile Sentry is a merciless creature whose purpose is to punish the Undead. Use the special soul of the Flexile Sentry to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth

### Flexile Sentry Soul Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Consuming it gives you 6000 Souls.
  * Can be traded with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") and 1,500 souls each for: 
    * [Warped Sword](/Warped+Sword#fex "Dark Souls 2 Warped Sword#fex")
    * [Arced Sword](/Arced+Sword#fex "Dark Souls 2 Arced Sword#fex")
    * [Barbed Club](/Barbed+Club#fex "Dark Souls 2 Barbed Club#fex")

### DKS2 Flexile Sentry Soul Location

  * Obtained by defeating the [Flexile Sentry](/Flexile+Sentry "Dark Souls 2 Flexile Sentry") at [No-Man's Wharf](/No-Man%27s+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No-Man%27s Wharf").
