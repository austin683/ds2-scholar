**Divine Blessing** is a type of healing consumable in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Divine Blessing** , sacred water imbued with divine favor. Remedies status ailments and completely replenishes HP. This water bears the blessing of an ancient goddess, whose name has faded into obscurity. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Holy water endowed with a divine blessing. Cures status effects and fully restores HP.  
> Water blessed by an ancient goddess. Her name is long forgotten, and the Magic Academy of Melfia denies even her existence.  
> In any age, there are those who refuse to see reason. It is their meddling that distorts the truth.

### Divine Blessing Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Consume to fully restore HP and cure any negative status effects.

### Dark Souls 2 Divine Blessing Location

  * [Forest of Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants"), above the bonfire.
  * [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula"), as you enter the town on your left.
  * [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide%27s Tower of Flame"), at the end of the broken stairs as you head towards [Cathedral of Blue](/Cathedral+of+Blue "Dark Souls 2 Cathedral of Blue")
  * Sold by [Chancellor Wellager](/Chancellor+Wellager "Dark Souls 2 Chancellor Wellager") in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle") for 6000 souls in limited quantity
  * [Memory of Jeigh](/Memory+of+Jeigh "Dark Souls 2 Memory of Jeigh"). Can be farmed by burning [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic")
  * Dropped by [Grand Tusk](/Grand+Tusk "Dark Souls 2 Grand Tusk") in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora")
  * 2 in [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce") in an iron chest past the fountain and on a rooftop. Nearby is a [Soul Vessel](/Soul+Vessel "Dark Souls 2 Soul Vessel") and the [Sorcerer's Twinblade](/Sorcerer%27s+Twinblade "Dark Souls 2 Sorcerer%27s Twinblade").
  * 1 in [Black Gulch](/Black+Gulch "Dark Souls 2 Black Gulch") in a wooden chest past the worms in a cave.
  * Can be traded for Smooth and Silky Stone by [Dyna & Tillo ](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo")at [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt").
  * Can be picked up repetitively with Bonfire Ascetics in NG+ and beyond in the Giant Lord Boss area, the Memory of Jeigh.
  * 2 are given by the [Milfanito](/Milfanito "Dark Souls 2 Milfanito") in [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana") (one in the first building of the level and the other next to its end, inside the unhollowing shrine, each gives 1 blessing) by talking to them after freeing the trapped one in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle").
