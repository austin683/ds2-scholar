**Ring of Knowledge** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). The **Ring of Knowledge** , bestowed by the High Priest of Volgen, augments intelligence. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A ring blessed by the High Priest of Volgen. Increases intelligence. The clerics of the great city of Volgen are perennially entrenched in scandalous power struggles, but among them remain a few strong-willed, upstanding noblemen.

### Dark Souls 2 Ring of Knowledge Effects

**Durability** : 120

**Weight** :

  * 0.5

**Effect:**

  * Increases intelligence by 5

### Ring of Knowledge Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Sold by [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for 28,000 souls.

### DS2 Ring of Knowledge Ring Tips and Notes

  * Wearing this ring will **not** allow you to talk to NPCs that have intelligence requirements.
  * It will, however, allow you to cast spells you wouldn't otherwise be able to.
  * It will allow you to use weapons that require intelligence properly
