_Although it appears to be common armor, it has in fact been meticulously customized. Belonged to mild-mannered Pate._

_This has been considerably altered. Perhaps it was pillaged._

### Location

  * Sold by [Merchant Hag Malentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia) after killing [Mild-Mannered Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate).
  * Given to the player after helping [Mild-Mannered Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate) in his fight with [Creighton The Wanderer](/Creighton+the+Wanderer).
  * You can also obtain this armor as well as his shield and spear, by completing Pate's story which ends in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Pate's Set](/Pate's+Set).
