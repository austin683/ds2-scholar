### Location

  * Becomes available for 5,200 souls, after spending 15,000 with [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Alva Set](/Alva+Set "Dark Souls 2 Alva Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard")
  * "Alva" is a Swedish name, although in reality it is only used by females.
