**Fragrant Branch of Yore** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Fragrant Branch** , a tree branch emitting a faint sweet aroma. Use it to restore life to petrified entities, but prolonged inhalation may lead to coughing and nausea. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A Fragrant tree branch with a faint sweet smell. Restore the life of things turned to stone. Extended inhalation of the branch's scent can lead to coughing and nausea.

### Fragrant Branch of Yore — Where to Find (SotFS)

* **Merchant Hag Melentia** — 12,000 souls (Forest of Fallen Giants / Cardinal Tower area)
* **No-Man's Wharf** — chest in the house with poisoned jars, up the stairs from the bridge near Flexile Sentry
* **Lost Bastille** — chest near Lucatiel of Mirrah's tower (also contains Covetous Silver Serpent Ring)
* **Sinners' Rise** — chest after defeating The Lost Sinner
* **The Gutter** — corpse hanging from a plank
* **Black Gulch** — at the start, dropping down from The Gutter
* **Harvest Valley** — inside a cave in the first poison pit
* **Iron Keep** — Magerold of Lanafir sells one for 7,500 souls
* **Shaded Woods** — loot on a corpse in the room where you rescue Weaponsmith Ornifex
* **Shaded Ruins** — in the chest containing the Lion Mage Set
* **Shaded Woods** — talk to Manscorpion Tark with Ring of Whispers after defeating the area boss
* **Brightstone Cove Tseldora** — dropped by Duke Tseldora after the Duke's Dear Freja fight
* **Undead Crypt** — iron chest just before Velstadt, left of the stairs
* **Undead Crypt** — dropped by one of two Insolent Spirits that appear after lighting the statue brazier
* **Forest of Fallen Giants** — near Memory of Jeigh (requires King's Ring), body hanging off the ledge
* **Aldia's Keep** — Mimic in the last ceiling cage (closest to Guardian Dragon boss)
* **Dragon Shrine** — chest on the ledge after dropping down past Dragonfang Villard

### Dark Souls 2 Fragrant Branch of Yore Usage

  * Unpetrify the player or NPCs.

#### Scholar of the First Sin

  * **[Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt"):** Dissolve a statue in front of the final door - now guards an [Estus Flask Shard](/Estus+Flask+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Estus Flask Shard").
  * **[Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula") :** Dissolve [Rosabeth of Melfia](/Rosabeth+of+Melfia "Dark Souls 2 Rosabeth of Melfia") at the entrance to the Shaded Woods for access to [Pyromancies](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies") and the ability to upgrade the [Pyromancy Flames](/Flames "Dark Souls 2 Flames"). This is required to progress into the Shaded Woods.
  * **[Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide%27s Tower of Flame"):** Dissolve a statue in the flooded corridors after the [Dragonrider](/Dragonrider "Dark Souls 2 Dragonrider") fight and before the [No Man's Wharf](/No-Man%27s+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No-Man%27s Wharf") bonfire to gain access to an [Estus Flask Shard](/estus+flask+shard "Dark Souls 2 estus flask shard").
  * **[Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille"):** Entrance to the [Ruin Sentinels](/Ruin+Sentinel "Dark Souls 2 Ruin Sentinel") boss fight - leads to the [Servants' Quarters](/Servants%27+Quarters "Dark Souls 2 Servants%27 Quarters") bonfire and Belfry Luna (past a [Pharros' Contraption](/Pharros%27+Contraption "Dark Souls 2 Pharros%27 Contraption"))
  * **[Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille"):** Dissolve the petrified [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") to have access to his spells, armors, and boss soul items, as well as the [Straid's Cell](/Straid%27s+Cell "Dark Souls 2 Straid%27s Cell") bonfire.
  * **[Black Gulch](/Black+Gulch "Dark Souls 2 Black Gulch"):** Dissolve a statue blocking the last bonfire (second) as well as a summon sign for [Lucatiel of Mirrah](/Lucatiel+of+Mirrah "Dark Souls 2 Lucatiel of Mirrah").
  * **[Shaded Ruins](/Shaded+Ruins "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Ruins"):** Near [Manscorpion Tark](/Manscorpion+Tark "Dark Souls 2 Manscorpion Tark"), behind a tunnel leading from the sand pit - dissolving spawns the body of [Vengarl](/Head+of+Vengarl "Dark Souls 2 Head of Vengarl"). Killing it unlocks the [Head of Vengarl](/Head+of+Vengarl "Dark Souls 2 Head of Vengarl") as a summonable NPC.
  * **[Shaded Ruins](/Shaded+Ruins "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Ruins"):** Straight after Vengarl, there is another one. Dissolve it to create a shortcut back to the bridge and find a chest containing [Sublime Bone Dust](/Sublime+Bone+Dust "Dark Souls 2 Sublime Bone Dust"). (this only opens the shortcut, the chest can be reached from the bridge)
  * **[Shaded Ruins](/Shaded+Ruins "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Ruins"):** Dissolve a statue of a lion in front of a metal chest, containing the [Repair](/Repair "Dark Souls 2 Repair")[Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries"), 3 [Bleeding Serums](/Bleeding+Serum "Dark Souls 2 Bleeding Serum"), and 3 [Human Effigies](/Human+Effigies "Dark Souls 2 Human Effigies").
  * **[Shaded Ruins](/Shaded+Ruins "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Ruins"): **Dissolve a lion statue above [Darkdiver Grandahl](/Darkdiver+Grandahl "Dark Souls 2 Darkdiver Grandahl"), you'll get the [Lion Mage Set](/Lion+Mage+Set "Dark Souls 2 Lion Mage Set") and another branch.
  * **[Shaded Ruins](/Shaded+Ruins "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Ruins"): **Dissolve the statue near the tree bridge at the Shaded Ruins Bonfire and you'll get the [Fang Key](/Fang+Key "Dark Souls 2 Fang Key").
  * **[Shaded Ruins](/Shaded+Ruins "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Ruins"):** Dissolve the statue at the back of the [Shaded Ruins](/Shaded+Ruins "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Ruins") area where the golden lion roams, and when you kill the enemy he drops the [Warlock Mask](/Warlock+Mask "Dark Souls 2 Warlock Mask")
  * **[Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana")** : Dissolve the statues blocking the entrance to the Milfanito shrine (where you can pray to regain humanity)

#### Post King's Ring

  * **[Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s keep"):** Dissolve the Cyclops for the [Dragon Acolyte Mask](/Dragon+Acolyte+Mask "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Acolyte Mask").
  * **[Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s keep"):** Dissolve the left-hand statue in front of the large caged basilisk for 2 x bonfire ascetics.
  * **[Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s keep"):** Dissolve the right-hand statue in front of the large caged basilisk for a 2 x [Wilted Dusk Herb](/Wilted+Dusk+Herb "Dark Souls 2 Wilted Dusk Herb") and 2 x [Elizabeth Mushroom](/Elizabeth+Mushroom "Dark Souls 2 Elizabeth Mushroom").
  * **[Dragon Aerie](/Dragon+Aerie "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Aerie") :** Dissolve the statue on the rope bridge blocking the entrance to the [Dragon Aerie](/Dragon+Aerie "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Aerie").

#### Regular Version

  * Needed to unpetrify [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis").
  * Needed to unpetrify [Rosabeth of Melfia](/Rosabeth+of+Melfia "Dark Souls 2 Rosabeth of Melfia").
  * Unpetrify the Ogre in Aldia's keep under the dragon skeleton to get a "Dragon Acolyte Mask".
  * Unpetrify 3 enemies in the 3rd area of the [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Forest "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Forest"). 
    * Past [Manscorpion Tark](/Manscorpion+Tark "Dark Souls 2 Manscorpion Tark") to get the [Black Knight Halberd.](/Black+Knight+Halberd "Dark Souls 2 Black Knight Halberd") (Not needed, can jump to this area from near the bonfire.)
    * In a broken building towards the boss, for a "Lion Mage" armor set and an extra branch (not needed but VERY hard to get without the branch).
    * Near the bonfire, drops the [Fang Key](/Fang+Key "Dark Souls 2 Fang Key").

### Fragrant Branch of Yore Location in DKS2

#### Scholar of the First Sin

  * **[Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia"):** Only one (12,000).
  * **[No-man's Wharf](/No-man's+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No-man's Wharf"):** In a chest in the house with poisoned jars. Found to the right up the stairs from the bridge that leads to the [Flexile Sentry](/Flexile+Sentry "Dark Souls 2 Flexile Sentry").
  * **[Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille"):** Near the tower where [Lucatiel of Mirrah](/Lucatiel+of+Mirrah "Dark Souls 2 Lucatiel of Mirrah") is, in a chest with a [Covetous Silver Serpent Ring](/Covetous+Silver+Serpent+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Covetous Silver Serpent Ring") and a [Human Effigy](/Human+Effigy "Dark Souls 2 Human Effigy")
  * **[Sinners' Rise](/Sinners'+Rise "Dark Souls 2 Sinners' Rise"):** In a chest after defeating [The Lost Sinner](/Lost+Sinner "Dark Souls 2 Lost Sinner").
  * **[The Gutter](/The+Gutter "Dark Souls 2 The Gutter"):** on a corpse hanging from a plank.
  * **[Black Gulch](/Black+Gulch "Dark Souls 2 Black Gulch"):** At the start, jumping down from [The Gutter](/The+Gutter "Dark Souls 2 The Gutter").
  * **[Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley"):** Found in a cave in the first poison pit.
  * **[Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep"):** [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") \- Only one (7,500).
  * **[Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods"):** Another in the room as loot on a corpse when you rescue [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex").
  * **[Shaded Ruins](/Shaded+Ruins "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Ruins"):** In the chest containing the [Lion Mage Set](/Lion+Mage+Set "Dark Souls 2 Lion Mage Set").
  * **[Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods"):** Talk to [Manscorpion Tark](/Manscorpion+Tark "Dark Souls 2 Manscorpion Tark") with the Ring of Whispers equipped (bought from [Sweet Shalquoir](/Sweet+Shalquoir "Dark Souls 2 Sweet Shalquoir") in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula")) after you've defeated the area boss.
  * **[Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora"):** A branch is still dropped by the [Duke Tseldora](/Duke+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Duke Tseldora") in the [Lord's Private Chamber](/Lord's+Private+Chamber "Dark Souls 2 Lord's Private Chamber") after the fight with [The Duke's Dear Freja](/The+Duke's+Dear+Freja "Dark Souls 2 The Duke's Dear Freja").
  * **[Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt"):** After lighting the statue brazier, two Insolent Spirits will appear in the room with the [Leydia Witches](/Leydia+Witch "Dark Souls 2 Leydia Witch") after the lone Imperious Knight. One of them drops a branch.
  * **[Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt"):** One in an iron chest, just before [Velstadt](/Velstadt,+the+Royal+Aegis "Dark Souls 2 Velstadt, the Royal Aegis"), on the left of the stairs.

###### Post King's Ring

  * **[Forest of the Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants"):** Near the Memory of Jeigh behind the King's Gate, the body hanging off the ledge now contains 2 x Twinkling Titanite and a branch.
  * **[Aldia's Keep](/Aldia's+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia's Keep"):** The last cage (closest to the Guardian Dragon boss) hanging from the ceiling has a Mimic that drops a branch.
  * **[Dragon Shrine](/Dragon+Shrine "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Shrine"):** after dropping down to the ledge after obtaining the Petrified Egg and defeating Dragonfang Villard, it is in a chest along with the Crystal Magic Weapon Sorcery.

#### Regular Version

  * [Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley"): One in the first poison fog area after [The Poison Pool](/Poison+Pool "Dark Souls 2 Poison Pool") bonfire, inside the cave guarded by the [Banedigger](/Banedigger "Dark Souls 2 Banedigger"). Once inside you'll see the item on a body hanging over a ledge, watch out for skeletons.
  * [Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley"): After progressing past [The Mines](/The+Mines "Dark Souls 2 The Mines") bonfire you'll see a room full of poison fog-filled pits and an item on a ledge. Enter the tunnel nearest to this ledge, and be ready to fight or run as you make an immediate right. In the quarry area with 4 sickle-wielding enemies, you'll see several boarded-up tunnels, the one you want is second from the right on the top level. Make a right turn inside the tunnel and you'll find yourself back out on the original ledge with the branch.
  * [The Gutter](/The+Gutter "Dark Souls 2 The Gutter"): Before the Bonfire at Black Gulch.
  * [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods"): Talk to [Manscorpion Tark](/Manscorpion+Tark "Dark Souls 2 Manscorpion Tark") with the [Ring of Whispers](/Ring+of+Whispers "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Whispers") equipped (bought from [Sweet Shalquoir](/Sweet+Shalquoir "Dark Souls 2 Sweet Shalquoir") in Majula) after you've defeated the area boss.
  * [Shaded Woods:](/Shaded+Woods#.UzgyufldUmM "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods#.UzgyufldUmM") One in the chest containing the [Lion Mage Set](/Lion+Mage+Set#.UzgzqfldUmM "Dark Souls 2 Lion Mage Set#.UzgzqfldUmM") (using a branch on the lion warrior outside will allow access).
  * [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora"): Dropped by the Hollow Duke of Tseldora in the Lord's Private Chamber after defeating Duke's Dear Freja.
  * [Sinners' Rise](/Sinners'+Rise "Dark Souls 2 Sinners' Rise"): Right next to the [Primal Bonfire](/Primal+Bonfire "Dark Souls 2 Primal Bonfire") (in a chest after defeating [Lost Sinner](/Lost+Sinner "Dark Souls 2 Lost Sinner")).
  * [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep"): [Magerold of Lanafir's](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") Shop (Cost: 7500 Souls).

### Dark Souls 2 Fragrant Branch of Yore Notes and Tips

  * Upon entering a new NG cycle, even if branches are present in your inventory, they cannot be used to unpetrify NPCs and enemies until the respective branches are picked up again in the current playthrough.

  * In Scholar of the First Sin, there are 17 branches and 17 statues to be dissolved per NG cycle.
