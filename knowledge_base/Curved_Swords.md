**Curved Swords** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are a type of [Weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") that's very similar to [Straight Swords](/Straight+Swords "Dark Souls 2 Straight Swords"), have shorter range. They are slightly faster than the Straight Swords, well-suited in confined spaces against lightly armored enemies, taking away their mobility to dodge in narrow areas. Curved Swords have a versatile movesets, mostly consisting of quick attacks in succession. Most of its moves consists of Slashing attacks, and a few of them have Thrusting attacks. Some Curved Swords can be power stanced to make a great combination. This page covers a list of all Curved Swords in Dark Souls 2.
