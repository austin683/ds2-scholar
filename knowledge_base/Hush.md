**Hush** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Hush** , a sorcery aiding in covert activities by diminishing the sound produced by its caster. In Mirrah, an eastern land known for proud knights, there exists a secretive order of shadow knights tasked with discreet and unspeakable missions. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "A sorcery that facilitates surreptitious activity. Reduces the sound made by its caster.  
>   
> The eastern land of Mirrah is not only the home of proud knights. There is also a traditional order of shadow knights, who quietly carry out unspeakable tasks."

### Dark Souls 2 Hush Acquired From

  * [Way of the Blue](/Way+of+the+Blue "Dark Souls 2 Way of the Blue"), Rank 2 reward.

Given by [Crestfallen Saulden](/Crestfallen+Saulden "Dark Souls 2 Crestfallen Saulden") for leveling up in [Way of Blue](/Way+of+the+Blue "Dark Souls 2 Way of the Blue") Covenant.

  * You can find the spell in [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille"), in the room where you fight the [ruin sentinel](/Ruin+Sentinel "Dark Souls 2 Ruin Sentinel") boss. there are 4 hidden doors at the bottom of the room. The left one has a ladder with a chest. 

Just push the action button on this wall.

### Hush Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Greatly reduces the range at which you will be detected by enemies who aren't facing you.
  * Lasts 90 seconds though this does vary with [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence").
  * Overwrites [Fall Control](/Fall+Control "Dark Souls 2 Fall Control"), see [Buffs & Debuffs](/Buffs+&+Debuffs "Dark Souls 2 Buffs & Debuffs").
