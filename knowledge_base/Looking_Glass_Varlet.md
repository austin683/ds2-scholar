**Looking Glass Varlet** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. The Looking Glass Varlets, emerging from mirrors, can appear either through the [Looking Glass Knight](/Looking+Glass+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Knight") boss's mirror shield or from the mirrors scattered throughout [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia's+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia's Keep"). [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Looking Glass Varlet Information in Dark Souls 2

Phantoms which emerge from mirrors. They can come out of the [Looking Glass Knight](/Looking+Glass+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Knight") boss's mirror shield, or out of the mirrors in [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s Keep").

### Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Varlet Location

  * [King's Passage](/King%27s+Passage "Dark Souls 2 King%27s Passage")
  * [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s Keep")
