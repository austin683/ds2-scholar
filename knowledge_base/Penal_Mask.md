_"Mask of the Lost Sinner. The spikes pointing inward suggest that this was not only used to blind prisoners, but to torture them as well."_

_"By now, no one knows who this was used to punish, or for what reason."_

### Location

  * Purchased from [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") after defeating the [Lost Sinner](/Lost+Sinner "Dark Souls 2 Lost Sinner") at [Sinners' Rise](/Sinners'+Rise "Dark Souls 2 Sinners' Rise"). You must first spend 1000 souls at his shop and reload the area.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Penal Set](/Penal+Set "Dark Souls 2 Penal Set").
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * Increases equip load by 3%
