##  Bonfire Location:

Located in [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods").   

##  Bosses:

  * [Scorpioness Najka](/Scorpioness+Najka "Dark Souls 2 Scorpioness Najka")

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here respawns the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * Boss: [Scorpioness Najka](/Scorpioness+Najka "Dark Souls 2 Scorpioness Najka")
  * Torch x4
  * Flame Butterfly
  * Human Effigy x3
  * Bleeding Serum x3
  * Titanite Chunk
  * Petrified Dragon Bone
  * Divine Blessing
  * Soul of a Brave Warrior x4
  * Skeptic's Spice
  * Pharros' Lockstone x2
  * Magic bolt x30
  * Lion Clan Warrior
  * Basilisk
  * Giant Basilisk (drops a petrified dragon bone, just like in NG)
  * Crystal Lizard

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here spawns the following new items/enemies:

  * Twinkling Titanite (x3 in NG+)
  * Item
  * Enemy
  * Enemy

##  Special Notes:

  * As with any bonfire Ascetic use, the wooden chests contain loot again, but the metal chests dont.
  * Any Lion Clan Warriors you unpetrified, stay unpetrified.
