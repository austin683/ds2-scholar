**Bone King Set** is an Attire or Armor in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds).

  
  
  
  
_"Skeleton lord Helmet/Armor/Gauntlets/Boots.High dark defense."_   
_Crown: "The Old Iron King led his best men on Undead hunts, but their memories were purged in rebirth."_   
_Rest of Set: "The very men who were ordered to hunt down the Undead were themselves hollowed, and founded a kingdom of bone."_

###  Set Location

  * Sold by [Titchy Gren](/Gren "Titchy Gren") (18,6k) who is located past [Executioner's Chariot](/Executioner%27s+Chariot) boss fight arena.
  * Must kill [Skeleton Lords](/The+Skeleton+Lords "Skeleton Lords") to unlock this armor set.

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Twinkling Titanite").
  * Cuffs offer second highest dark defense (equal to that of [Black Witch](/Black+Witch+Set) gloves and [Lion Mage](/Lion+Mage+Set) cuffs).
  * Boots offer second highest fire defense (equal to that of [Black](/Black+Set) boots, [Black Dragon](/Black+Dragon+Set) leggings and [Saint's](/Saint's+Set) boots and almost equal to that of [Alonne Knight](/Alonne+Knight+Set) and [Syan's](/Syan's+Set) leggings) and second highest dark defense.
  * Combine the robe with [Executioner's Leggings](/Executioner+Set) for aesthetical purposes.
