**Prisoned Sinner** is an [enemy](/enemies "Dark Souls 2 enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Prisoned Sinner** is a red phantom with low health that can be found at [Sinners' Rise](/Sinners'+Rise "Dark Souls 2 Sinners' Rise"). [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Prisoned Sinner Information in Dark Souls 2

_"In New Game Plus, a red phantom pyromancer, two of which appear in the room when The Lost Sinner drops to around 60% health. Note that the pyromancers do not appear with the use of a Bonfire Ascetic in NG.__"_
