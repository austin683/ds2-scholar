#  Twiggy Shei 

Updated Sep 13, 2015 8:36 pm 

  * Phantom that can be summoned for [Burnt Ivory King](/Burnt+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Burnt Ivory King") bossfight
  * Wields a [Dragonslayer Greatbow](/Dragonslayer+Greatbow "Dark Souls 2 Dragonslayer Greatbow") and fires [Iron Greatarrows](/Iron+Greatarrow "Dark Souls 2 Iron Greatarrow") from it, will use an [Estoc](/Estoc "Dark Souls 2 Estoc") if an enemy comes to close to him, much like Cerah the Old Explorer from the Cave of the Dead Dungeon Bossfight in the Sunken King DLC area
  * Wears [Old Bell Helm](/Old+Bell+Helm "Dark Souls 2 Old Bell Helm"), [Hard Leather Armor](/Hard+Leather+Armor "Dark Souls 2 Hard Leather Armor"), [Lion Mage Cuffs](/Lion+Mage+Cuffs "Dark Souls 2 Lion Mage Cuffs"), and [Alva Leggings](/Alva+Leggings "Dark Souls 2 Alva Leggings")
  * Does "Prostration" gesture upon summon
