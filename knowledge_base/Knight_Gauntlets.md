### Knight's Gauntlets Description

"Metal gauntlets worn by knights. Standard issue for new Drangleic knights.

Knights have long trusted these gauntlets for their excellent functionality"

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Purchasable from [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer).
  * At the bottom of the tower in [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame), past the sentinel in the flooded hall, on the way to [No-Man's Wharf](/No-Man%27s+Wharf).
  * +5 variants drop from black [phantom](/Phantoms) in [New Game Plus](/New+Game+Plus) near [Mild-mannered Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate) located in [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants).
