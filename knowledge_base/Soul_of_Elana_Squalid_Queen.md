**Soul of Elana, Squalid Queen** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of Elana, Squalid Queen** , the essence of Elana, the Squalid Queen, who resides beside the slumbering dragon in the Eternal Sanctum. This offspring of Dark shares company with the dragon, patiently accumulating souls in anticipation of the impending day of reckoning. This remarkable soul can be exchanged with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for the [Wrathful Axe](/Wrathful+Axe "Dark Souls 2 Wrathful Axe"). Consuming it yields 30,000 souls. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of Elana, the Squalid Queen, who rest beside the sleeping dragon of the Eternal Sanctum. This child of Dark accompanies the dragon, slowly amassing souls in anticipation of the coming day of vengeance. The wondrous soul of this augur of wrath. Can be used to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Soul of Elana, Squalid Queen Usage in DKS2

  * Can be traded to [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for [Wrathful Axe](/Wrathful+Axe#fex "Dark Souls 2 Wrathful Axe#fex").
  * Gives 30,000 souls when consumed.

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of Elana, Squalid Queen Location

  * Obtained from killing [Elana, the Squalid Queen](/Elana%2C+Squalid+Queen "Dark Souls 2 Elana%2C Squalid Queen").
