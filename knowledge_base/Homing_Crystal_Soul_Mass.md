**Homing Crystal Soul Mass** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Homing Crystal Soul Mass** , an enhanced sorcery that transforms soul masses into lethal crystallized projectiles, ensuring heightened power. Upon release, these crystal-infused entities adeptly seek out and assail their designated target. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "Soulbeads made more lethal with crystallization.  
> When fired, they home in on their target.
> 
> Crystallization makes souls and sorceries all the more powerful."

### Dark Souls 2 Homing Crystal Soul Mass Acquired From

  * [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana"): Rhoy's Resting Place bonfire, Outside the cave and around the main area with [Archdrake Pilgrims](/Archdrake+Pilgrim "Dark Souls 2 Archdrake Pilgrim"), on a corpse lying on a broken pillar.

### Homing Crystal Soul Mass Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * At 50 INT 5 orbs are cast.
  * Orbs last 30 seconds or until they collide with a character or object and are unaffected by the [Lingering Dragoncrest Ring](/Lingering+Dragoncrest+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Lingering Dragoncrest Ring").
  * Its damage scales with Magic BNS only (the strength of the catalyst doesn't matter).
  * Not affected by [Unleash Magic](/Unleash+Magic "Dark Souls 2 Unleash Magic").
