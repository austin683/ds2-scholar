**Melfia** is a mentioned-only location in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). It was founded by sorcerers who escaped the king of Olaphis' decree long ago and is where the Melfian Magic Academy currently resides. The Academy is a place of study for both [sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") and [pyromancies](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies"), making it quite unique in that regard. [Hexes](/Hexes "Dark Souls 2 Hexes") however are admonished by nearly all manner of sorcerer.

[](/Slumbering+Dragoncrest+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Slumbering Dragoncrest Ring")

### Melfia Lore

A former king launched a campaign to capture these terrors of the high seas, but rather than imprison them, forced them into hard labor at [No-man's Wharf](/No-man's+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No-man's Wharf"). The king of Olaphis, wracked with fear and suspicion, began to see the progenitor of the curse in anything and everything. During his reign, sorcerers in particular suffered dreadful persecution. [The Lost Bastille](/The+Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 The Lost Bastille") was turned into a prison, and was left abandoned to rot with its prisoners. The [sentry](/Flexile+Sentry "Dark Souls 2 Flexile Sentry") crammed inmates of the overflowing Bastille onto a rickety ship, and cast them out to the open sea. The majority of them drowned or starved but a few hardy survivors made land to the south, and imparted their knowledge of sorcery to the people there. Olenford, revered as the father of sorcery in Melfia, sought knowledge that surpassed mankind's ken, and so ventured into the [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt"), and did away with his humanity. Some hold the theory that Melfian sorceries and pyromancies originated within the boundaries of Drangleic, in ages past. Though the precise origins of the Magic Academy are hazy, for over its long history, its faculty has been dissolved and reestablished at least a dozen times.

Special quartz rings are bestowed upon students of a certain standard at the Melfian Magic Academy. Pyromancer Glocken crafts various replicas, much to the dismay of the conservative old-guard faculty. Astrologists believe that magical powers can be obtained at moments of special heavenly alignment. Even the collective wisdom of the Melfian Magic Academy cannot pull magic out of the sky, but the ideas of the astrologists led to the creation of new and very useful magic devices. Sorcerers at the Melfian Magic Academy once attempted to imbue [titanite](/titanite "Dark Souls 2 titanite") with various elements, but are said to have failed. Someone must have succeeded, though; what else would explain the altered states of titanite? The Melfian Magic Academy was also able to rework an ancient sorcery into [Soul Spear Barrage](/Soul+Spear+Barrage "Dark Souls 2 Soul Spear Barrage"), it was a simple idea, but potentially highly effective. [Soul Greatsword](/Soul+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 Soul Greatsword") was popular with the magic warriors of Melfia due to its similarity to swordplay. Wielding a sword and casting a sorcery are two different things, and some members of the Melfian Magic Academy view these varieties of spells such as [Magic Weapon](/Magic+Weapon "Dark Souls 2 Magic Weapon") impure. This is because the old purists of the Melfian Magic Academy view swordplay as a barbaric form of engagement. Still a rare weapon used by very few warriors was the [Sorcerer's Twinblade](/Sorcerer's+Twinblade "Dark Souls 2 Sorcerer's Twinblade") originating in Melfia that doubles as a catalyst for sorcery.
