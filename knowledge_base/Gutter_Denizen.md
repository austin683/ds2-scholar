**Gutter Denizen** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") encountered in Dark Souls 2.

### Information

_"When Zullie the Witch learned of Alva's dedication_ _to Saint Serreta, she used all manner of tricks and_ _deceit to ruin him, but in the end she would_ _spend her life with him, supporting his endeavors._

_The witch, as unloving as she was unloved, finally_ _found illumination through her blighted existence."_

### Gutter Denizen Location

  * [The Gutter](/The+Gutter "Dark Souls 2 The Gutter"), must light every sconce in the area to trigger the invasion. (sotfs only)

### Gutter Denizen Drops

  * [Black Witch Set](/Black+Witch+Set "Dark Souls 2 Black Witch Set") (with [Black Witch Veil](/Black+Witch+Veil "Dark Souls 2 Black Witch Veil") as the headpiece)
  * [Human Effigy](/Human+Effigy "Dark Souls 2 Human Effigy")
  * 975 [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") in NG, 1,950 [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus").

### Gutter Denizen Notes & Tips

  * Uses the following [sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries"): [Soul Greatsword](/Soul+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 Soul Greatsword"), [Crystal Soul Spear](/Crystal+Soul+Spear "Dark Souls 2 Crystal Soul Spear"), [Great Soul Arrow](/Great+Soul+Arrow "Dark Souls 2 Great Soul Arrow"),   
[Heavy Soul Arrow](/Heavy+Soul+Arrow "Dark Souls 2 Heavy Soul Arrow"), [Soul Spear Barrage](/Soul+Spear+Barrage "Dark Souls 2 Soul Spear Barrage"), and [Focus Souls](/Focus+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Focus Souls").
