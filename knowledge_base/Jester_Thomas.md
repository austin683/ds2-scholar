**Jester Thomas** is an [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") in **Dark Souls 2.** Jester Thomas is a hostile NPC invader, who can be encountered in the [Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King") DLC. He is a mysterious and enigmatic character, clad in colorful jester attire and wielding pyromancy flames. Thomas is known for his aggressive and unpredictable fighting style, making him a challenging opponent for players to face.

### Jester Thomas Information in Dark Souls 2 

An NPC Phantom that can be summoned for the [Mytha, the Baneful Queen](/Mytha,+the+Baneful+Queen "Dark Souls 2 Mytha, the Baneful Queen") fight at the end of [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak") which he can easily solo, even on higher NG+ difficulties as he uses powerful pyromancies, like [Forbidden Sun,](/Forbidden+Sun "Dark Souls 2 Forbidden Sun") [Flame Swathe](/Flame+Swathe "Dark Souls 2 Flame Swathe"), [Great Combustion](/Great+Combustion "Dark Souls 2 Great Combustion") and [Great Chaos Fireball](/Great+Chaos+Fireball "Dark Souls 2 Great Chaos Fireball") to great effect (with unlimited uses). his Summon sign is right before the two Grave Wardens on the right of the staircase where the pool of poison was. (or is if you haven't burned the windmill) He uses dual Pyromancy Flames and the Jester's Set.

When going through [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon's+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon's Sanctum") he will invade just before the fog gate leading to the **Lair of the Imperfect** bonfire. He can be noted doing the ¨This One's Me¨ gesture and then will proceed to attack.

### Jester Thomas Encounters in Dark Souls 2

  * [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak"). Near the staircase guarded by two Grave Wardens before Mytha's fog wall. His summon sign is submerged in poison if you haven't burned the windmill.
  * [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon's+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon's Sanctum"). He will invade the player in the [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon's+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon's Sanctum"), just before the fog wall to the Lair of the Imperfect.

### Dark Souls 2 Jester Thomas Trivia

  * The [Jester's Set](/Jester's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Jester's Set") makes the wearer impervious to backstabs.
