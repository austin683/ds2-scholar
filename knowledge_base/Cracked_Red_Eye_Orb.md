**Cracked Red Eye Orb** is a [Multiplayer Item](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Cracked Red Eye Orb** , an online play item used to invade other worlds. By defeating the master of the invaded world, you acquire a Token of Spite. [Multiplayer Items](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitive gameplay like PVP.a Multiplayer item in **Dark Souls 2**. 

> Online play item. Invade other worlds. Defeat the master of the world you have invaded to acquire a Token of Spite. The residual sins of those who have succumbed to dark temptations are contained in the form of the Cracked Red Eye Orb.

### Cracked Red Eye Orb Usage in DKS2

  * Used to invade the worlds of other players.

### Dark Souls 2 Cracked Red Eye Orb Requirements

  * Must be Human.
  * Must be in an area where PvP is allowed.

### Cracked Red Eye Orb Location in Dark Souls 2

  * 1x on a corpse on the right-side tutorial path in [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt"). Found by climbing up the ladder and then dropping down and entering a door in an alcove.
  * 2x found in Lower Cardinal Tower by the Fire Salamanders.
  * 3x found in the area near the [Soldier's Rest](/Soldier%27s+Rest "Dark Souls 2 Soldier%27s Rest") bonfire in the [Cardinal Tower](/Cardinal+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Cardinal Tower").
  * 1x [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep"). Go up the ladder past the Mimic Chest and drop down to the ledges below.
  * On a corpse above [Bridge Approach](/Bridge+Approach "Dark Souls 2 Bridge Approach") bonfire in [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman%27s+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman%27s Copse").
  * 2x on a corpse on a ledge above the staircase that leads up from the [Executioner Chariot](/Executioners+Chariot "Dark Souls 2 Executioners Chariot") boss to the [Brotherhood of Blood](/Brotherhood+of+Blood "Dark Souls 2 Brotherhood of Blood") bonfire. 
    * Carefully walk along the narrow ledge to reach the alcove at the far end above the staircase.
  * Drop from Fire Salamanders in the cave under the [Cardinal Tower](/Cardinal+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Cardinal Tower") bonfire.
  * Drop from Torturers at the bridge to the [Undead Purgatory](/Undead+Purgatory "Dark Souls 2 Undead Purgatory") in [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman%27s+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman%27s Copse").
  * Drop from the three Piglets to the right of the [Majula Mansion](/Majula+Mansion "Dark Souls 2 Majula Mansion"). (The three piglets, after farming them for a while, will become a spawn of two boars with a higher drop rate, and when exhausted, one big boar, for a single drop of three orbs. Tested on multiple characters, NG, game finished)
  * Can be traded for with [Smooth & Silky Stones](/Smooth+%26+Silky+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Smooth %26 Silky Stone") given to [Dyna and Tillo](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo").
  * 5 can be bought from Titchy [Gren](/Gren "Dark Souls 2 Gren") for 5,000 souls each (NG+, does not work with [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic")).
  * Reward for winning a duel in [Undead Purgatory](/Undead+Purgatory "Dark Souls 2 Undead Purgatory").
  * Drops from the Executioner Chariot set wearing Red phantom in heide (ng+ or bonfire ascetics near the enterance to dragon riders arena)

### DKS2 Cracked Red Eye Orb Notes and Tips

  * If the host dies, the Invader receives 1 [Token of Spite](/Token+of+Spite "Dark Souls 2 Token of Spite") and gains souls 10% equivalent to the cost of leveling up of the opponent.
  * If used in the [Dark Chasm of Old](/Dark+Chasm+of+Old "Dark Souls 2 Dark Chasm of Old"), the Invader will spawn as an Abyss Phantom _(NPC Phantoms are also hostile towards the Invader)_.
  * Small chance to be summoned by Looking Glass Knight anywhere in Drangleic Castle (got summoned while invading in Throne of Want.)
  * Restores health, flasks, spells charges upon return regardless if the invaded host was vanquished.

### Cracked Red Eye Orb Multiplayer Time Limits in DKS2

  * An invader using the Cracked Red Eye Orb has 15 minutes to defeat the host.
