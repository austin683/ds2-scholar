**Dragon Torso Stone** is a special [covenant](/covenants "Dark Souls 2 covenants") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Dragon Torso Stone** , a stone infused with the might of dragons. Changes the body into a roaring dragon, a transformation that endures until death. Bestows the armor, gloves, and leggings of the Black Dragon Armor. Reusing the stone prompts the player to roar, consuming a significant amount of stamina, mildly staggering enemies, and providing a damage boost for approximately 6 seconds. [Tools](/Tools "Dark Souls 2 Tools") are unique, reusable items which assist the player during various facets of gameplay ranging from basic communication to assisting in boss encounters.

> A stone containing the strength of dragons. Transforms the body into a roaring dragon, an effect that remain until death.  
> The Zealots who sought everlasting life are said to have conducted rites to imbue stones with the strength of dragons, but the truth of such claims is questionable.

### Dragon Torso Stone Usage in DKS2

  * Grants the armor, gloves, and leggings of the [Black Dragon Armor](/Black+Dragon+Set "Dark Souls 2 Black Dragon Set").
  * Using the stone again makes the player roar; using up a large amount of stamina, slightly staggering enemies, and giving the player a damage boost for about 6 seconds.
  * The roar does not break objects.

### Dark Souls 2 Dragon Torso Stone Location

  * Earned from [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep") after attaining rank 2 in the [Dragon Remnants](/Dragon+Remnants "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Remnants") covenant.

### Dragon Torso Stone Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Helmet is received from the [Dragon Head Stone](/Dragon+Head+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Head Stone") which is earned at rank 1 of the Dragon Remnants.
  * Cannot be removed until the wearer dies.
  * Very light armor.
  * Damage boost works with [Pyromancies](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies"), [Hexes](/Hexes "Dark Souls 2 Hexes"), [Miracles](/Miracles "Dark Souls 2 Miracles"), and [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries").
  * The damage buff of the roar is affected by the Lingering Dragoncrest ring.
