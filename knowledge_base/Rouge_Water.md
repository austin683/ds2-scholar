**Rouge Water** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Rouge Water** , scarlet liquid of mysterious origin. It replenishes both HP and spell uses. While recognized by some as red holy water, not everyone acknowledges its sacred nature. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Crimson water of unknown origin. Restores HP and spell uses. Though known to some as red holy water, not everyone acknowledges its holy status.

### Rouge Water Usage in Dark Souls 2

Restorative item.

  * Restores 850 HP and 50% of spell uses.
  * Will not restore uses to spells that have 1 use/cast ([Soul Geyser](/Soul+Geyser "Dark Souls 2 Soul Geyser"), [Forbidden Sun](/Forbidden+Sun "Dark Souls 2 Forbidden Sun")) unless you have more than one use available.

### Dark Souls 2 Rouge Water Locations

  * One can be found on a corpse directly after opening the passageway to [Huntman's Copse](/Huntsman's+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman's Copse").
  * Rare drop from [White Hollow Mage](/White+Hollow+Mage "Dark Souls 2 White Hollow Mage").
  * Treasure in the [Ruin Sentinels](/Ruin+Sentinel "Dark Souls 2 Ruin Sentinel") room, behind an [illusory wall](/Illusory+Wall "Dark Souls 2 Illusory Wall").
  * A maximum of 3 can be bought from [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis").
