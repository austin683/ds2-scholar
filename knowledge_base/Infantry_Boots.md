**Infantry Boots** is a **Legging** and a part of [Infantry Set](/Infantry+Set "Dark Souls 2 Infantry Set") in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Infantry Boots Description

"Boots are worn by Drangleic infantry.  
Apiece of basic, minimal equipment. At least it's light and easy-to-use."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Armor, gloves, and boots sold by Maughlin the Armourer in Majula.

### Notes

  * Upgraded with[Titanite Shard, Large Titanite Shard, Titanite Chunk, Titanite Slab](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores")
