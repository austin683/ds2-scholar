**Old Dragonslayer Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Old Dragonslayer Soul** , the soul of the Old Dragonslayer. Bearing resemblance to a certain knight from ancient legends, the Old Dragonslayer strikes a familiar chord. Consume this unique soul for 10,000 souls or use it to craft a valuable item. Additionally, you can trade it with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") to obtain the [Dragonslayer Spear](/Dragonslayer+Spear "Dark Souls 2 Dragonslayer Spear"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Old Dragonslayer.  _The Old Dragonslayer is reminiscent of a certain knight that appears in old legends._  
> _Use the special soul of the Old Dragonslayer to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth._

### Old Dragonslayer Soul Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Can be consumed for 10,000 souls.

  * Trade with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for [Dragonslayer Spear](/Dragonslayer+Spear "Dark Souls 2 Dragonslayer Spear")

### Dark Souls 2 Old Dragonslayer Soul Location

  * Acquired after defeating [Old Dragonslayer](/Old+Dragonslayer "Dark Souls 2 Old Dragonslayer") in the [Cathedral of Blue](/Cathedral+of+Blue "Dark Souls 2 Cathedral of Blue")
