**Hidden Weapon** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Hidden Weapon** , an ancient sorcery with origins lost to memory. Renders the weapon in the other hand invisible. Its effectiveness, like any tool, depends on the ingenuity of the caster. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "A sorcery that dates farther back than recollection. Makes weapon in other hand invisible.  
>   
> Effective use of this, like any tool, perhaps, is reliant upon the ingenuity of its caster."

### Dark Souls 2 Hidden Weapon Acquired From

  * Achieving the second rank in the [Bell Keepers Covenant](/Bell+Keepers "Dark Souls 2 Bell Keepers") and speaking to the covenant leader.

### Hidden Weapon Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Makes the weapon invisible; the effect ends if the weapon is switched.  
Best used out of sight of opponents.  
Count as a weapon buff (like Crystal Weapon) and as such cannot stack with other weapon buffs.  
Lasts 90 seconds. (affected by [Lingering Dragoncrest Ring](/Lingering+Dragoncrest+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Lingering Dragoncrest Ring") as well)  
Extremely useful if paired with [Blue Flame](/Blue+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Blue Flame"), it creates an illusion of one casting a spell with bare hands.
