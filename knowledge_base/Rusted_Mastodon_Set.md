**Rusted Mastodon Set** is an Attire or [Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor") in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

> Helm/Armor/Gauntlets/Boots worn by the Primal Knights of Drangleic castle. Durability is low due to rusting.  
>    
>  Its weight would normally crush a man, but to the brutish Primal Knights it may as well be made of papyrus.

###  Set Location

  * Dropped by [Primal Knights](/Primal+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Primal Knight") in [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros").

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
