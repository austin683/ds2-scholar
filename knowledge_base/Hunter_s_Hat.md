_"A leather hat with a large brim.__Traditionally used by bow hunters._

_The hunting goddess Evlana was no goddess at all, but rather a brave and highly skilled bow huntress. Long after her demise, the passing of lore transformed her into a deity."_

### Location

  * At the [Soldiers' Rest](/Soldier's+Rest "Dark Souls 2 Soldier's Rest") bonfire in the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants") it is out and to the left on top of a wooden structure in a chest. 
    * You will need the key from [The Last Giant](/The+Last+Giant "Dark Souls 2 The Last Giant") to access this area.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Hunter Set](/Hunter+Set "Dark Souls 2 Hunter Set")
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
