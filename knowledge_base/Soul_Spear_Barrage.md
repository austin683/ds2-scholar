**Soul Spear Barrage** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul Spear Barrage** , hurls a barrage of soul spears that inflict substantial damage upon multiple hits. A reimagined version of an ancient spell developed at the Melfian Magic Academy. A straightforward concept, yet with the potential for remarkable effectiveness when wielded skillfully. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "Fires a flurry of soul spears. inflicts high damage when multiple spears hit.  
> A reworking of an ancient spell concocted at the Melfian Magic Academy.  
> A simple idea, but potentially highly effective. When used properly, of course."

### Dark Souls 2 Soul Spear Barrage Acquired From

  * [Carhillion of the Fold](/Carhillion+of+the+Fold "Dark Souls 2 Carhillion of the Fold") for 2600 Souls.

### Soul Spear Barrage Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * This spell can be aimed manually with the help of Binoculars, thus becoming very useful against moving targets or for roll-catching in PvP.
  * This sorcery can be quite effective on larger enemies at close ranges due to them taking the full effect of the sorcery, it can also stun opponents. It is recommended to use this against single opponents, as the sorcery does little damage to groups and most projectiles will usually miss their targets if attempted to be used this way.  
Not to be confused with [Soul Geyser](/Soul+Geyser "Dark Souls 2 Soul Geyser"), which fires multiple large soul spears, this one fires small projectiles.
