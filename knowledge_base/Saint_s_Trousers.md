**Saint's Trousers** is a **Legging** and a part of [Saint's Set](/Saint's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Saint's Set") in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Saint's Trousers Description

"Dress worn by miracle-casting clerics. belonged to Licia of Lindelt.  
While its true Licia dedicated herself to miracles, its rare for anyone to pass through life without trouble. even clerics arent always who they seem."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Received from [Licia of Lindeldt](/Licia+of+Lindeldt "Dark Souls 2 Licia of Lindeldt") when talking to her with 30 Faith. (Faith gained from stat-boosting gear and rings does not count toward the 30 faith requirement, only level ups).
  * Can be purchased from [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia") for 4800 souls after killing Licia of Lindeldt without completing her quest.
  * Obtained alonside the rest of her set and the [Soothing Sunlight](/Soothing+Sunlight "Dark Souls 2 Soothing Sunlight") Miracle upon completion of her quest.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Saint's Set](/Saint's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Saint's Set"). 
    * Every piece of the set has 16 a [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith") requirement.
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
