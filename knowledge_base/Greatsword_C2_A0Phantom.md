## Greatsword Phantom Information

Red Phantom Purgatory Guardian

_"Dark spirit that appears in NG+ just past the bridge from[Huntsman's Corpse](/Huntsman%27s+Copse) to [Undead Purgatory](/Undead+Purgatory). Fights with a [Greatsword](/Greatsword) +3 and [Orma's Greatshield](/Orma%27s+Greatshield) (small % chance to drop either). Will switch between shield fighting and a two handed sword style quite regularly. Also able to effectively utilize rolls to dodge player attacks. _
