_"Armor of bandits of the Forossan outskirts._

_The territory of Forossa became lawless after the kingdom fell to war. Citizens became bandits, and scattered to other lands."_

### Location

  * Starting Bandit Class.
  * Sold by [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Bandit Set](/Bandit+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab).
