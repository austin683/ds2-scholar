**Corrosive Urn** is a type of [Projectile](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2). Corrosive Urn** , a container brimming with secretions from colossal corrosive ants. Potent acid that harms equipment. Causes 5 durability damage to weapons/shields, 12 to armor, and 10 to rings. [Projectiles](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") are offensive items that are usually thrown at hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") to inflict a small amount of damage. Works similarly to [Acid Surge](/Acid+Surge "Dark Souls 2 Acid Surge").

> Urn filled with secretions of giant corrosive ants. Powerful acid inflicts damage on equipment.
> 
> Giant anthills created by corrosive ants line the horizon of the desert land of Jugo. Ant larvae are considered a delicacy, but he who enters an anthill uninvited will have their flesh melted to the bone.

### Dark Souls 2 Corrosive Urn Usage

  * Acid thrown to destroy enemy's equipment.
  * Does 5 durability damage to weapons/shields, 12 to armor, and 10 to rings.
  * Applies a 5% [debuff](/Buffs+&+Debuffs "Dark Souls 2 Buffs & Debuffs") to enemy defenses when procced.
  * Blocking seems to decrease the effect dramatically.

### Corrosive Urn Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Sold by the [Rat King](/Rat+King "Dark Souls 2 Rat King") for 700 souls.
  * The [Gutter](/Gutter "Dark Souls 2 Gutter").
  * Dropped by corrosive ants near the third bonfire in [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana").
