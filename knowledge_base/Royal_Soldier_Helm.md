_"Royal Drangleic soldier helm.__Equipment crafted by the royal blacksmith. Finely polished and ready for battle."_

### Location

  * Sold by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer) in [Majula](/Majula).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Rogue Set](/Rogue+Set). 
    * Must have spent 1000+ souls for the Royal Soldier set to appear in his inventory.
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab).
