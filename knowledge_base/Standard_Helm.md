### Location

  * Starting gear for the [Warrior Class](/Warrior).
  * Sold by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer) in [Majula](/Majula).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Hard Leather Set](/Hard+Leather+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Ores).
