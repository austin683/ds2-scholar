### Imported Manchettes Description

_"Traveler's manchettes. Won't provide much in the way of protection._

_The cursed souls who wander the lands have a strange way of ending up here, as if drawn from afar by some force."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Starting equipment for all players.

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shards, Chunks](/Ores)
  * [NG+]The Things Betwixt Black phantom that is wielding a Heavy Crossbow at the third Tutorial tree drops +5 pieces of the Set
