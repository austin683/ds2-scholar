**Leggings** are a type of **[Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor")** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**. **Leggings** refer to a type of armor worn on the player character's legs. These are part of the overall armor set that players can equip to enhance their defense against enemy attacks.

### What are Leggings in DS2

In Dark Souls 2, **Leggings** are a form of protective armor designed for the player character's legs. They constitute an integral part of the complete armor set, serving to bolster the character's defense against enemy attacks. **Leggings** , along with other armor pieces such as [Helmets](/Helms "Dark Souls 2 Helms"), [Chest Pieces](/Chest+Pieces "Dark Souls 2 Chest Pieces"), and [Gauntlets](/Gauntlets "Dark Souls 2 Gauntlets"), contribute to the overall protective capabilities of the player character.

Leggings in Dark Souls II, like other armor pieces, come in various types and have different stats. They provide defense against physical and elemental damage, as well as varying levels of poise and other attributes. Players can mix and match leggings with other armor pieces to create a customized combination that suits their playstyle and preferences.

Players need to find a balance between defense, mobility, and other factors when selecting leggings for their character. The choice of leggings, along with the rest of the armor set, contributes to the overall strategy and effectiveness of the player's character.
