**Loyce Knight** is a potential ally that can be encountered in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). They appear in the [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King") DLC.

_These Knights of Loyce were devoted guardians of this land that delved valiantly into the depths of chaos, never to return._

_The few that survived remain in Eleum Loyce, now frozen over, awaiting the call of their master._

### Loyce Knight Location

  * [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce"), four in total, one of them is provided by [Alsanna, Silent Oracle](/Alsanna%2C+Silent+Oracle "Dark Souls 2 Alsanna%2C Silent Oracle").  

    * [Inner Wall](/Inner+Wall "Dark Souls 2 Inner Wall"), reach the chest guarded by three [Rampart Golems](/Rampart+Golem "Dark Souls 2 Rampart Golem") and find a path to the right that was previously barred off with Ice. Follow this path until you reach a door, behind which the Loyce Knight can be interacted with.
    * [Lower Garrison](/Lower+Garrison "Dark Souls 2 Lower Garrison"), After the [White Covetous Demon](/Covetous+Demon "Dark Souls 2 Covetous Demon"), find an exit from the cave on the left side. Following this path will lead to an elevator up, that leads to a temple inside which the Loyce Knight can be interacted with.
    * [Abandoned Dwelling](/Abandoned+Dwelling "Dark Souls 2 Abandoned Dwelling"), Progress through until the ramparts area and access the staircase that was previously barred off with ice. At the bottom there is a shortcut to the [Inner Wall](/Inner+Wall "Dark Souls 2 Inner Wall") bonfire and the invasion spawn for [Castaway Witch Donna](/Castaway+Witch+Donna "Dark Souls 2 Castaway Witch Donna"). After this there is a temple where the Loyce Knight can be found.

### Loyce Knight Notes & Tips

  * The player must free them in order to obtain their help with tackling the [Burnt Ivory King](/Burnt+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Burnt Ivory King") by sacrificing themselves and stopping the portals that spawn [Charred Loyce Knight](/Charred+Loyce+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Charred Loyce Knight") enemies in the area.
  * They will respawn and assist the player until the Burnt Ivory King is defeated.
  * If any Loyce Knights survive after the boss, they will stay in their positions above the portal and will not follow you down again unless you use a Bonfire Ascetic or go through another NG+ cycle.
