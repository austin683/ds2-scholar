**Fume Sorcerer Boots** is a **Legging** and a part of [Fume Sorcerer Set](/Black+Set "Dark Souls 2 Black Set") in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Fume Sorcerer Boots Description

"Boots worn by fume sorcerers.   
After the Old Iron King sunk into the lava, scores of men were dispatched to this land to tap the replete stores of iron. But they soon lost their nerve when faced with the child of Dark, and all but the most steadfast of them became servants of the black fog."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Dropped by [Fume Sorcerers](/Fume+Sorcerer "Dark Souls 2 Fume Sorcerer") in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower").

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shard](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard")
  * Part of [Fume Sorcerer Set](/Fume+Sorcerer+Set "Dark Souls 2 Fume Sorcerer Set")
