**Executioner Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Executioner Set](/Executioner+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Executioner Leggings Description

_"Leggings worn by the executioner of the Undead Purgatory. The spikes are designed to evoke fear._  
_The executioner mercilessly put down countless Undead, but how could he know that it was never his own will, but that of the chariot horse?"_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * The Executioner's Set can be Purchased from [Titchy Gren](/Gren) only AFTER entering [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle).

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite.](/Twinkling+Titanite)
  * Worn by the character riding the Chariot in the boss fight.
