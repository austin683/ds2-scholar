**Archdrake Chime** is a **[Sacred Chime](/Chimes "Dark Souls 2 Chimes")** and one of the available **[Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons")** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). Archdrake Chime **acts as a catalyst for both miracles and hexes. It can be obtained from [Felkin the Outcast](/Felkin+the+Outcast "Dark Souls 2 Felkin the Outcast") in [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman's+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman's Copse") by purchasing it from him for 4000 souls. This chime is suitable for those who use miracles and hexes equally, providing a balanced approach without excelling in either.

> Sacred chime used by members of the Archdrake Sect of Lindelt. A catalyst for miracles and hexes.   
>   
>  The land of Lindelt is governed by stringent laws, and those who dare defy them are punished without mercy.  
>    
>  Scholar of the First Sin added:   
>   
> It is often the sinful who seek piety, spurred only by a selfish desire for salvation. 

### How to get Archdrake Chime in Dark Souls 2

  * Sold by [Felkin the Outcast](/Felkin+the+Outcast "Dark Souls 2 Felkin the Outcast") for 4000 souls.

### DS2 Archdrake Chime Hints and Tips

  * This chime is good for those who use miracles and hexes equally. It is a jack of all trades, but does not bring out the ultimate power of either miracles or hexes.
  * Upgraded with regular titanite shards.
  * 366 lightning bns with 50 faith. Mediocre all around.

This resulting bonus damage is added to the base physical damage of the weapon and is shown in the equipment screen in blue numbers as a "+ X".

Durability: The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!".
