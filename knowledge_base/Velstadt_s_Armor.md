_"Armor worn by the Royal Aegis. Originally imbued with the power of miracles, now soaked with dark after extended exposure in the Undead Crypt._

_A knight from a faraway land was lured to this accursed land, but forgot even why he came, eventually reduced to a shadow of his former self."_

### Location

  * Bought from [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer) merchant in [Majula](/Majula) after you have defeated the [Velstadt, The Royal Aegis](/Velstadt%2C+The+Royal+Aegis). You must have spent at least 16,000 souls at Maughlin's shop for the armor to appear in his inventory.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Velstadt's Set](/Velstadt's+Set).
  * Upgraded from [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
