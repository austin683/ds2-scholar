#  Bridge Approach

* * *

##  Location:

You reach this bonfire soon after the first huntsman's copse bonfire by going through the cave and a building, a shortcut just outside of this bonfire in the form of a kickable tree can be found just outside.

##  Special Notes:

When using the ascetic, Forlorn will invade again.   
A good way to farm soul spears would be to ascetic this bonfire and then teleport to undead lockaway and run to the sphere, leap attack the Syan knight, pick up the soul arrow and teleport back.
