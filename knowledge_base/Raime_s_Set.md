###  Set Location

  * Sold by [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") (37,2k) in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep") once [Fume Knight](/Fume+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Fume Knight") is defeated.

  
  

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * Upgraded physical defenses are only 25% higher.
