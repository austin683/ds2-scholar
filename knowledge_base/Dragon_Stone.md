**Dragon Stone** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Dragon Stone** , an exceptionally sturdy stone. Employed to activate the Eternal Sanctum apparatus. The cherished relic of a civilization that encountered a dragon, revered its grandeur, and met its end in its company. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> An extremely solid stone. Used to operate the Eternal Sanctum contraption. The heirloom of a race that discovered a dragon, worshipped its magnificence, and perished by its side.

### Dragon Stone Usage in DKS2 

  * Used in the final part of [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum") to lower the path that will lead to [Dragon's Rest](/Dragon%27s+Rest "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Rest")

### Dark Souls 2 Dragon Stone Location

  * Chest in [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum"), in the room with spikes on the floor.
  * [Location Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ypoxlcigfhw)
