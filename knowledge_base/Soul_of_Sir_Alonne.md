**Soul of Sir Alonne** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of Sir Alonne** , the essence of Sir Alonne, who once faithfully served the Old Iron King. Originating from the eastern lands, Sir Alonne earned the position of the King's most trusted knight. Trade this soul with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for the [Bewitched Alonne Sword](/Bewitched+Alonne+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Bewitched Alonne Sword"). Consuming it provides 16,000 souls. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of Sir Alonne, who once served the Old Iron King.  
> Sir Alonne came from an eastern land, and became the King's most trusted knight.  
> Use the wondrous soul of this knight to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Soul of Sir Alonne Usage in DKS2

  * Trade with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for [Bewitched Alonne Sword](/Bewitched+Alonne+Sword#fex "Dark Souls 2 Bewitched Alonne Sword#fex")
  * Consuming gives 16 000 souls

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of Sir Alonne Location

  * Obtained by killing [Sir Alonne](/Sir+Alonne "Dark Souls 2 Sir Alonne")
