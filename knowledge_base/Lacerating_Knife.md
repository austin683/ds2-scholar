**Lacerating Knife** is a type of [Projectile](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2).** **Lacerating Knife** , a throwing knife with a keen blade designed to induce bleeding rather than purely inflict damage. This straightforward weapon is easily deployed, with limited range and minimal damage. Yet, if the knife causes bleeding, the target's maximum HP will be reduced. [Projectiles](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") are offensive items that are usually thrown at hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") to inflict a small amount of damage.

> A throwing knife. Its sharp blade is intended to cause bleeding rather than simply inflict damage. This simple weapon is easily utilized, but has limited range and deals little damage. However, if the knife can cause bleeding, the target's max HP will be lowered.

### Dark Souls 2 Lacerating Knife Usage

  * Sharp throwing knife that causes bleeding.
  * Physical and bleed proc both scale with Dexterity.

### Lacerating Knife Location in Dark Souls 2

  * 5 can be found at the bottom of the elevator at [Sinners' Rise](/Sinner%27s+Rise "Dark Souls 2 Sinner%27s Rise").
  * Sold by [Laddersmith Gilligan](/Laddersmith+Gilligan "Dark Souls 2 Laddersmith Gilligan"). (350 souls, unlimited)
