**Penal Skirt** is a **Legging** and a part of [Penal Set](/Penal+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Penal Skirt Description

Skirt worn by the Lost Sinner. A tattered skirt that the guilty wear in shame."  
"By now, no one knows who this was used to punish, or for what reason."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Buy from [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer) after defeating the [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille) boss, [Lost Sinner](/Lost+Sinner). You must first spend 1000 souls at his shop and reload the area.

### Notes

  * Upgraded with Twinkling Titanite
  * Mask increases equip load by 3%
  * Handcuffs increase the strength of pyromancy by 7.4% (+/-) (My combustion did 337 dmg before and 362 after)
