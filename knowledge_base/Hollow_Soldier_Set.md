**Hollow Soldier Set** is an Attire or [Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor") in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

> Helm/Armor/Gloves/Leggings worn by Hollowed Royal Soldiers.  
>   
> Appears to be crafted with quality materials, but is highly degraded and close to falling apart.  
>   
> Wearing this will be of little use.

###  Set Location

  * Dropped by [Hollow Soldiers](/Hollow+Royal+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Royal Soldier") in [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants").

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite")[.](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores")
  * Leggings offer no Poise bonus.

###  Piece Information & Upgrades

First row = Regular. Second row = +10
