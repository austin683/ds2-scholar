**Northern Ritual Band** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Northern Ritual Band** , a hidden treasure from Aldia, enhances spell uses at the expense of HP. This unassuming yet powerful ring raises questions about the dark deeds that shaped its creation, echoing the tragic fate of Aldia's inhabitants. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> One of the secret treasures restored in Aldia. Increases spell uses, but at the cost of HP.
> 
> This modest-looking ring contains very powerful magic. To imagine what unspeakable deeds were performed to create such rings, one need only recall the cruel fate of the residents of Aldia.

### Dark Souls 2 Northern Ritual Band Effects

**Durability** :

  * 0 = 120
  * +1 = 90
  * +2 = 75

**Weight** :

  * +0=0.5
  * +1=0.8
  * +2=1.0

**Effect:**

  * Increases spell uses by 10%, +1 = 20%, +2 = 30%,
  * Reduces HP by 20%, +1 = 25%, +2 = 50%

### Northern Ritual Band Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Behind the Hidden Door In [Sinners' Rise](/Sinners'+Rise "Dark Souls 2 Sinners' Rise"). When you go down the elevator to fight the lost sinner, right when you get off and walk down into the water make a immediate right and there is a hidden door that opens if you press the action button.
  * +1: Have 30 Intelligence and speak with [Carhillion of the Fold](/Carhillion+of+the+Fold "Dark Souls 2 Carhillion of the Fold") and he will give it to you.
  * +2: [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s Keep")

### DS2 Northern Ritual Band Tips and Notes in Dark Souls 2

  * Increases spell uses by 10/20/30% and reduces your HP by 20/35/50%.
  * When equipping the ring your health will be reduced immediately. When the ring is removed you will gain your MAX HP back but you will not have your CURRENT HP adjusted.
