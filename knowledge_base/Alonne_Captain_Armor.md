**Alonne Captain Armor** is a [Chest Piece](/Chest+Pieces "Dark Souls 2 Chest Pieces") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). Worn by the captain of the Alonne Knights, this armor is solid and beautifully crafted, with a brilliant shine that persists over time. The chest piece offers the highest fire resistance in the game when fully upgraded for any heavy armor. **Alonne Captain Armor** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Armor Worn by the captain of the Alonne Knights. The accoutrements of the Alonne Knights, who served the Old Iron King, were created with an advanced casting technique. Solid and handsomely crafted, their shine remains brilliant to this day.

### Dark Souls 2 Alonne Captain Armor Information

  * **Part of[Alonne Captain Set](/Alonne+Captain+Set "Dark Souls 2 Alonne Captain Set").**
  * **There are only 2 Alonne Captain pieces (the helm and the chest-piece). The Gauntlets and Leggings are from the regular Alonne Knight set.**
  * Armor set has very good poise, and good defense for its weight.
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab").
  * The chest offers extremely high amount of fire resistance, highest in the game if fully upgraded for any heavy armor.

### Dark Souls 2 Alonne Captain Armor Location

  * Dropped by Alonne Captains in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep"). Identified by their lightning sword or red bow.
