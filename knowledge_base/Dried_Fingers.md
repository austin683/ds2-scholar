**Dried Fingers** are [Multiplayer Items](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Dried Fingers** , shriveled fingers with multiple knuckles. While appearing dried and shrunken, they radiate a peculiar warmth. These fingers are used to reset the invasion timer and, notably, are not consumable, having unlimited uses. [Multiplayer Items](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitive gameplay like PVP.

> Dried fingers with multiple Knuckles. Shriveled, but radiating a peculiar warmth. "By the number of knuckles, it couldn't have been human, but perhaps some questions are best left unasked."

### Dark Souls 2 Dried Fingers Usage

  * Used to reset the invasion timer.
  * Not a consumable, it has unlimited uses.
  * Online only

### Dried Fingers Location in DKS2

  * In the [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt") next to the Undead Ditch Bonfire. Take a right out of the doorway until you can see a door in front of you and to the right of you, instead of going out the door to the right there is a ladder that leads up to a [Leydia Witch](/Leydia+Witch "Dark Souls 2 Leydia Witch") and a chest containing the dried fingers.
