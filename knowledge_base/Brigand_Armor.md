_"Skeleton lord Armor.__High dark defense._

_The very men who were ordered to hunt down the Undead were themselves hollowed, and founded a kingdom of bone."_

### Location

  * [No-Man's Wharf](/No-Man%27s+Wharf) in a chest.
  * +5 pieces dropped by the red phantom in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak) near final bonfire.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Brigand Set](/Brigand+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Ores).
