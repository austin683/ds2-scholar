> Helm/Armor/Gauntlets/Leggings worn by Alonne Knights.  
>    
>  The bonds of the Alonne Knights, who served the Old Iron King, were mightier than the land's iron, but in the end the knights were subsumed by the flames that brought the castle down.

###  Set Location

  * Dropped by [Alonne Knights](/Alonne+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Alonne Knight") in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep").

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite")[.](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores")
  * The set offers very high poise for relatively low weight.
  * Leggings offer second highest physical defenses among armor pieces upgraded with regular Titanite. Other best pieces are: [Gyrm Warrior Greathelm](/Gyrm+Warrior+Set "Dark Souls 2 Gyrm Warrior Set") and chestpiece, [Steel](/Steel+Set "Dark Souls 2 Steel Set") / [Drakekeeper](/Drakekeeper+Set "Dark Souls 2 Drakekeeper Set") / [Mastodon](/Mastodon+Set "Dark Souls 2 Mastodon Set") gauntlets and Mastodon leggings.
  * Leggings also offer second highest fire defense (almost equal to that of [Black](/Black+Set "Dark Souls 2 Black Set") boots, [Black Dragon](/Black+Dragon+Set "Dark Souls 2 Black Dragon Set") leggings, [Bone King](/Bone+King+Set "Dark Souls 2 Bone King Set") Skirt, [Saint's](/Saint's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Saint's Set") Trousers and [Syan's](/Syan's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Syan's Set") leggings).
