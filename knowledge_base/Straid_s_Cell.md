#  Straid's Cell

* * *

##  Location:

At the [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Lost Bastille"), in a room filled with harmless [Rupturing Hollows](/Rupturing+Hollow "Rupturing Hollows") and two locked cells. There's a [statue](/Fragrant+Branch+of+Yore) that's blocking one of the cells. After un-petrifying it, you realized it's the [great sorcerer of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis).
