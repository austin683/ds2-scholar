**Crushed Eye Orb** is a [Multiplayer Item](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Crushed Eye Orb** , an eye-shaped orb in a crushed and unfocused state. This eerie eye writhes unsteadily, seeking out dishonorable miscreants. It has the unique ability to be used to invade the traitorous Licia of Lindeldt, and the invasion occurs in the rotunda leading to Heide's Tower of Flame. [Multiplayer Items](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitive gameplay like PVP.

> A crushed eye orb with an unfocused gaze. The crush eye writhes unsteadily, in search of dishonorable miscreants. Where are the fiends, where are they...."

### Crushed Eye Orb Usage in DKS2

  * Can be used to invade the traitor [Licia of Lindeldt](/Licia+of+Lindelt "Dark Souls 2 Licia of Lindelt"). You can invade her in the rotunda leading to the [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide%27s Tower of Flame")
  * **Note** : This will only work after you have lit 3 primal bonfires! Probably you also need to kill the invader [Nameless Usurper](/Nameless+Usurper "Dark Souls 2 Nameless Usurper").
  * If you get killed upon invasion with Crushed Eye Orb, you'll become hollowed, wear [Ring of Life Protection](/Ring+of+Life+Protection "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Life Protection") to prevent hollowing.
  * **NOTE: Killing Licia, even through this invasion, will end all progress toward the Gathering of Exiles achievement for the current playthrough.**

### Dark Souls 2 Crushed Eye Orb Location

  * In the [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt"): After you pass Agdayne and enter the main, cavernous area with the bridge, fall to your immediate left. there will be 2 hollows and a body with a soul and simpleton's spice on it. Climb up the ladder you come to, watching out for the ghost that pops out about 3/4 of the way up, and the Crushed Eye Orb is in a chest at the top.
