**Magic Arrow** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Magic Arrows** , projectiles enchanted with sorcery and coated with aromatic Ooze. Armaments strengthened with magic exhibit heightened effectiveness against heavily armored adversaries or creatures adorned with thick scales. A valuable resource for those lacking in magical prowess or those whose magical reservoirs have been depleted. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Arrows imbued with magic. Tipped with aromatic Ooze. Weapons fortified with magic are particularly effective against heavily armored foes or creatures with thick scales.  
> Useful in the short term for those who have learned no magic, or whose magic is exhausted."

### Magic Arrow Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Bow ammunition.
  * 50 Physical ATK 160 Magic ATK

### DKS2 Magic Arrow Location

  * Sold by [Chancellor Wellager](/Chancellor+Wellager "Dark Souls 2 Chancellor Wellager") in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle"). Unlimited supply, cost (60) souls.
