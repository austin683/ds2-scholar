**Agdayne's Black Robes** is a [Chest Piece](/Chest+Pieces "Dark Souls 2 Chest Pieces") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). Robes worn by Agdayne of the Undead Crypt. These robes resemble the toga, tunic, and sandals of Ancient Rome and Greece. **Agdayne's Black Robes** is an armor piece worn over or around the player's torso to obtain an increase of defense and resistance. It also changes the appearance as well when it is equipped.

> Robes worn by Agdayne of the Undead Crypt. Life itself is suffering; or karma, as some have called it. The embrace of death awaits all things. But does death mean an end to suffering?

### Dark Souls 2 Agdayne's Black Robes Information

  * Part of the [Agdayne's Set](/Agdayne's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Agdayne's Set"). Pieces include [Agdayne's Cuffs](/Agdayne's+Cuffs "Dark Souls 2 Agdayne's Cuffs") and [Agdayne's Black Kilt](/Agdayne's+Black+Kilt "Dark Souls 2 Agdayne's Black Kilt").
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * The armor resembles the Ancient Antiquity toga, tunic, and sandals from Ancient Rome and Greece.

### Agdayne's Black Robes Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Receive from [Agdayne](/Grave+Warden+Agdayne "Dark Souls 2 Grave Warden Agdayne") after obtaining the [King's Ring](/King's+Ring "Dark Souls 2 King's Ring").
  * Dropped from [Agdayne](/Grave+Warden+Agdayne "Dark Souls 2 Grave Warden Agdayne") if you decided to kill him, it's possible to get 2 duplicates if doing both.
