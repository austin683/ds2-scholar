_"The Defender has stood by the throne for ages. Will his wait be worth the while?"_

### Location

  * Bought from [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") merchant in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula") after you have defeated the Throne Defender & Throne Watcher.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Throne Defender Set](/Throne+Defender+Set "Dark Souls 2 Throne Defender Set").
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
