**Staves** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are a type of [Weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") that are used to cast [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") or [Hexes](/Hexes "Dark Souls 2 Hexes"). Some Staves can deal Strike or Thrust damage when their strong attack is performed. Staves are also the primary tool for a spell casting build, but can also be used by different builds, considering an amount of Int requirement stat is met. The type of staff will work best depending multiple factors, such as the player's base levels, spell buff, and scaling. This page covers a list of all Staves in Dark Souls 2.
