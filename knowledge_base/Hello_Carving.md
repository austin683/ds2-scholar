**Hello Carving** is a special multiplayer item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Hello Carving** , faces crafted from aged tree wood that speak when thrown to the ground. They are utilized for communication with other players in Co-op or PvP modes. [Tools](/Tools "Dark Souls 2 Tools") are unique, reusable items which assist the player during various facets of gameplay ranging from basic communication to assisting in boss encounters.

> Faces carved from the wood of aged trees. They speak when thrown upon the ground. The fine craftsmanship of these faces is  
> evident even after much aging, but no one knows even the name of the artist.

### Hello Carving Usage in DKS2

  * Used to communicate with other players in Co-op or PvP modes.
  * When used, this carving says "Hello."
  * This item has unlimited uses.

### Dark Souls 2 Hello Carving Location

  * Bought from [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") in the [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep") for 2,000 souls.
