**Goldenfruit Balm** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Goldenfruit Balm** , an extract from a rare plant, temporarily boosts faith. This unique fragrant oil influences the mind, providing a brief sense of empowerment. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A balm extracted from a rare plant. Temporarily increases faith. This special fragrant oil affects the mind, granting a fleeting sense of empowerment

### Dark Souls 2 Goldenfruit Balm Usage

  * Increases [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith") by 5 for 45 seconds.

### Goldenfruit Balm Location in Dark Souls 2

  * [Shulva - Sanctum City](/Shulva+-+Sanctum+City "Dark Souls 2 Shulva - Sanctum City")
