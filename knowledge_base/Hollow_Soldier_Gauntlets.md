### Hollow Soldier Gauntlets Description

_"Gauntlets worn by Hollowed Royal Soldiers. Appears to be crafted with quality materials, but is highly degraded and close to falling apart. Wearing this will be of little use."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Drops off of the hollowed soldier's located in the Forest of Giants area.
