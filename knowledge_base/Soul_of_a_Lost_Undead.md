**Soul of a Lost Undeadl** is a type of [Consumable](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of a Lost Undead** , the essence of an Undead who long ago succumbed to Hollowing. Use it to acquire souls. The soul, origin of all life, remains a coveted pursuit even in undeath. When consumed, it bestows 200 souls upon the user. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are consumable items categorized as remnants of fallen warriors, consuming souls collects a certain number of souls that is added to the player's stock. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are required for players to become stronger in the game by raising their soul level as well as buying and upgrading their equipment.

> Soul of a lost Undead who has long ago gone Hollow. Use to acquire souls. The soul is the source of all life, and even in undeath, or hollowing, the mind seeks souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of a Lost Undeadl Usage

  * Grants 200 souls when consumed.

### Soul of a Lost Undeadl Location in DKS2

  * 1x found behind a wooden cart near the [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt") bonfire.
  * Drop from dogs in No-Man's Wharf unknown rarity with Traveling Merchant Hat equiped
  * Forest of The Fallen Giants
