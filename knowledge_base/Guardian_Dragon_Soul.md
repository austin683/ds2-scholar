**Guardian Dragon Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Guardian Dragon Soul** , the essence of a dragon that fiercely protects the path to the shrine. Acquired by defeating the [Guardian Dragon](/Guardian+Dragon "Dark Souls 2 Guardian Dragon"), the formidable boss residing in [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia's+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia's Keep"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of a dragon that guards the path to the shrine.  
> Do the dragons watch over the land of their own will, or are they in the grip of one of Aldia's spells?  
> Use the special souls of this dragon to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Guardian Dragon Soul Usage in DKS2

  * Consuming gives 14 000 souls.
  * Trade with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for: 
    * [Spitfire Spear](/Spitfire+Spear#fex "Dark Souls 2 Spitfire Spear#fex")
    * [Drakewing Ultra Greatsword](/Drakewing+Ultra+greatsword#fex "Dark Souls 2 Drakewing Ultra greatsword#fex")

### Dark Souls 2 Guardian Dragon Soul Location

  * Dropped by the [Guardian Dragon](/Guardian+Dragon "Dark Souls 2 Guardian Dragon") boss in [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s Keep").
