**Crown of the Ivory King** is a **[DLC](/DLC "Dark Souls 2 DLC")** for **Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)** , released on September 30th / October 1st 2014. The DLC was later bundled as part of the [Scholar of the First Sin Edition](/Dark+Souls+II+Scholar+of+the+First+Sin "Dark Souls 2 Dark Souls II Scholar of the First Sin") of the game. This DLC is the third set of the Lost Crowns trilogy. The trilogy contains the following Downloadable Content released in the same order: [Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King"), [Crown of the Old Iron King](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King"), [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King"). These DLC releases, added new content to the Dark Souls 2 experience, including all new [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies"), [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses"), [Locations](/Locations "Dark Souls 2 Locations") and [Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons"). Minor modifications have also been made to some details and descriptions during the [Scholar of the First Sin](/Dark+Souls+II+Scholar+of+the+First+Sin "Dark Souls 2 Dark Souls II Scholar of the First Sin") Remaster. All episodes were sold for either $9.99 a piece, or added as part of the Dark Souls II Season Pass for $24.99. [Official Trailer Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=g_24S4j-jWU). [Community Review of the Crown of the Ivory King DLC](https://fextralife.com/review-crown-of-the-ivory-king-dark-souls-ii-dlc/)

> Crown of the Ivory King is the third of 3 large-scale DLC additions to DARK SOULS™ II, and includes all-new stages, maps, boss characters, weapons & armor! In this third DLC, players will find themselves in a world shining bright with the glow of treacherous ice. Can you find and recover the lost crown?

### All Lost Crowns Trilogy DLC

This [DLC](/DLC "Dark Souls 2 DLC") was released s a trilogy that was released one after the other with about a month apart. Players were able to purchase these DLC packs individually or as a bundle. Those who purchased the Season Pass had access to all three DLC packs as they were released. Purchasing the remastered release, [Scholar of the First Sin](/Dark+Souls+II+Scholar+of+the+First+Sin "Dark Souls 2 Dark Souls II Scholar of the First Sin") also gave players access to all three DLC packs.

  * [Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King") \- July 22, 2014
  * [Crown of the Old Iron King](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King") \- August 26, 2014
  * [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King") \- September 30, 2014

### How to Access the Crown of the Ivory King DLC

You must approach the altar at the [Shrine of Winter](/Shrine+of+Winter "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Winter") and your player will be transported to the new DLC area. You will need the [Frozen Flower](/Frozen+Flower "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Flower") to open the door to enter [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce").  
Closest bonfire to the shrine is in Shaded Woods, Ruined Fork Road. Door on the upper floor on your left as you spawn in, and then follow the path.
