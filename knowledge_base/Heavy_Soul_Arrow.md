**Heavy Soul Arrow** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Heavy Soul Arrow** , an advanced sorcery unleashing a powerful, dense soul arrow, though with extended casting time in proportion to its potency. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "Advanced soul spell.  
> Fires a devastating, thick soul arrow.  
> But beware. The more powerful the soul arrow, the longer the casting time."

### Heavy Soul Arrow Acquired From

  * [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") at Iron Keep for 2000 Souls.
  * [Carhillion of the Fold](/Carhillion+of+the+Fold "Dark Souls 2 Carhillion of the Fold") at No Man's Wharf for 2000 Souls.

### Heavy Soul Arrow Notes

  * Shoots a projectile that deals magic damage. A less effective [Great Heavy Soul Arrow](/Great+Heavy+Soul+Arrow "Dark Souls 2 Great Heavy Soul Arrow").
