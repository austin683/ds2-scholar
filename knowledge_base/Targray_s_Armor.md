_"The Blue Sentinels have vowed to uplift_ _themselves from tireless training and_ _selfless adherence to order and justice."_

### Location

  * Purchased from [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia) after murdering [Targrey](/Blue+Sentinel+Targray), found after killing the [Old Dragonslayer](/Old+Dragonslayer), or by getting rank 3 in the Blue Sentinels covenant and talking to targray.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Targray's Set](/Targray's+Set).
  * Upgraded from [Titanite](/Titanite+Shard).
