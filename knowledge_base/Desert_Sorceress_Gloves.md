### Desert Sorceress Gloves Description

_"Sorceress gloves from the distant land of Jugo. Appears to be a plain, thin fabric, but is permeated with powerful magic._  
_Desert Sorceresses have enchanting looks, and they use them to catch people off guard._  
_Oddly enough, even those who are perceptive enough to realize the ploy fall prey to their seductions with alarming regularity."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Drops from [Desert Sorceress](/Desert+Sorceress "Dark Souls 2 Desert Sorceress") in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak").
