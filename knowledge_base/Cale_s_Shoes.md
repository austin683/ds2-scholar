**Cale's shoes** is a **legging** and a part of [Cale's Set](/Cale's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Cale's Set") in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Cale's shoes Description

_"Leggings of a light mirrah foot soldier. belonged to Cale the Cartographer.  
Apparently Cale procured this inexpensively second-hand, but it's surprisingly well-made."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Talk to [Cale](/Cale+the+Cartographer "Dark Souls 2 Cale the Cartographer") in abandoned house after lighting all regular bonfires.

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with normal Titanite
  * The helmet is part of Royal Sorcerer Navlaan's storyline.
