**Rusted Coin** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Rusted Token** , an ancient corroded coin. Pressing the coin momentarily enhances luck. This timeworn token provides a slight boost in luck, facilitating the discovery of items and the like. The coin is adorned with the image of a deity worshipped in ancient times, yet its true origin remains a mystery. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> An old rusted coin. Crushing the coin temporarily boosts luck.  
> This rusted coin gives you that little extra bit of luck, making items and the like easier to find.  
> The coin is engraved with the image of a god that was worshipped in ancient times, but no one knows its true origins.

### Rusted Coin Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Using this item increases luck and item discovery calculation by 100 points in addition to base 150 - for 5 minutes.
  * Will also increase the chances of gaining a rarer item from [Dyna and Tillo](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo")
  * These effects do not stack if more than one is used at a time.
  * Effects persist after quitting to the menu and returning to your character and after resting at a bonfire.
  * Effects do **NOT** persist after dying or using a [Homeward Bone](/Homeward+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone") / [Aged Feather](/Aged+Feather "Dark Souls 2 Aged Feather") (since the area is reloaded)
  * it should be noted that when used near an enemy you can see a particle effect

### Dark Souls 2 Rusted Coin Location

  * 1x (5x NG+) Rusted Coin in [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt"), just before you go to the Firekeeper's hut. In the grassy plain you arrive at, head to the right and there will be a small passage. Just inside the passage to the left, you'll see a body with the coin on it.
  * One is found in a chest in the tunnel leading to Forest of the Fallen Giants from Majula. (Chest is a mimic in NG+)
  * Dropped by the [Bell Keepers ](/Bell+Keepers "Dark Souls 2 Bell Keepers")in [Belfry Luna](/Belfry+Luna "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Luna") and [Belfry Sol](/Belfry+Sol "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Sol").
  * One behind a secret door in [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille"), at the bottom of the room where you battle the [Ruin Sentinels](/Ruin+Sentinel "Dark Souls 2 Ruin Sentinel")
  * 10 in a trapped chest in Pate's Den in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora")
  * 10 found at [Shulva - Sanctum City](/Shulva+-+Sanctum+City "Dark Souls 2 Shulva - Sanctum City") in the building before the bridge that Sinh breathes fire on. They can be farmed by burning [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") at Tower of Prayer bonfire (additional Bonfire Ascetic can be replenished at Dragon's Sanctum)
  * 5 are sold by [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir"), the merchant in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep")

### Rusted Coin Notes and Tips in DKS2

  * Counts as a [Body Buff](/Buffs+&+Debuffs "Dark Souls 2 Buffs & Debuffs"), and is perhaps the only one to display an icon beneath the health and stamina bars when active.
