**Lingering Dragoncrest Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Lingering Dragoncrest Ring** , once utilized in a distant land that predates Drangleic, this ring prolongs the duration of spell effects. While it is believed to have been employed by a renowned sorcerer, no concrete evidence remains. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A ring used long, long ago in a land that existed where Drangleic is now. Extends the length of spell effects. Presumably, this ring was used by a high sorcerer, but no proof of such remains.
> 
> Scholar of the First Sin changed to: A ring used long, long ago in a land that existed where Drangleic is now. Extends the length of spell effects. The ancient dragons were once worshipped in several nations, and rumors concerning objects of similar enchantments to this uncommon ring abound in many lands, though their origins are no longer verifiable.

### Dark Souls 2 Lingering Dragoncrest Ring Effects

**Durability** :

  * +0 = 120
  * +1 = 90
  * +2 = 75

**Weight** :

  * +0 = 0.2
  * +1 = 0.5
  * +2 = 0.8

**Effect:**

  * Extends length of spell effects by a given percentage.
  * +0 = 15%
  * +1 = 25%
  * +2 = 40%
  * This effect stacks additively with the effects of the   
[Northwarder Set](/Northwarder+Set "Dark Souls 2 Northwarder Set"). For example, a full Northwarder Set and a +1 ring will increase spell duration by 50%.

### Lingering Dragoncrest Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis")
