# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Old+Dead+One's+Soul

* A Soul obtained from defeating **[The Rotten](/The+Rotten)** on New Game+ (using Bonfire Ascetic in the Hidden Chamber Works as well) 
    * _This once-magnificent soul continues to exert influence over the land, even after the eons have reduced it to these remnants. Use it to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth._
      * Can be traded in to [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex) for the Ultra Greatsword: [Crypt Blacksword](/Crypt+Blacksword). (Requires 10,000 Soul to trade)
      * Trade with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis) to obtain [Numbness](/Numbness)  

      * Can be consumed to acquire 60,000 Souls
      * May be a reference to Gravelord Nito.
