**Rat Tail** is a [Multiplayer Item](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") and covenant item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Rat Tail** , the dismembered tail of a rat. Display loyalty by presenting it to the Rat King, fortifying the connection with your covenant. [Multiplayer Items](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitive gameplay like PVP.

> A severed rat's tail Demonstrate fidelity by offering it to the Rat King, and strengthen the bond with your covenant. The Rat King is proud and merciful, as any king true to his stature should be. The Rat King looks favorably upon those who follow the path of right, even disgraceful humans

### Dark Souls 2 Rat Tail Usage

  * Used to deepen [Rat King Covenant](/Rat+King+Covenant "Dark Souls 2 Rat King Covenant")

    
    
    | Rank | Requirement | Reward |
    | --- | --- | --- |
    | 0 | Upon entering covenant | Crest of the Rat |
    | 1 | 10 Rat Tails | 10 Small Smooth & Silky Stone |
    | 2 | 20 Rat Tails | 10 Smooth & Silky Stone |
    | 3 | 35 Rat Tails | Slumbering Dragoncrest Ring |
    

### Rat Tail Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Received upon the defeat of the [Royal Rat Vanguard](/Royal+Rat+Vanguard "Dark Souls 2 Royal Rat Vanguard")[.](/Royal+Rat+Vanguard "Dark Souls 2 Royal Rat Vanguard")
  * Received upon the defeat of the [Royal Rat Authority.](/Royal+Rat+Authority "Dark Souls 2 Royal Rat Authority")
  * Rare drop from [Hunting Rats](/Hunting+Rat "Dark Souls 2 Hunting Rat") and [ Mongrel Rats](/Mongrel+Rat "Dark Souls 2 Mongrel Rat").
  * Received by beating a summoned grey phantom when wearing the Crest of the Rat.

### Rat Tail Notes and Tips in DKS2

  * Assuming you have the same amount of [bonfire ascetics](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") as the amount of Rat Tails you want, then these can be farmed quite rather quickly by using a bonfire ascetic, respawning, and then defeating the [Royal Rat Vanguard](/Royal+Rat+Vanguard "Dark Souls 2 Royal Rat Vanguard"); This boss is very easy to kill, even on NG7+ ([Flame Swathe](/Flame+Swathe "Dark Souls 2 Flame Swathe"), weapons with wide sweeping attacks, etc.), and there is also a bonfire right next to the boss room.
