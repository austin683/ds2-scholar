**PVP Souls Value** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** is a set amount depending on the player's Soul Level. Unlike the first installment of the series where you gained 10% of the cost of souls required to reach your opponent soul level, you now obtain held souls from vanquished players with a percentage formula determining how much you gain. The new formula nets you an amount based on the cost of souls required to reach that player's Soul Level from their previous one. For example, if you invaded a SL150 player using a [Cracked Red Eye Orb](/Cracked+Red+Eye+Orb "Dark Souls 2 Cracked Red Eye Orb") and vanquished him, you will gain 2,987 souls. This page covers relevant information of PVP Soul Level Value for **Dark Souls 2**.

## PvP Soul Value Guide for Dark Souls 2

### 10% Rewards Soul Value in Dark Souls 2

When invading another player and killing the host or their summoned phantom, you will obtain 10% souls of their leveling cost. Includes defeating a host:

  * using a [Cracked Red Eye Orb](/Cracked+Red+Eye+Orb "Dark Souls 2 Cracked Red Eye Orb")
  * as a [Bell Keeper](/Bell+Keepers "Dark Souls 2 Bell Keepers") invader
  * as a [Red Sign Soapstone](/Red+Sign+Soapstone "Dark Souls 2 Red Sign Soapstone") summon
  * as a [Dragon Remnant](/Dragon+Remnants "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Remnants") summon

  
Also, defeating a red phantom as a [Blue Sentinel](/Blue+Sentinels "Dark Souls 2 Blue Sentinels") member. Hosts also gain 10% when defeating players for consensual summons such as:

  * Defeating Red Phantoms by [Red Sign Soapstone](/Red+Sign+Soapstone "Dark Souls 2 Red Sign Soapstone")
  * Defeating [Dragon Remnant](/Dragon+Remnants "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Remnants") members by Dragon Eye
  * Defeating [Rat King](/Rat+King+Covenant "Dark Souls 2 Rat King Covenant") members by Rat King summon.

### 37.5% Rewards Soul Value in Dark Souls 2

When a host defeats an invading player, the host obtains 37.5% of the player's leveling cost. Includes defeating:

  * a [Bell Keeper](/Bell+Keepers "Dark Souls 2 Bell Keepers") invader
  * a [Cracked Red Eye Orb](/Cracked+Red+Eye+Orb "Dark Souls 2 Cracked Red Eye Orb") invader
  * a [Blue Sentinel](/Blue+Sentinels "Dark Souls 2 Blue Sentinels") invader by [Cracked Blue Eye Orb](/Cracked+Blue+Eye+Orb "Dark Souls 2 Cracked Blue Eye Orb")

Also includes defeating a [Rat King](/Rat+King "Dark Souls 2 Rat King") member when you are summoned as a Grey Spirit.

### Zero Rewards Value in Dark Souls 2

A Blue Sentinel will never gain souls by defeating the host using a [Cracked Blue Eye Orb](/Cracked+Blue+Eye+Orb "Dark Souls 2 Cracked Blue Eye Orb"). Additionally, [Blue Sentinels](/Blue+Sentinels "Dark Souls 2 Blue Sentinels") and [Brotherhood of Blood](/Brotherhood+of+Blood "Dark Souls 2 Brotherhood of Blood") covenants will never obtain any souls when winning in the arena.
