**Symbol of Avarice** is a [Helm](/Helms "Dark Souls 2 Helms") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki").

_Headpiece depicting the avaricious creature that mimics treasure chests._   
_Increases soul absorption and item discovery, but steadily drains HP._   
_There is a curious tale of an incorrigibly covetous ancient deity. Clearly it has currency amongst some miscreants, or this would have never been crafted._

### Location

  * Dropped by a [Mimic](/Mimic "Dark Souls 2 Mimic") found in [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce"), in a room next to Inner Wall bonfire. Note that all mimics in the area are frozen until [Alsanna](/Alsanna,+Silent+Oracle "Dark Souls 2 Alsanna, Silent Oracle") stops the storm.

### Symbol of Avarice Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
  * Upgraded stats are only 50% higher and physical defenses are only 25% higher.
  * Increases [item discovery](/Item+Discovery "Dark Souls 2 Item Discovery"), raises soul income by 2.5% and drains 2.5 HP per second.
  * Lifedrain effect can be countered by wearing [Ring of Restoration](/Ring+of+Restoration "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Restoration") and either [Loyce Shield](/Loyce+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Shield") or [Charred Loyce Shield](/Charred+Loyce+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Charred Loyce Shield").
