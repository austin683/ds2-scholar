**Ring of Restoration** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Ring of Restoration** , a safeguarding heirloom of House Osteria. Slowly rejuvenates health. Within Lindelt's hallowed grounds, this ring embodied fortune and enduring life for the esteemed House of Osteria. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A protective ring passed down in House Osteria. It gradually restores the wearer's HP, but breaks if the wearer takes a certain amount of damage.  
> In the sacred land of Lindelt, it was said that as long as House Osteria held this ring, the peace and safety of the people would be assured.

### Dark Souls 2 Ring of Restoration Effects

**Durability** : 70

**Weight** :

  * 1.0

**Effect:**

  * Restores 2 HP every second

### Ring of Restoration Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Inside a wooden chest below the [Cardinal Tower](/Cardinal+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Cardinal Tower") Bonfire in the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants"). Go down the ladder next to the bonfire, across the bridge, unlock the door with the [Soldier Key](/Soldier+Key "Dark Souls 2 Soldier Key").

### DS2 Ring of Restoration Tips and Notes

  * Restores 2 HP per second.
  * If you destroy the chest, it will give you only the three [torches](/Torch "Dark Souls 2 Torch") and the ring will be lost for that playthrough.
  * Can be respawned by burning [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") on the [Cardinal Tower](/Cardinal+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Cardinal Tower") Bonfire.
  * Effect stacks with [Charred Loyce Shield](/Charred+Loyce+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Charred Loyce Shield"), [Loyce Shield](/Loyce+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Shield") and [Crown of the Ivory King helm](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King+helm "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King helm"). 1 of those items (0.666... HP/sec) + the Ring (2 HP/sec) are enough to counter the life loss of [Symbol of Avarice](/Symbol+of+Avarice "Dark Souls 2 Symbol of Avarice") (2.5 HP/sec).
