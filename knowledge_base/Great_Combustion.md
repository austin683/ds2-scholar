**Great Combustion** is a [Pyromancy](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Great Combustion** , an advanced version of Combustion. Unleashes an intense flame in front of the caster. Powerful yet deceptively simple, making it somewhat awkward to wield. [Pyromancies](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies") are offensive fire spells that do not demand [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith") or [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") prerequisites and can be executed with a [Pyromancy Flame](/Pyromancy+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancy Flame") or [Dark Pyromancy Flame](/Dark+Pyromancy+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Dark Pyromancy Flame"). The fire damage scales with [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") and [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith"), yet there are no specific stat requirements for casting pyromancies. However, opponents resistant to fire damage remain unaffected by [Pyromancy Spells](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies").

> "Superior spell to Combustion. Releases intense flame in front of the caster.  
> A very powerful spell, but so simple that it can be awkward to use."

### Dark Souls 2 Great Combustion Acquired From

  * Sold by Titchy [Gren](/Gren "Dark Souls 2 Gren") for 4800 souls
  * Found in a chest on the right of [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle") entrance

### Great Combustion Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

    * Very useful against [The Duke's Dear Freja](/The+Duke%27s+Dear+Freja "Dark Souls 2 The Duke%27s Dear Freja").
    * Has great uses in PvP, namely as a wake-up tool as well as to punish parry attempts. However, each casting consumes a tremendous amount of stamina.
