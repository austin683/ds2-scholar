**Strength Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in **Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)**. **Strength Ring** , a strangely contorted iron ring. Augments the strength of the wearer. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> An oddly deformed iron ring. Increases the strength of its wearer. The Old Iron King ruled over a great age of iron. The King's power allowed him to mold iron freely, as if he held sway over the forces of life and creation."

### Dark Souls 2 Strength Ring Effects

**Durability** : 120

**Weight** :

  * 1.5

**Effect:**

  * Increases Strength by 5

### Strength Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Crown of the Iron King Dlc: After activating the elevators in the foyer, take the first elevator down and then the second one. While going down the second elevator you should see a smaller platform above your destination. Jump on this platform and follow the path to find an iron chest containing the Strength ring.
  * [Location video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gzEbNxHU9ak&feature=youtu.be)
  * [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower")
