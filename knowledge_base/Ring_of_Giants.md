**Ring of Giants** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Ring of Giants** is a cherished ring once worn by the valiant Shieldless Lothian, hailing from Forossa. Bestows increased poise upon the wearer. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> The beloved ring of the gallant Shieldless Lothian, formerly of Forossa.   
> Increases poise.
> 
> Lothian was born a peasant, and died a general. His determination and diligence were unmatched, especially on the battlefield, where he earned his name by choosing to fight without a shield."

### Dark Souls 2 Ring of Giants Effects

**Durability** :

  * +0=150
  * +1=115
  * +2=90

**Weight** :

  * +0=2.0
  * +1=3.0
  * +2=5.0

**Effect:**

  * Increases poise by 10
  * +1 = 20
  * +2 = 30

### Ring of Giants Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Sold by [ Gavlan](/Gavlan "Dark Souls 2 Gavlan") for 5,000 souls. (6,000 in vanilla ds2)
  * +1: [Black Gulch](/Black+Gulch "Dark Souls 2 Black Gulch"), in a chest behind the two [giant warriors](/Giant+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Giant Warrior") in the cave beneath the level.
  * +2: [Giant Lord](/Giant+Lord "Dark Souls 2 Giant Lord"), only with bonfire intensity 2+ at [The Place Unbeknownst](/The+Place+Unbeknownst "Dark Souls 2 The Place Unbeknownst") bonfire.
