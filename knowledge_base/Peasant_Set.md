**Peasant Set** is an Attire or [Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor") in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

> Hat commonly worn by peasants. Designed to block sunlight, more fit for farming than fighting.  
>   
> Attire/long gloves/trousers commonly worn by peasants. Normal workwear, more fit for farming than fighting.

###  Set Location

  * Dropped by [Hollow Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant") in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora").
  * The Peasant Attire (chest) +5 and Peasant's Helm +5 is a drop of the Peasant Red Phantom in Brightstone Cove Tseldora. [Also drops in SotFS]

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
  * Hat grants +2 to FTH and -1 to INT, other pieces grant +2 to ADP and -1 to INT each.
