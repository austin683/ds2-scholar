**Dark Spirit - Abyss Ironclad** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. The **Abyss Ironclad** , encountered in the Dark Chasm of Old, is one of the many travelers whose identity remains unknown. This Dark Spirit assumes the form of an Ironclad and wields twin Great Hammers, showcasing a rare and formidable Dual Wield technique. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Dark Spirit - Abyss Ironclad Information

_One of many travelers of the Dark Chasm of Old, identity unknown. This Dark Spirit takes the form of an Ironclad with twin Great Hammers which it uses with a rare and frighteningly powerful Dual Wield technique._

### Dark Spirit - Abyss Ironclad Location in Dark Souls 2

  * [Dark Chasm of Old](/Dark+Chasm+of+Old "Dark Souls 2 Dark Chasm of Old")

  * Souls: NG: 2,400 NG+: 4,800 NG+7: 9,600
