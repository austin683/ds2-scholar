**Illusory Ring of the Guilty** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in **Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)**. The **Illusory Ring of the Guilty** , molded from black stone with subtle irregularities, imparts invisibility to select equipment. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc. [Unused Items](/Unused+Items "Dark Souls 2 Unused Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/About+Dark+Souls+2 "About Dark Souls 2") (DKS2) are Items listed in the game code, but not available in-game.

> A ring of black stone. The stone is misshaped, perhaps imperceptibily. Renders certain equipment invisible. Do not judge your foes by their appearance. He who approaches with no sword carries two on his back."
