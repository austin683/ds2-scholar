**Lacerating Arrow** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Lacerating Arrows** , projectile ammunition designed to inflict bleeding wounds. The razor-sharp arrowheads, coated in blood-inducing agents, have the capacity to cause adversaries to bleed, resulting in a reduction of their maximum health. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Arrows that cause bleeding. Tipped with finely sharpened arrow heads. Bleeding foes' max HP is lowered.

### Lacerating Arrow Usage in DKS2

  * Ammunition for bows
  * 50 Physical Damage
  * Causes 300 Bleed buildup on target

### Dark Souls 2 Lacerating Arrow Location

  * 15 can be found on a corpse on top of one of the destructible wooden towers in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora"), at the Royal Army Campsite.
  * 40 can be found in the necromancer cave in the [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman's+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman's Copse"), past the Undead Lockaway (SotfS only)
