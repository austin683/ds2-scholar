**Dung Pie** is a type of [Projectile](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2).** **Dung Pie** , a sizable excrement, still retaining its warmth. Toss it at an enemy for a swift onset of poison, but be mindful, as you too will be afflicted. [Projectiles](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") are offensive items that are usually thrown at hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") to inflict a small amount of damage.

> A large excrement. Still rather warm. Throw at enemy to cause rapid onset of poison, but you too will be subjected to the same bane. Most warriors are loath to come near a thing like this, much less carry it around, but therein lies the advantage.

### Dark Souls 2 Dung Pie Usage

  * Interestingly, despite saying that it inflicts poison on both the user and target, it actually seems to inflict Toxic on opponents.
  * Extremely useful against bosses that are able to poisoned, such as the [Smelter Demon](/Smelter+Demon "Dark Souls 2 Smelter Demon").
  * It is recommended to have [Poison Moss](/Poison+Moss "Dark Souls 2 Poison Moss") next on your item belt.

### Dung Pie Location in DKS2

  * Sold by the [Rat King](/Rat+King "Dark Souls 2 Rat King") for 600 souls.
