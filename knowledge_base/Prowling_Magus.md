**Prowling Magus** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Warlocks in Aldia gave rise to wicked things, known as the **Prowling Magus**. They even cast forbidden rituals upon themselves. No one knows if they were born mad or if their own misdeeds drove them over the edge. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Prowling Magus Information in Dark Souls 2
