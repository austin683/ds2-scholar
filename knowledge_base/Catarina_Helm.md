_"_ Helm shaped apparel worn by the Knights of Catarina.

Outside Catarina, they are often ridiculed for their onion-like shape, infuriating the proud knights, but masterfully forged curved design makes it very effective for parrying."

### Location

  * Rare drop from NPC Dark Spirit [Catarina Knight](/Catarina+Knight) in forest of the giants (NG+).
  * He also drops Heavy Cross Bow +5 and Broadsword +5

### Notes

  * Part of the [Catarina Set](/Catarina+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+titanite).
