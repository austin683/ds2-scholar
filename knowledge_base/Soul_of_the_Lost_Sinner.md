**Soul of the Lost Sinner** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **The Soul of the Lost Sinner** represents the essence of the imprisoned figure found in Sinners' Rise. The Lost Sinner, in perpetual atonement for past sins, can bestow 25,000 Souls when consumed. Additionally, you have the option to trade this soul with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") to acquire the [Lost Sinner's Sword](/Lost+Sinner's+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Lost Sinner's Sword"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Lost Sinner, prisoner of Sinners' Rise.  
> The Lost Sinner eternally punishes herself for the sins of her past.  
> Use the special soul of the Lost Sinner to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Soul of the Lost Sinner Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Can be consumed for 25,000 Souls.
  * Trade with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for [Lost Sinner's Sword](/Lost+Sinner%27s+Sword#fex "Dark Souls 2 Lost Sinner%27s Sword#fex")

### DKS2 Soul of the Lost Sinner Location

  * Obtained by defeating the [Lost Sinner](/Lost+Sinner "Dark Souls 2 Lost Sinner") at [Sinners' Rise](/Sinners'+Rise "Dark Souls 2 Sinners' Rise").
