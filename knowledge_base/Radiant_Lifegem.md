**Radiant Lifegem** is a type of healing consumable in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Radiant Lifegem** , a gemstone composed of crystallized souls. Slowly restores HP. The lackluster gleam of these enigmatic stones intensifies over time. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Stone made up of crystallized souls. Gradually restores HP.  
> The dull glimmer of these mysterious stones brightens with the passage of time.

### Radiant Lifegem Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Restorative item. Stronger than a normal [Lifegem](/Lifegem "Dark Souls 2 Lifegem").
  * Restores 850 HP over 19 seconds
  * Can be used on a ladder.

### Dark Souls 2 Radiant Lifegem Location

  * The starting gift [Healing Wares](/Gifts "Dark Souls 2 Gifts") contains 5 Radiant Lifegems as well as other items.
  * Ten sold by [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchants#Merchants--Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchants#Merchants--Melentia") for 1000 souls when she is in Majula.
  * Five sold by [Chancellor Wellager](/Chancellor+Wellager "Dark Souls 2 Chancellor Wellager") for 2,500 souls each.
  * One can be found on a corpse inside a pot as you enter [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora") from the [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros")
  * 3 in Forest of Fallen Giants right before Pursuer replaces 3 Lifegems(NG+).
  * 1 on a corpse at the end of the narrow path between the stairs right before the [Lost Sinner](/Lost+Sinner "Dark Souls 2 Lost Sinner")
