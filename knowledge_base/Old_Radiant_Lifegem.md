**Old Radiant Lifegem** is a type of healing consumable in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Old Radiant Lifegem** , a gemstone composed of crystallized souls. Slowly restores a substantial amount of HP. A scarce stone with such a dazzling brilliance would command a high price. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Stone made up of crystallized souls. Gradually restores a large amount of HP. A rare stone with so brilliant a shine would fetch a handsome price. But what value could it really have, without knowing who died to leave it behind?

### Old Radiant Lifegem Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Restores 1300 HP over 29 seconds
  * Gradually restores a large amount of HP, more than [Lifegem](/Lifegem "Dark Souls 2 Lifegem") and [Radiant Lifegem](/Radiant+Lifegem "Dark Souls 2 Radiant Lifegem").
  * Can be used while on a ladder.

### Dark Souls 2 Old Radiant Lifegem Location

  * [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide%27s Tower of Flame") from a corpse just before the mist leading to the [Old Dragonslayer](/Old+Dragonslayer "Dark Souls 2 Old Dragonslayer") boss
  * Sold by [Chancellor Wellager](/Chancellor+Wellager "Dark Souls 2 Chancellor Wellager") in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle") for 2,500 souls in limited quantity.
  * One can be found in the Iron Keep after shutting off the furnace along with a green blossom
  * Three are found at the Foyer Bonfire. Go towards the elevators, turn left, and open the door (you need the tower key). To your left is a small room with a wooden chest that contains them.
  * Dropped by rats in Grave of Saints.
  * Dropped by [Archdrake Pilgrims](/Archdrake+Pilgrim "Dark Souls 2 Archdrake Pilgrim") in Shrine of Amana.
