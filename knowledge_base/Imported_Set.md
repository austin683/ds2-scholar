**Imported Set** is an Attire or [Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor") in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

> Hemp hood that fully covers the face. Provides protection from the elements, and little more.  
>   
> Traveler's tunic/manchettes/trousers. Won't provide much in the way of protection.  
>    
>  The cursed souls who wander the lands have a strange way of ending up here, as if drawn from afar by some force.

###  Set Location

  * Starting equipment for all classes (in addition to class-specific armor sets or pieces).
  * Pieces of the set (in +5 state) are dropped by crossbow-wielding dark spirit that invades in [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt") on [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus").

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
