**Charcoal Pine Resin** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Charcoal Pine Resin** , resin resembling black charcoal. Infuses fire into the right-hand weapon, causing it to deal fire damage for a short duration. Particularly effective against enemies vulnerable to fire, such as Undead or beasts. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Black charcoal-like pine resin. Applies fire to right-hand weapon. The affected weapon inflicts fire damage for a short time. Particularly effective against enemies vulnerable to fire, such as Undead or beasts.

### Dark Souls 2 Charcoal Pine Resin Usage

  * Boosts existing fire damage by 30%
  * Adds 50 fire attack value of the right-hand weapon for 90 seconds

### Charcoal Pine Resin Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Sold by [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep") for 1,500 souls.
  * 3 Found in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora"). After the Magus and Congregation Boss fight jump down on to the ledge on the left. In this room there are 3 in a breakable pot.
  * Drops from [Ashen Warriors](/Ashen+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Ashen Warrior") at the [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower").
  * Drops from the [Rupturing Hollow](/Rupturing+Hollow "Dark Souls 2 Rupturing Hollow") in [Dragon Aerie](/Dragon+Aerie "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Aerie")
