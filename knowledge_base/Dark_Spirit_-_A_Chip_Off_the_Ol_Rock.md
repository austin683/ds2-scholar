**Dark Spirit - A Chip Off the Ol' Rock** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Known as '**A Chip Off the Ol' Rock** ,' this Dark Spirit takes on the form of an Ironclad, wielding twin Great Hammers with a rare and frighteningly powerful Dual Wield technique. Encountered in the Dark Chasm of Old. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.
