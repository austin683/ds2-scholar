**Unleash Magic** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Unleash Magic** , amplifies the magic within the body beyond one's limits, albeit at a significant cost to HP. The abominable experiments conducted in Aldia inadvertently gave rise to numerous shadowy entities, each hauntingly misshapen. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "Boosts magic within the body beyond even your own limits, but at great cost to HP.  
> The terrible deeds carried out in Aldia led to the unintentional birth of several shadowy things, all of them eerily malformed."

### Dark Souls 2 Unleash Magic Acquired From

  * [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan "Dark Souls 2 Royal Sorcerer Navlaan") \- For completing the fourth and last of his assassination requests. You need to be hollow to talk to him for each step of the quest line. 

### Unleash Magic Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * 20% increase to all spell damage: [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries"), [Pyromancies](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies"), [Miracles](/Miracles "Dark Souls 2 Miracles"), [Hexes](/Hexes "Dark Souls 2 Hexes"); but 30% decreased HP.  
Lasts 60 seconds.
  * Its duration is not affected by intelligence. (Tested at 50 INT, still 60 seconds)
  * [Resonant Flesh](/Resonant+Flesh "Dark Souls 2 Resonant Flesh") is dispelled before applying Unleash Magic.
  * Effects of [Rusted Coin](/Rusted+Coin "Dark Souls 2 Rusted Coin") and Unleash Magic are mutually exclusive—using one dispels the effect of the other.
  * Does not boost the damage of the [Moonlight Greatsword](/Moonlight+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 Moonlight Greatsword") or its heavy attack bullets.
  * Does not boost the elemental damage of any other weapons.
