**Bracing Knuckle Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). The **Bracing Knuckle Ring** , worn by Rhoy the Explorer, slows down equipment degradation. In the challenging journey of life, expensive and flashy gear may not always be the solution. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> Knuckle ring worn by Rhoy the Explorer. Slows equipment degradation. Expensive, flashy gear will not always make life easier for you. Sometimes, you just need your tried-and-true equipment to last the long haul."

### Dark Souls 2 Bracing Knuckle Ring Effects

**Durability** :

  * 0 = 140
  * 1+ = 85
  * 2+ = 70

**Weight** :

  * +0=0.5
  * +1=0.8
  * +2=1.0

**Effect:**

  * Reduce weapon degradation by 20%, +1 = 35%, +2 = 50%,
  * Armor degradation by 20%, +1 = 25%, +2 = 35%,
  * Ring degradation by 20%, +1 = 25%, +2 = 35%

### Bracing Knuckle Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * The Bracing Knuckle Ring is located in the [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille"). If you port to the [Salt Fort](/The+Saltfort "Dark Souls 2 The Saltfort") bonfire in [Sinner's Rise](/Sinner%27s+Rise "Dark Souls 2 Sinner%27s Rise"), go down the ladder and take an immediate left. Run across the bridge there and into the doorway at the other end. There are some stairs to the right. Go down them. There is a cell in front of you as you come down and a door right to the left of it. Go through the door and straight to the back. There is a false wall here. Press (X) on PS3 or (A) on Xbox to open the false wall. Inside there is another false wall to your left. Again press (X) or (A). To your left is the Bracing Knuckle Ring on a corpse about 30m away.
  * +1: [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle") Sold by [Chancellor Wellager](/Chancellor+Wellager "Dark Souls 2 Chancellor Wellager") in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle") for 9000 souls.
  * +2: [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt") In the second room with breakable stones and bells. Upon entering the room, to your right is a [Leydia Witch](/Leydia+Witch "Dark Souls 2 Leydia Witch") up on a ledge above. Down and to her right will be a Pyromancer Statue and a bell, next to it will be a small pile of bones containing the Bracing Knuckle Ring +2
  * Close to the second bonfire, on a body in the middle of the arwa were undeads spawn infinitely. Just look and loot the area,

### DS2 Bracing Knuckle Ring Tips and Notes

  * Reduces durability used by special strong attacks for special weapons (e.g. Spider Fang)
