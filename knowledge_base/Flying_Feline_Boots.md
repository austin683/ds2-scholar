**Flying Feline Boots** is a **legging** in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Flying Feline Boots Description

_"Boots crafted with brown fur._  
_Absorb shock and reduce fall damage. These boots, used in a celebrative festival, are light, rugged, and retain warmth."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Sold by [Sweet Shalquoir](/Sweet+Shalquoir "Dark Souls 2 Sweet Shalquoir") (6k) once both [Royal Rat Authority](/Royal+Rat+Authority "Dark Souls 2 Royal Rat Authority") and [Royal Rat Vanguard](/Royal+Rat+Vanguard "Dark Souls 2 Royal Rat Vanguard") are defeated.

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * Offer second highest curse resistance.
  * Reduce fall damage (effect stacks with other items).
