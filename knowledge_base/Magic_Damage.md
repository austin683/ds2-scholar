**Magic Damage** is one of the [Damage Types](/Damage+Types "Dark Souls 2 Damage Types") in **Dark Souls 2**. It is one of the seven main Damage Types and belongs to the Elemental Damage category. Damage Types are a major part of the calculation that determines how much damage is dealt in [Combat](/Combat "Dark Souls 2 Combat") and are affected by many factors including your equipment, spells and temporary buffs.

### Dark Souls 2 Magic Damage Guide

**Magic Damage** is a type of Elemental Damage dealt by both players and enemies alike. Magic Damage is elemental damage that falls outside of [Fire Damage](/Fire+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Fire Damage"), [Lightning Damage](/Lightning+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Lightning Damage"), and [Dark Damage](/Dark+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Dark Damage") types. Magic Damage is often effective against most enemies with high Physical resistance or are heavily armored.

Most [Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") can be upgraded via the Magic Upgrade Path in order to imbue them with Magic Damage properties. There are also Weapons that can be found which innately deal Magic Damage. See the [Upgrades](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades") and individual weapon pages for details.

Most offensive [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") deal Magic Damage.
