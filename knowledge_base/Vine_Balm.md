**Vine Balm** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Vine Balm** , an extract from a rare plant, temporarily enhances dexterity. This unique fragrant oil influences the mind, providing a fleeting sense of empowerment. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A balm extracted from a rare plant. Temporarily increases Dexterity. This special fragrant oil affects the mind, granting a fleeting sense of empowerment.

### Dark Souls 2 Vine Balm Usage

  * Increases [Dexterity](/Dexterity "Dark Souls 2 Dexterity") by 5 for 45 seconds.

### Vine Balm Location in DKS2

  * [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce")
  * [Shulva, Sanctum City](/Shulva+-+Sanctum+City "Dark Souls 2 Shulva - Sanctum City"), low chance drop from [Sanctum Soldiers](/Sanctum+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Soldier")
