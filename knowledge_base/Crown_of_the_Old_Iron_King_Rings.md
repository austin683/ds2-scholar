**Crown of the Old Iron King Rings** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** refers to **[Rings](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings")** that are only available with the **[Crown of the Old Iron King DLC](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King")**. In **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** , the **[Crown of the Old Iron King DLC](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King")** introduces several rings that are specific to the expansion content. These rings offer unique effects to assist players in overcoming the challenges presented in the new areas introduced by the DLC.
