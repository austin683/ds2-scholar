**Heavy Iron Key** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Heavy Iron Key** , a piece of iron shaped like a key. Beyond the altar deep within the Iron Keep and through a door lies the tower where the Old Iron King once produced iron. Unlocks the door leading to [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower"), the initial location in the [Crown of the Old Iron King](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King") DLC. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> A piece of iron shaped like a key. Past the altar deep within the Iron Keep and through a door is the tower that the Old Iron King used to produce iron.  
> The unearthy weight of this chunk of iron is a reminder of the Iron King's own immeasurable strength.

### Heavy Iron Key Usage in DKS2

  * Opens the door that leads to [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower"), the first location of the [Crown of the Old Iron King](/Crown+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Old Iron King") DLC.

### Dark Souls 2 Heavy Iron Key Location

  * Added to inventory after buying the DLC.
  * In [Scholar of the First Sin](/Dark+Souls+II+Scholar+of+the+First+Sin "Dark Souls 2 Dark Souls II Scholar of the First Sin"), the key is located on a skeleton in the pit with the [Flame Lizards](/Flame+Lizard "Dark Souls 2 Flame Lizard") in the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants").
