#  Tower of Prayer (Shulva)

>> [Bonfires](/Bonfires "Dark Souls 2 Bonfires")   
Updated Oct 8, 2014 12:47 pm 

* * *

##  Location:

On the top level of a building that the player can reach by moving platforms in the end area of [Shulva - Sanctum City](/Shulva+-+Sanctum+City "Dark Souls 2 Shulva - Sanctum City")   

##  Bosses:

  * No boss in this area

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here respawns the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * 1x [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic")
  * 3x [Petrified Dragon Bone](/Petrified+Dragon+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Petrified Dragon Bone") and [3x Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite") (1 Chest)
  * 1x [Soul of a Hero](/Soul+of+a+Hero "Dark Souls 2 Soul of a Hero") and 3x [Dragon Charm](/Dragon+Charm "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Charm") (1 Corpse Loot)
  * 3x [Boltstone](/Boltstone "Dark Souls 2 Boltstone") and 3x [Bleed Stone](/Bleed+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Bleed Stone") (1 Chest)
  * 2x [Human Effigy](/Human+Effigy "Dark Souls 2 Human Effigy")
  * 1x [Focus Souls](/Focus+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Focus Souls")
  * 1x [Repair Powder](/Repair+Powder "Dark Souls 2 Repair Powder")
  * 10x [Rusted Coin](/Rusted+Coin "Dark Souls 2 Rusted Coin")
  * Enemy
  * Enemy

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here spawns the following new items/enemies:

  * Item
  * Item
  * Enemy
  * Enemy

##  Special Notes:

Due to the fact that 3 Twinkling Titanite and 3 Petrified Dragon Bones reset with each ascetic, and the fact that a bonfire ascetic resets as well, this bonfire is a great place for farming these upgrading materials.
