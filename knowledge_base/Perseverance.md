**Perseverance** is a Miracle in Dark Souls 2. To cast a Miracle, you must use a chime or Special Weapons that can cast Miracle.

> "Enwreathes its caster with a pure veil.  
> Grants temporary resistance to status effects.  
>   
> Lindelt ascetics are sent to distant lands for proselytizing and self-training, but many of them decide not to return."

### Acquired From

  * Acquired from [Cromwell the Pardoner](/Cromwell+the+Pardoner "Dark Souls 2 Cromwell the Pardoner") for 3500 souls.

### Perseverance Notes

  * Reduces received status effect buildup by 25%
