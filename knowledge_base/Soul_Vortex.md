**Soul Vortex** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul Vortex** , one of the forgotten sorceries preserved exclusively in the Undead Crypt. Unleashes a sequence of homing souls. An exceptionally challenging sorcery to evade, rendering it highly effective against agile adversaries. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "One of the lost sorceries preserved only in the Undead Crypt.  
> Fires successive homing souls.  
>   
> Extremely difficult sorcery to evade, making it very effective against frisky foes."

### Dark Souls 2 Soul Vortex Acquired From

  * [Brightstone Cove Tseldora -](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora")From the Lower Cove bonfire, going down a set of ziplines and hiding behind a bookshelf - along with the [Crescent Axe](/Crescent+Axe "Dark Souls 2 Crescent Axe")  
The Pit in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula") \- behind the Door requiring the [Forgotten Key](/Forgotten+Key "Dark Souls 2 Forgotten Key")  
Sold by [Grave Warden Agdayne](/Grave+Warden+Agdayne "Dark Souls 2 Grave Warden Agdayne").

### Soul Vortex Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Shoots a large hurricane of souls that deal magic damage. Slow casting speed and low damage. The orbs around the central eye can stun small enemies. Very useful for setting up other spells in PvP, ie. [Crystal Soul Spear](/Crystal+Soul+Spear "Dark Souls 2 Crystal Soul Spear").
