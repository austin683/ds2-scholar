**Black Leather Boots** is a **legging** and a part of [Black Leather Set](/Black+Leather+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Black Leather Boots Description

_"Boots dyed black. Allows the wearer to blend in with the dark. Often used by those with ill-intent. Meticulously crafted, and provides fair defense."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Sold by [Laddersmith Gilligan](/Laddersmith+Gilligan).

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shards, Large Titanite Shard, Titanite Chunks, Titanite Slab](/Ores)
  * The 'Thief Mask' looks dramatically different on male and female characters. The male version covers all but the eyes, whereas the female version is a small mask that only covers the mouth.
