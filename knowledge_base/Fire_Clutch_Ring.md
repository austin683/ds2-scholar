**Fire Clutch Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in **Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)**. The **Fire Clutch Ring** , featuring an image of a hand clasping a stone, amplifies fire attack capabilities while diminishing physical defense. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> Ring depicting a hand grasping a stone. Increases fire attack, but reduces physical defense. The origin of this ring is unknown, but its design suggests one of the darker deities. Effective use of this ring requires skill on the part of the wearer."

### Dark Souls 2 Fire Clutch Ring Effects

**Durability** : 30

**Weight** :

  * 2.0

**Effect:**

  * Increases Fire damage to weapons with Fire Infusion or base Fire Damage.
  * Decreases Physical Defenses by 80 points each.

### Fire Clutch Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

Treasure chest in the[ Inner Wall](/Inner+Wall "Dark Souls 2 Inner Wall") bonfire: After you beat [Avaa, the King's Pet](/Aava,+the+King's+Pet "Dark Souls 2 Aava, the King's Pet") and get the Storm gone. Right before you open the door to the first Eleum Loyce Knight, turn back and go though the left passage. On the right wall is an Illusory wall. Follow the way till you have to jump on a bridge, kill the Ice Soldier and behind him there is the Chest.

### DS2 Fire Clutch Ring Tips and Notes

  * Rules of the Fire AR increase:

  1. Weapons must have Fire elemental damage. The buff is static regardless of reinforcement or player build.
  2. Regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite") reinforced weapons will receive +15 Attack Rating
  3. [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite") and [Petrified Dragon Bone](/Petrified+Dragon+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Petrified Dragon Bone") reinforced weapons will receive up to +30 Attack Rating. The bonus amount is directly related to the weapon's Fire Infusion bonus. So weapons who don't receive good Fire Infusion ratings will give you a low bonus with this ring.
  4. [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite") and [Petrified Dragon Bone](/Petrified+Dragon+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Petrified Dragon Bone") reinforced weapons that had a pre-existing Fire element will receive more than +30 Attack Rating. The bonus amount is directly related to the weapon's Fire Infusion bonus. So weapons who receive good Fire Infusion ratings will give you a better bonus with this ring.
