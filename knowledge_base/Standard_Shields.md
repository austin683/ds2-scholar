**Standard Shields** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are types of [Shields](/Shields "Dark Souls 2 Shields") that have better stability and damage reduction than [Small Shields](/Small+Shields "Dark Souls 2 Small Shields"). Standard Shields are heavier than Small Shields but are lighter the [Greatshields](/Greatshields "Dark Souls 2 Greatshields"). Standard Shields can also perform a parry that has a uniform animation throughout. Standard Shields are often employed by players who need a shield but don't have enough points to invest into the Strength stat. This page cover a list of all Standard Shields in Dark Souls 2.
