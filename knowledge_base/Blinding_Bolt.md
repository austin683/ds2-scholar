**Blinding Bolt** is a [Miracle](/Miracles "Dark Souls 2 Miracles") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Blinding Bolt** , A divine miracle crafting a colossal soul mass transformed into a radiant orb of lightning. Forged in antiquity by the God of Sun, yet later proscribed by the very deity. To cast a [Miracle](/Miracles "Dark Souls 2 Miracles"), you must use a chime or Special Weapons that can cast Miracle.

> A miracle that creates a giant soul mass and transforms it into a lightning-spouting orb of light.  
>   
> Crafted in ancient times by the God of Sun, but later forbidden by the same deity. Was it to protect the world from hatred, or sorrow?"

### Dark Souls 2 Blinding Bolt Acquired From

  * Trade the [Old King Soul](/Old+King+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Old King Soul") and 10,000 souls with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis").

### Blinding Bolt Notes & Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Fires a small ball of light high into the air, eventually making a complete stop where it will explode in a flurry of lightning bolts. If it is interrupted before coming to a stop, it will dissipate and deal little damage. 
  * As of the newest patch, this miracle was majorly buffed, making it a very viable spell in PvP due to moderate damage and high firing speeds. Best used on large targets or groups due to its inaccurate nature. It also creates an opportunity for healing.
  * Since it only requires 1 slot, it is highly recommended to get multiple copies to get more casts by farming [Old Iron King](/Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Old Iron King").
  * At 65 faith, with the combination of [Lightning Dragon Chime +5](/Dragon+Chime "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Chime"), [Sun Seal](/Sun+Seal "Dark Souls 2 Sun Seal"), and [Lightning Clutch Ring](/Lightning+Clutch+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Lightning Clutch Ring") it deals approximately 700-800 damage per hit.
  * As of calibration 1.10, it deals less poise damage.
