_"Hood favored by pyromancers. They appear tattered but in fact the fabric is reinforced with pyromancy._  
_Magic is no show. It is an art that allows mere mortals to glimpse into the very fabric of what is and may be."_

### Location

  * Dropped by the Spider Monkeys in [No-man's Wharf](/No-man%27s+Wharf)
  * Full set can be found in [The Gutter](/The+Gutter).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Tattered Cloth Set](/Tattered+Cloth+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab).
