**Soul of the Ivory King** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of the Ivory King** , embodying the essence of the proud ruler of Eleum Loyce. The Ivory King, known for his boundless mercy, dedicated himself to safeguarding his grand domain until the flames of chaos consumed him. Acquired by overcoming the formidable boss, [Burnt Ivory King](/Burnt+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Burnt Ivory King"), within the [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King") DLC. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Ivory King of Eleum Loyce.
> 
> The proud Ivory King was ever-merciful, and devoted himself to the protection of his great land, until he was devoured by the flames of chaos.
> 
> The wondrous soul of this great king can be used to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Soul of the Ivory King Usage in DKS2

  * Trade with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") along with 5000 souls to obtain: [Ivory King Ultra Greatsword](/Ivory+King+Ultra+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 Ivory King Ultra Greatsword")
  * Consume for 30,000 souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of the Ivory King Location

  * Obtained by defeating the boss [Burnt Ivory King](/Burnt+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Burnt Ivory King") from the [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King") DLC.
