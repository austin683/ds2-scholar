**Darksign** is a special tool item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Darksign** , a cursed mark. The Darksign brings about death, sending the player back to the last rested bonfire, but at the expense of all held souls. Gather the fragments and piece them together before the will to do so wanes. [Tools](/Tools "Dark Souls 2 Tools") are unique, reusable items which assist the player during various facets of gameplay ranging from basic communication to assisting in boss encounters.

> An accursed mark. The Darksign induces death, returning the player to the last bonfire rested at, at the cost of all souls held.  
> Do what you must to gather the pieces, scraping them into some semblance of a whole, before the will to do so fades.

### Darksign Usage in DKS2

  * Kills you, sending you back to the last bonfire used and deleting all souls. This does **not** drop a bloodstain, and does **not** hollow you.
  * Has unlimited uses.

### Dark Souls 2 Darksign Location

  * In inventory from the beginning of the game.
