**Belfry Gargoyle Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Soul of the Belfry Gargoyle. Extracted from the Gargoyle of the Lost Bastille, a once-inanimate stone statue brought mysteriously to life. Consume for 6,000 souls or trade with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for the [Gargoyle Bident](/Gargoyle+Bident "Dark Souls 2 Gargoyle Bident"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Gargoyle of the Lost Bastille.  
> The elaborate stone statue on the belfry mysteriously came to life.  
> Use the special soul of this living statue to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Dark Souls 2 Belfry Gargoyle Soul Usage

  * Trade with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for [Gargoyle Bident](/Gargoyle+Bident "Dark Souls 2 Gargoyle Bident")
  * Consumed for 6000 souls

### Belfry Gargoyle Soul Location in DKS2

  * Obtained by defeating the [Belfry Gargoyles](/Belfry+Gargoyle "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Gargoyle") in Belfry Luna.

### Belfry Gargoyle Soul Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * The Belfry Gargoyles are a spiritual successor to the Bell Gargoyles from the original Dark Souls.
  * Consuming the soul yields 6,000 souls. It is generally recommended to trade it for the unique equipment unless the player has no use for a Strength-based spear.
