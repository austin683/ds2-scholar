The **Scorching Iron Scepter** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Scorching Iron Scepter** , an intensely heated iron scepter. Employed to set in motion mechanisms by conveying heat to [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower"). Discovered at the base of a tower near the primary structure of [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower"). [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> A scorching-hot iron scepter. Used to activate contraptions by transferring heat to Brume Tower.  
> When the Old Iron King wrested this dilapidated region from kingdom of Venn, the act required all the resources the enfeebled lord could muster. But with the discovery of this iron-producing miracle, he was reborn as a powerful leader."

### Scorching Iron Scepter Usage in DKS2

  * Placed in a slot in a structure in front of the [Foyer](/Foyer "Dark Souls 2 Foyer") bonfire, it activates all the stopped elevators in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower")

### Dark Souls 2 Scorching Iron Scepter Location

  * At the bottom of a tower adjacent to the main building of [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower").
