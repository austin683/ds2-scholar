_"Helm of the Black Drakeblood Knights,  
who came from a land long forgotten. _

_The Drakeblood Knights, who worshipped the blood of dragons, were led by Sir Yorgh in a siege of the Eternal Sanctum, but sunk into the Sanctum when the sleeping dragon awoke._

_The red cloth wrapped around the black armor represents the sacred blood of dragons."_

### Location

  * [Dragon's Rest](/Dragon%27s+Rest) on a corpse (except for [the shield](/Spirit+Tree+Shield) and [sword](/Drakeblood+Greatsword)).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Drakeblood Set](/Drakeblood+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab).
