**Dragon Talon** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Dragon Talon** , An incredibly weathered dragon talon. The tale suggests that within the farthest corners of the [Black Gulch](/Black+Gulch "Dark Souls 2 Black Gulch"), concealed behind a door barred from within, lies a splendid city crafted for a colossal dormant dragon. While this talon bears the unmistakable marks of antiquity, it exudes a formidable life force. Unlocks the entrance to [Shulva](/Shulva+-+Sanctum+City "Dark Souls 2 Shulva - Sanctum City"), the [Crown of the Sunken King DLC](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King") domain. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> A terribly worn dragon talon.  
> Legend has it that in the deepest reaches of the Black Gulch, behind a door locked from the inside, is a magnificent city built for a great sleeping dragon.  
> This talon clearly dates to ancient times, but great vitality emanates from it still.

### Dragon Talon Usage in DKS2

  * Opens the door to [Shulva](/Shulva+-+Sanctum+City "Dark Souls 2 Shulva - Sanctum City"), the [Crown of the Sunken King DLC](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King") area. The door can be found by reaching the primal bonfire chamber in the [Black Gulch](/Black+Gulch "Dark Souls 2 Black Gulch") and examining the obelisk.

### Dark Souls 2 Dragon Talon Location

  * Added to inventory after the player purchases the [Crown of Sunken King DLC](/DLC "Dark Souls 2 DLC") or the Lost Crowns Season Pass.
  * In _**Scholar of the First Sin,**_ after acquiring the [forgotten key](/Forgotten+Key "Dark Souls 2 Forgotten Key") down in the gulch, head back to Majula and jump down the well. There will be a ladder that will lead to a forgotten door. inside will be 3 chests and a corpse. The corpse contains the Dragon Talon. If you pay [Laddersmith Gilligan](/Laddersmith+Gilligan "Dark Souls 2 Laddersmith Gilligan") 12,000 souls for the longest ladder, getting to the forgotten door is easier. (Automatically added to inventory on Xbox 360 and PS3).
