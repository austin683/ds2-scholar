**Catarina Knight** is an [enemy](/enemies "Dark Souls 2 enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. The **Catarina Knight** , a red spirit clad in Catarina Knight's armor, occupies the top of the building near the Soldier's Rest Bonfire. He alternates between dual-wielding his sword and crossbow and two-handing the sword. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Catarina Knight Information in Dark Souls 2

_"Outside Catarina, they are often ridiculed for their Onion-like shape, infuriating the proud knights, but masterfully forged curved design makes it very effective for parrying."_

A red spirit wearing Catarina Knight's armor. He's located on top of the building with the Soldier's Rest Bonfire._

### Dark Souls 2 Catarina Knight Notes and Tips

  * He spawns without the invasion message.
  * He changes between dual wielding his sword and crossbow and two-handing the sword.
  * Spawns once. Can be respawned by using a Bonfire Ascetic on the Soldiers' Rest bonfire in the Forest of Fallen Giants.
  * Defeating the Catarina Knight will subtract 40 seconds from the duration of a co-op session. See [Online](/Online "Dark Souls 2 Online") for more detailed information about Multiplayer Time Limits.
