**Fist Weapons** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are a type of [Weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") that covers the hands, and has the shortest range among all weapon types. They also attack in quick succession, though some Fist Weapons don't do much damage on critical attacks, there are Claws that have a slight advantege when it comes to range, and they deal Slash damage. Some of these can inflict Bleed damage, one of the fastest weapon types that can inflict bleed. This page covers a list of all Fist Weapons in Dark Souls 2.
