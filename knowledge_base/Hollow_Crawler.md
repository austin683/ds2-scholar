**Hollow Crawler** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. The **Hollow Crawlers** , part of the Prowling Magus congregation, move slowly across the floor, attempting to crawl towards and attack the player. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Hollow Crawler Information in Dark Souls 2

Members of [Prowling Magus](/Prowling+Magus+and+Congregation "Dark Souls 2 Prowling Magus and Congregation") congregation who are crawling on the floor. They will (slowly) crawl after and attempt to attack the player. Believed to be consist of [Hollow Prisoners](/Hollow+Prisoner "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Prisoner") and hollowed [Milfanito](/Milfanito "Dark Souls 2 Milfanito"), can also be seen defending the [Aldia Warlock](/Aldia+Warlock "Dark Souls 2 Aldia Warlock") in [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana") after the 4th bonfire ( [Rise of the Dead](/Rise+of+the+Dead "Dark Souls 2 Rise of the Dead")).
