**Titanite Chunk** is a type of [Upgrade Material](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Titanite Chunk** , a precious piece of titanite unearthed from an ancient layer of earth, rumored to be a divine offering. Titanite chunks are exceedingly rare, with only a scarce few believed to exist in the world. They are instrumental in reinforcing equipment, enhancing it from +6 up to +9. [Upgrade Materials](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades") are items that are used to strengthen the statistical value and to increase the effect of a certain piece of equipment.

> Titanite chunk used to reinforce equipment.  
> Reinforces equipment from +6 up to +9.  
> Titanite was discovered in an ancient layer of earth, and is said to be a gift of the gods. There are said to be only a scarce few titanite chunks in the world.

### Dark Souls 2 Titanite Chunk Usage

  * Used to reinforce standard equipment from +6 to +9

### Titanite Chunk Location IN Dark Souls 2

  * A reward for killing a belltower intruder as a member of the [Bell Keepers](/Bell+Keepers "Dark Souls 2 Bell Keepers") covenant
  * Possible trade with [Dyna and Tillo](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo") the Crows
  * Buy from [Stone Trader Chloanne](/Stone+Trader+Chloanne "Dark Souls 2 Stone Trader Chloanne"), 6000 each after [King's Ring](/King%27s+Ring "Dark Souls 2 King%27s Ring") is obtained. (10 limit; unlimited after you defeat Nashandra.)
  * A chest in the [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods") after the third bonfire (Shaded Ruins - the one just after the fogged area). After fighting the first two lion warriors, move up the ramp and turn around, then jump across the gap to an easily visible chest. There's a [Petrified dragon bone](/Petrified+dragon+bone "Dark Souls 2 Petrified dragon bone") in the chest, as well.
  * In the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants") Cardinal Tower bonfire, In the room to the right just before [Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate") with the 4 big ballistae pointing at you, below is a chest that will contain x1 Titanite Shard. (NG++ there are x3 Titanite Chunks instead).
  * One drops from the Crystal Lizard by the gate in the middle of the skeleton-filled caves in [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman%27s+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman%27s Copse").
  * In the first poison pool in [Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley"), the rightmost item is one Titanite Chunk (3 in NG+ and up). Excellent farming place if you keep using a [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic"). There is another one in the same poison pool, to the right of the ramp leading up to the rest of the area.
  * 1 in a wooden chest in Doors of Pharros. In between two lockstone doors, above the cave with 2 rat things.
  * 1 on a corpse on a ledge in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora") immediately after the Pate/Creighton room.
  * 1 on a corpse in the room beyond the red crystal lizard in Brightstone Cove Tseldora, (3 chunks and 1 slab in NG+).
  * 1 on a Crystal Lizard to the left of the stone opening after the door under the Chapel Threshold bonfire in Brightstone Cove Tseldora. Doing a quick plunging attack can kill it before it can run.
  * 1 on a corpse on a ledge immediately following the previous Crystal Lizard.
  * 2 on a corpse on an ashen ledge accessible in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower") after activating the elevators and going down one level from the Foyer bonfire.
  * 3 on an ash corpse in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower") in the area before you encounter the [Fume Knight](/Fume+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Fume Knight").
  * 3 on a Crystal Lizard at the bottom of the stairs outside the front of Drangleic Castle.
  * 1 on a corpse in [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt") just beyond the second bonfire in the room filled with breakable rocks and the two statues protected by ghost mages.

### DKS2 Titanite Chunk Dropped By

  * Large Gyrm at [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros"). ~3% when wearing Jesters Helm, Prisoners Tatters, and Covetous Gold Serpent Ring +2
  * Dropped by [Coal Tar](/Darksucker "Dark Souls 2 Darksucker") in [Black Gulch](/Black+Gulch "Dark Souls 2 Black Gulch") (beasts that hide in the small pools) 5% chance of titanite chunk, 10% chance of large titanite
  * The Undead Peasants in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora"), 3% chance (or less?) (Farming them for chunks is not recommended. The 3 in front of the upper gate to Prowling Magus appear to have a higher drop rate.)
  * Dropped by [Possessed Armor](/Possessed+Armor "Dark Souls 2 Possessed Armor") in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower").
