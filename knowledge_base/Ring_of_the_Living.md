**Ring of the Living** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in **Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)**. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A ring bearing a sharp stud. Appear human when a phantom. This ring jabs into the skin, creating a throbbing pain that serves as a constant reminder of one's humanity.

### Dark Souls 2 Ring of the Living Effects

**Durability** : 130

**Weight** :

  * 0.2

**Effect:**

  * Appear human when you're a phantom/spirit.

### Ring of the Living Locations in Dark Souls 2

[Frigid Outskirts](/Frigid+Outskirts "Dark Souls 2 Frigid Outskirts") area, found on a corpse before the chasm leading to the bosses [Lud, the King's Pet](/Lud+and+Zallen,+the+King's+Pets "Dark Souls 2 Lud and Zallen, the King's Pets") and [Zallen, the King's Pet](/Lud+and+Zallen,+the+King's+Pets "Dark Souls 2 Lud and Zallen, the King's Pets")
