**Hard Leather Set** is an Attire or [Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor") in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

> Helmet made of iron.  
>   
> Armor/Gauntlets/Boots made of sturdy leather.  
>    
>  A commonplace piece of armor. No embellishments. Very solid.

###  Set Location

  * Starting gear of the Warrior class.
  * Sold by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") (4,1k).

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Gyrm+Worker "Dark Souls 2 Gyrm Worker").
  * Helm is the only item in the set offering Poise bonus.
  * [Mild Mannered Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate") is shown wearing this armor set in his concept art.
