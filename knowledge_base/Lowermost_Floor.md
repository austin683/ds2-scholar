>> [Bonfires](/Bonfires)   
Updated Sep 14, 2014 4:29 am

##  Location:

It's the deepest point in Brume Tower only accesible after you obtained the [Scorching Iron Scepter](https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Scorching+Iron+Scepter), to right end of the hallway before you enter the boss fog.   
  

##  Bosses:

  * [Fume Knight](/Fume+Knight)

  

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here respawns the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * Item
  * Item
  * [Iron Warrior](https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Iron+Warrior)
  * Enemy

  

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here spawns the following new items/enemies:

  * Item
  * Item
  * Enemy
  * Enemy

  

##  Special Notes:

The area is only accesible after activating the elevators.   
Be wary of 2 Iron Warrios on the way to the bonfire since they can be healed by the [Ashen Idols](https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Ashen+Idol).   
  

##  Notable Summoning/PvP Spots:

  * [Steelheart Ellie](https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Steelheart+Ellie) in the bonfire room.
  * [Carhillion of the Fold](https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Carhillion+of+the+Fold) just before the fog gate.
