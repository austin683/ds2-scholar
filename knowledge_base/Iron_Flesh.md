**Iron Flesh** is a [Pyromancy](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Iron Flesh** , pyromancy that internalizes the power of flame. Transforms the body into iron, enhancing defense and resistance. However, the resulting rock-solid flesh significantly impedes movement, posing potential challenges if employed at an ill-timed juncture. [Pyromancies](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies") are offensive fire spells that do not demand [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith") or [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") prerequisites and can be executed with a [Pyromancy Flame](/Pyromancy+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancy Flame") or [Dark Pyromancy Flame](/Dark+Pyromancy+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Dark Pyromancy Flame"). The fire damage scales with [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") and [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith"), yet there are no specific stat requirements for casting pyromancies. However, opponents resistant to fire damage remain unaffected by [Pyromancy Spells](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies").

> A pyromancy that internalizes the power of flame. Turns the body into iron. Increasing defense and resistance.  
> As one might guess, the rock-solid flesh enabled by this spell dramatically slows movement.  
> Guaranteed to cause trouble if used at an inopportune moment."

### Dark Souls 2 Iron Flesh Acquired From

  * From [Rosabeth of Melfia](/Rosabeth+of+Melfia "Dark Souls 2 Rosabeth of Melfia") for 3,500

### Iron Flesh Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Increases defense and resistance, in exchange for adding massive weight (65 weight) to the caster.
  * With enough VIT, it is possible to fast roll with it. The Royal Soldier Ring and the Third Dragon Ring help greatly for this purpose.
  * This spell lasts for 25 seconds, 35 seconds if the [Lingering Dragoncrest Ring](/Lingering+Dragoncrest+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Lingering Dragoncrest Ring") +2 is equipped, and 40 seconds if the full [Northwarder Set](/Northwarder+Set "Dark Souls 2 Northwarder Set") is equipped along with the ring.
  * Provides to the user: 100 physical defense, 200 to all elemental defenses, 200 to all status resistances, 100 poise, a 40% damage reduction and -10% movement speed.
  * Unlike its incarnations in other games, this spell does not give the user a weakness to Lightning damage, and actually boosts resistance to it.
