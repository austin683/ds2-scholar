**Throne Watcher Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of the Throne Watcher.** The throne of the King is sacred, demanding vigilant watchers. Acquired by overcoming both the Throne Watcher and Throne Defender in Throne of Want. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Throne Watcher.  
> The throne of the King is sacred, and must be observed closely at all times.  
> Use the special soul of the watcher to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Throne Watcher Soul Usage in DKS2

  * Consuming gives 16,000 Souls.
  * Trade with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for: 
    * [Watcher Greatsword](/Watcher+Greatsword#fex "Dark Souls 2 Watcher Greatsword#fex")
    * [Watcher's Shield](/Watcher%27s+Shield#fex "Dark Souls 2 Watcher%27s Shield#fex")

### Dark Souls 2 Throne Watcher Soul Location

  * Obtained by defeating the [Throne Watcher and Throne Defender](/Throne+Watcher+and+Throne+Defender "Dark Souls 2 Throne Watcher and Throne Defender") in [Throne of Want](/Throne+of+Want "Dark Souls 2 Throne of Want").
