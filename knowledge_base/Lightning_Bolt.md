**Lightning Bolt** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)****. Lightning Bolt** , crossbow bolts imbued with lightning. Tipped with Gold Pine Resin. Weapons enhanced with lightning are effective against foes resistant to magic and fire. Crossbows, when paired with specialized bolts, offer a solution for warriors with low dexterity to deliver elemental damage. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Bolts imbued with lightning. Tipped with Gold Pine Resin. Weapons fortified with lightning are effective against foes that are resistant to magic and fire. Crossbows and specialized bolts allow warriors with low dexterity to inflict elemental damage.

### Lightning Bolt Usage in DKS2

  * Crossbow ammunition
  * 70 Physical Damage
  * 160 Lightning Damage

### Dark Souls 2 Lightning Bolt Location

  * Sold by Chancellor in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle") for (80) souls per bolt in an Unlimited supply
