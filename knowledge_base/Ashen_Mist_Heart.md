**Ashen Mist Heart** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Ashen Mist Heart** , a tangible form of ashen mist granted by the ancient dragon. This ancient dragon's magic enables the exploration of memories from days gone by. Acquired by engaging in conversation with the Ancient Dragon in the Dragon Shrine. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> Manifestation of ashen mist received from the ancient dragon.  
> The magic of the ancient dragon allows one to delve into the memories of the withered.

### Ashen Mist Heart Usage in Dark Souls 2:

Have it in your inventory and examine the overgrown body of a giant to access a memory.   
Use to trigger the giant memories in **[Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants"), [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora"),** and **[Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt")**

  * [Memory of Vammar](/Memory+of+Vammar "Dark Souls 2 Memory of Vammar")
  * [Memory of Orro](/Memory+of+Orro "Dark Souls 2 Memory of Orro")
  * [Memory of Jeigh](/Memory+of+Jeigh "Dark Souls 2 Memory of Jeigh")
  * [Dragon Memories](/Dragon+Memories "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Memories")
  * [Memory of the King](/Memory+of+the+King "Dark Souls 2 Memory of the King") ([DLC](/DLC "Dark Souls 2 DLC"))
  * [Memory of the Old Iron King](/Memory+of+the+Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Memory of the Old Iron King") ([DLC](/DLC "Dark Souls 2 DLC"))

### Dark Souls 2 Ashen Mist Heart Location

  * Obtained by speaking with the [Ancient Dragon](/Ancient+Dragon "Dark Souls 2 Ancient Dragon"), in the [Dragon Shrine](/Dragon+Shrine "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Shrine")
  * Also dropped by the [Ancient Dragon](/Ancient+Dragon "Dark Souls 2 Ancient Dragon") when defeated, if you manage to aggro it before talking to it.
