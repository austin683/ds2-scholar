**Black Separation Crystal** is a [Multiplayer Item](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Black Separation Crystal** , a symbol of farewell handed down since time immemorial. This crystal either sends phantoms back to their homes or returns you to yours. [Multiplayer Items](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitive gameplay like PVP.

> A symbol of farewell that has been passed down since time immemorial. The crystal sends phantoms back to their homes, or sends you back to yours. Whether ending in hope or despair, encounters are valuable experiences. Beware fickle use of this item.

### Black Separation Crystal Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Used to send summoned players back to their world or to leave the world of a host.
  * It can also be used to leave the zone when summoned by a rat king covenant member.
  * It can also be used to concede duels in the Blue Sentinels or the Brotherhood of Blood.
  * Has unlimited uses.

### DKS2 Black Separation Crystal Location

  * Given to you at the start of the game.
