**Rampart Soldier** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Rampart Soldiers** are frozen warriors found throughout Eleum Loyce. Empowered by the Old Chaos, they wield the Ice Rapier, a weapon capable of shooting magical projectiles. However, they are vulnerable to backstabbing and attacks infused with Dark. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Rampart Soldier Information in Dark Souls 2

Frozen soldiers around the Eleum Loyce, they're enpowered by the [Old Chaos](/Old+Chaos "Dark Souls 2 Old Chaos"), wielding the [Ice Rapier](/Ice+Rapier "Dark Souls 2 Ice Rapier"), a weapon which shoots magical projectile afar. They're however, vulnerable to backstabbing or Dark.
