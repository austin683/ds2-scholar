**Curved Greatswords** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are a [Weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") type that's larger and more powerful that their counter part which are the [Curved Swords](/Curved+Swords "Dark Souls 2 Curved Swords"). Curved Greatswords are typically used as a two-handed weapon, they're slower than [Greatswords](/Greatswords "Dark Souls 2 Greatswords") in general but they are faster than [Ultra Greatswords](/Ultra+Greatswords "Dark Souls 2 Ultra Greatswords"). Curved Greatswords excel at damage output with speeds comparable to smaller weapons, these weapons mostly use slash attacks but each striker carries more weight behind it as to why it has a higher damage output. The angles of which the Curved Greatswords swings make their range less effective than the traditional Greatswords, but they are still faster which makes them effective when facing multiple opponents. This page covers a list of all Curved Greatswords in Dark Souls 2.
