**Dragon Scale** is a [Multiplayer Item](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") and covenant item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Dragon Scale** , a relic from the body of an ancient dragon. Present it to the dragon to draw your own essence nearer to that of the timeless ancient dragon. Contribute Dragon Scales to ascend in the [Dragon Remnants](/Dragon+Remnants "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Remnants") covenant. [Multiplayer Items](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitive gameplay like PVP.

> A scale from the body of an ancient dragon. Offer to the dragon to bring your own flesh closer to that of the eternal ancient dragon. Touching an ancient dragon scale gives one a glimpse into the abyss. Believers in the dragon will rise above this petty corporeal existence.

### Dragon Scale Usage in DKS2

  * Turn in Dragon Scales to gain rank in the [Dragon Remnants](/Dragon+Remnants "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Remnants") covenant
  * Unlike Dark Souls 1's counterpart, this one is not a weapon upgrading material.

    
    
    | Rank | Requirement | Reward |
    | --- | --- | --- |
    | 0 | Initial | Dragon Eye , Ancient Dragon Seal |
    | 1 | 10 Dragon Scales | Dragon Head Stone |
    | 2 | 20 Dragon Scales | Dragon Torso Stone |
    | 3 | 30 Dragon Scales | Black Dragon Greatsword |
    

### Dragon Scale Locations in DKS2

  * Rare drop from [Black Dragon Knights](/Black+Dragon+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Black Dragon Knight") before [Ancient Dragon](/Ancient+Dragon "Dark Souls 2 Ancient Dragon")
  * Rare drop from [Dragonfang Villard](/Dragonfang+Villard "Dark Souls 2 Dragonfang Villard")
  * Acquire by invading the world of another player, using the [Dragon Eye](/Dragon+Eye "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Eye"), and defeating the player.
  * Summoning a member of the [Dragon Remnants](/Dragon+Remnants "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Remnants") covenant as an invader (using the [Dragon Eye](/Dragon+Eye "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Eye")) and defeating them.
  * On a body near the elevator found after defeating the [Guardian Dragon](/Guardian+Dragon "Dark Souls 2 Guardian Dragon") in [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia%27s Keep"). 
    * This Dragon Scale can be respawned using a [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") at the [Ritual Site](/Ritual+Site "Dark Souls 2 Ritual Site") bonfire at [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia's+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Aldia's Keep"). Note that in the current version of the original release and all versions of SotFS, you will have to kill the [Guardian Dragon](/Guardian+Dragon+ "Dark Souls 2 Guardian Dragon ") before you're allowed to burn another Ascetic.
  * [The Imperfect](/The+Imperfect "Dark Souls 2 The Imperfect") near the [Lair of the Imperfect](/Lair+of+the+Imperfect+ "Dark Souls 2 Lair of the Imperfect ") bonfire of [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum")
