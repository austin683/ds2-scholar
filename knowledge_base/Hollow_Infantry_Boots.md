**Hollow Infantry Boots** is a **Legging** and a part of [Hollow Infantry Set](/Hollow+Infantry+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Hollow Infantry Boots Description

_Boots worn by Hollowed infantry.  
  
A piece of basic, minimal equipment.  
Worse yet, it's worn and nearly falling apart.  
Not recommended unless your options are spent._

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Drop from [Hollow Infantry](/Hollow+Infantry)

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shard, Large Titanite Shard, Titanite Chunk, Titanite Slab](/Ores)
  * The Infantry Set is the exact same weight but is superior in every other way.
