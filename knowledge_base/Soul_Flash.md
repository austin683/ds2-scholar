**Soul Flash** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Soul Flash, an ancient sorcery lost in the icy embrace of Eleum Loyce. Unleash a rapid barrage of small souls. Designed more for the protection of its caster than inflicting harm upon enemies. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "One of the ancient sorceries that were lost when Eleum Loyce froze over. Rapidly fire a barrage of small souls.  
> Intended to protect its caster more than damage enemies."

### Dark Souls 2 Soul Flash Acquired From

  * After the first bonfire of the Ivory King DLC you find a room with a lever and a path blocked by an elevator with coffins; pull the lever at the upper floor to unlock the path to a chest containing this sorcery.

### Soul Flash Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * [How to find Soul Flash](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=weZ3wm0rHjs&feature=youtu.be)
  * Similar to [Wrath of the Gods](/Wrath+of+the+Gods "Dark Souls 2 Wrath of the Gods"), fires a force formed with sorcery, and deals less damage but it may stunlock or even a knockback.
  * DLC: [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King").
