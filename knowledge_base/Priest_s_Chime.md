**Priest's Chime** is a **[Sacred Chime](/Chimes "Dark Souls 2 Chimes")** and one of the available **[Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons")** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**. **Priest's Chime** can be used by high-ranking clerics as a catalyst for [Miracles](/Miracles "Dark Souls 2 Miracles") and [Hexes](/Hexes "Dark Souls 2 Hexes").It boasts fast casting speed and high lightning power, making it an excellent choice for pure miracle casters. It is recommended to infuse it with Lightning as it scales better with Lightning damage than with Dark damage.

> Sacred chime granted to high-ranking clerics. A catalyst for miracles and hexes.   
>   
>  Drangleic was the home of many clerics, but King Vendrick held a lukewarm opinion of their powers, and kept them only as attendants to ceremony. In this sense, Drangleic was not the place for a cleric with any ambition.

### How to get Priest's Chime in Dark Souls 2

  * Found in a chest beneath the [Servants' Quarter](/Servants'+Quarters "Dark Souls 2 Servants' Quarters") bonfire in the [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille") after the Ruin Sentinel boss. Take the ladder Down and be aware of the dog (in Scholar of the First Sin edition, be extra careful: instead of having to fight a dog, _The Pursuer_ will spawn in that area instead).
  * Can be bought from [Titchy Gren](/Titchy+Gren "Dark Souls 2 Titchy Gren") in Undead Purgatory for 4000 souls.
  * One that has been upgraded to +8 can be found in Frozen [Eleum Loyce](/Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Eleum Loyce").

### DS2 Priest's Chime Hints and Tips

  * A solid chime for any pure miracle caster, it has a fast casting speed and high lightning power, and is obtained for free while progressing through the game. It can easily be used for the rest of the game if you aren't looking to branch out into hexes.
  * It is recommended that it be imbued with _Lightning_ when possible, as it scales better with Lightning damage than Dark damage.
  * 396 lightning bonus with 50 faith.

Parameter Bonus: Strength, Dexterity, Magic, Fire, Lightning and Dark bonuses - The scaling multiplier applied to the [Attack: stat]. Scaling quality is from highest to lowest as follows: S/A/B/C/D/E.

This resulting bonus damage is added to the base physical damage of the weapon and is shown in the equipment screen in blue numbers as a "+ X".  
Attack Type: Defines what kind of moveset the weapon has.  
Physical Damage falls under three categories: [Thrust](/Thrust+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Thrust Damage") (T), [Slash](/Slash+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Slash Damage") (Sl), [Strike](/Strike+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Strike Damage") (St).

Stability: How well the player keeps stance after being hit.  
Durability: The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!".  
Weight: How much the item weighs when equipped.
