**Throne Watcher Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Throne Watcher Set](/Throne+Watcher+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Throne Watcher Leggings Description

_"Helm/Armor/Gauntlets/Leggings worn by the Throne Watcher. Has high magic defense._  
_The Watcher waited by the throne for ages. Will all his waiting ever be rewarded?"_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Sold by [Maugilin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer#.UzMjBPldVdU)after killing the [Throne Watcher and Defender](/Throne+Watcher+and+Throne+Defender#.UzMjm_ldVdU).

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
