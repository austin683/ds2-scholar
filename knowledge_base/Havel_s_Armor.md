_"The origin of the name Havel is not clear. Some say it was the warrior who wore the armor, but others say that it was the name of a great kingdom ruined in a barbaric war."_

### Location

  * Use the [Forgotten Key](/Forgotten+Key) to unlock the door in [The Gutter](/The+Gutter).

### Notes

  * Part of [Havel's Set](/Havel's+Set). 
    * Set looks different comparing to Dark Souls plus it seems to be made from metal, not from stone.
    * When fully upgraded the entire set is the best for Physical Defense except for the Helm which is beaten by the Smelter Demon Helm.
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
  * In order to get the key for this door, you must proceed into the black gulch and drop down onto a hidden platform in between the two black worm creatures, You will see the locked door. Facing away from the door go left and there will be a 2nd platform below. and then down another below that one, and kill two giants.
