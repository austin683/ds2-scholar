# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Old+Souls

Remnants of the Four Great Souls from Dark Souls. Acquired along with the Four Great Souls in NG+ and any subsequent difficulty, or by use of [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") in NG.

###  The Old Souls

  
Refers to the following items:

  1. [Old Paledrake Soul ](/Old+Paledrake+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Old Paledrake Soul")   
Remnants of the soul of [Seath the Scaleless](https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Seath+the+Scaleless "Dark Souls 2 Seath the Scaleless"), found by defeating [The Duke's Dear Freja](/The+Duke%27s+Dear+Freja "Dark Souls 2 The Duke%27s Dear Freja") (Brightstone Cove Tseldora).
  2. [Old Witch Soul](/Old+Witch+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Old Witch Soul")   
Remnants of the soul of [The Witch of Izalith](https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/The+Bed+of+Chaos "Dark Souls 2 The Bed of Chaos"), found by defeating [The Lost Sinner](/Lost+Sinner "Dark Souls 2 Lost Sinner") (Sinnerâ€™s Rise).
  3. [Old King Soul](/Old+King+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Old King Soul")   
Remnants of the soul of either [The Four Kings](https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Four+Kings "Dark Souls 2 Four Kings") or [Gwyn, Lord of Cinder](https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Gwyn+Lord+of+Cinder "Dark Souls 2 Gwyn Lord of Cinder"),  found by defeating [The Old Iron King](/Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Old Iron King") (Iron Keep).
  4. [Old Dead One Soul](/Old+Dead+One+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Old Dead One Soul")   
Remnants of the soul of [Gravelord Nito](https://darksouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Nito "Dark Souls 2 Nito"), found by defeating [The Rotten](/The+Rotten "Dark Souls 2 The Rotten") (Black Gulch).

Collecting these 4 souls will allow progression through [Winter's Keep](/Shrine+of+Winter "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Winter") into [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle"). The final area through this route is [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt") where the boss is Velstadt, guard of King Vendrick. Near to Vendrick is the King's Ring, needed to gain access to Aldia's Keep, Throne of Want and Dream of the Giant.
