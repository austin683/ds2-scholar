**Tights of Judgement** is a **Legging** and a part of [Judgment Set](/Judgment+Set "Dark Souls 2 Judgment Set") in Dark Souls 2.

### Tights of Judgement Description

_"Tights honoring an ancient goddess._  
_Sin was said to be her domain, but the_  
 _name of this goddess is long forgotten._

_Little is known about what purpose the_  
 _mask served, only that it was worn by_  
 _a person of great authority."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Chest near the first [Drakekeeper](/Drakekeeper "Dark Souls 2 Drakekeeper") needs a [Pharros's Lockstone](/Pharros'+Lockstone "Dark Souls 2 Pharros' Lockstone")

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite")
