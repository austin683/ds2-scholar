**Soul of a Great Hero** is a type of [Consumable](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of a Great Hero** , the majestic soul of a great hero who succumbed to Hollowing long ago. Consume to acquire a substantial number of souls. The soul, the essence of life, persists even in the throes of hollowing. Grants 20,000 souls when consumed. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are consumable items categorized as remnants of fallen warriors, consuming souls collects a certain number of souls that is added to the player's stock. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are required for players to become stronger in the game by raising their soul level as well as buying and upgrading their equipment.

> Is a soul of a great hero who has long ago gone Hollow. Used to acquire a good amount of souls. The soul is the source of all life, even in hollowing.   
> The mind seeks souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of a Great Hero Usage

  * Consume for 20,000 souls.

### Soul of a Great Hero Location in DKS2

  * [Dragon Aerie](/Dragon+Aerie "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Aerie"); on a corpse by the second drake, by the third drake and on the platform dropped on to from the zip line above the bonfire(farmable in scholar of the first sin, because of the bonfire astetic found after the first bridge)
  * [Memory of Vammar](/Memory+of+Vammar "Dark Souls 2 Memory of Vammar"); one on top of the roof with the Giant Mages and the other is on a corpse by the [Soul of a Giant](/Soul+of+a+Giant "Dark Souls 2 Soul of a Giant")
  * [Memory of Orro](/Memory+of+Orro "Dark Souls 2 Memory of Orro"), Go right to the first hallway, go outside and immediately turn right, you should see a lootable body, it contains a [Soul of a Great Hero](/Soul+of+a+Great+Hero "Dark Souls 2 Soul of a Great Hero") and a [Soul Vessel](/Soul+Vessel "Dark Souls 2 Soul Vessel")
  * [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt"); after the room with [Agdayne](/Grave+Warden+Agdayne "Dark Souls 2 Grave Warden Agdayne"), at the bottom of the ladder, two undead are next to it
  * 1 on a corpse in Dragon's Sanctum near the area with [The Imperfect](/The+Imperfect "Dark Souls 2 The Imperfect").
