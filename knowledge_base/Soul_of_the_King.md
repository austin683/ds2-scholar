**Soul of the King** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of the King** , the soul of Vendrick, the once-mighty ruler of Drangleic. The King, now a mere shadow of his former self, clung to something dear. Use this soul of the one destined to link the flame to amass a multitude of souls or forge something of significant value. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

### Soul of the King Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Consuming gives 50,000 Souls.
  * You must pick up the King's Set in the same room to be able to trade the soul. 
  * Trade with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for: 
    * [Ruler's Sword](/Ruler%27s+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Ruler%27s Sword")
    * [King's Ultra Greatsword](/King%27s+Ultra+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 King%27s Ultra Greatsword")
    * [King's Shield](/King%27s+Shield "Dark Souls 2 King%27s Shield")

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of the King Location

From the second bonfire in [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana"), follow the path until the high wall structure. There is an [Amana Priestess](/Amana+Priestess "Dark Souls 2 Amana Priestess") directly on the left of that structure. From her position, go left and you'll find a submerged brick path. Be careful of another Priestess hidden in the structure, who will be able to target from behind while you follow the path. At the end of the path, there stands a [Dragonrider](/DragonRider "Dark Souls 2 DragonRider") guarding tree roots blocking the door, destroying the roots gives you access to the door, behind it is a chair with the Soul of the King on it and to the left, there is a path leading to his set. 

Only obtainable after having defeated [Vendrick](/Vendrick "Dark Souls 2 Vendrick") in [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt"). The door can only be opened if you have restored your humanity.

### Soul of the King Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * This Item will not respawn with the use of a [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic")
  * You need take the armor to exchange the soul. (It's in the same room)
  * Using Bonfire Ascetic to defeat Vendrick again does NOT respawn soul
