_"Armor worn by the Dragonriders, King Vendrick's royal guard._

_According to legend, the Dragonriders straddled not horses, but wyrms."_

### Location

  * Sold by [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir) at Bonfire Intensity 2+ and after defeating the [Dragonriders](/Dragonrider) in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Dragon Rider Set](/Dragonrider+Set). 
    * Upgraded stats are only 40% higher, not the usual 50%.
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
  * Chestpiece offers second highest poise (almost equal to that of [Gyrm Warrior set](/Gyrm+Warrior+set) chestpiece).
