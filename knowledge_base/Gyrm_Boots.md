**Gyrm Boots** is a **legging** and a part of [Gyrm Set](/Gyrm+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Gyrm Boots Description

_"Leggings of the nomadic Gyrm. Excellent poise but heavy"  
"The stocky Gyrm are kind-natured, but humans deemed them impure, and drove them underground."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Found on regular [Gyrm](/Gyrm) (The ones with the anvil on a chain) in [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros)
