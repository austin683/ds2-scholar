##  Bonfire Location:

Located in [Undead Purgatory](/Undead+Purgatory).   
  

##  Bosses:

  * [Executioner's Chariot](/Executioner%27s+Chariot)

  

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here respawns the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * Boss: [Executioner's Chariot](/Executioner%27s+Chariot)
  * Item: Notched Whip
  * Item: Parros lockstone,Toke of Fidelity (on the broken pillar in front of the Bridge Approach Bonfire)
  * Enemy: Phantom in front of the mist gate
  * Enemy: Purgatory Guardians before the wooden bridge
  * Enemy: Captive Undead (right before the Notched Whip)
  * Enemy: Purgatory Guardian and 2 Captive Undead (after the first stone bridge)

  

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here spawns the following new items/enemies:

  * Item
  * Item
  * Enemy
  * Enemy

  

##  Special Notes:

  * [Titchy Gren](/Gren) will not sell [Cracked Red Eye Orb ](/Cracked+Red+Eye+Orb)until NG+; Ascetics will have no effect.
  * The Red NPC Phantom at the front gate will continue to respawn either 12 times or indefinitely (?) once the Bonfire Ascetic is used. He also will be unable to be seen or damaged by co-op partners, though he will try to attack them but won't do any damage.
