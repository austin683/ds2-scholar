**Fire Bolt** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Fire Bolt** , crossbow bolt imbued with fire. Tipped with Charcoal Pine Resin. Weapons enhanced with fire are effective against foes susceptible to fire, such as Undead or beasts. Crossbows, in conjunction with specialized bolts, provide a means for warriors with low dexterity to deal elemental damage. Available for purchase from Weaponsmith Ornifex at 80 souls per bolt, with an unlimited supply. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Bolt imbued with fire. Tipped with Charcoal Pine Resin. Weapons fortified with fire are effective against foes vulnerable to fire, such as Undead or beasts. Crossbows and specialized bolts allow warriors with low dexterity to inflict elemental damage.

###  Fire Bolt Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Crossbow ammunition
  * 70 Physical Damage
  * 160 Fire Damage

### Dark Souls 2 Fire Bolt Location

  * Can be bought from [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for 80 souls per bolt and in unlimited supply.
