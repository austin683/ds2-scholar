**Rhoy's Stone** is a type of [Item](/Items "Dark Souls 2 Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Rhoy's Stone** , an item referenced in the game code but not accessible in-game. Its purported function is to increase the strength of enemies in a specific area until death or until a loading screen is encountered. Essentially akin to a bonfire ascetic but without the need for an actual bonfire. ### Rhoy's Stone Usage in DKS2

  * It makes the enemies in an area stronger until death or until you see a loading screen of some sort.
