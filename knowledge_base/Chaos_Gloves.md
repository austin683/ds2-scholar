### Chaos Gloves Description

_"Hood/Robe/Gloves/Leggings worn by a chaos sorcerer._  
_He calls himself Navlaan, now."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Given to after completing [royal sorcerer Navlaan](/royal+sorcerer+Navlaan "Dark Souls 2 royal sorcerer Navlaan") quest when you talk to him as hollow
  * Can be purchased from [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia") after you kill [Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan "Dark Souls 2 Royal Sorcerer Navlaan").

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with Twinkling Titanite
  * The hood improves casting speed and increases intelligence by +3.
  * Requires 16 Int and 16 Faith to use.
  * The chest piece offers the highest fire defense in the game when fully upgraded.
