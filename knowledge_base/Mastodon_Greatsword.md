**Mastodon Greatsword** is a **[Greatsword](/Greatswords "Dark Souls 2 Greatswords")** and one of the available **[Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons")** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**. **Mastodon Greatsword** is a powerful weapon that players seek for its strong attacks and versatility. This weapon can be upgraded to +10 using regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite") and has the same moveset as the [Bluemoon Greatsword](/Bluemoon+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 Bluemoon Greatsword"). 

> Greatsword of the Primal Knights that defend Drangleic Castle.  
>   
> The brutish and mighty Primal Knights wield this sword like a feather-light twig, but to the ordinary warrior it is a chore to even hold up, owing to its great weight.  
>   
> Terrible experiments were said to have taken place in a hidden manor in Drangleic. The mastermind presiding over the deeds was known as Lord Aldia.

### How to get Mastodon Greatsword in Dark Souls 2

  * Treasure in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle") near the first bonfire. Take a right out of the King's Gate bonfire to find a room with six soul-activated doors - three on either side (five filled with Ruin Sentinels, so take care.) The Mastodon Greatsword is found on the right side of the room in the middle doorway.
  * Drops by [Ducal Spider](/Ducal+Spider "Dark Souls 2 Ducal Spider") in lower [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora").
  * Drops by [Primal Knight](/Primal+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Primal Knight") enemies.

### DS2 Mastodon Greatsword Hints and Tips

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite") to +10.
  * Uses [Bluemoon Greatsword](/Bluemoon+Greatsword "Dark Souls 2 Bluemoon Greatsword") moveset.
  * Primarily intended for a strength or quality build as a [Claymore](/Claymore "Dark Souls 2 Claymore") and will do nearly the same damage when infused with an element.

  
**Moveset:**

  * One-handed r1: Horizontal Slash.
  * One-handed r2: Overhead Slash. Similar to the Zweihander one-handed r2.
  * Two-handed r1: Horizontal Slash.
  * Two-handed r2: 360 degree spin attack.
  * One-handed roll attack: Quick thrust.
  * Two-handed roll attack: 360 degree spin attack.
  * One-handed and two-handed sprint attack: 360 degree spin attack.

**Parameter Bonus** : Strength, Dexterity,Magic, Fire, Lightning and Dark bonuses - The scaling multiplier applied to the [Attack: stat]. Scaling quality is from highest to lowest as follows: S/A/B/C/D/E.  
The higher the player's [Str, Dex, Mag, Fire, Light] stat, the higher the [Attack Bonus: Stat] is (found on the player status screen). The higher the scaling letter, the higher the percent multiplier applied to the [Attack: Stat].  
This resulting bonus damage is added to the base physical damage of the weapon and is shown in the equipment screen in blue numbers as a "+ X".    
**Durability:** The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!" at this point the weapon does not perform at it's best.   
**Weight:** How much the item weights when equipped.    
**Stability:** How well the player keeps stance after being hit   
**Attack Type:** Defines what kind of swing set the weapon has.   
Regular(R), Thrust(T), Slash(Sl), Strike(St)
