**Katanas** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are a [Weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") type that's considered as an all-around balanced weapon. It has a good combination both slash and thrust attacks which all Katanas share in common. They can also be used effectively at all ranges. When Katanas are equipped in the offhand, they have the ability to [Parry](/Parry "Dark Souls 2 Parry"). It makes this weapon effective when dual-wielded since you can do Bleed damage with each successive strike, trimming down your opponent's health, and can be used to parry to take advantage of your counterattack. Do note that Katanas do break quickly because of their low durability. This page covers a list of all Katanas in Dark Souls 2.
