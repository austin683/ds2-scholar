#  Steel-willed Lorrie

    * [NPCs](https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/NPCs)
    * [Phantoms](https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Phantoms)

  
  
  
She's one of the 2 NPC summons that can be summoned at the [Iron Passage](https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Iron+Passage) and in the [Memory of the Old Iron King](https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Memory+of+the+Old+Iron+King). She wears the full [Mastodon Armor Set](https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Mastodon+Set) and wields a [Mastodon Greatsword](/Mastodon+Greatsword) and [Greatshield](/Mastodon+Greatshield).   
  

###  Notes:

  * As soon as she's summoned she will use the "Welcome" gesture.
  * If she is ignored during summoning, she'll attempt to greet you during battle.
  * If interupted during her gesture she'll do it again until the gesture is finished.
  * Her attacks do surprisingly low damage
  * She performs the "Joy" Gesture after defeating a boss if she survives
