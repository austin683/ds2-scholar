**Varangian Set** is an Attire or Armor in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds).

  
  
  
  
_" Helm (Amour. cuffs, leggings) worn by Varagians, that terrorized nearby seas. The coastline stretches far in northern Drangleic. Beyond this Northern Sea is an unexplored continent, said to be the home of things inhuman."_

###  Set Location

  * Dropped by [Varangian Pirates](/Hollow+Varangian) in [No-Man's Wharf](/No-Man%27s+Wharf).
  * [Scholar of the First Sin] Two extra pirates are found at back entrance to [The Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille), close to Exile Holding Cells bonfire.

  
  

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite).
  * Helm is the only piece offering Poise bonus.
