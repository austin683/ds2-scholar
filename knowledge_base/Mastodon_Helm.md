_"Helm worn by the Primal Knights of Drangleic castle._

_Their weight would normally crush a man, but the Primal Knights wear them as if they were silk, so fearsome is their brutal strength._

_The King restored a forbidden, long-lost art to create these inhuman abominations."_

### Location

  * Dropped by the [Primal Knights](/Primal+Knight) at [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Mastodon Set](/Mastodon+Set). 
    * While [Gyrm Warrior Set ](/Gyrm+Warrior+Set)has the highest physical defense for headpiece (greathelm) and armor, [Mastodon Set](/Mastodon+Set) has the highest physical defense for gloves and boot, among all armors under normal titanite upgrading path.
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab).
