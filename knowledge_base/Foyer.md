##  Location:

From the room with the [Iron Warrior](/Iron+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Iron Warrior") and the third [Ashen Idol](/Ashen+Idol "Dark Souls 2 Ashen Idol"), go outside trough the metal doors. Follow the path on the right, and go down the ladder. On the right, there will be a fog gate taking you back inside the tower. Head right in the circular room and you will find the bonfire in a opening on the right.
