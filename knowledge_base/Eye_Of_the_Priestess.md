**Eye of the Priestess** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Eye of the Priestess, grants the ability to perceive the unseen. Discovered at a colossal flaming Altar along the primary route past the second bonfire of [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce"). [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> The eye of the First Priestess who watched over Eleum Loyce. Allows one to see the unseen. All the great priestesses replaced  
> one of their birth eyes with this, returning it after their term was complete"

### Eye of the Priestess Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Allows you to see enemies with a cloak effect. This includes [Aava, the King's Pet](/Aava%2C+the+King%27s+Pet "Dark Souls 2 Aava%2C the King%27s Pet"), and invisible soldiers through the Ivory Crown DLC.
  * Allows you to see cloaked enemies in the [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods"), both the fog area and the ones in Shaded Ruins. (SotFS only)
  * Allows you to see a hidden ladder on the rampart closest to the starting bonfire (Eye is not required to climb).
  * Allows you to see the summon sign of Masterless Glencour on the path to the Aava boss fight.

### DKS2 Eye of the Priestess Location

  * Found at a giant flaming Altar after you follow the main path after the second bonfire of [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce"). This is past the large gate guarded by the lance ice soldier. [Location Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8-zHeDdcnhU&feature=youtu.be)
  * DLC [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King")
