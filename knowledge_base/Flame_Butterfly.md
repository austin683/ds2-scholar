**Flame Butterfly** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Flame Butterfly** , a vessel filled with dried red butterflies. Stimulating these rare butterflies produces a small flame, enabling the lighting of torches on the go. The butterflies excrete secretions that ignite upon exposure to air, providing them protection from natural predators. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Bottle filled with dried red butterflies. Stimulation of these rare butterflies creates a small flame, allowing torches to be lit on the go.  
> These butterflies exude secretions which ignite upon exposure to air, protecting them from natural predators. Known and loved as handy fire-lighters, but feared as the catalysts of disastrous fires when found in swarms.

### Flame Butterfly Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Relight torches without the need of a bonfire. (Light torch autonomously)

### Dark Souls 2 Flame Butterfly Location

  * Sold by [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora") for 600 souls each.
  * One on a corpse behind 2 large pots in a house in [No-man's Wharf](/No-man%27s+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No-man%27s Wharf"). The house is near the large ship (after you have rung the bell).
  * Three on a corpse in the cave near the waterfall in [Undead Lockaway](/Undead+Lockaway "Dark Souls 2 Undead Lockaway"), [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman's+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman's Copse").
  * One on a corpse near Weaponsmith Ornifex
  * Three on a corpse on top of a tree in the [Scorpioness Najka](/Scorpioness+Najka "Dark Souls 2 Scorpioness Najka") boss arena in Shaded Woods, you have to make her hit the tree to drop the body.
  * Five in a chest immediately following the boss fight with the [Smelter Demon](/Smelter+Demon "Dark Souls 2 Smelter Demon") in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep").
  * 10 found in Drangleic Castle in the room/shortcut near Central Castle Drangleic bonfire, the room also contains 5 Repair Powder and an Estus Flask Shard.
  * 3 on top of the courtyard roof in the [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille") (SotFS)
