_"Armor worn by Titchy Gren._

_Blessed with the protection of the god of blood Nahr Alma. This armor is much heavier than it looks, overwhelmed by the blood that it has absorbed."_

### Location

  * Kill [Titchy Gren](/Titchy+Gren "Dark Souls 2 Titchy Gren") after defeating Executioner's Chariot in the [Undead Purgatory](/Undead+Purgatory "Dark Souls 2 Undead Purgatory"). The hood will drop.
  * After reaching range 3 in [Brotherhood of Blood](/Brotherhood+of+Blood "Dark Souls 2 Brotherhood of Blood") covenant, talk to Titchy Gren and exhaust his dialogue, he will give you his set.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Nahr Alma Set](/Nahr+Alma+Set "Dark Souls 2 Nahr Alma Set").
  * Increases soul gain by 2.5%
