**House Key** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **House Key** , a key to the deserted house in Majula. Gifted by Cale the Cartographer. In bygone eras, Majula acted as a repository for dreadful things, yet now it serves as a refuge for those with nowhere else to turn. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> Key to the abandoned house in Majula. Recieved from Cale the Cartographer.  
> In the past, Majula served as a dumping ground for horrible things, but is now a gathering place for those with no better place to go. It seems this house in Majula was the final home for a few such souls

### House Key Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Opens the abandoned house in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula") (Referred to as "[Mansion](/Majula+Mansion "Dark Souls 2 Majula Mansion")")

### Dark Souls 2 House Key Location

  * From the Cardinal Tower bonfire in the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants"), go to the area where you climb a big tree root/branch across scaffolding.
  * Drop from the branch onto the rooftops below
  * Jump from house scaffolding to scaffolding with archer
  * Walk through the cave entrance, stop, and wait for the boulder to pass
  * Talk to the nearby NPC, called [Cale the Cartographer](/Cale+the+Cartographer "Dark Souls 2 Cale the Cartographer") until he gives you the key.
