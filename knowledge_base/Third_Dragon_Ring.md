**Third Dragon Ring** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). The **Third Dragon Ring** , a dragon-engraved signet ring, enhances HP, [Stamina](/Stamina "Dark Souls 2 Stamina"), and maximum load. However, its durability is a weakness. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> A dragon signet ring. Grants its bearer the protection of dragons. Increases HP, stamina, and maximum load, but is easily broken. Raulmond, knight of Drangleic, served the king by guarding the fort until his own demise, and was said to have cherished this ring throughout his life of service."

### Dark Souls 2 Third Dragon Ring Effects

**Durability** :

  * 1st = 30
  * 2nd = 25
  * 3rd = 20/20

**Weight** :

  * 1st=0.5
  * 2nd=0.8
  * 3rd=1.0

**Effect:**

  * Increases HP by 3%, stamina by 5% and maximum load by 5%
  * 2nd = 5%; 10%; 10%
  * 3rd = 7.5%; 12.5%; 12.5%

### Third Dragon Ring Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * 1st: Given to the player upon reaching rank 2 of the [Company of Champions](/Company+of+Champions "Dark Souls 2 Company of Champions")
  * 2nd: Dropped by [Manscorpion Tark ](/Manscorpion+Tark "Dark Souls 2 Manscorpion Tark") or given by [Manscorpion Tark](/Manscorpion+Tark "Dark Souls 2 Manscorpion Tark") as a reward for killing The Duke's Dear Freja
  * 3rd: [Dragon Shrine](/Dragon+Shrine "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Shrine") After the first bonfire, walk up the first set of stairs past the sword/shield giant, run past the mace-wielding giant and then all the way up the stairs to encounter a sword/shield giant and a mace giant. At the stairs, walk forward slightly and turn left to a path covered with boxes. Break the boxes and jump down to see two chests (the right one is a Mimic). Run further in to encounter another mace giant guarding a door. Get past it, into the door, and the ring is in the chest.

### DS2 Third Dragon Ring Tips and Notes

  * The [First](/First+Dragon+Ring "Dark Souls 2 First Dragon Ring"), [Second](/Second+Dragon+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Second Dragon Ring") and [Third Dragon Ring](/Third+Dragon+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Third Dragon Ring") are treated as the Regular, +1 and +2 versions of the same ring, in that only one of them can be worn at once.
  * It is possible to obtain two [Second Dragon Ring](/Second+Dragon+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Second Dragon Ring") of these rings by first killing [The Duke's Dear Freja](/The+Duke%27s+Dear+Freja "Dark Souls 2 The Duke%27s Dear Freja"), speaking to [Manscorpion Tark](/Manscorpion+Tark "Dark Souls 2 Manscorpion Tark") to obtain his reward, and then killing him afterwards.
