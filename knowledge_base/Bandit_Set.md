**Bandit Set** is an Attire or [Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor") in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

> Helmet/Gauntlets/Armor/Boots of bandits of the Forossan outskirts.  
>    
>  The territory of Forossa became lawless after the kingdom fell to war. Citizens became bandits, and scattered to other lands.

###  Set Location

  * Starting armor of Bandit class.
  * Sold by [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") (5,1k) in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep").

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
