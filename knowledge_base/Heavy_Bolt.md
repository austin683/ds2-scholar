**Heavy Bolt** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Heavy Bolt** , conventional crossbow bolts crafted from iron. Efficient at piercing enemies. Particularly effective in ranged assaults, although their usage can be somewhat costly. Heavy Bolts can also be employed to safely eliminate hazardous pots. Available for purchase from Blacksmith McDuff at 50 souls each. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Standard crossbow bolts made of iron.  
> Effective at piercing enemies. Very effective in ranged attacks,  
> though somewhat expensive to use willy-nilly.

### Heavy Bolt Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Crossbow ammunition
  * 100 Physical Damage
  * Heavy Bolts can be used to destroy hazardous pots from a safe distance

### Dark Souls 2 Heavy Bolt Location
