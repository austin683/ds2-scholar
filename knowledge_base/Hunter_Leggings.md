**Hunter Leggings** is a **legging** and a part of [Hunter Set](/Hunter+Set "Dark Souls 2 Hunter Set") in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Hunter Leggings Description 

_"The hunting goddess Evlana was no goddess at all, but rather a brave and highly skilled bow huntress.  
Long after her demise, the passing of lore transformed her into a deity."_

### Where to Find \ Location

    * At the [Soldier's Rest](/Soldier's+Rest "Dark Souls 2 Soldier's Rest") bonfire in the [Forest of The Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants") it is out and to the left on top of a wooden structure in a chest. 
      * You will need the key from [The Last Giant](/The+Last+Giant "Dark Souls 2 The Last Giant") to access this area.

### 

### Notes

  * Part of the [Hunter Set](/Hunter+Set "Dark Souls 2 Hunter Set").
  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
