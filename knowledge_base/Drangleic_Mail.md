_"Traditional Drangleic Armor. Belonged to Captain Drummond._

_Drummond's ancestors have served Drangleic for generations, principally as defenders of the great fort, but Captain Drummond is the last in this proud line."_

### Location

  * The [Drangleic Set](/Drangleic+Set) (without Helm) can be found after you defeat the [Pursuer](/Pursuer). Walk past the area you fought him in and you will see a hole in the ground. Dropping into the hole will reveal a corpse, which will include the set as well as a Greatsword and Shield.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Drangleic Set](/Drangleic+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
