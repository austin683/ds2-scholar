**Small Smooth & Silky Stone** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Small Smooth & Silky Stone**, serves to gently restore HP. The distinct shine of this stone is no ordinary polish; it can only be attained through the passage of an extended period. Certain individuals in this region actively seek out such unique stones. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A smooth and silky stone. Use to slightly restore HP. The shine of this stone is no ordinary polish,  
> and can only be achieved over a long period. Some in this land are in search of such stones.

### Dark Souls 2 Prism Stone Usage

  * When consumed, they provide a small amount of HP.
  * Can be traded with [Dyna and Tillo](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo") for a random low-mid-tier item.

### Prism Stone Location in Dark Souls 2

  * 1x in [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt") under the waterfall.
  * 3x found in [No Man's Wharf](/No+Man%27s+Wharf "Dark Souls 2 No Man%27s Wharf") just past the [Pharros Lockstone](/Pharros+Lockstone "Dark Souls 2 Pharros Lockstone") on a ledge.
  * 3x found in the [Grave of Saints](/Grave+of+Saints "Dark Souls 2 Grave of Saints")
  * 3x found in the poison pits in [Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley")
  * Drop from the [Hollow Peasants](/Hollow+Peasant "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Peasant") and the [Undead Devourers](/Undead+Devourer "Dark Souls 2 Undead Devourer") (Enraged Pigs) in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora"), near the first bonfire ([Royal Army Campsite](/Royal+Army+Campsite "Dark Souls 2 Royal Army Campsite")).
  * 10x rewarded for achieving the [Rat King Covenant](/Rat+King+Covenant "Dark Souls 2 Rat King Covenant") rank 1.

### DKS2 Prism Stone Notes and Tips

  * **Dyna & Tillo will not accept stacked stones.** You may stand in the nest, select "Leave" and drop them individually to receive a reward. You may drop multiple individual stones one at a time and receive all the rewards at once.
