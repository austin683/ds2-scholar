**Soul of Nashandra** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of Nashandra** , the Queen of Drangleic. Nashandra emerged from the Dark, driven by an insatiable craving for power. Utilize the unique soul of this captive of desire to amass numerous souls or forge something of significant value. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of Nashandra Usage

  * Consuming gives 30,000 Souls.
  * Can be traded with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for: 
    * [Scythe of Want](/Scythe+of+Want#fex "Dark Souls 2 Scythe of Want#fex")
    * [Chime of Want](/Chime+of+Want#fex "Dark Souls 2 Chime of Want#fex")
    * [Bow of Want](/Bow+of+Want#fex "Dark Souls 2 Bow of Want#fex")

### Soul of Nashandra Location in DKS2

  * Obtained by defeating [Nashandra](/Nashandra "Dark Souls 2 Nashandra") in [Throne of Want](/Throne+of+Want "Dark Souls 2 Throne of Want").
