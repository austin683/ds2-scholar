_"Armor worn by once-proud clerics._

_Relics of a party who long ago attempted to conquer the Undead Crypt._

_For this sin their deaths have been postponed, so they may serve as crypt sentries."_

### Location

  * (Vanilla ds2) Bought from [Grave Warden Agdayne](/Grave+Warden+Agdayne "Dark Souls 2 Grave Warden Agdayne") in the [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt").
  * Costs 5400/9000/6300/7200 souls
  * In Softs edition you need to light 2 sconces and kill 12 red phantom insolent warriors and be wary that the drops are rng for some and 3 drops are guaranteed and the other armor pieces pure random.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Insolent Set](/Insolent+Set "Dark Souls 2 Insolent Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+SLab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite SLab").
