**Dark Pine Resin** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Dark Pine Resin** , hexed pine resin. Infuses a dark flame into the right-hand weapon, causing it to deal dark damage for a short duration. Particularly effective against foes vulnerable to darkness, such as clerics and magical creatures. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> Hexed pine resin. Applies dark flame to right-hand weapon. The affected weapon inflicts dark damage for a short time. Particularly effective against enemies that fear dark, such as clerics and magical creatures.

### Dark Pine Resin Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Boosts existing dark damage by 30%
  * Adds 50 dark attack value of the right-hand weapon for 90 seconds
  * This item can not be used for weapons that are impossible to buff with items, such as weapons infused with an element.

### Dark Souls 2 Dark Pine Resin Location

  * Dropped by [Artificial Undead](/Artificial+Undead "Dark Souls 2 Artificial Undead") in Harvest Valley near the 2nd bonfire.
  * Bought from [Felkin The Outcast](/Felkin+The+Outcast "Dark Souls 2 Felkin The Outcast") (1500 souls, unlimited) at the first bonfire in [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman%27s+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman%27s Copse").
  * Drops from the [Rupturing Hollow](/Rupturing+Hollow "Dark Souls 2 Rupturing Hollow")s in [Dragon Aerie](/Dragon+Aerie "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Aerie")
