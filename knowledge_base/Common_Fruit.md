**Common Fruit** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Common Fruit** , temporarily enhances poison resistance. This ordinary fruit lacks flavor, but when confronted with a true test of resilience, its benefits could mean the difference between life and death [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A spotted fruit. Temporarily boosts poison resist.  
> This common fruit has no taste, but when facing a true test of mettle, its benefits could make the difference between life and death.

### Common Fruit Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Boosts poison DEF by 200 for 90 seconds

### Dark Souls 2 Common Fruit Location

  * Drop from the Royal Swordsmen in [The Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille").
  * Dropped by Ogres.
  * Sold by the [Rat King](/Rat+King "Dark Souls 2 Rat King") for 1600 souls.
