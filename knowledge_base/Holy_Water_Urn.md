**Holy Water Urn** is a type of [Projectile](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2). Holy Water Urn** , a receptacle holding crystal-clear water. Initially crafted as a calming elixir, this water sears the flesh of Undead and Hollows, dealing substantial damage to such foes. Notably effective against the sentinels in Heide's Tower of Flame. [Projectiles](/Projectiles "Dark Souls 2 Projectiles") are offensive items that are usually thrown at hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") to inflict a small amount of damage.

> An urn filled with crystal-clear water. Originally intended as a soothing potion, this water scalds the skin of Undead and Hollows.
> 
> In the old capital of Mirrah, an ancient, well-protected spring produces holy water, but the water has been difficult to acquire ever since Mirrah became embroiled in war.

### Dark Souls 2 Holy Water Urn Usage

  * Thrown like a [Firebomb](/Firebomb "Dark Souls 2 Firebomb"), it deals large amounts of damage to undead enemies. It can also deal tremendous amounts of damage to the sentinels in [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide%27s Tower of Flame").

### Holy Water Urn Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Sold by [Blue Sentinel Targray](/Blue+Sentinel+Targray "Dark Souls 2 Blue Sentinel Targray") in the [Cathedral of Blue](/Cathedral+of+Blue "Dark Souls 2 Cathedral of Blue") for 300 souls each.
  * Three are found in the well at the [Royal Army Campsite](/Royal+Army+Campsite "Dark Souls 2 Royal Army Campsite"), located in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora").
  * Dropped by the ArchDrake Priestesses in the Shrine Of Amana.
  * 5 on a corpse in the spiked ground area in [Dragon's Sanctum](/Dragon%27s+Sanctum "Dark Souls 2 Dragon%27s Sanctum").

### Dark Souls 2 Holy Water Urn PvP Notes

  * No longer does any damage to enemies that are not hollow.
