### Imperious Gloves Description

_"Gauntlet worn by once-proud knights._  
_Relics of a party who long ago attempted to conquer the Undead Crypt._  
_For this sin their deaths have been postponed, so they may serve as crypt sentries."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Armor can be dropped by the enemies with twin shields in the Undead Crypt.
  * Parts drop from the second red phantom in crystal cove right before the bridge over the crystal bed (NG+).
