_"A traditional sacred headcloth/longshirt/gloves/skirt crafted at Lindelt Monastery. Made of lightweight, flowing white fabric._  
  
_The ceremonial design suggests that they revered whatever it was they watched over."_

### Location

  * Pieces of the armor can be dropped from a Black phantom found behind a painting in [Aldia's Keep](/Aldia%27s+Keep) (NG+).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Monastery Set](/Monastery+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
  * Painting Guardians from Dark Souls 1.
  * Farming advice: bring a lot of lightning and poison arrows for the two trolls before the boss entrance. They don't aggro if you have a little bit of distance.
