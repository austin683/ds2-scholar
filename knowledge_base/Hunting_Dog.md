**Hunting Dog** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Hunting Dog** , a mutated canine created through experiments by Lord Aldia. These dogs are not a naturally occurring species and are found only in areas where they were introduced by the Dragon Acolytes. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Hunting Dog Information

_A mutated canine born from the experiments conducted by Lord Aldia. Hunting Dogs are not a naturally-occurring species and are therefore only found in areas to which they were introduced by the Dragon Acolytes. A number of them were dumped into The Gutter over the course of Lord Aldia's research and continue to prowl its depths in search of anything that might serve as a meal._

### Hunting Dog Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Some of their attacks have weird hitboxes. The most notable being their bite attack, which can damage you if you are attacking the dog from behind.
  * The bite attack can be parried, but the dog cannot be riposted. However, it is stunned for quite some time.
