##  Bonfire Location:

Located in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle).   
Moving from the room with Nashandras painting in it, go up the stairs with the Syan Soldier all the way up, looking immediately to the left and you'll see a ladder, go down the ladder and on the small platform there is an Illusion wall behind the ladder. leading to a small room with only a bonfire.
