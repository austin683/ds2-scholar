**Homeward Bone** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Homeward Bone** , a bone as white as ash, allows you to return to the last bonfire rested at. Born from the remains of an adventurer whose journey was cut short, it holds the power to traverse to bonfires. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A white-ashen bone. Return to the last bonfire rested at. Bonfires burn on the bones of Undead. And this bone, belonging to one whose journey was cut short, has the power to travel to bonfires. As if it yearns to resume its futile quest...

### Homeward Bone Usage in DKS2

  * Consumable item. When used, it returns the player to the last bonfire they rested at **without losing any souls**
  * Can be utilized to flee a boss battle. Can be tricky however as you're vulnerable during the use animation.
  * Once obtained, the [Aged Feather](/Aged+Feather "Dark Souls 2 Aged Feather") will have the same effect with unlimited uses.
  * Both this and the Aged Feather can be used as an early warning system against invaders or can reveal that you have been invaded if you happen to miss the message. If you are invaded, the item will be darkened out.

### Dark Souls 2 Homeward Bone Location

  * [Sweet Shalquoir ](/Sweet+Shalquoir "Dark Souls 2 Sweet Shalquoir") in Majula sells up to 10 of them at 600 Souls each 
    * She will sell an unlimited amount after the [Shrine of Winter](/Shrine+of+Winter "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Winter") is opened
