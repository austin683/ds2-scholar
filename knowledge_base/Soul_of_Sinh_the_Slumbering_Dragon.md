**Soul of Sinh, Slumbering Dragon** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of Sinh, the Slumbering Dragon** , the essence of Sinh the Dragon, who slumbers in the deepest depths of this land. The King built a splendid city, and the dragon rested peacefully. However, Sir Yorgh disrupted its slumber with a single mighty strike, causing the dragon to release its venomous store. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of Sinh the Dragon, who sleeps in the lowermost depths of this land. The King erected a magnificent city, and the dragon slept soundly. Until Sir Yorgh disturbed it with a single great strike, and the dragon could bear its store of poison no longer. The rain of death toppled the city, but restored the dragon's purity.  
> The wondrous soul of this dragon can be used to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Soul of Sinh, Slumbering Dragon Usage in DKS2

  * Can be traded to [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for [Yorgh's Spear](/Yorgh%27s+Spear#fex "Dark Souls 2 Yorgh%27s Spear#fex").
  * Gives 16,000 when consumed.

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of Sinh, Slumbering Dragon Location

  * Obtained by killing [Sinh, the Slumbering Dragon](/Sinh%2C+the+Slumbering+Dragon "Dark Souls 2 Sinh%2C the Slumbering Dragon")
