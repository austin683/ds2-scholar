**Soul of Velstadt** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of Velstadt** , the soul of the devoted defender of the King within the Undead Crypt. Velstadt stood faithfully at the King's side, a shadow to his lord. When the King withdrew to the Undead Crypt, Velstadt followed, never to return. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of Velstadt, defender of the King inside the Undead Crypt. Velstadt was always at the King's side, as if he were his lord's own shadow. After the King retired to the Undead Crypt, Velstadt followed, never to return.  
> Use the special soul of this knight to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of Velstadt Usage

  * Consuming gives 15 000 souls.
  * Trade with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for [Sacred Oath](/Sacred+Oath#fex "Dark Souls 2 Sacred Oath#fex").
  * Trade with [weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for [Sacred Chime Hammer](/Sacred+Chime+Hammer#fex "Dark Souls 2 Sacred Chime Hammer#fex").

### Soul of Velstadt Location in DKS2

  * Obtained by defeating [Velstadt, the Royal Aegis](/Velstadt%2C+the+Royal+Aegis "Dark Souls 2 Velstadt%2C the Royal Aegis") in [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt "Dark Souls 2 Undead Crypt").
