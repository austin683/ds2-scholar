### Brigand Gauntlets Description

_"Hood/Armor/Gauntlets/Trousers donned by looting brigands."_  
_"This armor may appear shoddy, but is in fact a very functional piece of work, its materials meticulously crafted together."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * [No-Man's Wharf](/No-Man%27s+Wharf) in a chest.
  * Dropped by [Crescent Sickle Phantom](/Crescent+Sickle+Phantom).

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [titanite shards, large titanite shards, titanite chunks and titanite slab](/Ores).
