_"Hemp hood that fully covers the face. Provides protection from the elements, and little more."_

### Location

  * Starting equipment for all players.  

### Notes

  * Part of every [Starting Class](/starting+classes).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Ores).
  * [NG+]The Things Betwixt Black phantom that is wielding a Heavy Crossbow at the third Tutorial tree drops +5 pieces of the Set
