**Giant Lord Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Giant Lord Soul** , the soul of the commanding Giant Lord who once laid siege to Drangleic. These Giants, arriving on the northern shores, besieged King Vendrick's castle in pursuit of a priceless treasure. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

### Giant Lord Soul Usage in DKS2

  * Consuming gives 25000 Souls.
  * Trade with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for [Repel.](/Repel#fex "Dark Souls 2 Repel#fex")
  * A considerable farming target for unlocking [Shrine of Winter](/Shrine+of+Winter "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Winter") gate in NG+ or further

### Dark Souls 2 Giant Lord Soul Location

  * Obtained by defeating the [Giant Lord](/Giant+Lord "Dark Souls 2 Giant Lord") in [Memory of Jeigh](/Memory+of+Jeigh "Dark Souls 2 Memory of Jeigh").

### Giant Lord Soul Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Does not substitute for a [Soul of a Giant](/Soul+of+a+Giant "Dark Souls 2 Soul of a Giant")
