_"Helmet worn by once-proud knights._

_Relics of a party who long ago attempted to conquer the Undead Crypt._  
_For this sin their deaths have been postponed, so they may serve as crypt sentries.__"_

### Location

  * Armor can be dropped by the enemies with twin shields in the [Undead Crypt](/Undead+Crypt).
  * Parts drop from the second red phantom in crystal cove right before the bridge over the crystal bed (NG+).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Imperious Helm](/Imperious+Helm).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite).
