**Sanctum Repeating Crossbow** is a [weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") in **Dark Souls 2.** **Sanctum Repeating Crossbow** , a formidable weapon wielded by the sanctum knights, is a testament to their unwavering commitment and the mysterious rituals conducted in the great depths. This specialized [Crossbow](/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows"), designed for the eternal stewardship of the Sanctum, boasts a unique feature in its strong attack, unleashing dark bolts with powerful force.

> Crossbow wielded by sanctum knights.   
>   
>  Strong attack fires dark bolts.   
>   
>  These guardian knights were sentenced to eternal stewardship of the Sanctum in a clandestine ritual carried out in the great depths, but today the sanctum receives nary visitor. 

### How to get Sanctum Repeating Crossbow in Dark Souls 2

  * Dropped by the [Sanctum Knights](/Sanctum+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Knight"). on the Crown of the Sunken King DLC

### DS2 Sanctum Repeating Crossbow Hints and Tips

  * Shoots three bolts in quick succession, similar to the [Avelyn](/Avelyn "Dark Souls 2 Avelyn").
  * As like all other crossbows, it is NOT possible to infuse this weapon with poison.
  * Two-handed strong attack on the supporting hand (left strong attack if equipped to right hand) unleashes a barrage of Dark Bolts.
  * Has no base dark damage, but gets an extra by scaling up to + 80.
  * It is not recommended to infuse the weapon with dark if you want to use it as a normal crossbow, as it loses damage this way.
  * However if you're dedicated on using the special attack, it is highly recommended to infuse with Dark, it boosts special attack damage.
  * The highest damage is dealt with lightning infusion and lightning bolts or with mundane infusion, depending on the character stats.
  * Dual SRCs with lightning infusion, lightning bolts, [Ring of Blades +2](/Ring+of+Blades "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Blades") and [Lightning Clutch Ring](/Lightning+Clutch+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Lightning Clutch Ring") deal about 700 damage.
  * the [Dark Clutch Ring](/Dark+Clutch+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Dark Clutch Ring") from the 3rd dlc increases the damage the special attack does by a significant margin
  * You can powerstance this with the avelyn.

### DS2 Sanctum Repeating Crossbow Trivia

  * The crossbow itself appears to be a reference to the manga "Berserk", where the protagonist uses an identical arm-mounted repeater crossbow.

Parameter Bonus: Strength, Dexterity, Magic, Fire, Lightning and Dark bonuses - The scaling multiplier applied to the [Attack: stat]. Scaling quality is from highest to lowest as follows: S/A/B/C/D/E.  
The higher the player's [Str, Dex, Mag, Fire, Lightning, Dark] stat, the higher the [Attack Bonus: Stat] is (found on the player status screen). The higher the scaling letter, the higher the percent multiplier applied to the [Attack: Stat].  
This resulting bonus damage is added to the base physical damage of the weapon and is shown in the equipment screen in blue numbers as a "+ X".  
Attack Type: Defines what kind of moveset the weapon has.  
Physical Damage falls under three categories: [Thrust](/Thrust+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Thrust Damage") (T), [Slash](/Slash+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Slash Damage") (Sl), [Strike](/Strike+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Strike Damage") (St).  
Elemental Damage Types: [Magic](/Magic+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Magic Damage"), [Fire](/Fire+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Fire Damage"), [Lightning](/Lightning+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Lightning Damage"), [Dark](/Dark+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Dark Damage").  
Auxiliary Effects: [Bleed](/Bleed "Dark Souls 2 Bleed"), [Poison](/Poison "Dark Souls 2 Poison"), [Petrification](/Petrification "Dark Souls 2 Petrification"), and [Curse](/Curse "Dark Souls 2 Curse").  
Stability: How well the player keeps stance after being hit.  
Durability: The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!".  
Weight: How much the item weighs when equipped.
