**Crest of the Rat** is a [Ring](/Rings "Dark Souls 2 Rings") in [Dark Souls 2 (DKS2)](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). **Crest of the Rat** , embrace this covenant and don this ring to entice intruders of the Rat King's realm into your domain. Receiving this ring is a chance to showcase your loyalty to a supremely noble entity. Players can equip up to 4 Rings, but equipping two of the same item is not possible. Some rings have upgraded versions, having a maximum of +3. Some can only be found in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus"). Rings can be repaired at a blacksmith, but not upgraded. Upgraded versions must be acquired throughout the game from corpses, chests, bosses, etc.

> Ring of the Rat King covenant. Join this covenant and wear this ring to lure trespassers of the Rat King's territory into your world. If you receive this ring, rejoice in the opportunity to demonstrate your allegiance to an eminently noble being."

### Crest of the Rat Effects

**Durability** : 100

**Weight** :

  * 0.2

**Effect:**

  * Allows Rat King matchmaking while being a Rat Servant and in a Rat King area
  * Increases poison attack: 
    * Adds 25 to poison attack if a weapon is infused with poison using a poison stone.
    * Adds 50 to poison attack if weapon has poison damage innately.
    * Adds 60 to poison attack if weapon has poison damage innately **and** is infused with poison using a poison stone.
    * (Infused weapons infos are not true, deep tests needed: Blue Flame gets only +15, Drakeblood Greatsword gets +28, Black Knight Greatsword gets +25)

### Crest of the Rat Locations in Dark Souls 2

  * Acquired by joining the [Rat King Covenant](/Rat+King+Covenant "Dark Souls 2 Rat King Covenant").

### DS2 Crest of the Rat Tips and Notes

  * Stacks with [Sanctum Soldier Gauntlets](/Sanctum+Soldier+Gauntlets "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Soldier Gauntlets").
  * Does not affect [Pyromancies](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies") or [Hexes](/Hexes "Dark Souls 2 Hexes")

### Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Time Limits

  * An invader summoned by the Crest of the Rat has 10 minutes to defeat the host.
  * See [Online](/Online "Dark Souls 2 Online") for a complete list of multiplayer timers.
