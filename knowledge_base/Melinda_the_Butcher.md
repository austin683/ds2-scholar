**Melinda the Butcher** is an [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Melinda the Butcher Information in Dark Souls 2

  * Wears the [Prisoner's Set](/Prisoner%27s+Set "Dark Souls 2 Prisoner%27s Set"), and carries a [Greataxe](/Greataxe "Dark Souls 2 Greataxe") +3.
  * This Phantom will return to her world if the player jumps to the platform with the two hollows before the Forgotten door.

### Melinda the Butcher Encounters in DKS2

[The Gutter](/The+Gutter "Dark Souls 2 The Gutter"). She invades as soon as you touch the wall ledge with the [Ring of Soul Protection](/Ring+of+Soul+Protection "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Soul Protection") chest. Her spawn location is a good distance away, on the wooden platform above the tar pools.
