**The Undead Lockaway Key** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **The Undead Lockaway Key** , a crucial key to the prison in Huntsman's Copse. During the era of the Old Iron King, this forest was a hunting ground for the Undead, and the cells where they were confined still remain. The key unlocks the prison in Huntsman's Copse, situated shortly after crossing the lowered bridge, allowing access to the area where Creighton the Wanderer is imprisoned. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> "Key to the prison in Huntsman's Copse. The Undead hunts during the reign of the Old Iron King took place in this forest, and the cells in which the Undead were held still stand there to this day. However, the march of time has eroded any difference between the captors and the captives.

### Undead Lockaway Key Usage in DKS2

  * Unlocks the prison in [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman's+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman's Copse"), located just after you cross the bridge you lowered, and freed [Creighton the Wanderer](/Creighton+the+Wanderer "Dark Souls 2 Creighton the Wanderer").

### Undead Lockaway Key Location in DS2

#### Original Game

  * On a corpse in a small hut as you travel on the left path along the cliffs past the pile of burning bodies.

#### Scholar of the First Sin

  * Cross the bridge in front of the prison, and follow the path until you find a path going up to the left. At the end of that path, [Merciless Roenna](/Merciless+Roenna "Dark Souls 2 Merciless Roenna") will invade, kill the invader, and pick up the key that is on the nearby body.
