# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Vogel+the+Sinner

###  **Location:**

  * [Belfry Luna](/Belfry+Luna "Belfry Luna")
  * Invades at the bottom of the ladder near the Belfy Luna Bonfire.

###  **Notes:**

  * Wields a [Winged Spear](/Winged+Spear "Winged Spear") and a medium shield.
  * He can drop a Winged Spear +5
  * it's highly recommanded to kill the hounds before he can spawn, as they can make the fight pretty difficult to handle.
