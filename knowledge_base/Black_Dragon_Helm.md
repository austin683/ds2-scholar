_""Eerily shaped helmet spoken of in legend, crafted with the scales of the Black Dragon._

_The Black Dragon was long considered a mere legend, but this proves its existence.""_

### Location

  * Achieve Rank 1 in the [Dragon Remnants](/Dragon+Remnants) covenant to obtain the [Dragon Head Stone](/Dragon+Head+Stone) for the helmet.

### Notes

  * Cannot be upgraded.
  * Part of the [Black Dragon Set](/Black+Dragon+Set). 
    * This set provides fair physical defense and excellent elemental defense at an incredibly low weight.
    * This set gives 0 poise when worn. The only poise you get is from your natural ADP and END.
    * Cannot be removed unless the wearer dies. [Ring of Life Protection](/Ring+of+Life+Protection) is recommended in this case if you want to purposefully unequip the set.

  * [Dragon Head Stone](/Dragon+Head+Stone) gives the player fire breath.
