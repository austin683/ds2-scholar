#  The Place Unbeknownst

>> [Back to Bonfires](/Bonfires)>>

* * *

##  Bonfire Location:

[Cardinal Tower](/Cardinal+Tower) -> Go down the ladder in the Ground and turn right, go over the bridge and open the door with the [Soldiers Key](/Soldiers+Key). Turn left and put on the [King's Ring](/King%27s+Ring). The Bonfire is behind that gate.

##  Bosses:

  * [Giant Lord](/Giant+Lord). You also need the [Ashen Mist Heart](/Ashen+Mist+Heart) to access this memory.

##  Items

  * [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite) x2

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here respawns the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite) x2 below the entrance to [Memory of Jeigh](/Memory+of+Jeigh) (removed in SOTFS edition)
  * [Memory of Jeigh](/Memory+of+Jeigh) (at the bonfire) 
    * Boss: [Giant Lord](/Giant+Lord)
    * Item: [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic), [Fire Seed](/Fire+Seed), [Divine Blessing](/Divine+Blessing)
    * [Massive Giant](/Giant+Warrior) x2
  * [Memory of Orro](/Memory+of+Orro) (behind the [Pursuer](/Pursuer))
    * Item: [Fading Soul](/Fading+Soul) by the [Pharros' Contraption](/Pharros%27+Contraption)
    * Item: [Soul of a Hero](/Soul+of+a+Hero) x3 after using the [Pharros' Contraption](/Pharros%27+Contraption)
    * Item: [Soul of a Brave Warrior](/Soul+of+a+Brave+Warrior) on the bridge after dropping down from the top
    * Items: [Soul of a Proud Knight](/Soul+of+a+Proud+Knight), [Soul of a Great Hero](/Soul+of+a+Great+Hero), [Soul Vessel](/Soul+Vessel) in the courtyard
    * Item: [Wilted Dusk Herb](/Wilted+Dusk+Herb) x3 on a roof in the courtyard
    * [Giant Warriors](/Giant+Warrior)
    * [Royal Soldiers](/Royal+Soldier)
  * [Memory of Vammar](/Memory+of+Vammar)
    * Items: [Smooth & Silky Stone](/Smooth+%26+Silky+Stone) x5, [Petrified Something](/Petrified+Something) in the chest near the entrance
    * Item: [Fire Seed](/Fire+Seed) in the corner where a giant takes a wall down
    * Item: [Giant Warrior Club](/Giant+Warrior+Club) next to the [Massive Giant](/Giant+Warrior) in the courtyard
    * Items: [Soul of a Great Hero](/Soul+of+a+Great+Hero), [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic) on the roof with two [Sorcerer Giants (fire)](/Giant+Warrior)
    * Item: [Crimson Water](/Crimson+Water) x3 below the Plateau at the end of the memory
    * Items: [Old Radiant Lifegem](/Old+Radiant+Lifegem), [Soul of a Great Hero](/Soul+of+a+Great+Hero), [Torch](/Torch) on the Plateau at the end of the memory
    * [Giant Warriors](/Giant+Warrior)
    * [Royal Soldiers](/Royal+Soldiers)
  * [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan) (spawns near the bonfire if he's released)

##  Special Notes:

  * Since one can obtain infinite supplies of Bonfire Ascetic at Memory of Jeigh, this makes this bonfire a popular place for farming souls, [Fire Seed](/Fire+Seed), [Divine Blessing](/Divine+Blessing) [Giant Lord Soul](/Giant+Lord+Soul) and [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite)(removed in SOTFS edition).
  * Also, a good place for farming [Awestone](/Awestone) from [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan) in one risked himself to release Navlaan.
  * The bonfire area is safe from invasion unless Navlaan is released.
