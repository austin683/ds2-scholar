_"The stocky Gyrm are kind-natured, but humans deemed them impure, and drove them underground."  
_

### Location

  * Dropped by [Gyrm Worker](/Gyrm+Worker "Dark Souls 2 Gyrm Worker") in [Shaded Woods](/Shaded+Woods "Dark Souls 2 Shaded Woods") (one, next to Old Akelarre bonfire) and in [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros "Dark Souls 2 Doors of Pharros").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Gyrm Set](/Gyrm+Set "Dark Souls 2 Gyrm Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores").
