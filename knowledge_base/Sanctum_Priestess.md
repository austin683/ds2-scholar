**Sanctum Priestess** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. The **Sanctum Priestess** , resembling plants, are Hexers known for casting hexes that players can use. They primarily rely on ranged attacks and lack melee capabilities. Upon defeat, they have a chance to emit poisonous fog. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Priestess Information

The priestesses sang to preserve the dragon's deep slumber, but one wonders if a single phrase of their song ever reached the dragon's ears. They're Hexers with the appearance of plants, tends to use hexes which are usable by the players, and they lacked melee attacks. Once defeated, they'll have a chance to emit poisonous fog.   
  
Contrary to common belief, while the [Sanctum Priestess Tiara](/Sanctum+Priestess+Tiara "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Priestess Tiara") they wear is supposed to be resistant to [Profound Still](/Profound+Still "Dark Souls 2 Profound Still") if worn by players, here, they still can be silenced by the spell, once silenced, they can not attack. Besides, they can be allured by [Yearn](/Yearn "Dark Souls 2 Yearn") sorcery and [Alluring Skull](/Alluring+Skull "Dark Souls 2 Alluring Skull").
