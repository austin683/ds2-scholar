**Leather Boots** is a **Legging** and a part of [Leather Set](/Leather+Set "Dark Souls 2 Leather Set") in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Leather Boots Description

"Boots made from soft leather. A commonplace piece of equipment.  
Few embellishments."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * At the Soldiers Rest bonfire in the Forest of The Fallen Giants it is out and to the left on top of a wooden structure in a chest.
  * (You will need the key from The Last Giant to access this area)

### Notes

  * Upgraded with [Titanite Shard](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard"), [Large Titanite Shard](/Large+Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Large Titanite Shard"), [Titanite Chunk](/Titanite+Chunk "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Chunk"), [Titanite Slab](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab")
