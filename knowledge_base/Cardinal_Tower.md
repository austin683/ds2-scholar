##  Bonfire Location:

[The Crestfallen's Retreat](/The+Crestfallen%27s+Retreat) -> Leave the bonfire and cross the river, go over the tree bridge and up the ramp to your right and up the ladder you'll find in that little cave. To your left you will see a fog gate, go through it. Follow the hallway ahead. When you are out in the open again you will see a ladder just after you left the hallway, take that ladder and look to your right, there is a gate that you can open. Just after that gate is the bonfire.   
  

##  Bosses:

  * [Pursuer](/Pursuer)
  * [The Last Giant](/The+Last+Giant)
