**Royal Soldier Set** is an Attire or [Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor") in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

> Royal Drangleic soldier helm/armor/gauntlets/leggings.  
>   
> Equipment crafted by the royal blacksmith. Finely polished and ready for battle.

###  Set Location

  * Sold by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") (8,4k) in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula").
  * Becomes avaliable after spending 1000+ souls.

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite").
  * Similarly looking, but worn out [Hollow Soldier set](/Hollow+Soldier+Set "Dark Souls 2 Hollow Soldier Set") is lighter and ~25% worse in all stats.
  * Similar to the Paladin Set from Dark Souls 1
