**Cale's Helm** is a [Helm](/Helms "Dark Souls 2 Helms") in Dark Souls 2.

_"Helm_  _of a light mirrah foot soldier. belonged to Cale the Cartographer._

_Apparently Cale procured this inexpensively second-hand, but it's surprisingly well-made.__"_

### Location

  * Talk to [Cale the Cartographer](/Cale+the+Cartographer "Dark Souls 2 Cale the Cartographer") in abandoned house after lighting all fires on the map. These will light up after reaching the following story milestones: 
    * Kill [The Last Giant](/The+Last+Giant "Dark Souls 2 The Last Giant")
    * Kill [The Lost Sinner](/Lost+Sinner "Dark Souls 2 Lost Sinner")
    * Kill [Old Iron King](/Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Old Iron King")
    * Kill [The Rotten](/The+Rotten "Dark Souls 2 The Rotten")
    * Kill [The Duke's Dear Freja](/The+Duke's+Dear+Freja "Dark Souls 2 The Duke's Dear Freja")
    * Obtain [King's Ring](/King's+Ring "Dark Souls 2 King's Ring")
    * Obtain [Ashen Mist Heart](/Ashen+Mist+Heart "Dark Souls 2 Ashen Mist Heart")
    * Kill [Throne Watcher & Defender](/Throne+Watcher+and+Throne+Defender "Dark Souls 2 Throne Watcher and Throne Defender")

### Cale's Helm Notes

  * Part of [Cale's Set](/Cale's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Cale's Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab").
  * The helmet is part of [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan "Dark Souls 2 Royal Sorcerer Navlaan")'s storyline.
  * The helmet gives +2 [Dexterity](/Dexterity "Dark Souls 2 Dexterity") and +1 [Endurance](/Endurance "Dark Souls 2 Endurance").
