**Soul of the Last Giant** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of the Last Giant** , the soul of the surviving giant bound below the Forest of Giants. Despite preparing for his final rest, his soul remained magnificent, a testament to his former strength. Consuming it grants 6,000 Souls or trade it with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") and 1,500 souls for the [Giant Stone Axe](/Giant+Stone+Axe "Dark Souls 2 Giant Stone Axe"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.**

### Soul of the Last Giant Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Consuming it gives you 6,000 Souls
  * Can be traded with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") and 1,500 souls for [Giant Stone Axe.  
](/Giant+Stone+Axe "Dark Souls 2 Giant Stone Axe")

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of the Last Giant Location

  * Obtained by defeating [The Last Giant](/The+Last+Giant "Dark Souls 2 The Last Giant") at the [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants")
