#  Titanite

_[Items](/Items "Dark Souls 2 Items") >> [Ores](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores") >>_   
  
  
_Titanite was discovered in an ancient layer of earth, and is said to be a gift of the gods._   
An ore-type used to reinforced most weapons. Such equipment can be reinforced to +10.

##  Types

  * [Titanite Shard](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard") (Reinforce to +3)
  * [Large Titanite Shard](/Large+Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Large Titanite Shard") (Reinforce from +4 to +6)
  * [Titanite Chunk](/Titanite+Chunk "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Chunk") (Reinforce from +7 to +9)
  * [Titanite Slab](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab") (Final reinforcement, up to +10)
