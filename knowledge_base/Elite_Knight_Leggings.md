**Elite Knight Leggings** is a **legging** and a part of [Elite Knight Set](/Elite+Knight+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Elite Knight Leggings Description

_"Leggings made of bradden steel. Provides sturdy defense, making it an old-time favorite of elite knights.  
"Bradden steel is used widely in Drangleic. This fine alloy is made from ores mined in the southern kingdom."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Bought from [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer) merchant in [Majula](/Majula). One must spend more than 1,000 souls at his shop, leave, rest at a bonfire, and return.
  * The Elite Knight Set +5 can drop from the red phantoms that spawn in ng+ (or ascetic) in front of the last king's gate before throne watcher/defender and Nashandra. Unsure if guarunteed drop or not.
