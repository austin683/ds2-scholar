**Immolation** is a [Pyromancy](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Immolation** , a pyromancy enveloping the caster in a fiery aura, enabling the incineration of nearby foes. Undoubtedly perilous, this spell inflicts burns upon the caster. For those deeply yearning, the flames may seem a trivial price to pay. [Pyromancies](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies") are offensive fire spells that do not demand [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith") or [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") prerequisites and can be executed with a [Pyromancy Flame](/Pyromancy+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancy Flame") or [Dark Pyromancy Flame](/Dark+Pyromancy+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Dark Pyromancy Flame"). The fire damage scales with [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") and [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith"), yet there are no specific stat requirements for casting pyromancies. However, opponents resistant to fire damage remain unaffected by [Pyromancy Spells](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies").

> "A pyromancy that creates a coating of flame, allowing the caster to incinerate nearby foes. Needless to say, this is a perilous spell that burns the caster while active.
> 
> If a person is truly desirous of something, perhaps being set on fire does not seem so bad."

### Dark Souls 2 Immolation Acquired From

  * Found in [Belfry Sol](/Belfry+Sol "Dark Souls 2 Belfry Sol"), in a treasure chest past a fog gate.

### Immolation Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Damage to self can not be reduced.
  * Damage to the enemy is independent of flame reinforcement.  
Can not be stacked with Flash Sweat.
  * This spell makes it easy to find invisible enemies in the Shaded Ruins  
It is possible to roll through the self-damage.
  * Casting Replenishment while Immolation is active will cancel Immolation. The reverse is also true; casting Immolation will cancel Replenishment.
  * Casting Immolation and running through the tar pits in the gulch will catch them on fire.
