**Soul of Zallen, the King's Pet** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Soul of Zallen, the King's Pet** , encapsulating the essence of Zallen, the beast in service to the Ivory King. Assigned a specific duty among the seven beasts under the Ivory King's command, Zallen's role was the merciful ending of the lives of exiles. This soul can be traded along with 1200 souls for the [Loyce Shield](/Loyce+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Shield") from [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") or consumed for 16,000 souls. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of Zallen, who served the Ivory King. Each of the Ivory King's seven beasts was conferred a specific duty.   
> Zallen's was the mercy killing of exiles.   
> The wondrous soul of this beast can be used to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Soul of Zallen, the King's Pet Usage in DKS2

  * Trade it along with 1200 souls for the [Loyce Shield](/Loyce+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Shield") from [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex")
  * Consume it for 16,000 souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Soul of Zallen, the King's Pet Location

  * Defeat [Zallen, the King's Pet](/Lud+and+Zallen%2C+the+King%27s+Pets "Dark Souls 2 Lud and Zallen%2C the King%27s Pets") at [Frigid Outskirts](/Frigid+Outskirts "Dark Souls 2 Frigid Outskirts")
