_"Hood worn by a chaos sorcerer._

_He calls himself Navlaan, now."  
_

### Location

  * Given to after completing royal sorcerer Navlaan's quest when you talk to him as a Hollow.
  * Can be purchased from Merchant Hag Melentia after you kill Navlaan.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Chaos Set](/Chaos+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite).
  * The hood improves casting speed and increases [Intelligence](/Intelligence) by +3.
  * Requires 16 [Intelligence](/Intelligence) and [Faith](/Faith) to equip.
