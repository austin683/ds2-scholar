**Smelter Wedge** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Smelter Wedge** , an iron wedge forged in this land, is used to destroy the ashen idol. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> An iron wedge forged in this land. Destroys the ashen idol.  
> The ashen idol is of great consequence to Nadalia, who, having renounced her flesh, entrusts her very being to it.

### Smelter Wedge Usage in DKS2

  * Used to destroy [Ashen Idols](/Ashen+Idol "Dark Souls 2 Ashen Idol").

### Dark Souls 2 Smelter Wedge Location

There are 11 in total

  * 6 At the start, right before the first giant chain
  * 1 After [Sir Alonne](/Sir+Alonne "Dark Souls 2 Sir Alonne")
  * 4 After activating the elevators at the third bonfire(Foyer) go up the elevator on the left, take the recessed elevator up right after and follow the path to the right, at the end there is a chest with 4 Smelter Wedges.
