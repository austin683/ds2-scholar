**Axes** in **[Dark Souls II](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")** are a type of [Weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") that mainly focuses with slash attacks with the use of a single hand. It is best utilized for hard hitting attacks against sidestepping opponents or when the player is facing a group of enemies. Most of its attacks have a wide horizontal arc, they tend to be shorter than swords so it lacks reach in longer ranges, it is best used when the player is up close to an opponent. Axes are similar to [Straight Swords](/Straight+Swords "Dark Souls 2 Straight Swords"), the main difference is that Axes do damage based on the stat of [Strength](/Strength "Dark Souls 2 Strength"), while Straight Swords depend on both Strength and [Dexterity](/Dexterity "Dark Souls 2 Dexterity"). This page covers a list of all Axes in Dark Souls 2.
