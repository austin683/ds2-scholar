### Dragon Acolyte Gloves Description

_"Gloves worn by acolytes of Aldia._  
_Several of the greatest minds converged in Aldia to weave strange new rituals, but rumors suggest that during the course of their work their thoughts were not their own."_

### Where to Find \ Location 

  * Can be dropped from the [Dragon Acolyte](/Dragon+Acolyte "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Acolyte") just before the [Guardian dragon](/Guardian+dragon "Dark Souls 2 Guardian dragon").
