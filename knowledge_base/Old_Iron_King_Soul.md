**Old Iron King Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Old Iron King Soul** , the soul of the Old Iron King who fell to the flames that engulfed his castle. Consuming this soul rewards you with 25,000 souls. Alternatively, you can trade it with Weaponsmith Ornifex to acquire the Iron King Hammer. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Old Iron King, who succumbed to the flames that swallowed his castle. The Iron King's flesh was charred, and his soul possessed by the things that lurk below. Use the special soul of the Old King to acquire numerous souls, or to create something of great worth.

### Old Iron King Soul Usage in DKS2

  * Consuming gives 25 000 souls.
  * Trade with [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") for [ Iron King Hammer](/Iron+King+Hammer#fex "Dark Souls 2 Iron King Hammer#fex").

### Dark Souls 2 Old Iron King Soul Location

  * Received upon defeating the [Old Iron King](/Old+Iron+King "Dark Souls 2 Old Iron King") in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep").
