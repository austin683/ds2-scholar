##  Bonfire Location:

Located in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep). On the area with the 6 fire statues, just before the boss, there is a ladder on the left side. After another one, you'll find the bonfire.   
  

##  Bosses:

  * [Old Iron King](/Old+Iron+King)

  

##  Bonfire Ascetic Use:

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here respawns the following items/enemies/bosses:

  * Boss: [Old Iron King](/Old+Iron+King)
  * Lightning Shortbow +1 between the fire-breathing gargoyles
  * Three Turtleknights in the vicinity
  * Enemy

  

####  Burning a bonfire ascetic here spawns the following new items/enemies:

  * Item
  * Item
  * Red Phantom on the platform below the bonfire, wielding [Drakekeeper's Great Hammer](/Drakekeeper%27s+Great+Hammer) and [Ironclad's helmet and body armor](/Ironclad+Set) (does not respawn after being killed)
  * Enemy
