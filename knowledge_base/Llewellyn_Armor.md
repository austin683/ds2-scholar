**Llewellyn Armor** is a [Chest Piece](/Chest+Pieces "Dark Souls 2 Chest Pieces") in Dark Souls 2.

_"Armor reinforced with rare geisteel. Belonged to Chancellor Wellager."_

_"Quality equipment that is both light and strong. Crafted by the castle's resident master smith Llewellyn, and supplied only to a selected few._

_His work easily identified by its lack of ostentation, Llewellyn focused solely on an economy of simplicity and strength."_

### Location

  * The full set drops from the stone statues outside of the [Looking-Glass Knight](/Looking+Glass+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Knight") boss fight in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle").
  * As of SotFS this set is no longer dropped by the statues. Instead, it is given to you by [Chancellor Wellegar](/Chancellor+Wellager "Dark Souls 2 Chancellor Wellager") after killing the [Giant Lord](/Giant+Lord "Dark Souls 2 Giant Lord").

### Llewellyn Armor Notes

  * Part of the [Llewellyn Set](/Llewellyn+Set "Dark Souls 2 Llewellyn Set").  

    * [Chancellor Wellager](/Chancellor+Wellager "Dark Souls 2 Chancellor Wellager") wears this set.
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
