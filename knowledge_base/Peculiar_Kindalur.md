**Peculiar Kindalur** is an [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. To respawn him, you can use a [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") on the [Rhoy's Resting Place](/Rhoy's+Resting+Place "Dark Souls 2 Rhoy's Resting Place") bonfire in the [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana"). [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Peculiar Kindalur Information in DS2

  * Using a [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") on the **Rhoy's Resting Place** bonfire in the [Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana") will respawn him.

### Dark Souls 2 Peculiar Kindalur Encounters

[Shrine of Amana](/Shrine+of+Amana "Dark Souls 2 Shrine of Amana"). After **Rhoy's Resting Place** bonfire, pass through the passage blocked by the Giant Acid Horn Beetle and enter the water. Reach the centre and she will spawn. In _Dark Souls II: Scholar of the First Sin_ , she will spawn in the pathway lined with pillars.
