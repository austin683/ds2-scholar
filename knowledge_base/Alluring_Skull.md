**Alluring Skull** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Alluring Skull** , a mysterious skull with an unknown owner. Breaking it releases traces of souls, drawing in nearby foes. While not effective on all enemies, it can prove useful in unexpected situations. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A skull of unknown ownership.  
> Smashing it releases traces of souls, which can attract nearby foes.  
> Does not work on all enemies, but can prove useful in unexpected ways.

### Alluring Skull Usage in DKS2

  * Can be thrown to attract certain enemies to that position.
  * The definition of "certain enemies" is mindless enemies such as hollows, [Lion Clan Warriors](/Lion+Clan+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Lion Clan Warrior"), and [Skeletons](/Skeleton "Dark Souls 2 Skeleton").
  * Invaders can use this item to aggro [Grave Warden Agdayne](/Grave+Warden+Agdayne "Dark Souls 2 Grave Warden Agdayne") in the host's world by luring the Torch Hollow to his place.
  * It lasts for 5 seconds.
  * Affects fewer enemies than Yearn (i.e Yearn will aggro [Rampart Soldiers](/Rampart+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Rampart Soldier"), [lancers](/Rampart+Golem "Dark Souls 2 Rampart Golem"), and [Retainers](/Retainer+Rogue "Dark Souls 2 Retainer Rogue"), but Alluring Skull will not)
  * See [Yearn ](/Yearn "Dark Souls 2 Yearn") for further explanation of its mechanics.

### Dark Souls 2 Alluring Skull Location

  * Small Mummys at [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille")
  * Skeletons near Undead Lockaway bonfire
  * [Sweet Shalquoir](/Sweet+Shalquoir "Dark Souls 2 Sweet Shalquoir") the Cat sells them in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula")
  * 3 can be found in the [King's Passage](/King%27s+Passage "Dark Souls 2 King%27s Passage") on the side of one of the walls upon entering (look to the right of the statue of a Knight and his Sword)
