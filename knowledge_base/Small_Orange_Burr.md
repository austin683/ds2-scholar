**Small Orange Burr** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Small Orange Burr** , briefly enhances fire defense. Historically utilized in ancient rituals. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A small orange burr. Temporarily boosts fire defense. These burrs bloom magnificently on a shrub every few years, their color and effect determined  
> by soil quality and recent weather. Known to have been used in rituals long ago

### Dark Souls 2 Small Orange Burr Usage

Temporarily boosts fire defense by 150 for 90 seconds when consumed.

### Small Orange Burr Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Sold by [Rosabeth of Melfia](/Rosabeth+of+Melfia "Dark Souls 2 Rosabeth of Melfia") for 1,200 souls after unpetrifying her.
  * Possible drop from enemies in [Huntsman's Copse](/Huntsman%27s+Copse "Dark Souls 2 Huntsman%27s Copse").
  * Possible drop from ashen warriors in Brume Tower and iron passage
  * Boosts Fire Defense by 150 points for 90 seconds

### DKS2 Small Orange Burr Notes and Tips

  * Counts as a [Body Buff](/Buffs+&+Debuffs "Dark Souls 2 Buffs & Debuffs"), and so does not stack with [Flash Sweat](/Flash+Sweat "Dark Souls 2 Flash Sweat").
