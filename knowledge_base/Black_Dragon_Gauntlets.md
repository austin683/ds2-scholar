_""Eerily shaped helmet/armor/gauntlets/leggings spoken of in legend, crafted with the scales of the Black Dragon.  
  
  
The Black Dragon was long considered a mere legend, but this proves its existence.""_   

###  Set Location

  * Achieve Rank 1 in the [Dragon Remnants](/Dragon+Remnants "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Remnants") covenant to obtain the [Dragon Head Stone](/Dragon+Head+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Head Stone") for the helmet.
  * Achieve Rank 2 in the Dragon Remnants covenant to obtain the [Dragon Torso Stone](/Dragon+Torso+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Torso Stone") for the rest of the set.

###  Notes

  * Cannot be upgraded.
  * This set provides fair physical defense and excellent elemental defense at an incredibly low weight
  * Cannot be removed unless the wearer dies. [Ring of Life Protection](/Ring+of+Life+Protection "Dark Souls 2 Ring of Life Protection") is recommended in this case if you want to purposefully unequip the set.
  * This set gives 0 poise when worn. The only poise you get is from your natural ADP and END

  
  
**Effects:**

  * [Dragon Head Stone](/Dragon+Head+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Head Stone") \- Fire Breath
  * [Dragon Torso Stone](/Dragon+Torso+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Torso Stone") \- Damage Buff
