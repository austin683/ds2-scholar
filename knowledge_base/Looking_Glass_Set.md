**Looking Glass Set** is an Attire or Armor in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

  
_"Mask/Armor/Gauntlets/Leggings worn by the Looking Glass Knight. Made of metal, but have high lightning defense._   
_Those who wish to serve the king loyal warriors must take the King's Passage and face the Looking Glass Knight._   
_Those who fail the test are sacrificed by the merciless specular monstrosity."_

###  Set Location

  * Sold by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") (42k) once [Looking Glass Knight](/Looking+Glass+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Knight") is defeated (spend 15k souls at Maughlin's shop to unlock boss armor sets).

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * Upgraded stats are only 40% higher, not the usual 50%.
  * Helm grants +1 to Faith.
  * Gauntlets offer the highest lightning defense (equal to that of [Black Witch](/Black+Witch+Set "Dark Souls 2 Black Witch Set") gloves), as well as second highest bleed resistance and poise (almost equal to that of [Smelter Demon](/Smelter+Demon+Set "Dark Souls 2 Smelter Demon Set") gauntlets).
  * Leggings offer second highest poise (almost equal to that of [Mastodon](/Mastodon+Set "Dark Souls 2 Mastodon Set") and [Smelter Demon](/Smelter+Demon+Set "Dark Souls 2 Smelter Demon Set") leggings).
