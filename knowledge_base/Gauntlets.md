### What are Gauntlets in Dark Souls 2

In Dark Souls 2, Gauntlets refer to a type of armor worn on the player character's hands and arms. These are part of the overall armor set that players can equip to enhance their defense against enemy attacks. DS2 Gauntlets, along with other armor pieces such as [Helmets](/Helms "Dark Souls 2 Helms"), [Chest Pieces](/Chest+Pieces "Dark Souls 2 Chest Pieces"), and [Leggings](/Leggings "Dark Souls 2 Leggings"), contribute to the overall protective capabilities of the player character.

In Dark Souls II, players can find various types of gauntlets throughout the game world. These **Gauntlets** may have different stats, providing varying levels of physical defense, elemental resistance, and other attributes. Players can choose their preferred combination of armor pieces based on their playstyle, the type of enemies they are facing, and the specific challenges they encounter in the game.

It's important to note that the effectiveness of armor in Dark Souls 2 is not only determined by its defensive stats. Factors such as weight and poise also play a role in how armor affects your character's performance. Players often need to find a balance between defense, mobility, and other attributes when selecting their armor sets in the game.
