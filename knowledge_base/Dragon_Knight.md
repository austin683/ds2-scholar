**Dragon Knight** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Dragon Knight** , initially found in the **Dragon Shrine**. These warriors don distinctive dragon-esque armor, displaying intense aggression and the ability to inflict significant damage. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Dragon Knight Information

Warriors in Dragon-esque armour, they are very aggressive and can deal huge amounts of damage if the player isn't careful.  
As of SotFS, they do not attack unless the player has a co-op phantom with them or runs from a fight with a [Drakekeeper](/Drakekeeper "Dark Souls 2 Drakekeeper"). However, if they have already encountered the Ancient Dragon, the player is able to run past the [Drakekeepers](/Drakekeeper "Dark Souls 2 Drakekeeper"), as well as have a co-op phantom alongside them.

### Dragon Knight Location in Dark Souls 2

  * [Dragon Shrine](/Dragon+Shrine "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Shrine")

  * [Black Dragon Warpick](/Black+Dragon+Warpick "Dark Souls 2 Black Dragon Warpick") (only dropped by those that wield it)
  * [Black Dragon Sword](/Black+Dragon+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Black Dragon Sword") (only dropped by those that wield it)
  * [Black Dragon Greataxe](/Black+Dragon+Greataxe "Dark Souls 2 Black Dragon Greataxe") (only dropped by those that wield it)
  * [Black Dragon Shield](/Black+Dragon+Shield "Dark Souls 2 Black Dragon Shield") (only dropped by those that wield it)

### Dragon Knight Strategy Tips in DKS2

  * **They are fairly easy to backstab, and you should easily be able to chain together multiple backstabs**
