**Cragslipper (Razorback Nightcrawler)** is an [enemy](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Razorback Nightcrawler** , a result of undisturbed evolution, are colossal worm monsters with many eyes that emerge from hole-lairs in the walls of Black Gulch. Limited by their lairs, they cannot chase players but pose a threat with a ranged attack range. While they have high health, they can be safely targeted with ranged attacks. [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") have their own behavior and unique attacks, killing enemies provide various drops that reward players with items and souls.

### Dark Souls 2 Cragslipper Information

  

_This giant millipede is found only in Black Gulch. In contrast to the artificial creatures roaming The Gutter, the Razorback Nightcrawler is a product of an undisturbed evolution. They are more capable of crushing a careless passerby with their great weight and tremendous power_  
Enormous, many-eyed worm monsters that crawl from their hole-lairs in the walls of Black Gulch. Because they cannot completely leave their lairs, they are unable to chase the player and have a limited attack range. They can safely be hit with ranged attacks, but have high health.

  

###  Strategy tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Rush or bait their attacks and dodge/block and then dispatch. Ranged classes take out from range.
