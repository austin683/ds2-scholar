**Rubbish** is a special item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Rubbish** , a worthless item with no apparent use. One person's trash may be another's treasure, as value is subjective. [Gavlan](/Gavlan "Dark Souls 2 Gavlan") is willing to purchase it for 10 souls, or it can be dropped from the inventory to serve as a makeshift landmark. [Tools](/Tools "Dark Souls 2 Tools") are unique, reusable items which assist the player during various facets of gameplay ranging from basic communication to assisting in boss encounters.

> A piece of rubbish that is good for nothing. One man's rubbish is another man's riches, for value, is in the eye of the beholder.  
> But utility is another matter, and this has none."

### Rubbish Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Can be sold to [Gavlan](/Gavlan "Dark Souls 2 Gavlan") for 10 souls.
  * Or drop this item from the inventory as a makeshift landmark.

### Dark Souls 2 Rubbish Location

  * Destroying a wooden chest before picking up the treasure.
  * Giving a [Prism Stone](/Prism+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Prism Stone") to [Dyna and Tillo](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo").

### Rubbish Notes and Tips in DKS2

  * Not able to trade Rubbish with [Dyna & Tillo](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo") (it is a possible reward, not what you trade in).
  * Be careful fighting an enemy around a wooden chest, as either you or the enemy might destroy the chest.
