**Penal Handcuffs** is a **gauntlet** and a part of [Penal Set](/Penal+Set "Dark Souls 2 Penal Set") in Dark Souls 2.

### Penal Handcuffs Description

_"Handcuffs of the Lost Sinner. Increases the power of pyromancy. A device used to restrict use of the hands." Effect: Raises power of pyromancies_ _"_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Buy from [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer") after defeating the [Lost Bastille](/Lost+Bastille "Dark Souls 2 Lost Bastille") boss, [Lost Sinner](/Lost+Sinner "Dark Souls 2 Lost Sinner"). You must first spend 1000 souls at his shop and reload the area.

### Penal Handcuffs Notes

  * Handcuffs increase the strength of [pyromancies](/Pyromancies "Dark Souls 2 Pyromancies") by 7.4% (+/-) (My combustion did 337 dmg before and 362 after) (this is likely to be a round 7.5%, consider unrounded damage example: 336.5 with an added 7.5%, resulting in 361.7)
