# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Caestus

Requirements: STR 10 / A (*B), DEX 7 / A (*C)

Weapon Type: Fist

Attack Type: Strike

Enchantable: Yes

*When infused: 63 physical dmg/63 elemental dmg and B scaling with chosen element. Str scaling changes to B. Dex scaling changes to C. 

**Caestus** is a [weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") in **Dark Souls 2**. **Caestus** is a hand-to-hand combat weapon designed for striking with fists. It scales primarily with Strength and Dexterity. You can purchase this weapon from [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia"). Additionally, a +8 version can be found in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower").

> Weapon used to strike with one's fists.   
>   
> The thick leather covering bears iron studs, and protects the hands while augmenting their striking power.   
>   
>  Attacks with the fists are solely dependent on one's strength. With this weapon, any encounter becomes a brawl. 

###  Acquired From

  * Sold by [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia") for 1000 souls.
  * A +8 version can be found in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower") in a wooden chest just before the Upper Floor bonfire.

###  Hints and Tips:

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite") to +10. Note: Cannot be infused with [Bleed Stone](/Bleed+Stone "Dark Souls 2 Bleed Stone").
  * The parry can deflect bombs.
  * When used in your left hand with the stats to power stance it, You can use the power stance mode with only a single Caestus in your left hand, and nothing in your right. This is useful if you wish to use the [Vanquisher's Seal](/Vanquisher%27s+Seal "Dark Souls 2 Vanquisher%27s Seal"), but have a better stagger ability with minimal damage loss.
  * The Caestus has 293 AR at 60 Str, 45 Dex.
  * The Caestus has 350 AR at 99 Str and Dex.

Parameter Bonus: Strength, Dexterity, Magic, Fire, Lightning and Dark bonuses - The scaling multiplier applied to the [Attack: stat]. Scaling quality is from highest to lowest as follows: S/A/B/C/D/E.  
The higher the player's [Str, Dex, Mag, Fire, Lightning, Dark] stat, the higher the [Attack Bonus: Stat] is (found on the player status screen). The higher the scaling letter, the higher the percent multiplier applied to the [Attack: Stat].  
This resulting bonus damage is added to the base physical damage of the weapon and is shown in the equipment screen in blue numbers as a "+ X".  
Attack Type: Defines what kind of moveset the weapon has.  
Physical Damage falls under three categories: [Thrust](/Thrust+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Thrust Damage") (T), [Slash](/Slash+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Slash Damage") (Sl), [Strike](/Strike+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Strike Damage") (St).  
Elemental Damage Types: [Magic](/Magic+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Magic Damage"), [Fire](/Fire+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Fire Damage"), [Lightning](/Lightning+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Lightning Damage"), [Dark](/Dark+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Dark Damage").  
Auxiliary Effects: [Bleed](/Bleed "Dark Souls 2 Bleed"), [Poison](/Poison "Dark Souls 2 Poison"), [Petrification](/Petrification "Dark Souls 2 Petrification"), and [Curse](/Curse "Dark Souls 2 Curse").  
Stability: How well the player keeps stance after being hit.  
Durability: The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!".  
Weight: How much the item weighs when equipped.
