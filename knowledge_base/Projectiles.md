# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Projectiles

**Projectiles** in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)** are offensive items used to inflict damage to [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses"). These projectiles can be thrown at hostile characters to inflict a small amount of damage. This page covers a list of all the projectiles in DKS2.
