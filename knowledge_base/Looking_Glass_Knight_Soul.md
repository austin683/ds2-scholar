**Looking Glass Knight Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**.**Looking Glass Knight Soul** , the soul of the Looking Glass Knight, who confronts challengers in the lordless castle. In days of yore, the King's Passage was a path trodden by the boldest warriors to prove their mettle. However, it now serves as a barrier hindering those who seek the elusive king. [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

> Soul of the Looking Glass Knight, who challenges visitors to the lordless castle.  
> Long ago, the King's Passage was a route taken by the bravest warriors to prove themselves, but now it only prevents one from pursuing the runaway king.

### Looking Glass Knight Soul Usage in DKS2

  * Trade it along with 3,000 souls for the [Thorned Greatsword](/Thorned+Greatsword#fex "Dark Souls 2 Thorned Greatsword#fex") from [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex")
  * Trade it along with 3,000 souls for the [King's Mirror](/King%27s+Mirror#fex "Dark Souls 2 King%27s Mirror#fex") from [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex")
  * Use it for 18,000 souls

### Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Knight Soul Location

  * Defeat the [Looking Glass Knight](/Looking+Glass+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Knight") in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle "Dark Souls 2 Drangleic Castle")
