**Fake Dead Talisman** is a type of [Item](/Items "Dark Souls 2 Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Fake Dead Talisman** , an item mentioned in the game code but not present in the game. [Unused Items](/Unused+Items "Dark Souls 2 Unused Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)** are Items listed in the game code, but not available in-game.

> A tiny charm embedded with imitative magic. Use to transform into a corpse. Use this talisman to trick invaders. Only provides the most superficial camouflage, but for those who fail to respect the dead, your disguise will be quite effective."

### Fake Dead Talisman Usage in DKS2

  *     * Disguises the user as a corpse

### Dark Souls 2 Fake Dead Talisman Location

  * Unavailable 

### Fake Dead Talisman Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

    * Removed from the final version of the game.
    * This along with it's counterparts were replaced by the [Silver Talisman](/Silver+Talisman "Dark Souls 2 Silver Talisman").
