**Unused Content** are **Cut content** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**. This content then become **[Secrets](/Secrets "Dark Souls 2 Secrets")** since they are not widely known by players of the game. Unused content is content that were usually once encountered in the game during Beta play and Network Tests, which are usually only open to a small pool of players. As developers continue to modify and make changes to better the game, improve balance, bugs and issues through updates some content is removed. Some evidences of this content may exist in the game, but are usually wiped after it has been cut. This content can range from any type of item or experience that was cut in the game. Most cut content consist of some Items and Consumables, Magic types or even some Enemies. This page lists content that exists or existed in the game at one point, but are no longer directly accessible by the player through the normal course of playing the game.
