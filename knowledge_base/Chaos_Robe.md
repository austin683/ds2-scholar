**Chaos Robe** is a [Chest Piece](/Chest+Pieces "Dark Souls 2 Chest Pieces") in Dark Souls 2.

_  
"Robe worn by a chaos sorcerer._  
_He calls himself Navlaan, now."_

### Location

  * Given after completing [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan "Dark Souls 2 Royal Sorcerer Navlaan")'s quest when you talk to him as hollow.
  * Can be purchased from [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia") after you kill [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan "Dark Souls 2 Royal Sorcerer Navlaan").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Chaos Set](/Chaos+Set "Dark Souls 2 Chaos Set"). 
    * Requires 16 [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") and 16 [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith") to equip.
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
  * Offers the highest fire defense in the game when fully upgraded.
