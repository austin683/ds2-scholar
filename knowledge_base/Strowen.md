**Strowen** is an [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") in **Dark Souls 2**. **Strowen** can be found in the town of [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula"). She is a Fire Keeper and tends to the bonfire near the entrance to the town. She is blind and relies on her other senses to perceive the world around her. She offers the player the ability to reinforce their [Estus Flask](/Estus+Flask "Dark Souls 2 Estus Flask"), which increases its healing power. 

### Strowen Information in Dark Souls 2

  * Strowen lives together with her sisters, **Morrel** and **Griant** , and their Housekeeper [Milibeth](/Milibeth "Dark Souls 2 Milibeth") in a house in [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt").  
The three sisters are also known as the Old Firekeepers.
  * She sits at the table between the other two. She is the only of the three Firekeepers to talk with the player.
  * She is also the Firekeeper who will help the player to reallocate [Stats](/Stats "Dark Souls 2 Stats") using a [Soul Vessel](/Soul+Vessel "Dark Souls 2 Soul Vessel").

### Strowen Encounters in Dark Souls 2

You will find her in the hut in Things Betwixt, sitting at the table with her sisters. She will give you [Human Effigy](/Human+Effigy "Dark Souls 2 Human Effigy") x6 if you have [King's Ring](/King's+Ring "Dark Souls 2 King's Ring").

### Strowen Drops in Dark Souls 2

  1. Human Effigy x6
