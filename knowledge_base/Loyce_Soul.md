**Loyce Soul** is a type of [Consumable](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki") [Crown of the Ivory King](/Crown+of+the+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Ivory King") DLC. The Knights of Loyce were taken by Chaos, and lost all sense of purpose and being. The few woeful souls that trudged back home were guided by some faint vestige of self. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are consumable items categorized as remnants of fallen warriors, consuming souls collects a certain number of souls that is added to the player's stock. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are required for players to become stronger in the game by raising their soul level as well as buying and upgrading their equipment.

### Loyce Soul Usage in DKS2

  * Can be consumed, but grant no [souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") and can't be sold.
  * Collecting them yields rewards from [Alsanna, Silent Oracle](/Alsanna,+Silent+Oracle "Dark Souls 2 Alsanna, Silent Oracle") in the [Grand Cathedral](/Grand+Cathedral "Dark Souls 2 Grand Cathedral").
  * Her rewards are: 
    * 5 Loyce Souls = [Loyce Gauntlets](/Loyce+Gauntlets "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Gauntlets"), [Loyce Leggings](/Loyce+Leggings "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Leggings")
    * 15 Loyce Souls = [Loyce Armor](/Loyce+Armor "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Armor")
    * 35 Loyce Souls = [Loyce Helm](/Loyce+Helm "Dark Souls 2 Loyce Helm")
    * 50 Loyce Souls = [Soul of Alsanna, Silent Oracle](/Soul+of+Alsanna "Dark Souls 2 Soul of Alsanna"), and access to [Ivory King Set](/Ivory+King+Set "Dark Souls 2 Ivory King Set") from [Maughlin the Armorer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer").

### Dark Souls 2 Loyce Soul Location

  * Low chance drop from [Charred Loyce Knights](/Charred+Loyce+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Charred Loyce Knight")
  * They can be found down with the Old Chaos, in the [Burnt Ivory King's](/Burnt+Ivory+King "Dark Souls 2 Burnt Ivory King") bossroom.
  * They spawn infinitely and very frequently prior to the Burnt Ivory King's defeat, after his defeat though they spawn less frequently and are susceptible to disappearing without joining the [Company of Champions](/Company+of+Champions "Dark Souls 2 Company of Champions").

### Dark Souls 2 Loyce Soul Notes & Tips

  * The [Soul of Alsanna, Silent Oracle](/Soul+of+Alsanna "Dark Souls 2 Soul of Alsanna") can be alternatively acquired by killing Alsanna, this however will forfeit other rewards, and the weapon gained from the soul isn't particularly desirable.
  * [Item Discovery](/Item+Discovery "Dark Souls 2 Item Discovery") boosting gear such as the [Covetous Gold Serpent Ring](/Covetous+Gold+Serpent+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Covetous Gold Serpent Ring"), [Symbol of Avarice](/Symbol+of+Avarice "Dark Souls 2 Symbol of Avarice"), and [Rusted Coin](/Rusted+Coin "Dark Souls 2 Rusted Coin") can be advantageous with the low drop rates.
  * They can still be dropped from Charred Loyce Knights whilst summoned as a phantom.
