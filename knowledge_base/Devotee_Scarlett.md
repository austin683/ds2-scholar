**Devotee Scarlett** is a [phantom](/Phantoms "Dark Souls 2 Phantoms") in **Dark Souls II** : Scholar of the First Sin.

### Description.

Devotee Scarlett is a white phantom that the player can summon in Dark Souls II: Scholar of the First Sin.

She will focus on melee combat and can soak up a lot of damage.

### Locations.

Heide's Tower of Flame - Found past the 3 Old Knights near the second lever. In the direction of the Old Dragonslayer. (Only appears after you have summoned her at Earthen Peak)  
Earthen Peak - Found in the room behind the first Grave Warden after the Lower Earthern Peak bonfire.  
Undead Crypt - Found behind the first set of pillars, to the right, in the room with the Leydia Witches.

### Equipment.

  * Mace
  * Iron Parma
  * Retainer Robe
  * Lion Mage Cuffs
  * Fume Sorcerer Boots

### Trivia.

  * Uses the "Welcome" Gesture when summoned.
  * Uses the "I wont bite" Gesture when you are approaching the Burnable windmill.
  * Uses the "Point" Gesture (at the windmill).
  * Uses the "Fist bump" Gesture after burning the windmill.
  * Uses the ''Wave" Gesture when Mytha is killed.
