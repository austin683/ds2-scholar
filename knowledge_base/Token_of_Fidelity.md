**Token of Fidelity** is a [Multiplayer Item](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Token of Fidelity** , a token acknowledging the owner's journeys across worlds to aid others. While in foreign realms, it can also be utilized to replenish the host's HP, though this is merely a secondary function of the item. The mere possession of these tokens reflects the bearer's profound commitment. [Multiplayer Items](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitive gameplay like PVP.

> Token recognizing that the owner has traveled worlds to help others. When in other worlds, it can also be used to restore the master of the world's HP, but this is only a secondary effect of the item. Simply carrying these tokens shows the depth of the holder's fidelity.

### Token of Fidelity Usage in DKS2

  * You can use this item at the [Cathedral of Blue](/Cathedral+of+Blue "Dark Souls 2 Cathedral of Blue") to join the [Blue Sentinels](/Blue+Sentinels "Dark Souls 2 Blue Sentinels").
  * Members of the **Blue Sentinels** can use the tokens to spar against each other using the statues found in the basement of the **Cathedral of the Blue**. Unlike a [Token of Spite](/Token+of+Spite "Dark Souls 2 Token of Spite"), the Token of Fidelity is consumed after starting a duel.
  * As in the item description, using this item as a phantom will restore the master's health. This does not work for red phantoms. (Possible to restore as a blue phantom?)
  * One can hold a maximum of 99 at once, and carry 99 in your bottomless box, bringing the total one can have to 198.

### Dark Souls 2 Token of Fidelity Location

  * By helping defeat a Boss as a [White Phantom](/Phantoms "Dark Souls 2 Phantoms") (to get a token of fidelity you need to use the [White sign soapstone](/White+Sign+Soapstone "Dark Souls 2 White Sign Soapstone"), not the [small white sign soapstone](/Small+White+Sign+Soapstone "Dark Souls 2 Small White Sign Soapstone")).
  * Acquired by Members of the Blue Sentinels upon successfully helping a Blue Apostle defeat an invading Red phantom.
  * A Token of Fidelity can be acquired by dropping down from the bridge leading to the Undead Purgatory. If you want to avoid the risky fall, reach the platform above the second bonfire's room in the Huntsman's Copse (go up the ladder and run off from the location the club-wielding rogue, or jump and do a plunging attack, so that the player doesn't roll).
  * (NG+ and beyond only) Guaranteed drop from each of the 3 red phantom knights in Heide's Tower of Flame just before the Blue Cathedral.

### Token of Fidelity Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * When in the [Heirs to the Sun](/Heirs+of+the+Sun "Dark Souls 2 Heirs of the Sun") covenant, instead of receiving tokens of fidelity from defeating a boss as a phantom will receive [Sunlight Medals](/Sunlight+Medal "Dark Souls 2 Sunlight Medal") instead.
