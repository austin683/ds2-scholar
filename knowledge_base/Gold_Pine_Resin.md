**Gold Pine Resin** is a type of [Consumable](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Gold Pine Resin** , a rare pine resin with a golden glint. Infuses lightning into the right-hand weapon. The affected weapon delivers lightning damage for a brief duration. Particularly effective against foes resistant to magic and fire. [Consumable Items](/Consumables "Dark Souls 2 Consumables") grant various effects such as restorative properties for recovering HP, removing certain negative status effects, applying temporary buffs and debuffs, as well as activating various actions and effects.

> A rare pine resin with a golden glint. Applies lightning to right-hand weapon.  
> The affected weapon inflicts lightning damage for a short time. Particularly effective against foes who are resistant to magic and fire.

### Gold Pine Resin Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Boosts existing lightning damage by 30%
  * Adds 50 lightning attack value of the right-hand weapon for 90 seconds
  * Can not be used for weapons that are impossible to buff with items, such as weapons infused with an element.

### Dark Souls 2 Gold Pine Resin Locations

  * In [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt"), up the left trail before the Firekeeper's hut.
  * Purchased from [Head of Vengarl](/Head+of+Vengarl "Dark Souls 2 Head of Vengarl") in Shaded Woods(1500 souls, unlimited)
  * Can be farmed from the [Rupturing Hollows](/Rupturing+Hollow "Dark Souls 2 Rupturing Hollow") enemies in [Dragon Aerie](/Dragon+Aerie "Dark Souls 2 Dragon Aerie"). Note: they also drop other resins (e.g. [Charcoal Pine Resin](/Charcoal+Pine+Resin "Dark Souls 2 Charcoal Pine Resin") and [Dark Pine Resin](/Dark+Pine+Resin "Dark Souls 2 Dark Pine Resin")).
