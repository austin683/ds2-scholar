# Source: https://darksouls2.wiki.fextralife.com/Souls

**Souls** in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)** are used to gain expendable souls to make purchases, level up, or repair your equipment. **Souls** are remnants of fallen warriors, consuming Souls collects a certain number of souls that is added to the player's stock. Souls are use as currency by players to become stronger in the game. They can be used to increase soul level as well as buying and upgrading equipment.
