**Bell of the Idol** is a type of [Item](/Items "Dark Souls 2 Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Bell of the Idol, a sanctified bell infused with the prayers of a revered saint. Serves as a catalyst for miracles and hexes. The saint journeyed to these lands from a tranquil realm. However, further details about her remain undisclosed to passing travelers. [Unused Items](/Unused+Items "Dark Souls 2 Unused Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)** are Items listed in the game code, but not available in-game.

> Sacred bell imbued with the prayers of a renowned saint. Catalyst for miracles and hexes.   
> The saint came to these lands from a peaceful country. However, no more can be divulged about her to such transient travellers."
