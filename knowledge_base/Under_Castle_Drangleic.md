##  Bonfire Location:

Located in [Drangleic Castle](/Drangleic+Castle). From the king's gate bonfire go to the room with the stone statues and enter the door with the ruin sentinel on the far left. The ground will break giving you access to the bonfire.

  * [Darklurker](/Darklurker)
