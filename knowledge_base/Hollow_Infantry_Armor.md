_"Armor worn by Hollowed infantry."_

_"A piece of basic, minimal equipment. Worse yet, they're worn and nearly falling apart. Not recommended unless your options are spent."_

### Location

  * Drop [Hollow Infantry](/Hollow+Infantry).

### Notes

  * Part of [Hollow Infantry Set](/Hollow+Infantry+Set). 
    * The Infantry Set is the exact same weight, but is superior in every other way.
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+SLab).
