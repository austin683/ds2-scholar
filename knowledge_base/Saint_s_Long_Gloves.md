### Saint's long Gloves Description

_"Dress worn by miracle-casting clerics. belonged to Licia of Lindelt._  
_While its true Licia dedicated herself to miracles, its rare for anyone to pass through life without trouble. even clerics arent always who they seem."_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Received from [Licia of Lindeldt](/Licia+of+Lindeldt "Dark Souls 2 Licia of Lindeldt") when talking to her with 30 Faith. (Faith gained from stat-boosting gear and rings does not count toward the 30 faith requirement, only level ups).
  * Can be purchased from [Merchant Hag Melentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia") for 4200 souls after killing [Licia of Lindeldt](/Licia+of+Lindeldt "Dark Souls 2 Licia of Lindeldt") without completing her quest.
  * Obtained alonside the rest of her set and the [Soothing Sunlight](/Soothing+Sunlight "Dark Souls 2 Soothing Sunlight") Miracle upon completion of her quest.

### 

### Notes

  * Part of the [Saint's Set](/Saint's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Saint's Set"). 
    * Every piece of the set has a 16 [Faith](/Faith "Dark Souls 2 Faith") requirement.
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Twinkling Titanite").
