**Llewellyn Set** is an Attire or Armor in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds).

  
  
  
_"Armor reinforced with rare geisteel. Belonged to[Chancellor Wellager](/Chancellor+Wellager)."_   
_"Gloves/Shoes reinforced with rare geisteel. Excellent defensive gear that is both light and sturdy."_   
  
_"Quality equipment that is both light and strong. Crafted by the castle's resident master smith Llewellyn, and supplied only to a selected few._   
  
_His work easily identified by its lack of ostentation, Llewellyn focused solely on an economy of simplicity and strength."_

###  Set Location

  * Dropped by [Stone Knights](/Stone+Knight "Stone Knights") in [King's Passage](/King's+Passage "King's Passage"). 
    * [Scholar of the First Sin] Given by [Chancellor Wellager](/Chancellor+Wellager "Chancellor Wellager") in once [Giant Lord](/Giant+Lord) is defeated.

### 

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite "Twinkling Titanite").
  * The helm that was previously associated with this set during the network test is now part of the [Drangleic Set](/Drangleic+Set).
  * [Chancellor Wellager](/Chancellor+Wellager) wears this set.
  * Known as the [Drang Armor Set](https://darksouls3.wiki.fextralife.com/Drang+Armor+Set) in Dark Souls III.
