**Caitha, Goddess of Tears** , is a mentioned-only character in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki"). She is a prominent [goddess](/Gods+of+Drangleic "Dark Souls 2 Gods of Drangleic") worshipped in Drangleic and is responsible for the red and blue tearstone rings, as well as a [hex](/Hexes "Dark Souls 2 Hexes")-casting [chime](/Chimes "Dark Souls 2 Chimes").

### Caitha, Goddess of Tears Lore

Caitha, the goddess of tears, is known as a compassionate being that helps those in need in times of tragedy, but some believe that she is a demoness that guides them toward misfortune. Caitha mourns the undeserving dead, shedding tears as red as blood, and it is said that the stone set in the [Red Tearstone Ring](/Red+Tearstone+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Red Tearstone Ring") is one such tear. Caitha mourns those who have lost loved ones by shedding pure tears of blue, and it is said that the stone set in the [Blue Tearstone Ring](/Blue+Tearstone+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Blue Tearstone Ring") is one such tear. Caitha is one of the selectable gods for the [Name-engraved Ring](/Name-engraved+Ring "Dark Souls 2 Name-engraved Ring"), which states that there are countless vestiges of long-lost gods in the ruins of Drangleic. Or perhaps they are the very same gods as ours, only known by different names. Her symbol on this ring is also the [Poison](/Poison "Dark Souls 2 Poison") status effect.
