**Large Soul of a Lost Undeadl** is a type of [Consumable](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Large Soul of a Lost Undead** , a substantial essence of an Undead who long ago succumbed to Hollowing. Use it to acquire souls. The soul, the fountain of all life, remains a sought-after prize even in undeath. When consumed, it bestows 400 souls upon the user. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are consumable items categorized as remnants of fallen warriors, consuming souls collects a certain number of souls that is added to the player's stock. [Souls](/Souls "Dark Souls 2 Souls") are required for players to become stronger in the game by raising their soul level as well as buying and upgrading their equipment.

> Large soul of a lost Undead who has long ago gone hollow.   
> Use to acquire souls. The soul is the source of all life, and even in undeath, or hollowing, the mind seeks souls.
