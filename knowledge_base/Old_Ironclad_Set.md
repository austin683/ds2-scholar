**Old Ironclad Set** is an Attire or [Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor") in **Dark Souls 2**. Armor sets do not provide specific benefits, so players are free to mix and match their preferred parts to optimize their [Builds](/Character+Builds "Dark Souls 2 Character Builds").

> Old helm/armor/gauntlets/leggings worn by ironclad soldiers. Boasts high defence, but extremely heavy.  
>    
>  One day, warriors wearing decrepit armor emerged from drangleic castle, and quietly assumed positions amongst the royal army.  
>    
> Not one of them ever spoke a word, or revealed the face under the mask."

###  Set Location

  * Dropped by [Old Ironclad Soldiers](/Old+Ironclad+Soldier "Dark Souls 2 Old Ironclad Soldier") in [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants")

###  Notes

  * Upgraded with regular [Titanite](/Titanite "Dark Souls 2 Titanite")
  * Most efficiently farmed from the Soldiers Rest Bonfire
  * Old Ironclad Armor (chestpiece) grants immunity to backstabs
