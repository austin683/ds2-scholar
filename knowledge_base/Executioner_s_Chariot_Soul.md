**Executioner's Chariot Soul** is a consumable [Boss Soul](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. The Soul of the Executioner's Chariot is a representation of the entity that oversees the Undead Purgatory. This gruesome chariot was designed specifically to torment Undead, taking on the form of a nightmarish, frenzied steed—a reflection of its master's soul. When consumed, the soul grants 8,000 Souls. It can also be traded with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for either the [Shield Crossbow](/Shield+Crossbow "Dark Souls 2 Shield Crossbow") or the [Chariot Lance](/Chariot+Lance "Dark Souls 2 Chariot Lance"). [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are materials used to trade for unlocking various upgrades and abilities, spells, as well as for crafting unique equipment. These [Boss Souls](/Boss+Souls "Dark Souls 2 Boss Souls") are obtained by defeating the various [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses") of the game.

### Executioner's Chariot Soul Usage in DKS2

  * Consuming gives 8,000 Souls.
  * Trade with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis") for: 
    * [Shield Crossbow](/Shield+Crossbow "Dark Souls 2 Shield Crossbow")
    * [Chariot Lance](/Chariot+Lance "Dark Souls 2 Chariot Lance")

### Dark Souls 2 Executioner's Chariot Soul Location

  * Obtained by defeating the [Executioner's Chariot ](/Executioners+Chariot "Dark Souls 2 Executioners Chariot")in [Undead Purgatory.](/Undead+Purgatory "Dark Souls 2 Undead Purgatory")
