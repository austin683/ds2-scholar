**Havel's Leggings** is a **legging** and a part of [Havel's Set](/Havel's+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Havel's Leggings Description

"The origin of the name Havel is not clear. Some say it was the warrior who wore the armor, but others say that it was the name of a great kingdom ruined in a barbaric war."

### Where to Find \ Location

  * You must have the [Forgotten Key](/Forgotten+Key)
  * Use the key in a locked door in [The Gutter](/The+Gutter).

### 

### Notes

  * It is recommended that you bring a torch to properly light the area and some Prism Stones. You may want to bring either the [Flying Feline boots](/Flying+Feline+boots) or the Silver Cat ring as well in order to reduce fall damage.
  * In order to get the key for this door, you must proceed into the black gulch and drop down onto a hidden platform in between the two black worm creatures, You will see the locked door. Facing away from the door go left and there will be a 2nd platform below. and then down another below that one, and kill two giants.
  * Upgraded with [Twinkling Titanite](/Twinkling+Titanite)
  * Set looks different comparing to Dark Souls plus it seems to be made from metal, not from stone.
  * When fully upgraded the entire set is the best for Physical Defense except for the Helm which is beaten by the Smelter Demon Helm.
