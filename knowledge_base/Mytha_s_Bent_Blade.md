**Mytha's Bent Blade** is a [weapon](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Mytha's Bent Blade** is a unique dagger known for its poisonous effects, inflicting both toxic and poison. It is recognized for its poisonous effects and cannot be infused or enchanted. Upgrading it with [Petrified Dragon Bone](/Petrified+Dragon+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Petrified Dragon Bone") to +5 enhances its overall performance. Dual-wielding this dagger while casting [Toxic Mist](/Toxic+Mist "Dark Souls 2 Toxic Mist") is recommended for more efficient toxic infliction

> Dagger forged from the soul of Mytha, the Baneful Queen. Coated with a potent poison.
> 
> Mytha was the fairest queen in the land, until something unhinged her. Was it the poison found deep within the earth, or the passion that consumed her heart?

### Dark Souls 2 Mytha's Bent Blade Acquired From

  * Trading [Mytha, the Baneful Queen Soul](/Mytha%2C+the+Baneful+Queen+Soul "Dark Souls 2 Mytha%2C the Baneful Queen Soul") \+ 1500 souls with [Straid of Olaphis](/Straid+of+Olaphis "Dark Souls 2 Straid of Olaphis")

### Mytha's Bent Blade Hints and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Upgraded with [Petrified Dragon Bone](/Petrified+Dragon+Bone "Dark Souls 2 Petrified Dragon Bone") to +5, cannot be infused or enchanted.
  * At 40 Dex, this weapon will output more physical damage than a +10 Poison [Dagger](/Dagger "Dark Souls 2 Dagger"), but less poison damage.
  * Try dual wielding this dagger as well as casting [Toxic Mist](/Toxic+Mist "Dark Souls 2 Toxic Mist") for better efficency on inflicting Toxic.
  * Builds toxic as well as poison.
  * Recommended to also wear [Sanctum Soldier Gauntlet](/Sanctum+Soldier+Gauntlets "Dark Souls 2 Sanctum Soldier Gauntlets")'s and the [Crest of the Rat](/Crest+of+the+Rat "Dark Souls 2 Crest of the Rat"), each adds 50 poison damage to the weapon for a total of 100+174 poison AR at 45 DEX.

Parameter Bonus: Strength, Dexterity, Magic, Fire, Lightning and Dark bonuses - The scaling multiplier applied to the [Attack: stat]. Scaling quality is from highest to lowest as follows: S/A/B/C/D/E.  
The higher the player's [Str, Dex, Mag, Fire, Lightning, Dark] stat, the higher the [Attack Bonus: Stat] is (found on the player status screen). The higher the scaling letter, the higher the percent multiplier applied to the [Attack: Stat].  
This resulting bonus damage is added to the base physical damage of the weapon and is shown in the equipment screen in blue numbers as a "+ X".  
Attack Type: Defines what kind of moveset the weapon has.  
Physical Damage falls under three categories: [Thrust](/Thrust+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Thrust Damage") (T), [Slash](/Slash+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Slash Damage") (Sl), [Strike](/Strike+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Strike Damage") (St).

Stability: How well the player keeps stance after being hit.  
Durability: The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!".  
Weight: How much the item weighs when equipped.
