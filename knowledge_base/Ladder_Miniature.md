**Ladder Miniature** is a [Key Item](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Ladder Miniature** , a small decorative ladder. It's a charming trinket, purely for display. Offered to [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan "Dark Souls 2 Royal Sorcerer Navlaan") as evidence of a successful assassination, fulfilling his quest. [Key Items](/Keys "Dark Souls 2 Keys") are found in specific locations or are given by a related [NPC](/NPCs "Dark Souls 2 NPCs") which are used to unlock areas, quests, diversify the inventories of [Merchants](/Merchants "Dark Souls 2 Merchants") and to further progress the game's story.

> A small ornamental ladder. A quaint little thing, but only for show.  
> This ornament does not seem to have any particular function, and should not be worth much, but is sold at an exorbitant price.

### Ladder Miniature Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Given to [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan "Dark Souls 2 Royal Sorcerer Navlaan") as proof' of assassination to complete his quest.

### Dark Souls 2 Ladder Miniature Location

  * Purchased from [Laddersmith Gilligan](/Laddersmith+Gilligan "Dark Souls 2 Laddersmith Gilligan"), located in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak"), for 6,399 souls.
  * Can also be purchased when he relocates to [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula") for 7,999 souls.
  * Gilligan also drops it, if you kill him.
