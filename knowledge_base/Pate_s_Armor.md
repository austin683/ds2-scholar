**Pate's Armor** is a [Chest Piece](/Chest+Pieces "Dark Souls 2 Chest Pieces")**** in Dark Souls 2.

_Although it appears to be common armor, it has in fact been meticulously customized._

_Belonged to mild-mannered Pate._

_This has been considerably altered. Perhaps it was pillaged._

### Pate's Armor Location

  * Sold by [Merchant Hag Malentia](/Merchant+Hag+Melentia "Dark Souls 2 Merchant Hag Melentia") after killing [Mild-Mannered Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate").
  * Given to the player after helping [Mild-Mannered Pate](/Mild+Mannered+Pate "Dark Souls 2 Mild Mannered Pate") in his fight with [Creighton The Wanderer](/Creighton+the+Wanderer "Dark Souls 2 Creighton the Wanderer").
  * You can also obtain this armor as well as his shield and spear by completing Pate's story which ends in [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak").

### Pate's Armor Notes

  * Part of the [Pate's Set](/Pate's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Pate's Set").
  * Upgraded from [Titanite](/Titanite+Shard "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard").
