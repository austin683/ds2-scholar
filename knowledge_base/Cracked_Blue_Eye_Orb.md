**Cracked Blue Eye Orb** is a [Multiplayer Item](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Cracked Blue Eye Orb** , an online play item used to invade the world of the guilty. By punishing the guilty, you strengthen the bond with your covenant. Employed to invade the realms of those marked as guilty. [Multiplayer Items](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitive gameplay like PVP.

> Online play item. Invade the world of the guilty. Punish the guilty to strengthen the bond with your covenant.

### Dark Souls 2 Cracked Blue Eye Orb Usage

  * Used to invade the worlds of [the guilty](/sin "Dark Souls 2 sin")
  * Invasion time lasts approximately 12 and a half minutes.

### Cracked Blue Eye Orb Requirements in DKS2

  * Must be Human.
  * Must be a member of the [Blue Sentinels](/Blue+Sentinels "Dark Souls 2 Blue Sentinels") Covenant.
  * Must be in an area where PvP is allowed.

### DKS2 Cracked Blue Eye Orb Location

  * Drop from Old Knights around [Heide's Tower of Flame](/Heide%27s+Tower+of+Flame "Dark Souls 2 Heide%27s Tower of Flame").
  * Can be obtained by winning duels via the Dueling Statues at the Blue Sentinels' Bonfire.
  * Can be obtained randomly from [Dyna and Tillo](/Dyna+and+Tillo "Dark Souls 2 Dyna and Tillo") by leaving smooth silky stones.
  * Can be purchased from [Blue Sentinel Targray](/Blue+Sentinel+Targray "Dark Souls 2 Blue Sentinel Targray") for 10,000 souls in [NG+](/New+Game+Plus "Dark Souls 2 New Game Plus") (unlimited quantity). 

### Cracked Blue Eye Orb Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Cracked Blue Eye Orbs will summon you to any location. Not necessarily the one the player is currently in.
  * In addition to invading areas elsewhere of the player's current location, the Cracked Blue Eye Orb can invade places where the [Guardian's Seal](/Guardian%27s+Seal "Dark Souls 2 Guardian%27s Seal") can't be used to be automatically summoned.
  * You do not gain any souls from killing your target whilst invading as an Arbiter Spirit.
  * Sold to Gavlan for 500 souls a piece.

### DKS2 Cracked Blue Eye Orb Lore

  * Eye Orbs were likely once derived from Behelits, eyed stones that serve as bridges between worlds .
