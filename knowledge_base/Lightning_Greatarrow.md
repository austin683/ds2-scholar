**Lightning Greatarrow** is a type of [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") found in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Lightning Greatarrows** , formidable projectiles for Greatbows, infused with the power of lightning. Enhanced with Gold Pine Resin, these arrows are particularly potent against adversaries resistant to magic and fire. Exclusively compatible with Greatbows, they are available through the merchant Royal Sorcerer Navlaan. [Ammunition](/Ammunition "Dark Souls 2 Ammunition") is a consumable item that is required to be able to use ranged weapons such as [Bows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Bows "Dark Souls 2 Bows") and [Crossbows](https://demonssouls.wiki.fextralife.com/Crossbows "Dark Souls 2 Crossbows") and are used to inflict damage onto hostile characters such as [Enemies](/Enemies "Dark Souls 2 Enemies") and [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses").

> Greatarrows Imbued with lightning. Tipped with Gold Pine Resin. Weapons Fortified with lightning are effective against foes resistant to magic and fire.
> 
> If you require magic but have learned non yourself, these will serve you well"

### Lightning Greatarrow Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * Like any other Greatarrow, it can only be used with Great bows.
  * Thrust Damage
  * 100 Physical 250 Lightning

### Dark Souls 2 Lightning Greatarrow Location

  * Sold by [Royal Sorcerer Navlaan](/Royal+Sorcerer+Navlaan "Dark Souls 2 Royal Sorcerer Navlaan") 180 each ∞ supply  
(Only if you free him then talk to him while human, or talking to him while dead after having completed his assassination contracts)
