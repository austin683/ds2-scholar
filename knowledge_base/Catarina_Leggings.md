**Catarina Leggings** is a **Legging** and a part of [Catarina Set](/Catarina+Set) in **Dark Souls 2**.

### Catarina Leggings Description

_"Kilt worn by Agdayne of the Undead Crypt._

_Life itself is suffering; or karma,_  
_as some have called it._

_The embrace of death awaits all things,_  
_But does death mean an end to suffering?"_

### Where to Find \ Location

  * Rare drop from NPC Dark Spirit [Catarina Knight](/Catarina+Knight "Catarina Knight") in forest of the giants (NG+).

### 

### Notes

  * Upgraded with Twinkling titanite.
  * He also drops Heavy Cross Bow +5 and Broadsword +5
