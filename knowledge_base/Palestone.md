**Palestone** is a type of [Upgrade Material](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**.**Palestone** , an altered state of titanite. Apply it to a weapon or shield to nullify all imbued powers. This action doesn't reverse reinforcement but removes effects like magic, fire, poison, etc. [Upgrade Materials](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades") are items that are used to strengthen the statistical value and to increase the effect of a certain piece of equipment.

> An altered state of titanite. Use on a weapon or shield to undo all imbued powers. This does not revert reinforcement, but undoes imbued powers such as magic, fire, poison, etc.

### Dark Souls 2 Palestone Usage

  * Can only be used at Blacksmith [Steady Hand McDuff](/Steady+Hand+McDuff "Dark Souls 2 Steady Hand McDuff")
  * Removes [infusions](/upgrades "Dark Souls 2 upgrades") from equipment

### Palestone Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Rare drop from [Old Knights](/Old+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Old Knight") in Heide's Tower of Flame.
  * Possible drop from [Blue Crystal Lizards](/crystal+lizard "Dark Souls 2 crystal lizard").
  * Dropped by [Stone Trader Chloanne](/Stone+Trader+Chloanne "Dark Souls 2 Stone Trader Chloanne") when killed
