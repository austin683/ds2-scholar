**Unique Armor Pieces** are a type of **[Armor](/Armor "Dark Souls 2 Armor")** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki") ** **Unique Armor Pieces** come with unique appearances, lore, and sometimes special effects or properties. These armor pieces are typically different from the more common and generic armor sets found throughout the game. Players may discover unique armor pieces by defeating certain [Bosses](/Bosses "Dark Souls 2 Bosses"), exploring hidden areas, completing quests, or acquiring them through other specific in-game actions.
