_"Armor worn by the Volgen Falconers."_

_"Domestic Volgen soldiers are infamously timid, so it is no wonder that this fierce band of mercenary falconers was hired to compensate._

_In practice they serve as bodyguards for the affluent elite, and they serve well, such that nobody dares scrutinize their backgrounds."_

### Location

  * Starting gear (no helmet) for [Knight Class](/Knight "Dark Souls 2 Knight").
  * Sold by [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer").
  * Obtained from [Volgen Falconer](/Falconer "Dark Souls 2 Falconer") enemies in NG+ in the start area of [Things Betwixt](/Things+Betwixt "Dark Souls 2 Things Betwixt").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Falconer Set](/Falconer+Set "Dark Souls 2 Falconer Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab").
