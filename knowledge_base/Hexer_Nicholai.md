#  Hexer Nicholai

Updated May 5, 2024

  * Can be completely avoided. After he spawns and begins to make his way towards you, run back to the elevator and go back up. He will then leave your world and won't invade again until you rest at a bonfire.
  * Found at the bottom of an Elevator in [Frozen Eleum Loyce](/Frozen+Eleum+Loyce "Dark Souls 2 Frozen Eleum Loyce") as invaders After speaking with [Alsanna, Silent Oracle](/Alsanna%2C+Silent+Oracle "Dark Souls 2 Alsanna%2C Silent Oracle")
  * Wears White Priest Hood and Priestess Robe, Manchettes of Judgement, and Tights of Judgement.
  * Wields Chime of Want, and the Cleric's Small Shield. Switches to Shortsword if you use Profound still.
  * Generally he casts [Profound](/Profound+Still "Dark Souls 2 Profound Still")-[Sti](/Profound+Still "Dark Souls 2 Profound Still")[l](/Profound+Still "Dark Souls 2 Profound Still")[l](/Profound+Still "Dark Souls 2 Profound Still"), [Dark Dance](/Dark+Dance "Dark Souls 2 Dark Dance") and [Promised Walk of Peace](/Promised+Walk+of+Peace "Dark Souls 2 Promised Walk of Peace").
  * Profound Still is not confirmed. He casts a heal (most likely Great Heal), A sunlight/lightning spear spell, Splintering Lightning Spear, & Heavenly Thunder.
  * Has no dialogue. Only invades.
