_Traditionally used by bow hunters._

_The hunting goddess Evlana was no goddess at all, but rather a brave and highly skilled bow huntress. Long after her demise, the passing of lore transformed her into a deity."_

_Armor made from soft leather. A commonplace piece of equipment._  
_Few embellishments._

### Location

  * At the [Soldier's Rest](/Soldier's+Rest "Dark Souls 2 Soldier's Rest") bonfire in the [Forest of The Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants") it is out and to the left on top of a wooden structure in a chest. 
    * You will need the key from [The Last Giant](/The+Last+Giant "Dark Souls 2 The Last Giant") to access this area.

### Notes

  * Part of the [Leather Set](/Leather+Set "Dark Souls 2 Leather Set").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Titanite+Slab "Dark Souls 2 Titanite Slab").
