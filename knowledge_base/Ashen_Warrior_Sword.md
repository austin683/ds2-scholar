**Ashen Warrior Sword** is a **[Straight Sword](/Straight+Swords "Dark Souls 2 Straight Swords")** and one of the available **[Weapons](/Weapons "Dark Souls 2 Weapons")** in **[Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**. Ashen Warrior Sword is a Straight Sword obtained as a drop from Ashen Warrior enemies. Ideal for a [Dexterity](/Dexterity "Dark Souls 2 Dexterity")-focused build without infusion or a Bleed-focused build when infused with Bleed, this sword excels in dual-wielding for inflicting bleed on opponents.

> Straight sword wielded by ashen warriors.  
> As brittle as it appears.  
> The jagged edge of this tired and worn blade tears into the hides of foes.

### How to get Ashen Warrior Sword in Dark Souls 2

  * Drop from [Ashen Warrior](/Ashen+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Ashen Warrior") enemies

### DS2 Ashen Warrior Sword Hints and Tips

  * Either Dex dedicated without infusion, or Bleed dedicated if infused with Bleed, slightly stronger than most Bleed based weapon, highly recommended for dual wielding for inflicting bleed.
  * This weapon is actually a very good candidate for infusions, and can be enchanted. It loses very little base damage upon infusion, and gains almost +130 AR on average
  * Higher Bleed base damage and scaling than [Bandit's Knife](/Bandit%27s+Knife "Dark Souls 2 Bandit%27s Knife") once fully upgraded and infused with Bleed.
  * With the help of [Shadow Gauntlet](/Shadow+Set "Dark Souls 2 Shadow Set") and [Crest of Blood](/Crest+of+Blood "Dark Souls 2 Crest of Blood"), Dual Bleed Ashen Warrior Swords +10 can iniflict bleed with very few powerstanced swings, as low as 2-3.
  * The only straight sword other than the [Foot Soldier Sword](/Foot+Soldier+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Foot Soldier Sword"), and [Shortsword](/Shortsword "Dark Souls 2 Shortsword") with a complete set of thrusting attacks.
  * At +10 with 40 str and 40 dex, this weapon has an Attack Rating of 299 (180+119).
  * The third longest straight sword losing out to only the [Drakekeepers Sword](/Drakekeepers+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Drakekeepers Sword") and the [Fume Sword](/Fume+Sword "Dark Souls 2 Fume Sword").

Parameter Bonus: Strength, Dexterity, Magic, Fire, Lightning and Dark bonuses - The scaling multiplier applied to the [Attack: stat]. Scaling quality is from highest to lowest as follows: S/A/B/C/D/E.  
The higher the player's [Str, Dex, Mag, Fire, Lightning, Dark] stat, the higher the [Attack Bonus: Stat] is (found on the player status screen). The higher the scaling letter, the higher the percent multiplier applied to the [Attack: Stat].  
This resulting bonus damage is added to the base physical damage of the weapon and is shown in the equipment screen in blue numbers as a "+ X".  
Attack Type: Defines what kind of moveset the weapon has.  
Physical Damage falls under three categories: [Thrust](/Thrust+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Thrust Damage") (T), [Slash](/Slash+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Slash Damage") (Sl), [Strike](/Strike+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Strike Damage") (St).  
Elemental Damage Types: [Magic](/Magic+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Magic Damage"), [Fire](/Fire+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Fire Damage"), [Lightning](/Lightning+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Lightning Damage"), [Dark](/Dark+Damage "Dark Souls 2 Dark Damage").  
Auxiliary Effects: [Bleed](/Bleed "Dark Souls 2 Bleed"), [Poison](/Poison "Dark Souls 2 Poison"), [Petrification](/Petrification "Dark Souls 2 Petrification"), and [Curse](/Curse "Dark Souls 2 Curse").  
Stability: How well the player keeps stance after being hit.  
Durability: The weapon's HP, when the durability hits 0, the effectiveness of its attacks become weakened to the point of almost uselessness. When an items durability is low, a message will come up saying "Weapon At Risk!".  
Weight: How much the item weighs when equipped.
