**Fall Control** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Fall Control** , a sorcery aiding in covert activities by reducing the damage incurred from falls. In Mirrah, an eastern land known for proud knights, there exists a traditional order of shadow knights employing any means necessary to execute their missions. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> "A sorcery that facilitates surreptitious activity. Reduces the damage taken from falls.  
>   
> The eastern land of Mirrah is not only the home of proud knights. There is also a traditional order of shadow knights, who use any means necessary to carry out their orders."

### Dark Souls 2 Fall Control Acquired From

  * Buy from [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") in the [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep") for 4800 souls.
  * Buy from [Weaponsmith Ornifex](/Weaponsmith+Ornifex "Dark Souls 2 Weaponsmith Ornifex") in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora") for 4800 souls.

### Fall Control Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Will not take damage from falls, but does not protect you from falls that would kill you anyway.
  * The duration of this spell depends on user [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") and equipment. 
  * Overwrites [Hush](/Hush "Dark Souls 2 Hush"), see [Buffs & Debuffs](/Buffs+&+Debuffs "Dark Souls 2 Buffs & Debuffs").
