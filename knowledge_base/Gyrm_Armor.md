_"The stocky Gyrm are kind-natured, but humans deemed them impure, and drove them underground."_

### Location

  * Found on regular Gyrm (The ones with the anvil on a chain) in [Doors of Pharros](/Doors+of+Pharros).

### Notes

  * Part of the [Gyrm Set](/Gyrm+Set).
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Ores).
