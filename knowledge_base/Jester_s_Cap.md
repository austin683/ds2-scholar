"A boldly-colored jester's cap. A nice bit of fun to try on..."

"Jesters are more than festival fixtures; some have a second face, hidden from public view."

### Location

  * Bought from [Magerold of Lanafir](/Magerold+of+Lanafir "Dark Souls 2 Magerold of Lanafir") in [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep").

### Notes

  * Part of the [Jester's Set](/Jester's+Set "Dark Souls 2 Jester's Set"). 
    * Requires 14 [Dexterity](/Dexterity "Dark Souls 2 Dexterity") to wear.
    * Worn by [Jester Thomas](/Jester+Thomas "Dark Souls 2 Jester Thomas").
  * Upgraded with [Titanite](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores").
  * Increases item discovery (50).
