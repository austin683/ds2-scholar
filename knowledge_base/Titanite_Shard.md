**Titanite Shard** is a type of [Upgrade Material](/Ores "Dark Souls 2 Ores") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. Titanite Shard, a shard of titanite used for reinforcing equipment. Unearthed from an ancient layer of earth, titanite is believed to be a divine gift. While titanite shards of this size possess limited power, they remain a rare discovery. It strengthens gear up to +3. [Upgrade Materials](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades") are items that are used to strengthen the statistical value and to increase the effect of a certain piece of equipment.

> Titanite shard used to reinforce equipment. Reinforces equipment up to +3.  
> Titanite was discovered in an ancient layer of earth, and is said to be a gift of the gods. Titanite this size has very little power, but it is still a rare find.

### Titanite Shard Usage in Dark Souls 2

  * [Reinforce](/Upgrades "Dark Souls 2 Upgrades") standard equipment up to +3 at [Blacksmiths](/Blacksmiths "Dark Souls 2 Blacksmiths")

### Dark Souls 2 Titanite Shard Location

  * Can be bought from [Blacksmith Lenigrast](/Blacksmith+Lenigrast "Dark Souls 2 Blacksmith Lenigrast") x10 at 800 souls.
  * Can be bought from [Stone Trader Chloanne](/Stone+Trader+Chloanne "Dark Souls 2 Stone Trader Chloanne") for 1000 souls. (Infinite supply after clearing [Iron Keep](/Iron+Keep "Dark Souls 2 Iron Keep").)
  * 1 is found in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula"), in a chest in [Maughlin the Armourer](/Maughlin+the+Armourer "Dark Souls 2 Maughlin the Armourer")'s attic.
  * 3 can be found in Majula, in a chest on the Mansion's second floor.
  * 4 can be found inside the cave near the [Skeleton Lords](/Skeleton+Lords "Dark Souls 2 Skeleton Lords") boss battle. The first one is right after the bonfire behind the closed gate next to a curse jar, the other four after the portcullis, near the broken bridge.
  * 1 is found in a chest below the ballista room in Forest of the Fallen Giants, near the Cardinal Tower bonfire
  * 1 is found on a corpse Below the ruined building in front of a portcullis in Forest of the Fallen Giants, near Cale the Cartographer's location.
  * 1 is found on a corpse on the opposite side of the portcullis in Forest of the Fallen Giants, accessible from the Soldier's Rest bonfire.
  * 3 can be found on a body in the area before the Covetous Demon boss fight, where you jump into the grated area with the poison beetles.
  * 10 in [Brume Tower](/Brume+Tower "Dark Souls 2 Brume Tower"). From the Upper Floor bonfire, hug the path then jump across the gap that contains an elevator. You will be in an area with several doors guarded by an [Iron Warrior](/Iron+Warrior "Dark Souls 2 Iron Warrior").

### DKS2 Titanite Shard Drops from:

  * Dropped by [Ogres](/Ogre "Dark Souls 2 Ogre") in [Forest of Fallen Giants](/Forest+of+Fallen+Giants "Dark Souls 2 Forest of Fallen Giants").
  * Common drop from Undead Blacksmiths in [Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley") or [Earthen Peak](/Earthen+Peak "Dark Souls 2 Earthen Peak"), near the second bonfire.
  * Common drop from Skeletons at the start of [Harvest Valley](/Harvest+Valley "Dark Souls 2 Harvest Valley").
  * Dropped by The villagers in [Brightstone Cove Tseldora](/Brightstone+Cove+Tseldora "Dark Souls 2 Brightstone Cove Tseldora"). (Lower drop rate than the Undead Blacksmiths, though there are more.)
  * Common drop from Crystal Lizard in Cardinal Tower bonfire in Forest of the Fallen Giants.
