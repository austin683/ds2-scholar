**Focus Souls** is a [Sorcery](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Focus Souls** , an age-old sorcery originating from Shulva, the Sanctum City. Releases a concentrated beam of souls. The progression of sorceries in Shulva was hindered due to their taboo nature. Nevertheless, a few exceptional creations emerged from a group of dedicated enthusiasts. [Sorceries](/Sorceries "Dark Souls 2 Sorceries") demand the use of a [Staff](/Staves "Dark Souls 2 Staves") or Special Weapons, requiring a certain [Intelligence](/Intelligence "Dark Souls 2 Intelligence") level for casting.

> An ancient sorcery of Shulva, the Sanctum City. Fires a focused beam of souls. The development of sorceries was stunted in Shulva, as they were considered taboo.  
> Even so, there were some occasional gems created by a group of enthusiasts.

### Focus Souls Dark Souls 2 Acquired From

  * At the bridge, where the stone dragon kills the 2 knights, before the bridge you can go right. Go on, and you'll find it on a corpse behind some vases.

### Focus Souls Notes and Tips in Dark Souls 2

  * Added with [DLC](/DLC "Dark Souls 2 DLC") | [Crown of the Sunken King](/Crown+of+the+Sunken+King "Dark Souls 2 Crown of the Sunken King")
  * This sorcery creates 4 [Soul Bolts](/Soul+Bolt "Dark Souls 2 Soul Bolt") in a square pattern in front of you that home in on multiple enemies; short range.
  * The range is longer if you're standing on an upper staircase and aiming for lower grounds.
  * Very useful in narrow paths, it will hit several times if the enemy is near you.
  * Additional copies can be obtained by burning [Bonfire Ascetic](/Bonfire+Ascetic "Dark Souls 2 Bonfire Ascetic") at the Tower of Prayer bonfire.
