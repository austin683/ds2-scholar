**Champion's Tablet** is a covenant item in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Champion's Tablet** , an item for online play that displays the names of the most triumphant members of the Company of Champions covenant. [Multiplayer Items](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitive gameplay like PVP.

> Online Play Item Shows the names of the greatest victors of the Company of Champions covenant. This tablet, chiseled from the Victor's Stone, is engraved with the names of the brave warriors who have offered the most awestones, as if they were epitaphs, honoring their lives.

### Dark Souls 2 Champion's Tablet Usage

  * Shows the names of the greatest victors of the [Company of Champions](/Company+of+Champions "Dark Souls 2 Company of Champions") covenant when used.
  * When used a leader board will be displayed showing the largest number of offerings given, ranging from largest amount to least amount.
  * The Champion's Tablet can be scrolled left and right to display even more pages of Champions.

### Champion's Tablet Location in Dark Souls 2

  * Given upon joining the [Company of Champions](/Company+of+Champions "Dark Souls 2 Company of Champions") at the Victor's Stone in [Majula](/Majula "Dark Souls 2 Majula").
