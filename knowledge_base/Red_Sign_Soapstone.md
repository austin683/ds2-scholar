**Red Sign Soapstone** is a [Multiplayer Item](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") in [Dark Souls 2](/Dark+Souls+2+Wiki "Dark Souls 2 Wiki")**(DKS2)**. **Red Sign Soapstone** , an online play item. Use it to leave an invasion sign and be summoned to another world as a dark spirit. [Multiplayer Items](/Multiplayer+Items "Dark Souls 2 Multiplayer Items") allow players to interact with each other in various ways, including leaving messages for other players to read, co-operative gameplay and competitive gameplay like PVP.

> Online play item. Leave invasion sign. Be summoned to another world as a dark spirit, and defeat the summoner to acquire a Token of Spite. Some Undead persevere along the honorable path even after crossing into the dark. The Red Sign Soapstone is for them.

### Dark Souls 2 Red Sign Soapstone Usage

  * Leave an invasion sign for PvP play, where the host summons you into their world for a fight.

### Red Sign Soapstone Location in DKS2

  * Can be bought from [Titchy Gren](/Titchy+Gren "Dark Souls 2 Titchy Gren") for 5,000 souls after joining the [Brotherhood of Blood](/Brotherhood+of+Blood "Dark Souls 2 Brotherhood of Blood") covenant.

### DKS2 Red Sign Soapstone Information

  * You must be in human form and in an online area.
  * You don't gain sin if you invade with this option.

### Red Sign Soapstone Notes in Dark Souls 2

  * Using a Red Sign Soapstone **DOES NOT** count towards ranking up in the [Brotherhood of Blood](/Brotherhood+of+Blood "Dark Souls 2 Brotherhood of Blood") covenant.
  * The Red Sign Soapstone can still be used after abandoning the [Brotherhood of Blood](/Brotherhood+of+Blood "Dark Souls 2 Brotherhood of Blood") covenant.
  * The player's Red Summon Sign will automatically disappear if a White/Gold Summon Sign is placed, and vice versa.
  * If the player leaves a Red Summon Sign outside of the [Looking Glass Knight](/Looking+Glass+Knight "Dark Souls 2 Looking Glass Knight") boss, they can be summoned by the Looking Glass Knight in place of the NPC that attempts to break through the mirror. The player will then have the opportunity to kill the challenging player. The reward for defeating the world's owner (and any summoned players) seems to be a sum of souls. The determined amount is currently unknown.
  * If the player leaves a Red Summon Sign in the [Dark Chasm of Old](/Dark+Chasm+of+Old "Dark Souls 2 Dark Chasm of Old") ([Pilgrims of Dark](/Pilgrims+of+Dark "Dark Souls 2 Pilgrims of Dark") Covenant), he can be summoned as an abyssal spirit and prevent the world master from finishing the area.
